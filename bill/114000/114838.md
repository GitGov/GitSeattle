 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Nine Million, Nine Hundred Forty Six Thousand, Four Hundred Thirty One and 75/100 dollars ($9,946,431.75) on Summit mechanical checks numbered, 4000790727 - 4000793071, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Seven Million, Nine Hundred Thirteen Thousand, Six Hundred One and 08/100 dollars, ($7,913,601.08) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCA.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million, Four Hundred Eighteen Thousand, Eight Hundred Fifty Three and 52/100 dollars ($24,418,853.52) on City General Salary Fund mechanical checks numbered 50822136 - 50824125 plus manual checks, agencies checks, and direct deposits numbered 120001 - 129463 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date March 9, 2004 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on March 18, 2004 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 22nd day of March, 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this March 22, 2004
