 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Ten Million, Six Thousand, Two Hundred Fifty Six and 48/100 dollars ($10,006,256.48) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000820553- 4000822743, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Forty One Million, Two Hundred Eighty Thousand, Two Hundred Thirty Seven and 93/100 dollars, (41,280,237.93) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million, Nine Hundred Thirty One Thousand, One Hundred Seventy Eight and 65/100 dollars ($24,931,178.65) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50835908 - 50838316 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 240001 - 249623 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date June 1, 2004 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on June 10, 2004 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 14th day of June, 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this June 14, 2004.
