 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the Payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Eight Million, Seven Hundred Eleven Thousand, Nine Hundred Seventy Three and 44/100 dollars ($8,711,973.44) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000843325- 4000845872 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, plus Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) of Thirty Seven Million, Six Hundred Thirty Five Thousand, Three Hundred Ninety Six and 72/100 dollars ($37,635,396.72) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW.

 SECTION 2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 16th day of Aug. 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Aug. 16, 2004.
