 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Seventeen Million, Seven Hundred Twenty Eight Thousand, Six Hundred Eighty Seven and 09/100 dollars ($17,728,687.09) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000575954- 4000573160, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council Aug. 8, 2002 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Five Million, Six Hundred Thirty Thousand, Four Hundred Eighty Three and 83/100 dollars ($25,630,483.83) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50727160- 50730116 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 320001 -329674 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date July 30, 2002 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Aug. 8, 2002 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 12th day of Aug. 2002 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Aug. 12, 2002.
