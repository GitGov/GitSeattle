 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twelve Million, Four Hundred Forty Four Thousand, Seven Hundred Thirty Seven and 50/100 dollars ($12,444,737.50) on Summit mechanical checks numbered, 40008407831 - 4000849924, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Seventeen Million, Six Hundred Seven Thousand, One Hundred Fifty One and 93/100 dollars ($17,607,151.93) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Five Million, Nine Hundred Sixty Two Thousand, Nine Hundred Forty Seven and 07/100 dollars ($25,962,947.07) on City General Salary Fund mechanical checks numbered 50844855 - 50847203 plus manual checks, agencies checks, and direct deposits numbered 320001 -329576 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date July 27, 2004 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Aug. 5, 2004 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 9th day of Aug, 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Aug. 9, 2004.
