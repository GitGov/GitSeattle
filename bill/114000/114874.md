 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Nine Million, Seven Hundred Seventy Four Thousand, Nine Hundred Two and 60/100 dollars ($9,774,902.60) on Summit mechanical checks numbered, 4000800289 - 4000802695, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Seven Million, Six Hundred Sixty Six Thousand, Eight Hundred Eighty Eight and 91/100 dollars, ($7,666,888.91) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCA.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million, Two Hundred Twenty Two Thousand, Eight Hundred Thirty Seven and 71/100 dollars ($24,222,837.71) on City General Salary Fund mechanical checks numbered 50826129 - 50828144 plus manual checks, agencies checks, and direct deposits numbered 160001 - 169501 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date April 6, 2004 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on April 15, 2004 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 19th day of April, 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this April 19, 2004.
