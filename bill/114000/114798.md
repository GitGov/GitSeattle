 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Sixteen Million, Two Hundred Ninety Nine Thousand, Three Hundred Thirty Two and 35/100 dollars ($16,299,332.35) on Summit mechanical checks numbered, 4000770852 - 4000773953, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Thirty One Million, Four Hundred Fifteen Thousand, Five Hundred Eighty Nine and 84/100 dollars ($31,415,589.84) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCA

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million, Nine Hundred Twenty Eight Thousand, One Hundred Thirty One and 63/100 dollars ($24,928,131.63) on City General Salary Fund mechanical checks numbered 50814503 - 50816443 plus manual checks, agencies checks, and direct deposits numbered 30001 - 39457 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Jan. 13, 2004 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Jan.22, 2004 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 26th day of Jan. 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Jan. 26, 2004.
