 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Eight Million, Seven Hundred Sixty Nine Thousand, Seven Hundred Seventy Six and 87/100 dollars ($8,769,776.87) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000822744- 4000825234 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, plus Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) of Eighteen Million, Five Hundred Sixty Nine Thousand, Six Hundred Seven and 94/100 dollars ($18,569,607.94) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW.

 SECTION 2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 21st day of June, 2004 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this June 21, 2004.
