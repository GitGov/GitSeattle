**Ordinance Number: 24089**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1835, for the improvement of the Alley in Block Twelve (12), Capitol Hill Addition, Division No. Three (3), to the City of Seattle, being the alley from East Prospect Street to East Ward Street, between Fourteenth Avenue North and Fifteenth Avenue North, by paving the same with vitrified brick or asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with such sewers, with their appurtenances, as may be necessary for the proper drainage of said alley, under Ordinance No. 19027, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
