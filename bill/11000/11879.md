 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 18th Avenue Northeast, and 19th Avenue Northeast, each form East 65th Street to East 75th Street,; and East 65th Street, East 68th Street, East 70th Street, East 73rd Street, each from 17th Avenue Northeast to 20th Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, and sub drainage; together with necessary wooden cross walks, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2736 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
