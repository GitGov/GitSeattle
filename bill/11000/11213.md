**Council Bill Number: 11213**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23752**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2136, for the improvement of Eastern Avenue, from North 50th Street to the south line of Smith's University Addition, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, together with necessary surface and sub-drainage; provided however, that on 1st Avenue Northeast between the south line of Smith's University Addition and a point 75 feet north of the north margin of North 42nd Street, no concrete sidewalk shall be constructed on the west side of said street and 1st Avenue Northeast from North 42nd Street to a point 75 feet north thereof, by constructing curbs and gutters, together with a concrete walk on the east side of the street, and the alley connecting Eastern Avenue and 1st Avenue Northeast, between the south line of Smith's University Addition and the Baltimore Addition, by grading and curbing the same, all in the City of Seattle, under Ordinance No. 21535 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
