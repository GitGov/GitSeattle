**Council Bill Number: 11876**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24350**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2138 for the improvement of Nagle Place, from East Denny Way to East Pine Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and by constructing therein a sewer of proper size and dimensions, where necessary, with connections to existing sewers, under Ordinance No. 21537, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
