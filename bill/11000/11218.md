**Council Bill Number: 11218**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23884**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2221, for the improvement of Virginia Street in the City of Seattle from 2nd Avenue to 3rd Avenue, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing brick gutters and granite curbs, and including as part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, by constructing concrete sidewalks and the construction or extension of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage and water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 22512 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
