**Ordinance Number: 24148**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2197 for the improvement of the alley in Block Fifty (50), Terry's First Addition to the Town (now City) of Seattle, being the alley from Madison Street to Spring Street and between Sixth Avenue and Seventh Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same and paving with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing sewers necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 22109, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
