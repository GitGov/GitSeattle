**Council Bill Number: 11215**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23754**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2143 for the improvement of 18th Avenue North, from the south line of East Roy Street to the north line of East Galer Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by installation of a system of lights along the curb line on each side of said streets and avenues as will not consume electric current to exceed that consumed by the arc and incandescent lights now installed in said district, together with the installation of such poles, conduits, wires and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper installation and equipment of such system, the poles to be of such design and construction as will accommodate one ball on the top, and in the future will accommodate cross-arms for a three-ball cluster system, when the same may be ordered by the City Council, under Ordinance No. 21598, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
