**Council Bill Number: 11271**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23877**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2014, for the improvement of East Madison Street from 19th Avenue to 29th Avenue and from Washington Boulevard to the east margin of Laurel Shade Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with stone blocks and asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with concrete, brick, stone or asphalt gutters, with concrete curbs and armored concrete and granite corners, and by the construction of all necessary watermains tees, crosses and other appurtenances, the necessary sewer and side sewer connections flush tanks, manholes, and all necessary surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 20419 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
