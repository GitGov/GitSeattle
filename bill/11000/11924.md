**Council Bill Number: 11924**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24098**
********

 An Ordinance accepting a deed of conveyance from the Oregon & Washington Railroad Company, conveying by deed of conveyance to the City of Georgetown, now the City of Seattle, by virtue of Ordinance No. 23814, providing for the annexation of the City of Georgetown, a municipal corporation of the third class, to the City of Seattle, a municipal corporation of the first class, and said deed being dated the 11th day of April, A. D., 1910, a tract of land lying and being in the City of Seattle, King County, State of Washington, more particularly described as follows: All that part of Lot Two (2), Block Eleven (11), Commercial Street Steam Motor Addition, an Addition to the City of Seattle, that lies easterly of westerly line of proposed extension of Colorado Avenue, containing an area of fifteen and eighty-two hundredths (15.82) square feet, more or less; said westerly line being more particularly described as follows: Beginning at the southeast corner of Block "M", Ladd's Factory Sites; thence running in a southeasterly direction, passing through the northeast corner of Lot Seven (7), Block Seven (7), Maple's Addition to the City of Seattle, and extending to an intersection with the west line of Bishop Street.
