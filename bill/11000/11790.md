**Ordinance Number: 23949**
********

 An ordinance authorizing the Corporation Counsel to incur an expense in the sum of $31.00 in the employment of the necessary clerical assistance in the preparation of transcript of record covering the issuance and sale of $65,000 negotiable funding bonds to provide money for the re-payment to the General Fund of the City of Seattle of money advanced from said fund to pay the existing general indebtedness of the former City of Ballard at the date of its annexation to the City of Seattle, and in procuring certified copies of proceedings had in the Superior Court of King County and the Supreme Court of the State of Washington affecting said bond issue.
