**Council Bill Number: 11694**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23961**
********

 An ordinance creating and establishing Local Improvement District No. 2320 for improvement of 26th Avenue North and 26th Avenue North produced, from the north boundary of Interlaken Addition to the south line of Lake Washington Canal right of way; East McGraw Street, Calhoun Street and Miller Street, each from 24th Avenue North to 25th Avenue North; 25th Avenue North, from Louisa Street to Roanoke Street; and Roanoke Street, from 24th Avenue North to the Parkway, all in the City of Seattle,, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, and including as part of said improvement the construction of a proper outfall to Lake Washington, in accordance with Resolution No. 2320 of the city council of the City of Seattle on the 11th day of April, 1910, and creating and establishing Local Improvement District No. 2320 to pay the cost and expense of such improvement, and authorizing and directing the advancement of a sum not to exceed $8,000.00 from the general fund to said Local Improvement District No. 2320 as a loan.
