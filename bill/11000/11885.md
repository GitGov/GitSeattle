**Ordinance Number: 24355**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Woodland Park Avenue, from West Green Lake Boulevard to North Eighty-fifth Street, all in the City of Seattle., by grading and curbing the same, constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden crosswalks thereon and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2716 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
