**Ordinance Number: 23917**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 52, A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle (being the alley from Olive Street to Pine Street, between 3rd and 4th Avenues) all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2673 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
