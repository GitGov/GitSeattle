**Ordinance Number: 23751**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2055, for the improvement of East and West Green Lake Boulevard, from North 77th Street to North 65th Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle; provided however, that the portion of East and West Green Lake Boulevard, from North 77th Street to North 65th Street, improvement will include only the 60 feet on the upland side thereof, by grading and curbing the same, together with the necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage; under Ordinance No. 20607 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
