**Council Bill Number: 11204**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23887**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Howard Avenue North and Minor Avenue North, as condemned by Ordinance No. 18905, each from Roanoke Street to the north line of Greene's Addition, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, by constructing such sewers and water mains and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2627 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
