**Ordinance Number: 23919**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East and West Green Lake Boulevard, from North 77th Street to North 65th Street; North 66th Street and North 67th Street, each from West Green Lake Boulevard to Aurora Avenue; North 70th Street and North 71st Street, each from West Green Lake Boulevard to Fremont Avenue; North 72nd Street from West Green Lake Boulevard to Aurora Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden cross walks thereon and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2672 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
