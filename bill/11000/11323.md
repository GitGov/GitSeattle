**Council Bill Number: 11323**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23957**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2036, for the improvement of Broadway and North Broadway, from the north line of East Pike Street to the south line of East Roy Street, all in the City of Seattle, by installation of a system of cluster lights or of the Daniel's Boulevard Lighting System along the curb line of each side of said streets, together with the installation of such poles, conduits, wires and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper installation and equipment of said system, and provided, that said lights be placed at a distance of about 90 feet; under Ordinance No. 20519 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
