**Council Bill Number: 11878**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24338**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2228 for the improvement of Boren Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Pike Street to Union Street, by paving the same with stone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing granite or armoured concrete curbs with curved granite corners; and providing for the construction or reconstruction of concrete walks and of all sewers, side sewers and water mains with their necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 22731, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
