**Council Bill Number: 11883**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24263**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Ninth Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Howell Street to Virginia Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing granite curbs and side sewer connections where necessary, together with such sewers and water mains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and by the construction of concrete sidewalks thereon, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2713 of the City Council of the City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
