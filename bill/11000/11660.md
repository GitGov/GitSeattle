**Council Bill Number: 11660**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24037**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fifth Avenue and Fifth Avenue South, from Main Street to Madison Street; Washington Street, from Fourth Avenue South to Sixth Avenue South; Yesler Way, from Fourth Avenue South to the east line of Fifth Avenue; Terrace Street, Jefferson Street, James Street, Cherry Street, Columbia Street and Marion Street, each from Fourth Avenue to Sixth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by regarding the same and making all necessary approaches to streets and alleys; by the construction of all sewers, side sewers, waterman's and their appurtenances; by the construction of a steel and concrete bridge on Yesler Way over Fifth Avenue South; by the construction of all necessary retaining walls and abutments, concrete walks and stairways; by the construction of temporary wooden walks, stairways and foot bridges; by planking and replanking; provided, that fifty (50) per cent of the cost of adjustment the 24-inch distribution main on said street shall be borne by the Water Fund; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2691 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
