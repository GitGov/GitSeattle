**Council Bill Number: 34761**
   
**Ordinance Number: 45052**
********

 An ordinance providing for the changing and establishing of the grades of West Marginal Way, as established by Ordinance No. 38205, from a point forty-six and Seventeen one-hundredths (46.17) feet south from the intersection of the center line of said West Marginal Way with the north boundary line of Section 30, Township 24 North, Range 4 East, W.M., to a point two-thousand one-hundred fifty (2,150) feet southerly therefrom, measured from said center line; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said way and for the cuts and fills upon property abutting upon said way and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law
