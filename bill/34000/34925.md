**Ordinance Number: 45226**

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 57th Avenue South, from the intersection of Seward Park Avenue and Orcas Street, as established by Ordinance No. 27888, northerly and northwesterly to the intersection of 55th Avenue South and Hudson Street; providing for the establishing of the grades of 57th Avenue South, from the intersection of Seward Park Avenue and Orcas Street, as established by Ordinance No. 27888, northerly and northwesterly to the intersection of 55th avenue South and Hudson Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described avenue, and for the establishing of the grades and for the grading of the above described avenue, in conformity with the grades established herein; providing for the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenue; and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law
