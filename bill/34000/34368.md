**Ordinance Number: 44689**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Myrtle Street, from Seward Park Avenue as now established, to 55th Avenue South, of 55th Avenue South, from Willow Street to Orchard Street as established herein; and of Orchard Street, from Seward Park Avenue as now established to Lake Washington; providing for the establishing of curb grades of Myrtle Street from Seward Park Avenue as now established to 55th Avenue South, of 55th Avenue South, from the intersection of Holly Street and Seward Park Avenue as now established to Orchard Street as herein established, and of Orchard Street, from Seward Park Avenue as now established, to Lake Washington; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described streets and avenues, and for the establishing of the grades and for the grading of the above described streets, avenue and the approaches thereto in conformity with the grades established herein; providing for the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said streets, avenue and approaches thereto; providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
