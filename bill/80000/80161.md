**Council Bill Number: 80161**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88700**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 16, Pleasant Valley Addition, being the alley between 32nd Avenue West and 33rd Avenue West from Ruffner Street to West Bertona Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 17977 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
