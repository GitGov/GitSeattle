**Council Bill Number: 80260**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88825**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of The City of Seattle at the special municipal election to be held therein on March 8, 1960 the proposition for the issuance and sale therefor of $26,628,000 general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds of said city to provide funds and matching funds for capital purposes to wit: construct comprehensive highway improvements to the city street system, including an expressway and undercrossing of the Lake Washington Ship Canal, interchanges and ramps, and other traffic facilities; and pay the principal and interest on such bonds by excess tax levies as permitted by law; and requiring the City Clerk to certify such propsition.
