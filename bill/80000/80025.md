**Ordinance Number: 88564**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West 67th Street from approximately 100 feet west of 35th Avenue Northwest to Seaview Avenue; and certain other streets, avenues and easements, by constructing sanitary sewers and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 17937 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
