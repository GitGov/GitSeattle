**Ordinance Number: 89081**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 39th Avenue North from McGilvra Street to approximately 400 feet north thereof; and certain easements; by the construction of a sanitary sewer system and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18151 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
