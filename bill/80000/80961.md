**Council Bill Number: 80961**
   
**Ordinance Number: 89495**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Seaview Ave. from approximately 360 feet south of west 60th Street to West 80th Street; and certain other streets, avenues and right of way; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 18289 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds."
