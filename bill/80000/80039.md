**Council Bill Number: 80039**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88577**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of that certain watermain easement conveyed to the City of Seattle identified as Comptroller's File No. 163958, and accepted by Ordinance No. 69582, said easement being located without specific description in the undedicated streets of the Unrecorded Plat of Sand Point Country Club and named in said plat as Fairway Drive and Fairway Drive (westerly roadway) from East 75th Street to Lakemont Drive on the north; and certain other undedicated streets; by constructing watermains and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18153 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
