**Council Bill Number: 80261**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88826**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of The City of Seattle at the special municipal election to be held therein on March 8, 1960 the proposition for the issuance and sale of $4,500,000 general obligation bonds of said city for capital purposes to wit: improvements to and expansion of the park system, including ballfields and night lighting thereof, beaches, fieldhouses, parks, playfields, playgrounds, moorage and boat launching ramp facilities, rehabilitation of Green Lake, and continued modernization of the Zoo; and pay the principal and interest on such bonds by excess tax levies as permitted by law; and requiring the City Clerk to certify such proposition.
