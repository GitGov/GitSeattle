**Ordinance Number: 89499**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 28, Lake Union Addition, as established by Ordinance No. 88566, being the alley in the block between Densmore Ave. and Wallingford Ave. from North 39th Street to North 40th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18388 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds."
