**Council Bill Number: 80123**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88663**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 27th Avenue Northeast from East 75th Street to approximately 300 feet north thereof; by constructing sewers and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 18120 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
