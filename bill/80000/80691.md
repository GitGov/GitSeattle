**Council Bill Number: 80691**
   
**Ordinance Number: 89230**
********

 An ordinance ratifying and confirming a loan in the sum of Twenty Six Million Six Hundred Twenty Eight Thousand Dolars ($26,628,000) and authorizing the issuance and sale of general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds therefor in said sum for the purpose of providing fund and matching funds for capital purposes to wit: construct comprehensive highway improvements to the city street system as submitted to the electors under Ordinance 88825; providing for the levy and collection of taxes in excess of the millage limitation contained in the State Construction and the State law for the payment of the principal and interest of said bonds; creating and establishing a Construction Fund and a Bond Interest and Redemption Fund and pledging a specified portion of the permissive property tax levy in connection therewith.
