**Council Bill Number: 80124**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88687**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block H, 4th Addition, as laid off by Wm. N. Bell, being the alley between 1st Avenue and 2nd Avenue from Eagle Street to Broad Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 18119 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
