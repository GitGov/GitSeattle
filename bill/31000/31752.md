**Ordinance Number: 42288**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 5th Avenue West, from West Dravus Street to West Bertona Street; 4th Avenue West, from West Dravus Street to a point 180 feet north thereof; West Dravus Street, form 4th Avenue West to 5th Avenue West; and West Bertona Street, form 3rd Avenue West to a point 127 feet east of 5th Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6605, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
