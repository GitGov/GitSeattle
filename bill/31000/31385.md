**Council Bill Number: 31385**
   
**Ordinance Number: 41936**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 3rd Avenue Northwest, from West 59th Street as established by Ordinance No. 39745, to West 58th Street; providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of a roadway connecting West 59th Street, as established by Ordinance No. 39745, over said widened portion of 3rd Avenue Northwest, to a point just north of its intersection with West 58th Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described avenue, and for the changing and establishing of the grades and for the grading and regrading of the above described roadway and approaches in conformity with the grades established herein; providing for the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said roadway and approaches thereto; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
