**Council Bill Number: 31051**
   
**Ordinance Number: 41649**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the appropriation and monthly transfer of funds form the earnings of the City Utilities to the General Fund for the purpose of reimbursing the General Fund for the payment of interest and principal upon utility bonds constituting a general indebtedness, and further providing for the transfer to the Water Fund of all moneys received from the sale of timber in the Cedar River Watershed, and repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
