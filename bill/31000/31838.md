**Council Bill Number: 31838**
   
**Ordinance Number: 42423**
********

 An ordinance conditionally extending the time for the construction of the bridge over the right of way of the Great Northern Railway Company at Thirty-first Avenue West (formerly Harley Avenue), provided for in Ordinance No. 29567, in consideration of the payment of Thirty-two Hundred Dollars ($3200.00) by said railway company, and providing, in lieu of immediate construction of said bridge, for the grading and graveling of a temporary roadway on Commodore Way from Twenty-seventh Avenue West to Thirty-third Avenue West, and for the expenditure of said Thirty-two Hundred ($3200.00) Dollars for such purpose, and appropriating money from the General Fund therefor, and declaring an emergency.

**Status:** Passed
