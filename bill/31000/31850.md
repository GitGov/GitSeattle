**Ordinance Number: 42396**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Findlay Street, from 46th Avenue South to a point 330 feet east of 51st Avenue South; Lucile Street, from 46th Avenue South to a point 220 feet east of 51st Avenue South; Brandon Street, from 46th Avenue South to 50th Avenue South; Mayflower Street, and same produced, Bennett Street and Dawson Street, each from 47th Avenue South to 48th Avenue South; 47th Avenue South, from Dawson Street to Orcas Street; and 48th avenue South, from Hudson Street to Dawson Street; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6699, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
