**Council Bill Number: 31050**
   
**Ordinance Number: 41648**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the appropriation and transfer from the earnings of the city utilities to the special fund created and established for the payment of special fund utility bonds, of a sum total equal to one-twelfth (1/12) of the amount of annual interest and principal falling due upon said bonds, for the purpose of paying said interest and principal and repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
