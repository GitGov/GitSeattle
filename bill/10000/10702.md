**Ordinance Number: 23120**

 An Ordinance accepting a deed from Merrimac Investment Company, a corporation, to the City of Seattle, dated the 30th day of January A. D. 1908, to the following described tract, lot or parcel of land, lying and being in the County of King, State of Washington, and particularly bounded and described as follows to wit: Tract One Hundred Twenty-five (125) of the Replat of John J. McGilvra's Addition to the City of Seattle and Blocks 13, 14, 18, 20 to 29 inclusive and south half of 19 of John J. McGilvras Second Addition to the City of Seattle. The same to be used for park and parkway purposes.
