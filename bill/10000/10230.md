 An Ordinance accepting a deed from the Oregon and Washington Railroad Company, a corporation duly organized and existing under and by virtue of the laws of the State of Oregon, and qualified to do business in the State of Washington, to the City of Seattle, a municipal corporation, dated June 7th, 1909, to the following described property: Beginning at the northwest corner of Lot Sixteen (16), Block twenty-seven (27), Ladd's First Addition to South Seattle; run thence South forty-one degrees one minute forty-five seconds East (S. 41o 01' 45" E.) to the East line of Lot sixteen (16); thence South thirty-three minutes fifteen seconds East (S.0o 33' 15"E.) along the East line of Lot Sixteen (16) to a point where a line drawn parallel to and twenty-four (24) feet distant when measured at right angles southwesterly from the first course would intersect the east line of Lot Sixteen (16), Block twenty-seven (27), Ladd's First Addition to South Seattle; run thence North forty-one degrees one minute forty-five seconds West (N. 41o 01' 45"W.) to the west line of Lot Sixteen (16); thence northwesterly along the west line of Lot sixteen (16) to the place of beginning, all in King County, Washington, for street purposes
