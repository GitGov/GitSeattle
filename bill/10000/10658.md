 An Ordinance accepting a deed from Louisa Schwers and C. H. Schwers, her husband and C. T. Stembach, a widower to the City of Seattle, dated the 10th day of September, 1908 to that portion of Block 48 Burkes second Addition to the City of Seattle, more particularly described as follows to wit: Beginning at the southeast corner of Lot Eight (8) Block Forty-eight (48) Burke's Second Addition to the City of Seattle; thence north along the east line of said Block Forty-eight (48) to the northeast corner of said block; thence west along the north line of said block a distance of 240 feet, more or less, to the northwest corner of said block, thence in a southeasterly direction diagonally across the block in a straight line to the southeast corner of Block Forty-eight (48), that being the point of beginning, all in the City of Seattle.
