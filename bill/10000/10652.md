 An Ordinance accepting a deed from Alonzo, Hull, and Miriam F. Hull, his wife, to the City of Seattle, dated the 27th day of June, 1908, to an undivided five-sixths (5/6) interest in the following described property; Commencing at the intersection of the northerly line of Lot 4 Block 55 Burke's Second Addition to the City of Seattle with the easterly line of 34th Avenue South; thence easterly along the northerly line of Lot Four (4), 15 feet; thence southerly parallel to and 15 feet distant from the easterly line of 34th Avenue south to the southerly line of Lot 6 said Block 55; thence westerly along the southerly line of Lot 6 to the easterly line of 34th Avenue South, thence northerly along the easterly line of 34th Avenue South to the point of beginning; the same to be used for Park and Parkway purposes (all in the City of Seattle).
