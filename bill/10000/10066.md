**Council Bill Number: 10066**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22835**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2090, for the improvement of Franklin Avenue, from a point sixty (60) feet north of the north margin of Nelson Place on the east to a point one hundred eighty (180) feet south of the south margin of East Park Drive on the east, and Nelson Place from Franklin Avenue to Eastlake Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, with all wyes, openings, flush tanks, manholes, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district under Ordinance No. 21184, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
