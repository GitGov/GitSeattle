**Council Bill Number: 10751**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23333**
********

 An Ordinance accepting a deed from Green Lake Home-Building and Guarantee Company, a corporation, to the City of Seattle, dated the 22nd day of January, 1907 to the following Lots and Blocks in the Replat of Green Lake Home Addition to the City of Seattle: All that part of Block 53, lying in Section 6, T. 25 N. R. 4 ELECTRICAL DAMAGES; all of Block fifty-four (54), all of Fifty-five (55), all of Block Fifty-six (56); all of that tract on the southerly side of Green Lake Boulevard bounded by N. 80th Street, Wallingford Avenue, produced southerly and Green Lake Boulevard. All of portion of Government Lot One (1) in Section 6, T. 25 N. R. 4 E. lying southerly and easterly of Green Lake Boulevard and southwesterly of the following line produced from the southerly margin of Green Lake Boulevard into Green Lake, to wit: That line which begins at a point in the westerly line of said Government Lot One (1) and which runs in a southeasterly direction, constituting its boundary line between the lands of Green Lake Home Building and Guarantee Company and S. Levinson.
