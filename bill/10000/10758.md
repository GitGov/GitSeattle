**Ordinance Number: 23340**

 An Ordinance accepting a deed from the Columbia & Puget Sound Railroad Company, a corporation, to the City of Seattle, dated the 4th day of December, 1907, to All that part of parcel of land known as tract #137 in the Replat of John J. McGilvra's Addition to the City of Seattle and Blocks 13, 14, 18, 20 to 29 inclusive and the south half of 19 of John J. McGilvra's Second Addition to the City of Seattle in Section 27, Township 25 North Range, 4 East W. M., beginning at a point on the east margin of hillside Drive which point is the intersection of the East side of Hillside Drive and the common boundary line of Tracts 137 and 138; thence by a straight line bearing North 63o 26' 15" East a distance of 138.15 ft more or less, to a point on the west margin of McGilvra Boulevard, which is a point of intersection of the West margin of said Boulevard with the common boundary of Tracts 137 and 138; thence northwesterly along the west boundary of McGilvra Boulevard 75 feet; thence by a straight line bearing south 63o 26' 15" West a distance of 105 feet, more or less, to a point on the on the East margin of Hillside Drive; thence southerly by a curve of uniform radius of 220 feet a distance of 4.85 feet, more or less, to the point of tangency; thence by a tangent 77.12 feet to the point of beginning, containing 0.029 acres. All in the City of Seattle.
