**Ordinance Number: 23118**

 An Ordinance accepting a deed from Crawford & Conover, a corporation, to the City of Seattle, dated the 29th day of March, 1909 to portions of Lots Ten (10), Eleven (11), Twelve (12), Thirteen (13), Fourteen (14) and Fifteen (15) of Block "E" of Madrona Heights addition to the City of Seattle, particularly described as follows: Beginning at the southwest corner of said Lot Ten (10) in said Block "E" and running thence northeasterly along the westerly boundary of Lots Ten (10), Eleven (11) and Twelve (12) to the northwest corner of said Lot Twelve (12), thence continuing northerly along the westerly boundary of Lots Thirteen 913), Fourteen (14) and Fifteen (15) to the northwest corner of said Lot Fifteen (15) in said Block "E"; thence southeasterly by direct course across and Lot Fifteen (15) to a point on the south line there of distant thirty (30) feet east from the southwest corner of said lot Fifteen (15); thence continuing southeasterly by direct course across Lots Fourteen (14) and Thirteen (13), to a point on the south line of Lot Thirteen (13), distant fifty (50) feet east from the southwest corner of said Lot Thirteen (13); thence continuing southerly by direct course across Lots Twelve (12), Eleven 911), and Ten (10) to a point on the south line of Lot ten (10), distant eighty-five (85) feet east from the southwest corner of said Lot Ten (10); thence westerly along the south line of said Lot Ten (10), eighty-five (85) feet to the southwest corner of said Lot Ten (10) and place of beginning, all in the City of Seattle.
