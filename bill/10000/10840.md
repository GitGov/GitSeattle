**Ordinance Number: 23102**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Garfield Street, from Fifteenth Avenue West across Smith's Cove Waterway to Twenty-third Avenue West; Twenty-third Avenue West, from West Garfield Street to West Lee Street; West Lee Street, from Twenty-third Avenue West to the center line of Thirty-second Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a plank roadway thereon, twenty-six (26) feet in width, and by providing for the necessary overhead crossings and bridges across Railroad Avenue and the Smith's Cove Waterway, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2555 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
