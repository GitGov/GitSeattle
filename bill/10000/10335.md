**Council Bill Number: 10335**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22732**
********

 An Ordinance submitting to the qualified electors of the City of Seattle at the general election to be held in said city on Tuesday the 8th day of March, 1910, the question of the issuance and sale of negotiable bonds of said city, in the amount of Two Million ($2,000,000.00) Dollars, to provide money for the purchase of land for parks, parkways and play grounds, the improvement thereof, and for the payment of the existing indebtedness incurred in the purchase or improvement of property for parks, parkways and play grounds, for the further improvement of parks, parkways and play grounds.
