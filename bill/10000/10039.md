**Ordinance Number: 22381**

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 22150 entitled: "An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 9th Avenue West, from West McGraw Street to West Barrett Street; of 8th Avenue West from the north line of block 5, Star Addition to the City of Seattle, to the north margin of West Ray Street; of West Armour Street, from 10th Avenue West to the west line of Grand View Addition to the City of Seattle; of West Ray Street from the east margin of 8th Avenue West to a pint 68.08 feet east from the said east margin; and of West Halladay Street, from 8th Avenue West to 9th Avenue West; all in the City of Seattle, providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law."
