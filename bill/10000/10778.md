**Ordinance Number: 23455**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Second Avenue Northeast, Latona Avenue and Fourth Avenue Northeast, each from East Forty-fourth Street to East Fiftieth Street; Thackery Place, from East Forty-fourth Street to East Forty-fifth Street; Fifth Avenue Northeast, from East Forty-fifth Street to East Forty-seventh Street, and East Fiftieth Street, from Latona Avenue to Fourth Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, by laying watermains therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2531 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
