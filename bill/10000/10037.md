**Ordinance Number: 22392**

 An ordinance authorizing and directing the Board of Public Works to construct temporary plank walks on the north side of Broad Street, from 5th Avenue North to 7th Avenue North, on the east side of 6th Avenue North from Broad Street to a point 360 feet northward, and on the west side of 5th Avenue North, from Harrison Street to Roy Street, all in the City of Seattle, provided that the city engineer is hereby authorized and directed to incur an indebtedness against the general fund for said improvement.
