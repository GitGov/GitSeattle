**Council Bill Number: 10062**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22731**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Boren Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Pike Street to Union Street, by paving the same with stone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing granite or armoured concrete curbs with curved granite corners; and providing for the construction or reconstruction of concrete walks and all sewers, side sewers and watermains with their necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2494 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
