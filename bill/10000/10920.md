**Council Bill Number: 10920**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23898**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1929, for the improvement of 23rd Avenue, from East Madison Street to Yesler Way and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation with concrete or brick gutters and concrete curbs with armored concrete and granite corners, with the exception of certain streets thereof, which shall be paved with stone blocks; also the construction ore reconstruction of all concrete walks necessary, together with necessary water mains of proper dimensions and materials and proper sewer connections, under Ordinance No. 19847 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
