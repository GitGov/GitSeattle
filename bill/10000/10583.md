**Council Bill Number: 10583**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23241**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2074 for the improvement of Spokane Street and West Spokane Street, from Thirteenth Avenue South to Thirty-fifth Avenue Southwest, all in the City of Seattle, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing watermains of proper dimensions and materials, together with all gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants, bridging, piling, channel crossings, pumps, pumping station, motors, tanks and other appurtenances, and the purchase of rights of way and sites for a pumping station and distributing tanks; provided, that the abutting property is to be assessed, as benefits, an amount equal to the cost of constructing a distribution main of proper size, together with all gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants, bridging, pilling and all other proper appurtenances necessary to afford reasonable water service and fire protection to the said abutting property, and that the balance of cost, only, is to be paid from the Water Fund; under Ordinance No. 20954, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
