**Ordinance Number: 23319**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2093 for the improvement of Hanford Street, from Thirty-seven Avenue South to Thirty-third Avenue South; Horton Street, from Thirty-seventh Avenue South to the west line of the York Addition and Walden Street from the west line of the York Addition to Rainier Avenue; Thirty-sixth Avenue South, Thirty-fifth Avenue South and Thirty-fourth Avenue South, each from Spokane Street to Hanford Street; Thirty-third Avenue South, from Horton Street to Hanford Street, by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage; and Horton Street, from Thirty-seventh Avenue South to the west line of the York Addition and Walden Street, from the west line of York Addition to Rainier Avenue by planking the same, under Ordinance No. 21187, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
