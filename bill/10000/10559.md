**Council Bill Number: 10559**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22890**
********

 An Ordinance submitting to the qualified electors of the City of Seattle at the general election to be held in said city on Tuesday the 8th day of March, A. D., 1910, the proposition and question of the issuance and sale of negotiable bonds of said city in the amount of Two Hundred and Fifty Thousand ($250,000.00) Dollars, to provide money for the purchase of sites for branch libraries, the construction of a branch library building to the known as the "Henry L. Yesler Branch Library", and for the payment of the existing indebtedness incurred in remodeling the public library to conform to the regrade of Fourth Avenue, in said city, and providing for the payment of said bonds and the interest thereon.
