**Ordinance Number: 22906**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twelfth Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Fuhrman Avenue, from Shelby Street to Allison Street; Boyer Avenue, from Fifteenth Avenue North to Twelfth Avenue North; Glen Road, from Delmar Drive to Boyer Avenue; Maple Place, from Delmar Drive to Boyer Avenue; Eleventh Avenue North, from Miller Street to Shelby Street; the Alley between Eleventh Avenue North and Twelfth Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Roanoke Street, from Federal Avenue to Eleventh Avenue North; Edgar Street, from Eleventh Avenue North to Twelfth Avenue North; Millard Street, from Harvard Avenue North to Fuhrman Avenue; and Shelby Street, from Tenth Avenue North, eastward to an outlet in Lake Union, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein, of proper dimensions and materials, together with the construction of a suitable outfall into Lake Union, and all other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2518 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
