Nelson Addition, being the alley between 23rd Avenue Northwest and 22nd Avenue Northwest, from West 83rd Street to West 85th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 13092 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
