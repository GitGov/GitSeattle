**Council Bill Number: 62252**
   
**Ordinance Number: 71031**
********

 An ordinance relating to and adopting a plan or system of additions, betterments, improvements and extensions to the existing municipal street railway and surface transportation system; declaring the estimated cost of such additions, betterments, improvements and extensions; providing for the refund of certain indebtedness of the city payable from the revenues of said system; providing for the issuance and sale of bonds to provide for the purposes hereof; creating and establishing a fund into which the proceeds of such bonds shall be paid; creating and establishing special funds to pay the principal of and interest on the bonds issued pursuant to this ordinance and to operate and maintain said system; and declaring an emergency.
