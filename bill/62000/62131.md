**Ordinance Number: 70967**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West Viewmont Way, from a point 125 feet, more or less, south of the south line of West Dravus Street to Emerson Street; and certin parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing sewers together with the necessary appurtenances and treatment plant, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12947 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
