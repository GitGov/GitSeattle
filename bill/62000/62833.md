**Ordinance Number: 71649**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Latimer Place, as established by Ordinance No. 65497, being the thirty foot street lying between 51st Avenue Northeast and 55th Avenue Northeast produced southerly, and extending from East 41st Street to East 41st Street, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13192 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
