**Council Bill Number: 62648**
   
**Ordinance Number: 71476**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West 60th Street, from 32nd Avenue Northwest to a point approximately 200 feet west of 36th Avenue Northwest; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13164 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
