**Council Bill Number: 68253**
   
**Ordinance Number: 76949**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Fauntleroy Avenue, from West Edmunds Street to California Avenue and from West Willow Street to West Fontanelle Street and from West Webster Street to Wildwood Place; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14566 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
