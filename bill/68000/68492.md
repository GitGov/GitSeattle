 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the Alleys in Block 11, Cowen's University Park Addition, being the alleys in the block bounded by East 62nd Street, East 61st Street, Roosevelt Way and 12th Avenue Northeast, and certain parts of certain other streets and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14686 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
