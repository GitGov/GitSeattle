**Council Bill Number: 68532**
   
**Ordinance Number: 77216**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Hillman's Lake Front Division No. 1, being the alleys bounded by North 74th Street, North 75th Street, Aurora Avenue, Linden Avenue and Winona Avenue; and certain other alleys, streets and avenues; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14611 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
