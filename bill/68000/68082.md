**Council Bill Number: 68082**
   
**Ordinance Number: 76781**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of East Boston Terrace, as established by Ordinances Nos. 75421, 75422, 75423 and 75424, from approximately 22 feet south of the lot line between Lots "G" and "H" to approximately 40 feet north of the lot line between Lots "U" and "V", Plat of Cape Horn Addition, unrecorded, by restoring the roadway with a creosoted trestle with concrete deck; all in accordance with property owners' petition, being City Comptroller's File No. 196358, to the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
