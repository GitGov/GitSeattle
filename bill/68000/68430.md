**Ordinance Number: 77122**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 7, Buckius Addition, and the Alley between Block 1, Gamma Poncin's Addition, and Block 1, Stander Addition, being the alley between 26th Avenue and 27th Avenue from East Marion Street to East Spring Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14653 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
