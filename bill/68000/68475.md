 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 3, J. B. Johnson Company's 1st Addition, being the alley between Beacon Avenue and Alamo Place, from Spokane Street to Hinds Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14796 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
