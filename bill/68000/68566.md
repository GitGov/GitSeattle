 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 8, Bell and Denny's Addition, being the alley between Western Avenue and Elliott Avenue from Vine Street to Cedar Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14948 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
