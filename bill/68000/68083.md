 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Francis Avenue, from North 42nd Street to the southern terminus thereof, as established in B. F. Day's 1st Addition; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with property owners' petition, being City Comptroller's File No. 191664, to the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
