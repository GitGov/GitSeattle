**Council Bill Number: 83875**
   
**Ordinance Number: 92381**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Northeast 40th Street from 48th Ave. Northeast to 50th Ave. Northeast; and certain other streets, avenues, alleys and an easement; by removing existing overhead utilities transmission lines including electrical transmission lines, fire alarm transmission lines, street light transmission lines, Telephone Company transmission lines and by constructing underground utilities transmission lines in place of those removed; installing surface mounted transformers; constructing a residential street lighting system with underground feed in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 144, Laws of 1957; all in accordance with Resolution No. 19207 of the City of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
