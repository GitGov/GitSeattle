**Ordinance Number: 92443**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley between Block 11, Plat of Alki Point and Block 2, A.A. Smith's 2nd Addition, being the aley between 59th Ave. Southwest and 60th Ave. Southwest, from Alki Ave. Southwest to Southwest Stevens St.; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 19241 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
