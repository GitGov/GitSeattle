**Ordinance Number: 91577**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of South Warsaw Street from Rainier Ave. South to approximately 100 feet east of 48th Ave. South; and certain other streets, avenues and easements; by constructing sanitary sewers and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18869 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
