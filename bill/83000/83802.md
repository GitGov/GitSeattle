**Ordinance Number: 92307**

 An ordinance relating to the licensing of Public Card Rooms and amending Sections 18, 65, 65-A and 68 of the License Code (Ordinance 48022) to delete the limitations upon the number of such licenses and the fee for transfers not involving a change in location, to establish the initial fee for new Public Card Room License and to require certain additional information from corporate Card Room Licenses and to change the permissable closing hours of Public Card Rooms.
