**Council Bill Number: 83801**
   
**Ordinance Number: 92305**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 28th Ave. Northeast across a portion of Blocks 2 & 3, Kennedy's Acre Tracts, 2nd Division, from a point approximately 80 feet north of the north margin of Northeast 137th Street to a point approximately 50 feet south of the south margin of Northeast 137th Street, and of 27th Ave. Northeast across a portion of Block 143rd Street; and for the establishing of grades on said streets; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of the necessary land, and for the right to construct slopes for cuts and fills on the adjoining property; and providing for payment from the City Street Fund.
