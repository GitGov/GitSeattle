**Council Bill Number: 26418**
   
**Ordinance Number: 37329**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West and North Forty-fifth Street, from a connection with the existing sewer in Eleventh Avenue Northwest to Palatine Avenue on the north, and from Greenwood Avenue to Phinney Avenue; West Forty-fourth Street, from Sixth Avenue Northwest to Leary Avenue; Leary Avenue, from West forty-fourth Street to West Forty-fifth Street; North Forty-seventh Street and North Forty-sixth Street, each from Phinney Avenue to Greenwood Avenue; The Alleys in Block 11, Block 12, Block 19 and Block 20, Malson's Addition to Salmon Bay City, from Greenwood Avenue to First Avenue Northwest; Greenwood Avenue, from North Forty-seventh Street to connect with existing sewer south of North Forty-fifth Street; Palatine Avenue, from North Forty-sixth Street Alley in Block 12, Malson's Addition to Salmon Bay City; First Avenue Northwest, from the Alley in Block 12, Malson's Addition to Salmon Bay City, to West Forty-fifth Street; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5478 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
