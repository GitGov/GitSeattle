 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Forty-second Avenue Southwest, from West Stevens Street to West Hanford Street, and of West Hanford Street from Forty-second Avenue Southwest to Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest, all in the City of Seattle, providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
