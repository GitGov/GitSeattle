**Council Bill Number: 26018**
   
**Ordinance Number: 37255**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing the Seattle and Rainier Valley Railway Company to surrender and release that portion of the franchise granted by Ordinance No. 15919 which covers Washington Street between the easterly margin of Thirteenth Avenue South and the north and south right-of-way of the company on Fourth Avenue South; accepting such surrender when filed, releasing the Seattle and Rainier Valley Railway Company, its successors and assigns, from all obligations and liabilities imposed by said Ordinance No. 15919 as the same applies to the above locations, and authorizing the removal of the tracks on Washington Street between the points designated.
