**Council Bill Number: 26772**
   
**Ordinance Number: 37680**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 36630, entitled: "An ordinance relating to, and specifying a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing water works owned and controlled by the City of Seattle, declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be, to the sum of Sixty Thousand Dollars ($60,000); and providing for the issuance and sale of Sixty Thousand Dollars ($60,000) of bonds of said city to provide the funds therefor, and for the creating of a special fund into which shall be paid certain revenues such water supply system to pay the principal and interest of such bonds and for the terms and conditions of such bonds and for their sale", approved October 31, 1916, and directing the City Comptroller to destroy the bonds therein authorized.
