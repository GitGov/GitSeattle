**Council Bill Number: 26085**
   
**Ordinance Number: 37058**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing and directing the payment from the General Fund of the City of Seattle of the cost and expense of improving that part of Alki Avenue within the limits of the former franchise grants to the Seattle Electric Company and the Puget Sound Traction, Light and Power Company, its successor, from West Atlantic Street to Rhode Island Street, and also for the cost and expense of improving that part of California Way (formerly Cascade Avenue) within the limits of the former franchise grants to the Seattle Electric Company and the Puget Sound Traction, Light and Power Company, its successors, from West Atlantic Street to California Place, all in the City of Seattle, and appropriating money from the General Fund for such purpose, and providing for the reimbursement of said fund.
