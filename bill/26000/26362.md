**Ordinance Number: 37351**

 An Ordinance granting to the Western Union Telegraph Company, a New York Corporation, its successors and assigns, the right, privilege and authority to construct, maintain and operate on, over or under the streets, alleys and other public palces of the City of Seattle, all conduits, ducts, poles, fixtures, wires and apparatus necessary for the purpose of supplying private wire connections for messenger service, time service and ticker service, and for local connections for long distance telephone service.
