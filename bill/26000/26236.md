**Council Bill Number: 26236**
   
**Ordinance Number: 37158**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fortieth Avenue, from East Olive Street to the south line of Lewis & Fehren's Addition; Evergreen Place, from East Olive Street to East Pine Street; East Olive Street, from Thirty-ninth Avenue to Fortieth Avenue; and East Pine Street, from Evergreen Lake to Fortieth Avenue; all in the City of Seattle; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5215 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
