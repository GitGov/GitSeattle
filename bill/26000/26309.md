 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 33445, entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the laying off, extending, opening and establishing of Fourteenth Avenue South, from Greeley Street to the south line of Section Twenty-nine (29), Township Twenty-four (24) North, Range Four (4) East, W.
