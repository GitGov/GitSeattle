**Council Bill Number: 26770**
   
**Ordinance Number: 37645**
********

 An ordinance authorizing the Board of Public Works to accept the bid of the Pacific States Lumber Company for the timber owned by the City of Seattle, and located on lands in township 22 north, range 8 east, township 22 north, range 9 east, and township 22 north, range 10 east, W.M., in the vicinity of Cedar Lake, and authorizing the Board of Public Works to sell to the Pacific State Lumber Company the present railroad of the City of Seattle in the vicinity of Cedar Lake for the sum of forty-five thousand dollars ($45,000.00) cash, as covered by its bid, and authorizing said timber and said railroad, and such other timber as the City of Seattle may hereafter acquire within the Cedar River Watershed form the United States, all in accordance with the bid of the Pacific States Lumber Company.
