 An Ordinance accepting the surrender of the franchise granted to Robert M. Jones, his heirs, associates, assigns, successors or executors, by Ordinance No. 520 of the City of Georgetown, entitled "An Ordinance authorizing and empowering Robert M. Jones, his heirs, associates, assigns, successors or executors to erect, maintain and operate gas, electrical light and power works, and to lay down pipes for the supply and distribution of heating and illuminating gas; and to construct, maintain and operate equipment and apparatus for an underground electrical system, and to erect and maintain poles, wires and appurtenances for the distribution of electric light, heat and power in, over and under the streets, alleys and public places of the City of Georgetown, Washington," approved January 31, 1910, and repealing said Ordinance No. 520.
