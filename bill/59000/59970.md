 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 39th Avenue Southwest, from West Holly Street to West Myrtle Street, and on the westerly side only, from West Myrtle Street to West Orchard Street; by constructing concrete sidewalks and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12379 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
