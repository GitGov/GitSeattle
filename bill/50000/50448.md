 An ordinance relating to and providing for a special municipal election to be held November 4, 1930; providing for the submission thereat of a proposition contemplating a general municipal indebtedness and the issuance and sale therefor of general negotiable interest-bearing serial coupon bonds of said city, for the purpose of paying the cost and expense of permanent approaches to University Bridge; and requiring the City Comptroller and ex-officio City Clerk to certify to the Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, the said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title and declaring an emergency.
