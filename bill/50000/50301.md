 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of West Bertona Street, from 36th Avenue West to Arapahoe Place; of 36th Avenue West 37th Avenue West, 38th Avenue West, 39th Avenue West, 40th Avenue West, and Arapahoe Place, each from Prosper Street to West Bertona Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and providing that the cost of said improvement be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
