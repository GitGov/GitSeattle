 Section 4. The requirement of SMC 23.22.070 that the facilities and improvements required to be provided have been completed or bonded is hereby superseded for the purpose of this ordinance; provided, however, that such facilities and improvements shall be constructed and installed within two years of the effective date of this ordinance.
