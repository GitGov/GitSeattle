 ORDINANCE NO.__________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Seventeen Million, Two Hundred Fifty Four Thousand Two Hundred Eighty Nine and 97/100 dollars ($17,254,289.97) on SFMS mechanical warrants numbered 4000194847- 4000197768, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council Jan 27,2000 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Five Million, One Hundred Eighty Two Thousand, Five Hundred Forty Three and 95/100 dollars ($25,182,543.95) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50516358- 50519555 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 510001-518839, representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Jan 18,2000 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Jan 27,2000 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City council on the 31st day of Jan. 2000 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Jan. 31st, 2000
