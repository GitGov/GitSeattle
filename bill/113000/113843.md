 ORDINANCE _________________

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Police Department; authorizing the execution of agreements with the Washington State Traffic Safety Commission for financial assistance to enhance traffic safety programs in the greater Seattle and King County area; accepting the money when received; increasing the 2001 Budget of the Police Department; ratifying and confirming prior acts; and making a reimbursable appropriation therefor; all by a three-fourths vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. As requested by the Chief of Police, the Chief of Police or his designee is authorized to execute for and on behalf of The City of Seattle, agreements with the Washington State Traffic Safety Commission for financial assistance in the total amount of Twenty Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen Dollars ($20,713) to purchase equipment that will enhance traffic safety programs in the greater Seattle and King County area. The funds, when received, are hereby accepted and shall be deposited to the credit of the Emergency Subfund.

 Section 2. To carry out the purposes of the grant agreements authorized in Section 1, and contingent upon the execution of those agreements, the following line of business in the 2001 Budget of the Police Department is increased as follows:

 P10 - Administration $20,713

 For such purposes, the sum of Twenty Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen Dollars ($20,713) is hereby appropriated from the Emergency Subfund for the purposes outlined above. The Emergency Subfund shall be reimbursed from funds to be received from the Washington State Traffic Safety Commission as authorized in Section 1.

 Section 3. Any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 4. The foregoing appropriation is made to meet actual necessary expenditures of the City for which no appropriation has been made due to causes which could not reasonably have been foreseen at the time of the making of the 2001 Budget in accordance with RCW 35.32A.060.

 Section 5. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
