 ORDINANCE NO.__________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twelve Million, Eight Hundred Thousand, Seven Hundred Seventeen and 37/100 dollars ($12,800,717.37) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000447520- 400045157, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council Sept. 6, 2001 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million, Fifty Four Thousand, Seven Hundred Thirteen and 37/100 dollars ($24,054,713.37) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50661790- 50665149 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 360001 -369421 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Aug. 28, 2001 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Sept. 6, 2001 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City council on the 10th day of Sept. 2001 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Sept 10, 2001.
