 ORDINANCE __________

 AN ORDINANCE accepting the deed to certain property in Block 2 of Walter's Sound View Addition for general municipal purposes, to be used initially for the expanded Broadview Branch Library.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. As requested by the City Librarian and recommended by the Board of Trustees of the Seattle Public Library and the Mayor, the bargain and sale deed executed by Lee Michael Harris, a single person and Michael R. Harris, a married man, each as their separate estate as Grantors, and recorded under King County Recording Number 20010723001353 conveying the following described real property situated in Seattle, King County, Washington, is hereby accepted and the real property conveyed therein is placed under the jurisdiction of the Seattle Public Library, to be used initially as the expanded Broadview Branch Library:

 Lot 11 in block 2 of Walter's sound view addition, division 2, according to plat recorded in volume 41 of plates at page(s) 42, in King County, Washington.

 Section 2. All proceeds from the use of said property pending development shall be deposited in Libraries for All UTGO Fund (#31910) and shall be used to partially offset the acquisition and maintenance costs of the property described in Section 1.

 Section 3. This transaction is exempt from the provisions of Chapter 8.26 RCW (Real Property Acquisition and Relocation Policy) because the acquisition did not occur in connection with a program or project receiving federal or state financial assistance.

 Section 4. Any acts made consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance are hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 5. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
