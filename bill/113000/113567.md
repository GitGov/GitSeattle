 ORDINANCE __________

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to settle the claim of Alma M. Hornby (C-64131), all by a two- thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Sixteen Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($16,500.00). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of Alma M. Hornby for an incident on October 12, 1998 in front of the University Branch Library at Roosevelt Way NE & NE 50th Street in Seattle, WA, as specified in Clerk's File C-64131. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
