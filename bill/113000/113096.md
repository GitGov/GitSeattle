

********

**Council Bill Number: 113096**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to Animal Control; defining exotic animals; prohibiting the display of certain exotic animals at facilities owned or operated by the City of Seattle or on property owned by the City of Seattle; amending Chapter 9.25 of the Seattle Municipal Code in connection therewith; and submitting a proposition to the qualified electros of the City of Seattle for their ratification or rejection.

**Status:** In Committee
   
**Note:** This bill failed to be introduced to the Council due to a lack of a second. Proposed referendum regarding exotic circus animals.

   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** February 7, 2000
   
**Committee:** Full Council
   
**Sponsor:** STEINBRUECK
   
   
**Index Terms:** ANIMAL-CONTROL, ANIMALS, ELECTIONS
