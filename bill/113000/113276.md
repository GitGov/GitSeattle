 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Center; authorizing the Seattle Center Director to execute a lease agreement with Theatre Puget Sound for use of space in Center House.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. As requested by the Seattle Center Director and recommended by the Mayor, the Seattle Center Director is authorized to execute, for and on behalf of The City of Seattle, a lease agreement with Theatre Puget Sound for use of space in Center House substantially in the form of the agreement attached hereto and identified as "LEASE BETWEEN SEATTLE CENTER AND THEATRE PUGET SOUND."

 Section 2. Any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
