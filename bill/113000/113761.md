 ORDINANCE __________

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Harbor Code, creating an exemption from certain speed limits, and amended Chapter 16.20 of the Seattle Municipal Code in connection therewith.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. Section 16.20.130 of the Seattle Municipal Code is amended by adding a new subsection as follows:

 SMC 16.20.130 Speed regulations -- Watercraft and vessels.

 Within the harbor limits of the City it shall be unlawful for any person to operate any watercraft or vessel at a speed in excess of the following maximum limits:

 * * *

 F. Subsections A and B of this Section shall not apply to non- motorized vessels and motorized vessels in direct support of non- motorized vessels. Motorized vessels providing support must be clearly marked as a support and/or rescue vessel and include the name or seal of the organization providing support. Motorized vessels, while exceeding the limit must do so only within obvious proximity to or in pursuit of vessel(s) being supported.

 Section 2. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
