 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to settle the claim of the Ioan and Claudia Aitonean (C-65252), all by a two-thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Twenty Thousand Eight Hundred Fifty-Three and 70/100 Dollars ($20,853.70). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of the Ioan and Claudia Aitonean for injuries resulting from an excessive force on May 21, 1999, at 111 NE 45th, Seattle, Washington, as specified in Clerk's File C-65252. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
