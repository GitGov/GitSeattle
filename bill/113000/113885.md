 ORDINANCE _________________

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Police Department; authorizing the execution of a supplemental agreement with King County for financial assistance to help address juvenile justice issues through expansion of the Seattle Team for Youth/Project Ride program; accepting the money when received; increasing the 2001 Budget of the Police Department; ratifying and confirming prior acts; and making a reimbursable appropriation therefor; all by a three-fourths vote of the City Council.

 WHEREAS, Ordinance 120217, passed by the Seattle City Council on December 15, 2000, authorized an agreement for and on behalf of The City of Seattle for financial assistance from King County to support Seattle Team for Youth/Project Ride activities; and

 WHEREAS, supplemental funding for Project Ride activities has been made available by King County as a result of underexpenditures in other programs in the Juvenile Crime Enforcement Coalition; and

 WHEREAS, King County desires to increase Seattle's allocation for Project Ride activities in order to fully expend the grant funds; and

 WHEREAS, the Seattle Police Department is interested in receiving the additional grant funds in order to further expand the Seattle Team for Youth/Project Ride program; Now, Therefore,

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. As requested by the Chief of Police, the Chief of Police or his designee is authorized to execute for and on behalf of The City of Seattle, a supplemental agreement with King County in the amount of Nineteen Thousand Three Hundred Twenty Five Dollars ($19,325) for overtime costs for Seattle Police officers working in the Seattle Team for Youth/Project Ride program. The funds, when received, are hereby accepted and shall be deposited to the credit of the Emergency Subfund.

 Section 2. To carry out the purposes of the grant agreement authorized in Section 1, and contingent upon the execution of that agreement, the following line of business in the 2001 Budget of the Police Department is increased as follows:

 P10 - Administration $19,325

 For such purposes, the sum of Nineteen Thousand Three Hundred Twenty Five Dollars ($19,325) is hereby appropriated from the Emergency Subfund for the purposes outlined above. The Emergency Subfund shall be reimbursed from funds to be received from King County as authorized in Section 1.

 Section 3. Any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 4. The foregoing appropriation is made to meet actual necessary expenditures of the City for which no appropriation has been made due to causes which could not reasonably have been foreseen at the time of the making of the 2001 Budget in accordance with RCW 35.32A.060.

 Section 5. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
