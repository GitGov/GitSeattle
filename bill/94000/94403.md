**Council Bill Number: 94403**
   
**Ordinance Number: 102847**
********

 AN ORDINANCE creating a special fund designated the "Personnel Services Operating Fund" for the payment of costs and expenses of the City's Personnel Office, providing for deposits in and expenditures from such fund, abolishing the "Training Operating Fund" and the "Emergency Employment Operating Fund" and transferring the unencumbered assets of said funds to the "Personnel Services Operating Fund."

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** December 28, 1973
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** October 9, 1973
   
**Committee:** Human Resources and Judicial
   
**Sponsor:** COOLEY
