 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Pioneer Square Historic District; amending Sections 2, 6 and 9 and adding a new Section 6A to Ordinance 98852 to increase the membership and establish terms for the Pioneer Square Historic Preservation Board, to provide for reappointment and reconfirmation of existing members, to regulate the erection, placement or changing of signs in said District, and to provide civil penalties in place of criminal penalties for violations.
