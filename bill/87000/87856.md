**Ordinance Number: 96326**

 AN ORDINANCE of the City of Seattle, Washington, providing for the submission to the qualified electors of the City at a special election to be held therein on February 13, 1968, of a proposition authorizing the City to issue its general obligation bonds in the principal amount of not to exceed $6,200,000 for the purpose of constructing fourteen new fire stations and one new fire equipment maintenance shop within the City and acquiring new fire fighting apparatus and equipment for the City.
