**Ordinance Number: 116612**

 AN ORDINANCE repealing Ordinance 18026, 24359, and 28758 which granted the right, privilege and authority to Oregon-Washington Railroad Company, its successors and assigns, to locate, construct, maintain and operate sundry railway tracks and the right-of-way for such tracks along and across certain sundry streets, avenues, alleys and other public places of the City; accepting easements; terminating the Indemnity Agreement between the City of Seattle and Oregon-Washington Railroad Company recorded on April 9, 1921; authorizing acceptance of funds as full and final compensation for long term maintenance of certain structures; and superseding Ordinance 116140.
