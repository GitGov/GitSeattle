

********

**Council Bill Number: 109935**
********

 AN ORDINANCE requiring the wearing of a bicycle helmet while cycling or riding on a bicycle in streets, parks and other public places; making a parent or guardian responsible for requiring use of such helmets by a minor; setting safety standards for bicycle helmets offered for sale or lease; providing remedies and prescribing penalties; and adding new Sections 11.44.350, -.360, -.370, -.380, and -.390 and Sections 18.12.285 and -.287 to Seattle Municipal Code therefor.

**Status:** New
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** October 25, 1993
   
**Committee:** Transportation
   
**Sponsor:** CHOE
   
   
**Index Terms:** BICYCLING, SAFETY
