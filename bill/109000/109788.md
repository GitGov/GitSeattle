**Ordinance Number: 116801**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Engineering Department; authorizing the Director of Engineering to proceed with the design of signal, street and sidewalk improvements (West Campus Garage - Street Improvements) made necessary by the University of Washington's West Campus Parking Garage Project; authorizing the Director of Engineering to execute interlocal agreements with the University of Washington for payment of City costs relating to the Project; authorizing the acceptance, deposit and appropriation of the funds to be received; making an increase of expenditure allowances in the 1993 Budget of the Engineering Department; and contingent upon the University of Washington's compliance with the State Environmental Policy Act and completion of the required permitting process, authorizing the hiring of consultants and proceeding with construction therefor.
