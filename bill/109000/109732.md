

********

**Council Bill Number: 109732**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to land use and zoning; restricting heliports and helistops in the Shoreline District to those serving public safety, news gathering and emergency medical care functions, those that are part of an approved transportation plan and are a public facility, and those that are part of an approved transportation plan and at least 2,000 feet from a residential zone by amending Sections 23.60.092, 23.60.664 and 23.60.730 of the Seattle Municipal Code.

**Status:** Retired 97
   
**Vote:** 9-0
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** August 5, 1996
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** June 14, 1993
   
**Committee:** Land Use
   
**Sponsor:** DONALDSON
   
   
**Index Terms:** ZONING, SHORELINE, HELICOPTERS
