**Council Bill Number: 109100**

 Relating to Seattle Schools; authorizing the Director of Administrative Services to enter into an inter-governmental agreement with Seattle School District No. 1 for leasing and maintaining of vehicles; increasing an expenditure allowance in the 1992 Budget of the Department of Administrative Services to acquire vehicles for lease to the Seattle School District; making a reimbursable appropriation from the Administrative Services Fund; authorizing the Director to enter into financing agreements; and authorizing the City Comptroller to propose the issuance of obligations of the City to provide alternative financing, all by three-fourths vote of the City Council.
