**Council Bill Number: 109930**
   
**Ordinance Number: 116924**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to used goods dealers; repealing the existing territorial limitation on pawnshops and substituting a prohibition upon location within 1,000 feet of school grounds, allowing pawnshops only in certain land use zones, requiring firearms dealer's licenses for used goods dealers handling firearms, requiring computerized reporting of pawnshop transactions, and correcting technical matters; placing a temporary moratorium on new pawnshop locations and limiting future growth in the number of pawnshop locations; amending Sections 6.288.010 and 6.288.040 and Subsections 6.288.030A and 6.288.120C and D of the Seattle Municipal Code, and adding new Sections 6.288.065, 6.288.085, and 6.288.130 in connection therewith.
