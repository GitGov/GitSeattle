**Ordinance Number: 116209**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the City's Health Care Program; authorizing the Personnel Director to contract for and administer an increase effective January 1, 1992, in the amount payable annually by Washington Dental Services to a maximum of One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00) each for certain covered persons under the City's dental plans; and authorizing the Personnel Director to contract for and administer certain modifications in the City's medical care plans effective March 1, 1992, covering certain eligible City officers and employees and their dependents.
