 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 3, Mt. Baker Park Addition, being the alley between 30th Avenue South and 31st Avenue South, from McClellan Street to Lander Street, by grading and paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13729 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
