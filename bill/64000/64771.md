**Ordinance Number: 73528**

 An ordinance relating to the municipal water supply system, defining the Cedar River Watershed, directing the Superintendent of Water to assume exclusive control of all access to and activities within said watershed and the issuance of revocable permits therefor, transferring four positions of Sanitary Patrolman from the Department of Health and Sanitation to the Water Department and providing for their salaries from the Water Fund as an emergency outlay, and repealing Ordinances Nos. 37296 and 52259.
