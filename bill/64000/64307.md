 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 11 and 12, Front Street Cable Addition, and between Blocks 11 and 12, Supplemental Plat to the Front Street Cable Addition, being the alley between 1st Avenue West and Queen Anne Avenue, from West McGraw Street to West Smith Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 13636 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
