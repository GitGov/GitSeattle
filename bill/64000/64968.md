 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 143, A. A. Denny's Broadway Addition, being the alley between Broadway and 10th Avenue, from East Columbia Street to East Marion Street, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 13766 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
