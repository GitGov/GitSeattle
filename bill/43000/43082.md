**Ordinance Number: 52956**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal street railway system; providing for the construction and acquisition of, and payment for, certain street railway lines, property and equipment in the vicinity of the University Stadium, on California Avenue, 15th Avenue Northwest and Fauntleroy Avenue; and declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; providing for the issuance and sale of warrants to provide funds therefor; creating and establishing a construction fund; and creating and establishing a special fund to pay the principal and interest of said warrants.
