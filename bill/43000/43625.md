**Council Bill Number: 43625**
   
**Ordinance Number: 53485**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting, a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal light and power plant and system; providing for the construction and acquisition of a wood stave pipe line at Cedar Falls from the masonry dam to the power house; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; creating a special fund and transferring thereto the sum of $50,000.00 by an appropriation from the surplus in the Light Fund, as a temporary advance to capital account, in order to defray the expense therefor; and authorizing expenditures and appropriating funds therefor.
