**Ordinance Number: 113685**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the City Light Department; declaring property known as the Georgetown Steam Plant and other abutting property, surplus to the City's needs and no longer required for continued public utility services; and authorizing said Georgetown Steam Plant site, a Seattle historical landmark, and other abutting land to be conveyed by lease/option and option to buy to the "Friends of the Georgetown Steam Plant" a non-profit organization, for the development of an electric museum.
