 AN ORDINANCE relating to the permit granted to J.R. Burke by Ordinance 106488 to use and occupy that portion of Fremont Avenue North under the Fremont Bridge lying north of the Lake Washington Canal; consenting to the transfer of the permission granted by Ordinance 107488 from J.R. Burke to the Fremont Dock Company; renewing the permit for an additional period (10) years; establishing the annual fee for the first five (5) years of the renewal period; and amending said ordinance to allow the future transfer of permission by Council resolution.
