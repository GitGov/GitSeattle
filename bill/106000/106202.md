**Council Bill Number: 106202**
   
**Ordinance Number: 113566**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Criminal Code; adding a new section to Chapter 12A. 24 (Liquor Offenses) of the Seattle Municipal Code to make it unlawful, in a public place, to open a container of liquor, consume liquor, or possess an open container of liquor except as permitted by state liquor control laws and providing a penalty therefor.

**Date passed by Full Council:** August 24, 1987
   
**Vote:** 6-0
