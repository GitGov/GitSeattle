**Ordinance Number: 114034**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to and governing solicitations for charitable contributions from City employees to be made by payroll deductions; establishing and providing for the appointment of members of a committee of City employees to recommend guidelines and to coordinate the conduct of such solicitations; authorizing the City Comptroller to adopt rules to control campaigns for solicitations of City employees and to formalize procedures for payroll deductions from City employees for charitable purposes; and adding a new chapter to the Seattle Municipal Code.
