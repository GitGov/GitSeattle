**Council Bill Number: 106602**
   
**Ordinance Number: 113872**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to payments and billings under one dollar, adding a new Seattle Municipal Code Section 4.20.700 to eliminate such pay to employees, a new section 5.12.060 to end drawing warrants for payments under one dollar to contractors; a new Section 5.20.050 to end billings for labor and materials under on dollar; and a new Section 5.24.035 for payments of claims.

**Vote:** 8-0
