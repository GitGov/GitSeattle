**Council Bill Number: 5311**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18371**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 5th Avenue Northeast, from Maple Leaf Place to East 77th Street; Latona Avenue from East Green Lake Boulevard to East 77th Street; 1st Avenue Northeast from North 75th Street to North 77th Street; Sunnyside Avenue and Bagley Avenue, each from East Green Lake Boulevard to North 77th Street; Home Place, from East Green Lake Boulevard to Crescent Place; all of Olympic Court lying to the eastward of East Green Lake Boulevard; Crescent Place, from Olympic Court to Bagley Avenue; Woodlawn Avenue from Maple Leaf Place to North 75th Street; North 75th Street, from Sunnyside Avenue to 1st Avenue Northeast; and North and East 77th Street, from East Green Lake Boulevard to 5th Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden cross walks, with all necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1263 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
