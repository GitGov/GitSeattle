**Council Bill Number: 5070**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18180**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 32nd and 33rd Avenues, each from the north line of Albion Heights Addition to the City of Seattle, to the south line of Waddell's Madrona Park Addition to the City of Seattle; East Pine Street, from 32nd Avenue to the east line of Buchanan's Addition to the City of Seattle; East Olive Street, from 32nd Avenue to 34th Avenue; East Howell Street, from 32nd Avenue to 38th Avenue; 37th Avenue form a point 81 feet north of East Olive Street to East Denny Way, all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, by constructing concrete sidewalks, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1194 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
