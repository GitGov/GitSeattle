**Council Bill Number: 5344**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18386**
********

 An ordinance transferring the sum of $1700.00 from the General Fund to the Library Fund as a temporary loan, for the purpose of enabling the Library Board to procure additional assistance and purchase supplies make necessary in keeping the library open daily until ten o'clock P. M. for the period from June 1, 1908 to March 1, 1909.
