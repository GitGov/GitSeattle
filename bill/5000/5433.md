**Ordinance Number: 18666**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Aurora Avenue, from North Sixty-sixth Street to North Eighty-fifth Street; Lincoln Avenue, from North Sixty-first Street to North Sixty-fifth Street; Woodstock Place, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street; Fremont Avenue, from North Sixty-first Street to North Sixty-fourth Street and from North Sixty-fifth Street to North Sixty-sixth Street; North Sixty-first Street, from Phinney Avenue to the east line of Whitman Avenue; North Sixty-fourth Street, from Phinney Avenue to the east line of Aurora Avenue; North Sixty-fifth Street, from Fremont Avenue to Linden Avenue; North Sixty-sixth Street, from Fremont Avenue to Aurora Avenue; North Sixty-eighth Street, from Greenwood Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard; North Seventy-second Street and North Seventy-third Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to Aurora to Aurora Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete walks and wooden crosswalks, together with all the necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage, in accordance with Resolution No. 1321 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
