**Council Bill Number: 5076**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18160**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1594, for the improvement of Jackson Street, in the City of Seattle from 2nd Avenue South to a point 52 feet east of the east margin of 3rd Avenue South, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with granite curbs, and by constructing the necessary bulkheads and retaining walls and making such extensions to the existing sewer and water main systems as may be necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage and water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 16977 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
