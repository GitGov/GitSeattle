**Council Bill Number: 5621**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18712**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fifteenth Avenue Northeast, from Brintnal Place to East Fiftieth Street; East Fortieth Street, from Fifteenth Avenue Northeast to Sixth Avenue Northeast; and Sixth Avenue Northeast, from Northlake Avenue to East Fortieth Street; East Forty-first Street, East Forty-second Street, East Forty-third Street, East Forty-fifth Street, East Forty-seventh Street and East Fiftieth Street, each from Fifteenth Avenue Northeast to Fourteenth Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, by sub-grading and paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing gutters of brick or other suitable materials, curbs of concrete with curved corners of granite or armoured concrete, together with all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, provided, that the portion of Sixth Avenue Northeast, between Northlake Avenue and East Forty-fifth Street be improved by planking the, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1341 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
