**Ordinance Number: 18471**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 45, A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, being the alley between 2nd Avenue and 3rd Avenue from Pine Street to Stewart Street, in the City of Seattle, by grading and paving the same with asphalt including all water mains, sewers and appurtenances necessary for the proper water service, fire protection, sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 34553, of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
