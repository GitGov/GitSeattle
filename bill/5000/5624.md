**Council Bill Number: 5624**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18696**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Queen Anne Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from West Garfield Street to West Boston Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation and by constructing bricks gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, the construction of all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenance necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1405 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
