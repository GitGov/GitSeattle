**Council Bill Number: 5328**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18380**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1678, for the improvement of the alleys in blocks 26 and 27 of additions to the Town (now City) of Seattle, as laid off by A. A. Denny, being the alleys between 1st Avenue and 2nd Avenue, from Pike Street to Stewart Street, by grading and paving the same with vitrified brick, or other suitable paving material, laid upon a concrete foundation, together with such sewers, catch basins, and other appurtenances as may be necessary, under Ordinance No. 17701 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
