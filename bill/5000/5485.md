**Ordinance Number: 18483**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 11th Avenue from the northerly margin of East Marion Street to the northerly margin of East Spring Street; and East Spring Street from the easterly margin of 11th Avenue to the southerly margin of East Madison Street, from the improvement of 11th Avenue, et al, by paving, as provided for by Ordinance No. 15074; and providing for the partial release of the Independent Asphalt Paving Company from its contract with the City of Seattle for said improvement; and providing for the refunding of certain paid assessments for said improvement; and providing for the cancellation of certain assessments for said improvement; and providing for the cancellation of certain bonds heretofore ordered issued in payment for said improvement.
