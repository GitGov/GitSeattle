**Ordinance Number: 18333**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West Stacy Street in the City of Seattle, from a point about 130 feet west of the west margin of Whatcom Avenue to a point about 750 feet west of the west margin of Whatcom Avenue, by constructing a plank roadway thereon 20 feet in width, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1083 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
