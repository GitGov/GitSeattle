**Council Bill Number: 5118**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18239**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Eastlake Avenue, in the City of Seattle between East Roy Street and the south end of the bridge across Lake Union, in the extension of 6th Avenue Northeast in the following manner to-wit: by paving the same with vitrified brick having a roadway 46 feet between curbs, between East Roy Street and the north margin of Almy Street, and with a roadway 50 feet between curbs, form the north line of East Boston Street to the north line of Fuhrman Avenue, and by planking the same with a roadway increasing Almy Street to 50 feet between curbs, between the west margin of Eastlake Avenue produced across Fuhrman Avenue and the south end of the bridge across Lake Union; all brick and asphalt to be laid upon proper concrete base; the curbs abutting brick and asphalt pavement to be of concrete with granite corners at street intersections and alley crossings; the curbs abutting planked roadway to be of wood, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1222 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
