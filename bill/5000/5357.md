**Ordinance Number: 18406**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Pine Street, from Westlake Avenue to the alley between 7th and 8th Avenues; Stewart Street, from Westlake Avenue to the alley between 8th and 9th Avenues; Olive Street, from Westlake Avenue to the alley between 8th and 9th Avenues; 5th Avenue from 200 feet south of the south line of a point 200 feet south of the south line of Pine Street to Westlake Avenue; 6th Avenue, from Pike Street to Westlake Avenue, by planking the same and constructing wood cross walks thereon, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1294 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
