**Council Bill Number: 5215**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18311**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Roy Street and West Roy Street, from 2nd Avenue North to 6th Avenue West; Valley Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to 2nd Avenue North; Aloha Street and West Aloha Street, form 2nd Avenue North to 1st Avenue West; Ward Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to Warren Avenue; West Prospect Street from 2nd Avenue West to 5th Avenue West and from 6th Avenue West to 8th Avenue West; 1st Avenue North and Warren Avenue, each form Roy Street to Prospect Street; 1st Avenue West from West Roy Street to Queen Anne Drive; 2nd Avenue West and 3rd Avenue West, each from West Roy Street to West Prospect Street; 5th Avenue West, from Olympic Place to Kinnear Place; Kinnear Place, from 5th Avenue West to West Prospect Street; Olympic Place and Olympic Way, from 5th Avenue West to West Prospect Street; Olympic Place and Olympic Way from 5th Avenue West to West Prospect Street; Park Place and Epler Place, each from Olympic Place to Kinnear Place, all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners, and including as part of said work, all necessary grading and re- grading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1228 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
