**Council Bill Number: 5681**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18720**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1665, for the improvement of Washington Street, in the City of Seattle, from the east margin of the alley between First Avenue South and Railroad Avenue to the east margin of Railroad Avenue, by paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing granite curbs and laying such sewers and water mains as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 17569 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
