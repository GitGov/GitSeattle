**Council Bill Number: 5489**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18485**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 17th Avenue South in the City of Seattle, as a public street and highway, over and across portions of block 14, Sander's Supplemental Plat to Sander's Addition to the City of Seattle; block 9, J. C. Kinnear's Addition to the City of Seattle; block 8, Bayside Addition to the City of Seattle, and blocks 1 and 2, C. Hanford's First Addition to the City of Seattle; and providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of 17th Avenue South from Atlantic Street to Beacon Avenue; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 17th Avenue South; and for the grading and regarding of said avenue and approaches thereto, in conformity with established grades, and providing for the construction of necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenues and approaches thereto; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
