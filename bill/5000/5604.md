**Council Bill Number: 5604**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18595**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 15251, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Madison Street from Nineteenth Avenue to one hundred twenty (120) feet east of Twenty-ninth Avenue from the south, by paving the same with sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite corners (except however, those portions of East Madison Street lying from one hundred sixty (160) feet east of the east margin of Twentieth Avenue on the north to one hundred forty (140) feet west of Twenty-second Avenue on the north; from one hundred twenty (120) feet east of Twenty-seventh Avenue on the north to one hundred twenty (120) feet east of Twenty-ninth Avenue on the south, which portions shall be paved with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing such sewers and water mains, together with appurtenances, as may be necessary, in accordance with Resolution No. 358 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle", approved January 24th, 1907.
