**Ordinance Number: 18690**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Seventeenth Avenue South, from Atlantic Street to Forest Street; College Street, from Seventeenth Avenue South to Sixteenth Avenue South; Sixteenth Avenue South, from a point one hundred (100) feet south of Plum Street to a point two hundred forty (240) feet south of Bay View Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and material, with all wyes, openings, flush tanks, manholes, lampholes, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1391 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
