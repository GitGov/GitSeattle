**Council Bill Number: 5066**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18176**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Denny Way, from 32nd Avenue North to 37th Avenue North; 32nd Avenue North from a point 120 feet north of East pine Street to East John Street; 33rd Avenue and 33rd Avenue North, each from a point 120 feet north of East pike Street to a point 100 feet north of East Denny Way; 34th Avenue and 34th Avenue North, from a point 120 feet north of East Pike Street to 35th Avenue North; 36th Avenue from East Howell Street to East Denny Way; the alley between East Howell Street and East Denny Way, from 36th Avenue to the west line of Lot 14, Block 8, of Waddell's Madrona Park Addition to the City of Seattle; 37th Avenue and 37th Avenue North from a point 100 feet south of East John Street to East Howell Street; East Howell Street from 37th Avenue to 38th Avenue; Florence Court from a point 100 feet west of 37th Avenue North to 34th Avenue North; East John Street from 31st Avenue North to 32nd Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1195 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
