**Council Bill Number: 5007**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18140**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Kirkwood Place, from East Green Lake Boulevard to Ladd Street; Keystone Place from East 55th Street to McKinley Place; Kenwood Place, from North 55th Street to East Green Lake Boulevard; Wallingford Avenue from North 50th Street to Woodlawn Avenue; Woodlawn Avenue from North 50th Street to North 55th Street; North 55th Street from East Green Lake Boulevard to Keystone Place; Ladd Street from Keystone Place to Kirkwood Place and McKinley Place from Wallingford Avenue to Kirkwood Place; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1173 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
