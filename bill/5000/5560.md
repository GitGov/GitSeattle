**Ordinance Number: 18692**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-fifth Avenue North, Twenty-sixth Avenue North, Twenty-seventh Avenue North, Twenty-eighth Avenue North and Twenty-ninth Avenue North, each from East Madison Street to East Roy Street; Union Boulevard, from East Roy Street to East Ward Street; Twenty-eighth Avenue North, from East Ward Street to Helen Street; East Roy Street, East Valley Street and East Aloha Street, each from Twenty-fourth Avenue North to Union Boulevard, and East Ward and Helen Street, each from Twenty-fifth Avenue North to Twenty-eighth Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by laying water mains therein of proper dimensions and material together with all hydrants, crosses, tees and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1368 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
