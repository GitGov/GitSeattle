**Ordinance Number: 18599**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1368, for the improvement of Twenty-third Avenue North, from East Thomas Street to East Roy Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and constructing concrete sidewalks and stairway, with all necessary crosswalks and including the construction of all necessary bulkheads or retaining walls for cuts or fills, and the construction of such sewers, catch basins and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage under Ordinance No. 15076 assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
