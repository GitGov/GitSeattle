**Ordinance Number: 18177**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 18th Avenue Northwest from West 65th Street to West 75th Street; 17th Avenue Northwest from West 67th Street to West 75th Street; 16th Avenue Northwest from West 67th Street to West 75th Street; West 70th Street, from 15th Avenue Northwest to 19th Avenue Northwest; and West 73rd Street from 15th Avenue Northwest to 19th Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and constructing concrete walks, with all necessary appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1199 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
