**Council Bill Number: 5563**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18695**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-seventh Avenue South, from Yesler Way to Dearborn Street; Lane Street and King Street, each from Twenty-third Avenue South to Thirteenth Avenue South; Main Street, from Twenty-third Avenue South to Twenty-fourth Avenue South, and from Temple Place to Twenty-eighth Avenue South; Temple Place, from Washington Street to Main Street, and Washington Street, from Twenty-fourth Avenue South to Twenty-eighth Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt and sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with concrete curbs with granite and armoured concrete corners, together with such water mains of proper dimensions and material, with appurtenances, as may be necessary, in accordance with Resolution No. 1373 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
