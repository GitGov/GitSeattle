**Council Bill Number: 5435**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18667**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Aloha Street and East Roy Street, each from North Broadway to Eleventh Avenue North; Tenth Avenue North and Eleventh Avenue North, each from the north line of the Lincoln Pontius Addition, to the City of Seattle to East Prospect Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary, grading and regrading, and the construction of all sewer and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1308; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
