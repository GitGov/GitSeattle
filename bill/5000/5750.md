**Council Bill Number: 5750**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18772**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1638, for the improvement of the alley in Blocks Nine (9) and Ten (10) of David S. Maynard's Plat of the Town (now City) of Seattle, being the alleys from Yesler Way to Main Street between Occidental Avenue and Second Avenue South, by filling and planking the alley in Block Nine (9), and by paving the alley in Block Ten (10) with vitrified brick, and constructing a concrete bulkhead therein, under Ordinance No. 17430, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
