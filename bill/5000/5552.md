**Council Bill Number: 5552**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18688**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Galer Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to the west margin of Seventh Avenue West; West Garfield Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to a point one hundred fifty-five (155) feet west of Seventh Avenue West; First Avenue West, Second Avenue West, Third Avenue West, Fourth Avenue West, Fifth Avenue West, Sixth Avenue West and Seventh Avenue West, each from West Galer Street to West Garfield Street, and Queen Anne Avenue from south margin of West Galer Street to north margin of West Garfield Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks the construction of all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; provided however, that when the grades exceed eight (8) per cent, the pavement may be of sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1346 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
