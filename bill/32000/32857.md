**Ordinance Number: 43294**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 42388 entitled: "An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of a public alley twenty (20) feet in width over and across a portion of Section nineteen (19), Township Twenty-four (24) North, Range Four (4) East, W. M., from East Marginal Way to Commercial Waterway No. 1; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described alley; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law", passed by the City Council the 20th day of June, 1921, and approved by the Mayor on the 22nd day of June, 1921; providing for the dismissal of action in the Superior Court of the State of Washington in and for King County based upon said Ordinance and being Cause No. 155098 of said Court; providing for the payment of all taxable costs and making an appropriation for such payment.
