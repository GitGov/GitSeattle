**Council Bill Number: 32824**
   
**Ordinance Number: 43279**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North and East 42nd Street, from the East margin of 8th Avenue Northeast to Wallingford Avenue; 1st Avenue Northeast, from the north margin of East 42nd Street on the east to the north margin of North 42nd Street on the west; 6th Avenue Northeast, from the south margin of East 42nd Street on the eats to the north margin of East 42nd Street on the west; and Sunnyside Avenue from North 40th Street to North 45th Street, all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6730, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
