**Ordinance Number: 42623**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of The West side of California Avenue, from West Myrtle Street to West Othello Street; The Southerly side of West Othello Street, from California Avenue to 44th Avenue Southwest; and The Easterly side of 44th Avenue Southwest, from West Othello Street to Ida Street; all in The City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6778 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
