**Council Bill Number: 32390**
   
**Ordinance Number: 42883**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Yale Avenue, from Melrose Avenue to Howell Street; Minor Avenue and Boren Avenue, each from Pine Street to Howell Street; and Terry Avenue, from Pine Street to Olive Street; all in The City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6540 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
