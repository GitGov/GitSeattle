 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Lake Park Drive, from a point opposite Lot Ten (10), Block Thirty-two (32), Mount Baker Park Addition, to Shoreland Drive; and Shoreland Drive, from Lake Park Drive to connection with sewer outfall between Lots One (1) and Two (2), Replat of Lots One (1) to Nineteen (10) inclusive, in Block Seventy (70), Mount Baker Park Addition; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6774 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds", and declaring an emergency.
