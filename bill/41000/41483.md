**Ordinance Number: 51422**
********

 An ordinance relating to the appeal to the Tax Commission of the State of Washington and to the courts from any adverse action, order or decision made by the Board of Equalization of King County, Washington, authorized by Ordinance No. 51390, in relation to the petition of the City of Seattle for an increase for taxation purposes of the leasehold interest of the Metropolitan Building Company; appropriating money to the use of the Corporation Counsel to defray the cost and expense necessary to be incurred in connection with such appeal, and declaring the emergency making necessary such appropriation and the immediate taxing effect of this ordinance.
