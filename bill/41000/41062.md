**Council Bill Number: 41062**
   
**Ordinance Number: 51034**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Lynn Street from Boyer Avenue to 24th Avenue North, and from 26th Avenue North on the north to approximately 180 feet east thereof; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 8276 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
