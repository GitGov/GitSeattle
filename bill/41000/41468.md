**Council Bill Number: 41468**
   
**Ordinance Number: 51435**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal light and power plant and system; providing for the construction and acquisition of a railroad from the Gorge Creek Crib Diversion Dam to Diablo Canyon on the Skagit River; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; creating a special fund and transferring thereto the sum of Three Hundred Fifty Thousand ($350,000.00) Dollars by an appropriation from the surplus in the Light Fund, as a temporary advance to Capital Account, in order to defray the expense thereof; authorizing and directing the preparation of plans and specifications for, and the construction and acquisition of, said improvements; and authorizing expenditures and appropriating funds therefor.
