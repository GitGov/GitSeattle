**Council Bill Number: 41121**
   
**Ordinance Number: 51089**
********

 An ordinance relating to the municipal light and power plant and system; providing for the purchase pursuant to Ordinances Nos. 36069 and 48405, of James Street and West Seattle Sub-stations of the Puget Sound Power and Light Company as additions and betterments thereto, and extensions thereof; providing for the payment therefor by money appropriated from the Light Fund as a temporary loan to capital account, and directing the Department of Lighting to furnish five thousand (5000 kw) kilowatts of power through said sub-stations to the municipal street railway system at the same rates as specified in the contract executed pursuant to Ordinance No. 39069.
