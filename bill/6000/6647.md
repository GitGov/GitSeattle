**Council Bill Number: 6647**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19690**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1353, for the improvement of Fifth Avenue North, from Harrison Street to Roy Street, by grading and regrading the same to the elevations prescribed by constructing approaches to the different streets intersecting the said streets for such distance back as may be necessary; for the construction of all necessary bulkheads and retaining walls and such temporary structures and embankments as may be necessary to provide for temporary crossing at Republican Street and Mercer Street; and providing for the regrading and residewalking of intersecting streets where necessary in order to provide approaches to the said streets and avenues, under Ordinance No. 15001 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
