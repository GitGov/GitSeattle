**Ordinance Number: 19509**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fifteenth Avenue West, in the City of Seattle, from the south margin of West Newton Street to the north margin of West Armour Street, by planking the same; provided, however, that fifty (50) per cent of the total cost of this improvement shall be paid for out of the General Fund of the City of Seattle, in accordance with Resolution No. 1659 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
