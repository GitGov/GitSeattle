**Ordinance Number: 19421**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 6th Avenue Northeast from Northlake Avenue to East 45th Street; East 45th Street, and North 45th Street, from 6th Avenue Northeast to Meridian Avenue; Meridian Avenue from North 42nd Street to North 45th Street; Kenwood Place from North 45th Street to McKinley Place; Thackery Place, from East 45th Street to East 50th Street; East 50th Street, from Thackery Place to Latona Avenue; Latona Avenue from East 50th Street to East 65th Street; East 65th Street, from Latona Avenue to 4th Avenue Northeast; 4th Avenue Northeast, from East 65th Street to Woodlawn Avenue; Woodlawn Avenue, from North 65th Street to Hillman Place; Sunnyside Avenue, from North 42nd Street to North 45th Street; 5th Avenue Northeast, from Northlake Avenue to East 42nd Street; all in the City of Seattle by attaching fire hydrants with necessary appurtenances to the existing water mains all in accordance with Resolution No. 1601 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
