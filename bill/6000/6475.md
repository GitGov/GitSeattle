**Council Bill Number: 6475**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22470**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1701, for the improvement of Olive Street from the alley between 9th Avenue and Terry Avenue to Melrose Avenue; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regarding the same to the elevations established in Ordinance No. 13797 and curbing where necessary, and including as a part of said grading and regarding the construction of all necessary bulkheads and retaining walls, the reconstruction of all sewers where necessary, and re-side walking and by making proper approaches where necessary; under Ordinance No. 18113 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
