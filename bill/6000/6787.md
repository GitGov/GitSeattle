**Ordinance Number: 19585**

 An Ordinance authorizing the discontinuance by The Seattle Electric Company of operation of the James Street line of its railway system for a period of two (2) weeks, commencing November 11, 1908, for the purpose of lowering the existing tracks between Third and Fourth Avenue to the established grade and between Fourth and Fifth Avenue approximately to the established grade as may be directed by the Board of Public Works.
