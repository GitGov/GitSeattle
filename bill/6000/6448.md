 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Lander Street, from Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest to California Avenue; Olga Street, from Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest, to Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest; West Wait Street, from Belvidere Avenue, to Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest; Grayson Street, from Belvidere Avenue, to Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest; Prescott Avenue from Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest to Thirty-seventh Avenue Southwest; Prescott Place, from Thirty-seventh Avenue Southwest to Belvidere Avenue; Forty-first Avenue Southwest, Walnut Avenue, Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest; Thirty-eighth Avenue Southwest, each from West Lander Street to West Wait Street; Thirty-seventh Avenue Southwest from West Lander Street to Prescott Avenue, Belvidere Avenue, from West Lander Street to Prescott Place, and Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest, from West Lander Street to Olga Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete walks thereon, together with all necessary appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 35482, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
