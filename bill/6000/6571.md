**Council Bill Number: 6571**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19583**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing The Seattle Electric Company, its successors and assigns, to temporarily cease the operation of its railway on certain portions of Melrose Avenue, East Pine Street, Summit Avenue and Bellevue Avenue, in the City of Seattle, until the expiration of ninety (90) days after said Melrose Avenue, East Pine Street, Summit Avenue and Bellevue Avenue shall have been regarded and ready for said railway to be restored thereon, and authorizing The Seattle Electric Company, its successors and assigns, to construct, maintain and operate its railway and tracks until said time on East Pike Street between Melrose and Belmont Avenues, on Belmont Avenue between East Pike and East Olive Streets and on East Olive Street between Belmont and Bellevue Avenues, and other streets and places in Seattle.
