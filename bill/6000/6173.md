**Council Bill Number: 6173**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19125**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1693, for the improvement of Mayfair Avenue, Second Avenue North and Warren Avenue each from Ray Street to Florentia Street; Third Avenue North from Florentia Street to Newell Street; Nob Hill Avenue from Fulton Street to a point one hundred eighty six and forty three one hundredths (186.43) feet south of the south margin of Newell Street; Fulton Street and Newell Street, each from First Avenue North to the east line of Mayfair Addition to the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete walks and alley crossings, and all necessary crosswalks and box drains, in accordance with plans prepared by the City Engineer under Ordinance No. 17939 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
