**Council Bill Number: 6756**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19772**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1749, for the improvement of Twenty-fifth Avenue South and Twenty-fifth Avenue South produced, from a point one hundred (100) feet south of Dearborn Street to Judkins Street, and Judkins Street, from Twenty-fifth Avenue South to a connection with the existing sewer in Twenty-sixth Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a sewer therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all wyes, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenance necessary for the proper sewering and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 18550, and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
