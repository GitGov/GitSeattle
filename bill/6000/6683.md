**Council Bill Number: 6683**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19531**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the changing and establishing of the grades of the alley in Block Thirty-four (34), Pontius Addition to the City of Seattle, and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said alley, in conformity with such established grades; and for the construction of all necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said alley; and providing that the payment for such improvement be made by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law; and repealing all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
