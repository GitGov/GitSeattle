**Ordinance Number: 19249**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Warrant Avenue, from Roy Street to existing manhole one hundred and twenty (120) feet south to the south margin of Harrison Street, and Second Avenue North, from Republican Street to Harrison Street, all in The City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and material, with all wyes, openings, flush tanks, manholes, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1560 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
