**Council Bill Number: 6815**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19615**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1716, for the improvement of alley in Block 59, A. A. Denny's Addition to The City of Seattle, being the alley from Pike Street to Pine Street between Sixth Avenue and Seventh Avenue, by grading the same and by constructing such bulkheads and sewers, with such appurtenances as may be necessary, under Ordinance No. 18205 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, declaring the same to be a first lien thereon, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
