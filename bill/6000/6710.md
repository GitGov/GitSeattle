**Ordinance Number: 19563**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Shelby Street and Hamlin Street, each from Tenth Avenue North eastward to Lake Union; Edgar Street, from Tenth Avenue North to Twelfth Avenue North; Roanoke Street, from Federal Avenue to Boyer Avenue as extended by Ordinance No. 17537; Millard Street, from Harvard Avenue North to Fuhrman Avenue; Allison Street, from Franklin Avenue to Fuhrman Avenue; Barton Place, from Allison Street to Fuhrman Avenue; Fuhrman Avenue, from Franklin Avenue to Shelby Street; Harvard Avenue North, from Franklin Avenue to Eastlake Avenue; Eleventh Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Twelfth Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; and Boyer Avenue, as extended by Ordinance No. 17537, from the west line of Delmar Park Addition to Twelfth Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading where necessary and constructing concrete or wood walks, and wooden crosswalks thereon, together with wood or iron railing and concrete stairways where necessary, in accordance with Resolution No. 1644 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
