 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Bagley Avenue, Meridian Avenue and Corliss Avenue, each from North Forty-fifth Street to North Thirty-seventh Street, and North Thirty-seventh Street, from Meridian Avenue to Sunnyside Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with such manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewering and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1508 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
