**Council Bill Number: 6335**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19289**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1725, for the improvement of West Lee Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to Fifth Avenue West; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, the construction of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 18310 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.

**Status:** Passed
