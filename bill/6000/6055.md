 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1635, for the improvement of the alley in Block Three (3), David S. Maynard's Plat of the Town (now City) of Seattle, being the alley from Jackson Street to Main Street, between First Avenue South and Railroad Avenue, by filling and planking the same; provide that the grade of said alley shall be a straight grade from Main Street to Jackson Street, under Ordinance No. 17427 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
