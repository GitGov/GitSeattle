**Council Bill Number: 6989**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19786**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-first Avenue South, from Jackson Street to a point seventy-five (75) feet south of Judkins Street; Thirtieth Avenue South, from Dearborn Street to Norman Street; Thirty-second Avenue South, from King Street to the south line of Bird & Esry's Addition and from the north line of Mt. Baker Addition to Irving Street; Thirty-third Avenue South, from King Street to Massachusetts Street; King Street and Lane Street, each from Thirtieth Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South; Dearborn Street, from Thirtieth Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South; Charles Street, from Twenty-ninth Avenue South to Thirtieth Avenue South, and from a point eighty-two (82) feet west of Thirty-third Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South; Norman Street, from Twenty-seventh Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South; Judkins Street, from Thirty-first Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South; Irving Street, from Thirty-second Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South; Day Street and Atlantic Street, each from Thirty-third Avenue South to Thirty-fourth Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and by the construction of all necessary drains and bulkheads, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1742 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
