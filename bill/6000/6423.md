 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 18180, entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-second Avenue and Thirty-third Avenue, each from the north line of Albion Heights Addition to the City of Seattle to the south line of Waddell's Madrona Park Addition to the City of Seattle; East Pine Street, from Thirty-second Avenue to the east line of Buchanan's Addition to the City of Seattle; East Olive Street from Thirty-second Avenue to Thirty-fourth Avenue; East Howell Street, from Thirty-second Avenue to Thirty-eighth Avenue; Thirty-seventh Avenue, from a point eighty-one (81) feet north of East Olive Street to East Howell Street, and Thirty-eighth Avenue, from East Olive Street to East Denny Way, all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and constructing concrete walks in accordance with Resolution No. 1194 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle," approved April 7, 1908.
