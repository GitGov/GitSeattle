**Ordinance Number: 19841**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Dayton Avenue, Phinney Avenue and Greenwood Avenue, each from Kilbourne Street to North Forty-second Street; Evanston Avenue and Sunset Place, each from Kilbourne Street to North Thirty-ninth Street; Palatine Avenue and First Avenue Northwest, each from Kilbourne Street to West Thirty-ninth Street; West Thirty-ninth Street, from Third Avenue Northwest to Palatine Avenue; Bowdoin Place, from Third Avenue Northwest to Greenwood Avenue and from Phinney Avenue to Dayton Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing water mains therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with such gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1704 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
