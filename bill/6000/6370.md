**Council Bill Number: 6370**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19257**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, extending, widening, and establishing of Fremont Avenue, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street, and from a point Four and Ten One- hundredths (4.10) feet south from the south margin of North Sixty-seven Street to a point One Hundred Twelve (12) feet south from the south margin of North Seventy-second Street; and of North Sixty-fifth Street, from Fremont Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard, all in the City of Seattle; and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and providing that the payment for such improvement be made by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
