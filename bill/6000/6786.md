**Ordinance Number: 19611**

 An Ordinance granting to The Seattle Electric Company permission and authority to discontinue operating cars on Mains Street, between Fourth Avenue South and Twelfth Avenue South; on Eighth Avenue South, between Main Street, and Washington Street; on Washington Street, between Eight Avenue South and Twelfth Avenue South; on Ninth Avenue South, between Main Street and Washington Street; on Twelfth Avenue South and Twelfth Avenue, between Washington Street and East Columbia Street; on East Columbia Street, between Twelfth Avenue and Fourteenth Avenue; on Fourteenth Avenue, between East Columbia Street and East Union Street, until the expiration of a reasonable length of time for reconstructing its tracks after the completion of the regrade of Jackson Street and the other streets affected by such regrade.
