**Council Bill Number: 6466**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19422**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Western Avenue, in the City of Seattle from the south margin of Virginia Street to Denny Way, by grading and regarding the same, to the grades established by Ordinance No. 18109, by constructing approaches where necessary at the intersecting streets, by constructing curbs and gutters and providing for the necessary surface drainage, and including as a part of said work the alteration, extension or construction of all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage, fire protection, and water service of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1615 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
