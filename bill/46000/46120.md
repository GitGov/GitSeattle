**Council Bill Number: 46120**
   
**Ordinance Number: 55891**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal street railway system; providing for the construction, acquisition and replacement of, and payment for, a double track street railway on Third Avenue, from Pine Street to Yesler Way, acquired with a view to the necessity of such replacement; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be, and providing for the issuance and sale of bonds to provide funds therefor; creating and establishing a construction fund; and creating and establishing a special fund to pay the principal and interest of such bonds.
