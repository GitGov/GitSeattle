**Ordinance Number: 56501**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Blocks 74 and 75, D. T. Denny's Home Addition, being the alley between Dexter Avenue and 7th Avenue North, from Harrison Street to Broad Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 9663 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
