**Council Bill Number: 46284**
   
**Ordinance Number: 56148**
********

 An ordinance providing for the construction of a trunk sewer and submerged outfall sewer along the following described route to-wit: Madison Street, from the outer Harbor Line to the end of the existing 36-inch brick sewer, 9.6 feet easterly of the easterly margin of Railroad Avenue; all in accordance with Resolution No. 9169 of the City Council of the Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
