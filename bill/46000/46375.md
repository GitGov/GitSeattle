**Council Bill Number: 46375**
   
**Ordinance Number: 56146**
********

 An ordinance relating to the municipal light and power plant and system; and authorizing and directing the City Engineer and the Constant Angle Arch Dam Company, as consulting engineer, to make certain preliminary surveys and studies in connection with the Gorge Creek and the Diablo Canyon units of the Skagit River power development, and the utilization of surplus water for power purposes at Lake Youngs; and making an appropriation from the Light Fund as a temporary advance to capital account therefor.
