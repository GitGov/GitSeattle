**Council Bill Number: 25723**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36678**
********

 An ordinance specifying and confirming a loan of the City of Seattle in the aggregate sum of $175,000 for the purpose of providing funds for additions and betterments to the existing water supply system of the city by the acquisition by purchase, etc, of lands within and near the watershed of Cedar River, necessary to be had to protect the said water supply system, commonly known as "Cedar River Water Supply System" from pollution, being a part of $1,000,000.00 bonds of the City of Seattle, authorized at an election held September 5, 1911, pursuant to Ordinance No. 27499; and directing the issuance of such bonds in the sum of $175,000.00 and the levy and collection of taxes for payment of the principal and interest thereof.
