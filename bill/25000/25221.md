**Council Bill Number: 25221**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36215**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2929, for the improvement of Western Avenue, from the south margin of University Street to Virginia Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick blocks, provided that Union street shall be paved with sandstone blocks; the construction of granite curbs, concrete sidewalks, concrete stairways; the construction or reconstruction of sewers, side sewers and watermains and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 35373 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
