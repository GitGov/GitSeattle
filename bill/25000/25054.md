**Council Bill Number: 25054**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36061**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Marginal Way from Spokane Street to the south city limits; and Whatcom Avenue, from Hinds Street to Spokane Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving with brick; provided however, that East Marginal Way, between Webster Street and the south city limits, shall be paved with Standard creosoted wood blocks; all laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by grading and filling where necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5132,of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
