**Council Bill Number: 25479**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36453**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2974, for the improvement of Rainier Avenue from Jackson Street to Massachusetts Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, the construction of brick gutters and armored concrete curbs; the construction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith, under Ordinance No. 35850 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
