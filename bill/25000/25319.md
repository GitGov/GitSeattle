**Council Bill Number: 25319**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36289**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2969, for the improvement of Thackeray Place, from East 45th Street to East 50th Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick gutters; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by the construction, alteration, or reconstruction of sewers, side sewers, and water mains, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary, under Ordinance No. 35795 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
