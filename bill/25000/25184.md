**Ordinance Number: 36184**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 47th Avenue Southwest, from West Juneau Street to West Graham Street; 46th Avenue Southwest, 45th Avenue Southwest, and 44th Avenue Southwest, each from West Juneau Street to West Murray Street; West Juneau Street, from 46th Avenue Southwest to 49th Avenue Southwest; and West Raymond Street and West Graham Street, each from 47th Avenue Southwest to California Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and constructing temporary sidewalks thereon, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5141, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
