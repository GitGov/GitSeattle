**Council Bill Number: 25113**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36119**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Wallingford Avenue, from West Green Lake Way to North 85th Street; Burke Avenue, from North 80th Street to North 82nd Street; and North 80th Street, from Wallingford Avenue to Burke Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers and side sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5107, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
