**Council Bill Number: 25753**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36709**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West Halladay Street and West Halladay Street produced, from 8th Avenue West to a point 29 feet east of Sixth Avenue West; and 8th Avenue West, from Queen Anne Boulevard at West Wheeler Street to Queen Anne Boulevard, at West Ray Street; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5286, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
