**Ordinance Number: 36117**

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of a public street and highway over and across a portion of Block Two (2), Olympic View Addition to the City of Seattle, for the purpose of making suitable connection between West Garfield Street and the upper roadway on 12th Avenue West; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
