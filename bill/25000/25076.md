**Council Bill Number: 25076**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36080**
********

 An ordinance providing for the payment of the cost and expense by the mode of payment in bonds, of the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Northlake Avenue between Fremont Avenue and Tenth Avenue Northeast; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; for the changing of the name of North 30th Street between Woodlawn Avenue and Meridian Avenue to "Northlake Avenue"; providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of said Northlake Avenue, between Woodlawn Avenue and Meridian Avenue and also between First Avenue Northeast and Sixth Avenue Northeast, and of the curb grades of Wallingford Avenue between Ewing Street and Northlake Avenue, as platted in Burke's First Addition to the City of Seattle, and approaches thereto, in conformity with such established grades; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said avenues, and approaches thereto, and for the cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenues and approaches thereto; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking of all rights necessary on account of the construction and maintenance of a bridge or viaduct on Wallingford Avenue over and across Northlake Avenue, all in the City of Seattle,.
