**Ordinance Number: 36306**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2985, for the improvement of the alley in block fifteen (15), J. H. Nagle's Addition, and block two (2), Sackman's Home Addition, being the alley between Harvard Avenue and Broadway, from East Pike Street to East Pine Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 35933 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
