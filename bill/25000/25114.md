**Council Bill Number: 25114**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36120**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2908, for the improvement of Utah Avenue from Massachusetts Street to Stacy Street, all in the City of Seattle,, by filling and planking the same and including as part of said work the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances, and the adjustment of water mains and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 35211 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
