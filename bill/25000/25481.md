**Council Bill Number: 25481**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36455**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2930, for the improvement of 35th Avenue Southwest, from Avalon Way to West Roxbury Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the east side thereof with concrete and the construction of a bridge with an asphalt top between West Oregon Street and Avalon Way; by grading and planking and temporary wood walks where necessary and providing for the necessary surface drainage, under Ordinance No. 35385 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
