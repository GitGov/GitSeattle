**Council Bill Number: 25057**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36064**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 39th Avenue South, from Juneau Street to Holly Street; 42nd Avenue South from Juneau Street to Graham Street; Bateman Street from 39th Avenue South to 44th Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, together with all necessary cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; also Graham Street form 39th Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; and Rainier Avenue from Graham Street to Kenny Street, all in the City of Seattle,, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, together with all necessary cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage,; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5088, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
