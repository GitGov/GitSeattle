**Council Bill Number: 25098**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36104**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2909, for the improvement of Hamlin Street, from Montlake Boulevard to West Park Drive; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; constructing armored concrete curbs and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers and side sewers and water mains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service, and fire protection of said district; the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary; by the installation of a system of cluster lights along the curb line on each side of said streets and avenues, together with the installation of such poles, conduits, wires and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper installation and equipment of said system, under Ordinance No. 35212 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
