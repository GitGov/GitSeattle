**Ordinance Number: 36038**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2883, for the improvement of Stroud Avenue, from a connection to the trunk sewer on East Green Lake Way to North 82nd Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by constructing of sewers of proper materials and dimensions together with all wyes, openings, catch basins, manholes, flush tanks, and all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, including the construction of side sewer connections where necessary, under Ordinance No. 35065 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
