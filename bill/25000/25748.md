**Ordinance Number: 36704**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2986, for the improvement of 22nd Avenue North and 24th Avenue North, each from East Lynn Street to Montlake Place, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks with the necessary wooden crosswalks; by constructing wooden curbs and gutters and temporary wooden walks where necessary; and by providing for the necessary surface drainage, under Ordinance No. 35934 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
