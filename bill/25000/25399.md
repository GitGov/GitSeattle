**Council Bill Number: 25399**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36379**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West 65th Street, from 14th Avenue Northwest to 15th Avenue Northwest; West 67th Street, from 14th Avenue Northwest to 17th Avenue Northwest; West 75th Street, from 14th Avenue Northwest to 15th Avenue Northwest; North and West 85th Street, from Greenwood Avenue to 15th Avenue Northwest; 14th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to West 75th Street; Mary Avenue and Mary Avenue produced from West 65th Street to West 77th Street; and 15th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to West 85th Street; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing thereon concrete sidewalks with the necessary wooden cross walks; by grading, regrading and resurfacing; by constructing wooden curbs and gutters where necessary, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; and by constructing in 15th Avenue Northwest, from the existing side sewer at West 75th Street to West 85th Street, sewers and side sewers of proper dimension and material, together with manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5306, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
