**Council Bill Number: 25132**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36138**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Rose Court, from 26th Avenue North to the easterly line of block eight (8), Interlaken Addition, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with concrete, by constructing armored concrete curbs; by the construction, alterations, or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, and watermains together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5078, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
