**Council Bill Number: 25276**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36266**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2943, for the improvement of 2nd Avenue West, from West Prospect Street to West Highland Drive, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with sandstone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with armored concrete curbs; by constructing concrete sidewalks and a concrete stairway where necessary; by the construction or reconstruction of such side sewers and watermains as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, by such grading and regrading as may be necessary; under Ordinance No. 35568 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
