**Ordinance Number: 36635**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2942, for the improvement of Harrison Street, from Westlake Avenue North to Fairview Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with creosoted wood blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with armored concrete curbs, by providing for the necessary surface drainage; by constructing concrete sidewalks; by doing such grading and regrading as may be necessary, under Ordinance No. 35567 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
