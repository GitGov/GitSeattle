**Council Bill Number: 25629**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36598**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East 71st Street, Eat 72nd Street and East 73rd Street, each from 10th Avenue Northeast, to 12th Avenue Northeast; East 75th Street, from 12th Avenue Northeast to 15th Avenue Northeast; 11th Avenue Northeast, from East 73rd Street to East 75th Street; and 12th Avenue Northeast, from East 70th Street to East 75th Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage; constructing concrete sidewalks with the necessary wooden cross walks; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5173, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
