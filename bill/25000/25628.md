**Ordinance Number: 36597**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 10th Avenue Northeast, from East 75th Street to East 85th Street; 11th Avenue Northeast, from East 73rd Street to East 75th Street; 12th Avenue Northeast, from East 70th Street to East 75th Street; East 71st Street, East 72nd Street and Eat 73rd Street, each from 10th Avenue Northeast to 12th Avenue Northeast; East 75th Street, from 10th Avenue Northeast to 15th Avenue Northeast; East 78th Street and East 79th Street, each from 5th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast; East 80th Street, from Latona Avenue to 15th avenue Northeast; 15th Avenue Northeast, from East 80th Street to East 85th Street; and East 84th Street from 5th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing water mains therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all gates, tees crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district. all in accordance with Resolution No. 5198, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
