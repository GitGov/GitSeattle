**Council Bill Number: 25631**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36599**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 49, A. A. Denny's Addition (Bell & Denny's 3rd) being the alley between Third Avenue and Fourth Avenue, from Virginia Street to Lenora Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with three-inch (3") creosoted wood blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5309, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
