**Council Bill Number: 25115**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36121**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2873, for the improvement of Rainier Avenue, from 42nd Avenue South to the south margin of Kenny Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing granite or concrete curbs and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, and water mains, with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; the construction, alteration, or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading or regrading as may be necessary; under Ordinance No. 34947 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
