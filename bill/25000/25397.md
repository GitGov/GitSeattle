**Council Bill Number: 25397**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36445**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2914, for the improvement of 47th Avenue Northeast, from the south margin of East 35th Street to the north margin of East 39th Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt , together with brick gutters, laid upon a proper concrete foundation where the grades are less than 6%, and paving the same with concrete where the grades exceed 6%, by constructing armored concrete curbs and concrete sidewalks where necessary, by providing for the necessary surface drainage; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary, under Ordinance No. 35232 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
