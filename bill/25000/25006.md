 An ordinance appropriating the sum of Two Hundred ($200.00) Dollars out of the general fund of the City of Seattle, in settlement of the claim of Wm. C. Farrow and Katie J. Farrow, his wife, for personal injuries sustained by said Kate J. Farrow, on the 9th day of November, 1915, by stepping into a hole approximately one hundred feet west of the intersection of said James Street with 2nd Avenue within six feet of the northerly rail of the northerly street car track on said James Street, in said city, and authorizing the drawing of a warrant therefor.
