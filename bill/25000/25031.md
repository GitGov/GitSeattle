**Council Bill Number: 25031**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36039**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2877, for the improvement of Utah Avenue from Stacy Street to Lander Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction of granite curbs and concrete sidewalks where necessary; by the construction of sewers, side sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district and by the necessary filling; under Ordinance No. 34989 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
