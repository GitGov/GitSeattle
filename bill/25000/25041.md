**Council Bill Number: 25041**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36630**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing water works owned and operated by the City of Seattle; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be, to be the sum of Sixty Thousand ($60,000.00) and providing for the issuance and sale of Sixty Thousand Dollars ($60,000.00) of bonds of said city to provide funds therefor, and for the creating of a special fund into which shall be paid certain revenues of such water supply system to pay the principal and interest of such bonds and for the terms and conditions of such bonds and for their sale.
