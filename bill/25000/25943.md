**Ordinance Number: 36892**

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified voters of the City of Seattle, at the general election to be held in such city on Tuesday, March 6, 1917, the proposition for the creation of an indebtedness in the sum of Two Hundred Seventy-five Thousand ($275,000.00) Dollars by the issuance and sale of the general negotiable interest bearing coupon bonds of the City of Seattle in said amount, for the construction of a bridge over and across the Lake Washington Canal at Montlake Avenue in said city.
