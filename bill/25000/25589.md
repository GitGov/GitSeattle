**Council Bill Number: 25589**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36562**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North 78th Street, from Greenwood Avenue to Fremont Avenue; and Dayton Avenue, from North 77th Street to North 79th Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, constructing concrete sidewalks, together with all necessary crosswalks, providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5235, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
