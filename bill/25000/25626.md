**Council Bill Number: 25626**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36595**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East 80th Street, from Corliss Avenue to 20th Avenue Northeast; East 82nd Street, from Corliss Avenue to Latona Avenue; Sunnyside Avenue, 1st Avenue Northeast and 2nd Avenue Northeast, each from East 77th Street to East 85th Street; all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same; by constructing wooden curbs and gutters; by constructing temporary wood walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5184, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
