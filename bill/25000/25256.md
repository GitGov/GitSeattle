**Council Bill Number: 25256**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36302**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 10th Avenue Northeast, from East 40th Street to East 45th Street, all in the City of Seattle,, by constructing concrete sidewalks, with all necessary crosswalks; providing for the necessary surface drainage; constructing wooden curbs and gutters where necessary; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4638, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
