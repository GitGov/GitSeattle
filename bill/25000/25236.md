**Council Bill Number: 25236**
   
**Ordinance Number: 36226**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Olive Street, from 9th Avenue to the east line of Howard Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick block, and including as a part of said work the construction and reconstruction of all sewers and watermains that may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5219, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
