 ORDINANCE _________________

 AN ORDINANCE relating to telecommunications, authorizing the Director of Finance to redirect funds remaining from the sale of bonds authorized by Ordinances 118191 and 117749 to fund other fiber optic installation projects that were previously approved in the Executive Services Department's 1998 Adopted Budget.

 WHEREAS, Ordinance 118191 authorized a fiber optic installation project consisting of cable from the Municipal Building to the Seattle Center, and provided authorization for debt funding for that project; and

 WHEREAS, the 1998 budget of the Executive Services Department provides appropriation authority for certain fiber optic installations; and

 WHEREAS, pursuant to Ordinance 117749, the City sold bonds, some of the proceeds of which were allocated to the Municipal Building to Seattle Center fiber optic link; and

 WHEREAS, proceeds will remain from the sale of bonds authorized by Ordinance 118191; NOW, THEREFORE,

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. The City Council authorizes the Director of Finance to redirect funds remaining from the sale of bonds authorized by Ordinances 118191 and 117749 to fund other fiber optic installation projects that were previously approved in the Executive Services Department's 1998 Adopted Budget.

 Section 2. Any acts made consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance are hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
