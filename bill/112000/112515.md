 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Public Utilities; authorizing the Director of the Seattle Public Utilities to execute a contract with the Washington State Major League Baseball Stadium Public Facilities District ("PFD") to construct improvements at the Baseball Stadium.

 WHEREAS, the PFD is responsible for developing a new retractable roof stadium suitable for major league baseball and associated facilities, including parking ("Ballpark").

 WHEREAS, the PFD plans to construct certain improvements designed to improve pedestrian access to and from the Ballpark; and

 WHEREAS, the Seattle Public Utilities and the PFD (the "Parties") agree that the most efficient means for the Parties to coordinate their efforts is for the PFD to pay the City to construct the improvements described in the attached agreement based upon design and engineering plans and permits provided by the PFD.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. The Director of the Seattle Public Utilities is hereby authorized for and on behalf of the City of Seattle to execute and deliver a contract with the Washington State Major League Baseball Stadium Public Facilities District, substantially in the form attached hereto. The Director of the Seattle Public Utilities is further authorized to enter into appropriate contracts and subcontracts as is necessary to construct the improvement described in the attached contract.

 Section 2. Execution of the contract authorized hereby and any other act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
