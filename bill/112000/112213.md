 ORDINANCE

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to be reimbursed by the Light Fund to settle the claim of John P. Pingree (C-62127), all by a two-thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of  Seventeen Thousand and 00/100 Dollars ($17,000.00). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of John P. Pingree for personal injury due to a vehicle accident with a Seattle City Light truck on Wallingford Avenue North, Seattle, WA, on August 16, 1995, as specified in Clerk's File C-62127. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes. The Judgment/Claims Fund shall be reimbursed by the Light Fund.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
