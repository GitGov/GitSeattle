 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 Be it ordained by the City of Seattle as follows:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twenty One Million, Nine Thousand, Five Hundred Fifty Three and 48/100 dollars ($21,009,553.48) on SFMS mechanical warrants numbered 41121686 41124488, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council March 25, 1999 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Million, Nine Hundred Seventy Five Thousand, Three Hundred Ninety Eight and 14/100 dollars ($20,975,398.14) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50446628 50449896 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 120001 -128458, representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date March 16,1999 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on March 25, 1999 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City council on the 29th day of March, 1999 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this March 29, 1999.
