 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to settle the claim of Farmers Insurance Group as subrogee for Whitney and Thomas Dawson (C-63255), all by a two-thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Thirteen Thousand One Hundred Thirty-One and 57/100 Dollars ($13,131.57). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of Farmers Insurance Group as subrogee for Whitney and Thomas Dawson for an incident occurring on January 22, 1998 at the intersection of 39th Avenue SW and SW Genessee Street, Seattle, WA, as specified in Clerk's File C-63255. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
