 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Cumulative Reserve Fund; abandoning certain Capital Improvement Program appropriations, and appropriating and reappropriating funds from various Cumulative Reserve Subfund accounts, all by a three-fourths vote of the City Council.

 WHEREAS, the City periodically inventories the status of projects and debt service within the Seattle Cumulative Reserve Subfund to better manage Cumulative Reserve Subaccount balances, and specifically in preparation for Summit, the City's new financial management system; and abandons underexpended and unobligated projects to make new funds available for appropriation from the Cumulative Reserve Subfund;

 WHEREAS, it is efficient to transfer funding for eligible projects within the Cumulative Reserve Subfund to the REET II Subaccount, a more restrictive subaccount, to make available new funds in less restrictive Cumulative Reserve Subfund subaccounts;

 WHEREAS, it is necessary to abandon project expenditure authority from former City departments and give the appropriate expenditure authority to the department that is responsible for the management of the project;

 WHEREAS, the projects that are to receive funding from the REET I Subaccount (Fund #11630) qualify for REET I funding and are public works projects specified in the capital facilities element of the comprehensive plan;

 WHEREAS, the projects that are to receive funding from the REET II Subaccount (Fund #11610) qualify for REET II Subaccount funding and are public works projects specified in the capital facilities element of the comprehensive plan;

 WHEREAS, the projects that are to receive funding from the Major Maintenance Reserve Account (Fund #11620) qualify for Major Maintenance funding;

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:
