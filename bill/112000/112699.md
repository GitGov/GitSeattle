 ORDINANCE ______

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to settle the claim of Premier Fleet Services (C-65032), all by a two- thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Sixteen Thousand Seven Hundred Sixty-Two and 03/100 Dollars ($16,762.03). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of Premier Fleet Services for property damage due to a vehicle accident on Union Street, Seattle, WA, on March 26, 1999 as specified in Clerk's File C-65032. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
