 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to settle the claim of the Julie Ann Bodle (C-63215), all by a two- thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Thirty-Five Thousand and 00/100 Dollars ($35,000.00). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of the Julie Ann Bodle for a trip and fall incident on October 26, 1997, at or near 1501 Alaskan Way in Seattle, as specified in Clerk's File C-63215. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
