 ORDINANCE NO.__________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twenty Three Million Eight Hundred Twenty Six Thousand Six Hundred Thirty Nine 89/100 ($23,826,639.89) dollars on SFMS mechanical warrants numbered 41045722- 41048854, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council Sept 24,1998 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Million One Hundred Thirty Nine Thousand Three Hundred Thirty Eight and 50/100 ($20,139,338.50) dollars on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 5038683050390282, plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 390001-398242 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Sept 9,1998 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Sept. 24, 1998 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved

 .

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 28, day of Sept. 1998, and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this 28, Sept. 1998.
