 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to be reimbursed by the Drainage & Wastewater Fund to settle the claim of Walter A. Carlson (C-62696), all by a two-thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Fifteen Thousand Three Hundred Fifty-Four and 00/100 Dollars ($15,354.00). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of Walter A. Carlson for side sewer repairs at the wye to the vertical standpipe on January 8, 1998 at 3011 NW 74th Street in Seattle, WA, as specified in Clerk's File {C-62696). The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes. The Judgment/Claims Fund shall be reimbursed by the Drainage & Wastewater Fund.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
