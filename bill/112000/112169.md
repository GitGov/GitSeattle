 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to be reimbursed by the Drainage & Wastewater Fund to settle the claim of Safe Haven Associates (C-62552), all by a two-thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of  Twelve Thousand Two Hundred Eight and 61/100 Dollars ($12,208.61). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of Safe Haven Associates for extra expenses incurred for sewer hook-up during new building construction because City records were incorrect as to the location of the main line sewer stub as specified in Clerk's File C-62552. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes. The Judgment/Claims Fund shall be reimbursed by the Drainage & Wastewater Fund.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
