 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Consolidated Customer Service System (CCSS); authorizing the Seattle Public Utilities Director and Seattle City Light Superintendent to execute a contract with Systems & Computer Technology Corporation (SCT), to acquire a customer information and billing software environment, implementation services, ongoing support, and to provide system integration services.

 WHEREAS, on October 7, 1996, the City Council adopted Resolution 29444, endorsing Seattle City Light, Seattle Water Department, Drainage and Wastewater Utility, and Solid Waste Utility to develop a single information system to bill and serve their customers; and

 WHEREAS, in compliance with Resolution 29753, the Seattle Public Utilities Director and Seattle City Light Superintendent have negotiated a contract with SCT to acquire this software environment, implementation services, ongoing support, and to provide system integration services, Now, Therefore,

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. The Seattle Public Utilities Director and Seattle City Light Superintendent are authorized to execute a contract with SCT substantially in the form attached, to acquire a customer service and billing system, implementation services, ongoing support, and to provide system integration services.

 Section 2. Any acts consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance are hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
