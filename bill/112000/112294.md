 ORDINANCE ______

 AN ORDINANCE related to Seattle Municipal court; authorizing the execution of a collection agreement.

 WHEREAS, the Contracting Services Division, on behalf of the Seattle Municipal Court, issued a Request for Proposals (RFP) for collection of delinquent judgments services; and

 WHEREAS, based on the responses received, the City was unable to negotiate an agreement that met the needs of the Seattle Municipal Court; and

 WHEREAS, the current service provider, The Continental Alliance, Inc., is willing to provide the services needed by the Seattle Municipal Court; Now, therefore,

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section The Executive Services Director or his designee and the Court Administrator for the Seattle Municipal Court are authorized to jointly execute an agreement with The Continental Alliance, Inc. consistent with the agreement attached hereto.

 Section Any acts made consistent with and prior to the effective date of this ordinance are hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
