 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE establishing three positions in the Legislative Department.

 WHEREAS, the Office of the City Clerk has established a Vital Records Preservation Initiative as a five year project to preserve and provide secure backup for the City's vital records; and

 WHEREAS, additional staff resources are required to develop electronic indexes and perform duties related to document preservation; and

 WHEREAS, the 1999 budget provides funding for three positions for this Initiative; NOW THEREFORE,

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. Three positions are established in the Legislative Department, Office of the City Clerk, as follows:

 Positions Title FTE

 1 Research and Evaluation Assistant I 1.0

 2 Administrative Specialist I 2.0

 Section 2. All of the positions created in Section 1 shall sunset on or before January 1, 2004.

 Section 3. Any acts made consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance are hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 4. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
