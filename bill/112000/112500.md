 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Center Department; accepting a gift of the Smart Trek Intelligent Transportation Systems Advanced Parking Information System (APIS) from the Washington State Department of Transportation for the purpose of providing parking information to visitors coming to Seattle Center.

 WHEREAS, Seattle was selected by the US Department of Transportation as one of four metropolitan areas nationwide to demonstrate Intelligent Transportation Systems; and

 WHEREAS, Seattle Center was selected as one of the project sites to demonstrate an Advanced Parking Information System; and

 WHEREAS, advanced information on the current availability of parking will reduce congestion in the Seattle Center vicinity during peak event periods and provide a greater level of service to our visitors; and

 WHEREAS, Seattle Center wishes to accept this gift and make use of it to better serve our visitors and the citizens of the region;

 NOW THEREFORE

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. As requested by the Director of Seattle Center and recommended by the Mayor in this ordinance file, the Director of Seattle Center is hereby authorized to accept for and on behalf of the City of Seattle, the gift of the Advanced Parking Information System, consisting of two variable message signs, a vehicle monitoring automated count system, communications software and computer hardware, from the Washington State Department of Transportation for the purpose of providing real-time parking information to Seattle Center patrons and employees.

 Section 2. Any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
