**Ordinance Number: 54396**

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of Queen Anne Boulevard from Prospect Street to Highland Drive, over and across portions of Blocks 6 and 7, Mercer's Addition to North Seattle, now a portion of the City of Seattle, as recorded in Volume 1 of Plats, Page 171; providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of said boulevard and 1st Avenue North, between Highland Drive and Prospect Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land in fee simple for general municipal purposes; and providing that the entire cost of improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law; except as hereinafter otherwise provided.
