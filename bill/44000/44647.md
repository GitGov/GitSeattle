**Council Bill Number: 44647**
   
**Ordinance Number: 54467**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of 19th Avenue North from Interlaken Boulevard to Boyer Avenue, providing for the establishing of the curb grades of said avenues; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of said 19th Avenue North, and necessary to obtain the right to grade said avenue in accordance with the grades established herein and for and for the making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting said avenue, and providing that the entire cost of improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
