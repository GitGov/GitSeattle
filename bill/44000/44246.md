**Council Bill Number: 44246**
   
**Ordinance Number: 54035**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the securing of a permit from the Federal Government for the acquisition and construction of a reinforced concrete dam of the so-called Ambrusen type across the Skagit River at the Hanging Rock Dam site, specified in Ordinance No. 54006, authorizing and directing the Board of Public Works to prepare and adopt plans and specifications for such dam, and designating the same as one of the additions and betterments to and extensions of the municipal light and power plant and system, contemplated by Ordinance No. 54006; making an appropriation therefor, and repealing Ordinance No. 53532.
