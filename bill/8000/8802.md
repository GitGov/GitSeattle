**Ordinance Number: 21360**

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 19011 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Boston Street, from 15th Avenue North to 12th Avenue North; East Lynn Street, from 12th Avenue North to 11th Avenue North; East Miller Street, from 11th Avenue North to Federal Avenue; 11th Avenue North from a point 300 feet south of the south margin of East Blaine Street to East Miller Street; Emmons Place from East Howe Street to East Lynn Street; 12th Avenue North from East Boston Street to East Lynn Street; 14th Avenue North from East Howe Street to East Boston Street; and 15th Avenue North from East Howe Street to East Boston Street; including the construction of a temporary overflow outfall with all necessary connections from the intersection of Federal Avenue and Roanoke Street to Lake Union, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1439 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved August 19, 1908.
