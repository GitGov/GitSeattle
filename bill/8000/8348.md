**Ordinance Number: 21198**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West 67th Street, from 8th Avenue Northwest to 14th Avenue Northwest; 9th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to West 70th Street; 12th Avenue Northwest and Eli Avenue Northwest each from West 67th Street to West 70th Street; all in the City of Seattle by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden cross walks thereon, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2111 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
