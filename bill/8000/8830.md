**Ordinance Number: 21557**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1556, for the improvement of Pine Court, from a point one hundred and forty-five (145) feet north of Crescent Drive to Madrona Drive; Madrona Drive, from Pine Court to Twenty-first Avenue North and from a point thirty (30) feet northeasterly from the intersection of Twenty-second Avenue North and Madrona Drive to Twenty-fourth Avenue North; Crescent Drive, from a point ninety (90) feet east of Twenty-first Avenue North to Twenty-fourth Avenue North, by constructing sewers thereon, together with all wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and other appurtenances; under Ordinance No. 16776, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
