**Council Bill Number: 8573**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21553**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1901, for the improvement of Taylor Avenue, East Queen Anne Driveway and Fifth Avenue North, a continuous street from the south line of Prospect Street to the north line of Lynn Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regarding, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, the construction of all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, and the construction of a wooden trestle bridge on Boston Street over the gulch between Second Avenue North and Nob Hill Avenue (provided, however, that where the grades exceed five and one-half (5 1/2) per cent, the pavement may be of brick or sandstone laid upon a proper concrete foundation), under Ordinacne No.19651, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
