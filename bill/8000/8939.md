**Council Bill Number: 8939**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21537**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Nagle Place, from East Denny Way to East Pine Street, and East Howell Street and East Olive Street, each from North Broadway to Nagle Place, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and by constructing therein a sewer of proper size and dimensions, where necessary, with connections to existing sewers, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2225 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
