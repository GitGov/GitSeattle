**Council Bill Number: 8648**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21458**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1984, for the improvement of 23rd Avenue North, in the City of Seattle, from East Madison Street to East Roy Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners, by constructing asphalt, concrete or brick gutters, and including as part of said work the alteration, extension or construction of sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 20389 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
