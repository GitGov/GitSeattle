**Council Bill Number: 8210**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21768**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, extending, widening and establishing of Fremont Avenue, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street, and from the north margin of North Sixty-seventh Street to a point one hundred twelve (12) feet south from the south margin of North Seventy-second Street; and North Sixty-fifth Street, from Fremont Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard, all in the City of Seattle; and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and providing that the payment for such improvement be made by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
