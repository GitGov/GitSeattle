**Ordinance Number: 21206**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North 82nd Street and North 84th Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; and Fremont Avenue and Dayton Avenue, each from North 81st Street to North 85th Street; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2105 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
