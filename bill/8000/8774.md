**Ordinance Number: 21492**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1895, for the improvement of Shelby Street and Hamlin Street, each from 10th Avenue North eastward to Lake Union; Edgar Street from 10th Avenue North to 12th Avenue North; Roanoke Street from Federal Avenue to Boyer Avenue, as extended by Ordinance No. 17537; Millard Street, from Harvard Avenue North to Fuhrman Avenue; Allison Street, from Franklin Avenue to Fuhrman Avenue; Barton Place, from Allison Street to Fuhrman Avenue; Fuhrman Avenue, from Franklin Avenue to Eastlake Avenue; 11th Avenue North from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street, 12th Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street and Boyer Avenue, as extended by Ordinance No. 17537, from the west line of Delmar Park Addition to 12th Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading where necessary and constructing concrete or wood walks, and wooden cross walks thereon, together with wood or iron railing and concrete stairways where necessary under Ordinance No. 19563 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
