**Council Bill Number: 8687**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21370**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 14th Avenue West, from the north margin of West Howe Street to the north margin of Gilman Avenue; 15th Avenue West, from the south margin of West Newton Street, to the north margin of North Armour Street; West Newton Street, West Boston Street, West Wheeler Street and Gilman Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete and wooden sidewalks, thereon and all necessary cross walks, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2173 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
