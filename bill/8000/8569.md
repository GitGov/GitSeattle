**Ordinance Number: 21118**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1867, for the improvement of Sixth Avenue Northeast, from Northlake Avenue to East Fortieth Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete walks and wooden crosswalks, together with all necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage; provided, that East Fortieth Street, from Latona Avenue to Seventh Avenue Northeast shall be improved by the construction of concrete walks on a double roadway, and stairways, under Ordinance No. 19513, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
