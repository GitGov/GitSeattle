**Council Bill Number: 8576**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21120**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1943, for the improvement of the alley in Block Fifteen (15), Boren, Denny & Yesler's Addition to the town (now City) of Seattle, being the alley from Spring Street to Seneca Street and between Third Avenue and Fourth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon proper concrete foundation, together with grading and construction of such sewers with their appurtenances a may be necessary for the proper drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 20118, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
