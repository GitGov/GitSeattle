**Ordinance Number: 21184**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Franklin Avenue, from a point sixty (60) feet north if the north margin of Nelson Place on the east to a point one hundred eighty (180) feet south of the south margin of East Park Drive on the east, and Nelson Place from Franklin Avenue to Eastlake Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, with all wyes, openings, flush tanks, manholes, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 2085 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
