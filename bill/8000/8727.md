**Council Bill Number: 8727**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21769**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, altering, extending and establishing of West Ray Street, from First Avenue West to Fourth Avenue West; of First Avenue West, from West Ray Street to West Florentia Street; of West Florentia Street, from First Avenue West to Third Avenue West; and of Fourth Avenue West, from West Ray Street to the north line of Queen Anne Fourth Addition to the City of Seattle, all in the City of Seattle; and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and providing that the payment for such improvement be made by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
