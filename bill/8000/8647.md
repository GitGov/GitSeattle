**Council Bill Number: 8647**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21337**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1968, for the improvement of Franklin Avenue from Shelby Street to Harvard Avenue North; in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, the construction and alteration or extension of concrete walks and including as a part of said work, all necessary grading, the construction, alteration or extension of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 20373 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
