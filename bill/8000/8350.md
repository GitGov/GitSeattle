 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 12th Avenue Northwest from West 70th Street to West 83rd Street; Eli Avenue Northwest from West 73rd Street to West 75th Street; West 75th Street, from 11th Avenue Northwest to 14th Avenue Northwest; West 77th Street, from 11th Avenue Northwest to 12th Avenue Northwest; West 80th Street and West 83rd Street, each from 10th Avenue Northwest to 12th Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2109 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
