**Ordinance Number: 20853**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1800, for the improvement of Washington Street, from a point one hundred thirty-six (136) feet east of the east margin of Twelfth Avenue South to Twentieth Avenue South; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt and granite blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with all necessary sewers, watermains, and other appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 18699, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
