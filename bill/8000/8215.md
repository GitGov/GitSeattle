**Council Bill Number: 8215**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21178**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Midvale Avenue and Midvale Avenue produced, from North Thirty-nine Street to North Fiftieth Street; North Thirty-ninth Street, from Midvale Avenue to Stone Way; North Forty-fourth Street and Allen Place, each from Stone Way to Midvale Avenue; North Forty-fifth Street from Stone Way to Fremont Avenue; North Forty-sixth Street, North Forty-seventh Street, North Forty-eighth Street, North Forty-ninth Street and North Fiftieth Street, each from Fremont Avenue to Midvale Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all manholes, flush tanks, catch-basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage and sewerage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 2049 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
