**Council Bill Number: 8026**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20953**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Market Street from Seventeenth Avenue Northwest to Twenty-fourth Avenue Northwest; Russell Avenue from Twentieth Avenue Northwest to Market Street, and Twenty-fourth Avenue Northwest from Market Street to West Sixty-fifth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick or other suitable material all to be upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, granite curbs and concrete sidewalks, and side sewer connections where necessary, together with such sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances, as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1967 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.

**Status:** Passed
