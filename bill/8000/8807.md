**Ordinance Number: 22149**

 An ordinance providing for the widening and extension of East 55th Street, from 5th Avenue Northeast to the west line of Cedar's Addition to the City of Seattle; of 8th Avenue Northeast from East 55th Street to the south line of Weedin's Division of Green Lake Addition to the City of Seattle; and of East 60th Street, from 5th Avenue Northeast to Ravenna Boulevard, all in the City of Seattle; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
