**Council Bill Number: 8771**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21462**
********

 An ordinance approving amended assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1813, for the improvement of Minor Avenue North and Pontius Avenue, each from Denny Way to Mercer Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners and including as part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks and of sewers and watermains with their necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; provided however, that when the grades exceed 8% the pavement may be of sandstone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation; under Ordinance No. 19905 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
