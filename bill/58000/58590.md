 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Green Lake Reservoir Addition, being the alley between 15th Avenue Northeast and 16th Avenue Northeast, from East 82nd Street to East 85th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12048 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
