**Council Bill Number: 58972**
   
**Ordinance Number: 67914**
********

 An ordinance accepting an amendment to franchise Nos. 577, 684 and 692, granted by director of highways of the state of Washington to the City of Seattle, which adds a paragraph to franchises Nos. 577 and 684, to beknown as paragraph 1-A, for the construction, operation, and maintenance, of an 8" water pipeline on the westerly side of state road #1 (Pacific Highway) in King County, 36 feet west of the center line, to be connected to the water pipeline constructed under franchises Nos. 577 and 684 on the easterly side of said highway.
