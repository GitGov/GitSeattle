**Ordinance Number: 67607**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 38 and 39, Carleton Park Addition, and Block 6, Briarcliff Addition, being the alley in the block bounded by West Ray Street, Crestmont Place, Westmont Way, West Viewmont Way, 40th Avenue West, and Parkmont Place; by grading, paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12057 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
