**Council Bill Number: 58997**
   
**Ordinance Number: 67932**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 43rd Avenue West, from West Barrett Street to a connection with the existing sewer approximately 60 feet south of the south line of Block 6, Carleton Park Heights Additions; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing sewers and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12118 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
