**Ordinance Number: 67549**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 24, Woodland Addition to Salmon Bay City, being the alley between North 46th Street and North 47th Street, from Fremont Avenue to Linden Avenue; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 12047 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
