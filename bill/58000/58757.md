 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 24, D. T. Denny's Home Addition, being the alley between Queen Anne Avenue and 1st Avenue North, from Republican Street to Mercer Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12103 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
