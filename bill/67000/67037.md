**Ordinance Number: 75758**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, R. B. Wark First Addition (Unrecorded), being the alley established by Ordinance 64684 between Fauntleroy Avenue and 46th Avenue Southwest, from West Trenton Street to West Cloverdale Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14317 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
