 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 5th Avenue Northeast, from East 85th Street to East 91st Street and 5th Avenue Northeast (East Side), from East 91st Street to East 96th Street Produced; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing concrete sidewalks and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14613 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
