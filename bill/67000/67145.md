**Ordinance Number: 75862**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, Ballard Water-Front Addition, being the alley between 32nd Avenue Northwest and 33rd Avenue Northwest from West 75th Street to West 77th Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14360 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
