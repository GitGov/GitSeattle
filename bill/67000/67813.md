 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alleys in Block 8, T. J. Carle's Supplemental Plat of Block 8, of D. T. Denny's 2nd Addition to North Seattle, being the alleys between Eastlake Avenue and Yale Avenue North from Valley Street to Aloha Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14783 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
