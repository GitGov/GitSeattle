**Ordinance Number: 75757**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Exposition Heights, being the alleys in the block bounded by 34th Avenue Northeast, 35th Avenue Northeast, East 50th Street, University View Place, and East 47th Street; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14079 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
