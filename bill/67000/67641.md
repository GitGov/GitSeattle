**Council Bill Number: 67641**
   
**Ordinance Number: 76346**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 1, 2 and 3, Seaboard Addition, being a continuous alley between Fauntleroy Avenue and 30th Avenue Southwest, from West Charlestown Street to Admiral Way; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14377 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
