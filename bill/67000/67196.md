**Council Bill Number: 67196**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75904**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 7, Kaufman's Addition to Green Lake Circle, being the alley between North 76th Street and North 77th Street, from Fremont Avenue to Linden Avenue; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14499 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
