**Council Bill Number: 67252**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75967**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, E. H. Jackson's Addition, being the alley between 18th Avenue South and 19th Avenue South, from Hinds Street to Horton Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14415 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
