**Ordinance Number: 76074**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 33 and 34, Denny and Hoyt's Addition, being the alley between North 34th Street and North 35th Street, from Aurora to Fremont Avenue; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14432 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
