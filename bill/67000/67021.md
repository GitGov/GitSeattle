**Ordinance Number: 75739**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 3, Alta Vista Addition, being the alley between 44th Avenue Southwest and 45th Avenue Southwest, from West Genesee Street to West Dakota Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14266 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
