**Ordinance Number: 76350**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 37, D. S. Maynard's Plat and Block 203, Seattle Tide Lands, being the alley between 5th Avenue South and 6th Avenue South from Lane Street to Dearborn Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14747 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
