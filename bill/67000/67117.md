 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, H. S. Turner Park Addition, being the alley between East Newton Street and East Boston Street, from 24th Avenue North to 25th Avenue North; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14414 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
