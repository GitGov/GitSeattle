**Ordinance Number: 76397**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 3 and 4, Replat of Blocks 3, 4, 5, 6 and 13 in Replat of Green Lake Home Addition, being the alley between East Green Lake Way and North 77th Street, from Corliss Avenue to Sunnyside Avenue; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14472 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
