**Council Bill Number: 42460**
   
**Ordinance Number: 52365**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of a public street and highway for an approach to West Garfield Street Bridge, from the intersection of West Garfield Street with 23rd Avenue east, northwesterly, westerly, southerly, westerly, northerly and northwesterly, to an intersection with 28th Avenue West and West Blaine Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and for the making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting said approach and providing that the entire cost of improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
