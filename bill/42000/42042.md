**Council Bill Number: 42042**
   
**Ordinance Number: 51990**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal waterworks and sewerage systems; providing for the acquisition from the Broadmoor Maintenance Commission of, and payment for, its water supply and sewerage systems, facilities and equipment, located in that certain unplatted tract known as "Broadmoor;" declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; authorizing a warrant on, and appropriating funds from the surplus in, the Water Fund in order to defray the expense thereof and declaring an emergency.
