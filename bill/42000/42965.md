**Council Bill Number: 42965**
   
**Ordinance Number: 52855**
********

 An ordinance ratifying and confirming a loan in the sum of Nine Hundred Thousand ($900,000.00) Dollars and authorizing the issuance and sale of general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds therefor in said sum, for the purpose of providing funds for constructing, furnishing and equipping a public auditorium, arena and recreation field on blocks 44, 45, 52 and 53, D.T. Denny's Home Addition to North Seattle, as submitted to the qualified voters (electors) under Ordinance No. 52296; providing for the levy and collection of taxes for the payment of the principal and interest of said bonds; and creating and establishing a construction fund and a bond interest and redemption fund.
