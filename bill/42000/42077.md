**Council Bill Number: 42077**
   
**Ordinance Number: 52006**
********

 An ordinance relating to the improvement of Columbian Way et al and providing for the improvement of Columbian Way, in its new location, as established by deed from the Ladd Estate Company and George A. Spencer, from the intersection of Spokane Street and 14th avenue South to its point of intersection with Columbian Way, as established by condemnation under Ordinance No. 39643, at a point 150 feet, more or less, south of the south line of Dakota Street, in lieu of the improvement of that portion of Columbian Way, as established by Ordinance No. 39643, lying between Spokane Street and a point 150 feet, more or less, south of the south line of Dakota Street, and certain other streets and avenues, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Petition No. 107312 to the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessment upon property of the petitioners as a part of Local Improvement District No. 4284, and declaring an emergency.
