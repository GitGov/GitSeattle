**Council Bill Number: 42759**
   
**Ordinance Number: 52651**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of 29th Avenue Northwest and 30th Avenue Northwest, each from West 75th Street to West 80th Street; of 31st Avenue Northwest from West 75th Street to a point approximately six hundred ten feet north of the north margin of West 80th Street; of West 75th Street and West 77th Street, each from 28th Avenue Northwest and 29th Avenue Northwest, from West 75th Street to West 80th Street; of the alley between 29th Avenue Northwest and 30th Avenue Northwest, from West 75th Street to West 77th Street; of the alley between 30th Avenue Northwest and 31st Avenue Northwest and of the alley between 31st Avenue Northwest and 32nd Avenue Northwest, each from West 75th Street to a point approximately six hundred ten feet north of the north margin of West 80th Street; providing for the establishing of curb grades of 29th Avenue Northwest and of 30th Avenue Northwest, each from West 75th Street to West 80th Street; and of 31st Avenue Northwest from West 75th Street to a point six hundred ten feet north of the north margin of West 80th Street; and of West 75th Street; West 77th Street and West 80th Street, each from 28th Avenue Northwest to 32nd Avenue Northwest; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and for the making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting said streets and avenues, and providing that the entire cost of improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
