**Council Bill Number: 42353**
   
**Ordinance Number: 52296**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of the City of Seattle, at the general municipal election to be held in said City on Tuesday, March 8, 1927, the proposition of creating an indebtedness in the sum of Nine Hundred Thousand Dollars ($900,000.00) and the issuance and sale therefor of general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds of said City in said amount, for the purpose of constructing, furnishing and equipping a public auditorium, arena and recreation field on Blocks Forty-four (44), Forty-five (45), Fifty-two (52), and Fifty-three (53), D.T. Denny's Home Addition to North Seattle, requiring the City Comptroller and Ex-officio City Clerk to certify to the Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title, and declaring an emergency.
