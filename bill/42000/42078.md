**Council Bill Number: 42078**
   
**Ordinance Number: 56720**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal street railway system; providing for the acquisition from the Pacific Northwest Traction Company of, and payment for, said company's North and West Eighty-fifth Street, street railway lines, property and equipment, and for the construction of, and payment for, track pavement upon said street railway lines; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; authorizing warrant on, and appropriating funds from the surplus in, the City Railway Fund in order to defray the expense thereof; and authorizing certain trackage privileges and specifying the terms and conditions upon which the same shall be granted.
