**Council Bill Number: 42912**
   
**Ordinance Number: 52934**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for additions and betterments to and extensions of the existing municipal light and power plant and system; providing for the construction and acquisition of a diversion tunnel and all necessary appurtenances in connection therewith; at the site of the proposed concrete arch dam across the Skagit River, at Diablo Canyon, as an incident to the construction of said dam, as authorized by Ordinance no. 52134; declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be; transferring to the Municipal Light Extension Bond 1926 Construction Fund, created and established by said ordinance, the sum of Eighty-five Thousand Dollars as a loan from the surplus in the Light Fund; authorizing and directing the preparation of plans and specifications for and the construction and acquisition of said improvements; and authorizing expenditures and appropriating funds therefor.
