**Council Bill Number: 22925**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34108**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2775 for the improvement of East Pike Street, from Broadway to East Madison Street, by paving the same with brick or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by widening the existing pavement; by the construction, alteration, or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, and water mains, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks; by the construction and reconstruction of concrete or armored concrete curbs.
