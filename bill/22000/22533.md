**Ordinance Number: 33768**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Mercer Street, from Washington Boulevard to 36th Avenue North; Ford Place, from Washington Boulevard to 36th Avenue North; 36th Avenue North, from Washington Boulevard to Mercer Street; 36th Avenue North, from East Thomas Street to East Republican Street; 34th Avenue North and 36th Avenue North, each from East Thomas Street to East Harrison Street; East Harrison Street, from 33rd Avenue North to Washington Boulevard; East Republican Street, from west marginal line of Tract 111, (The Pacific Coast Addition), to Washington Boulevard, Hillside Drive, from Washington Boulevard to Lambert Place; Lambert Place, from Hillside Drive to McGilvra Boulevard; McGilvra Boulevard, from Denny-Blaine Addition to center line of East Mercer Street produced east, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt and other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick gutters, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4268, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
