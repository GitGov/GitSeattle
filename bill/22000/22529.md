**Council Bill Number: 22529**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33765**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2769, for the improvement of East Howe Street, from East Lynn Street to East Blaine Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with concrete pavement for the full width of East Howe Street and for a width of 18 feet on 32nd Avenue North; by constructing concrete sidewalks on 32nd Avenue North; by constructing armored curbs; providing for the necessary surface drainage; by constructing sewers and side sewers where necessary and by making all additions and alterations necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; by constructing concrete sidewalks on the easterly side of East Blaine Street, from the intersection of East Howe Street to Boyer Avenue, and on the northeasterly side of Boyer Avenue, from 23rd Avenue North to 19th Avenue North, together with such wooden cross walks as may be necessary and that such other work be done as may be necessary in connection therewith.
