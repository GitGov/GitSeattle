**Council Bill Number: 22877**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34056**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2762 for the improvement of Forty-second Avenue Northeast (formerly Surber Avenue) from East Forty-fifth Street to Forty-third Avenue Northeast (formerly Laurel Avenue) all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs where necessary; by the alteration, construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction, reconstruction or alteration of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary.
