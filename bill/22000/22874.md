**Council Bill Number: 22874**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34053**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2754 for the improvement of Eastlake Avenue, from Almy Street to East Galer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs with armored concrete or granite corners; by the construction and reconstruction of all sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage and water concrete side walks where necessary; by the reconstruction of the existing wood bulkheads where necessary; by such said grading as may be necessary.
