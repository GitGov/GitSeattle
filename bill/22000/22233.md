**Council Bill Number: 22233**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33498**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Mercer Street, from Queen Anne to Beach Drive, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of all sewers, watermains and side sewers necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4449 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
