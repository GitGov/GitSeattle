**Ordinance Number: 33761**
********

 An ordinance granting to the Hotel Savoy Company, a corporation, its successors and assigns, a franchise to construct, maintain and operate a tunnel across and underneath the alley in block 5, A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, for the purpose of connecting the basement and first or ground floor of the building situated on lot 4, in said block 5, with the sub-basement of building to be built on lot 3, and the north half of lot 6, in block 5, of said addition, and also for the purpose of providing additional space to be used in connection with the first or ground floor of building located on said lot 4.
