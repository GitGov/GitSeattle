**Council Bill Number: 22988**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34178**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North Seventy-sixth Street, from Woodland Park Avenue to the westerly margin of Fremont Avenue, all in The City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, by making such provisions for the surface drainage as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4568 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
