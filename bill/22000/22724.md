**Ordinance Number: 33936**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Eli Avenue, from West 70th Street to West 75th Street; 12th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to West 85th Street; 13th Avenue Northwest, from West 83rd Street to West 85th Street; West 83rd Street, from 12th Avenue Northwest to 13th Avenue Northwest; 14th Avenue Northwest, from West 80th Street to West 85th Street; West 70th Street, from Eli Avenue to 12th Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4457, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
