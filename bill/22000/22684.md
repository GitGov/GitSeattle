**Council Bill Number: 22684**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33901**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2715, for the improvement of 8th Avenue South, from Charlestown Street to the railroad crossing four hundred (400) feet north of the north line of Maple's Addition, which was the former boundary between Seattle and Georgetown, by grading, constructing concrete walks, and by paving the same with brick or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs, under Ordinance No. 31978 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
