**Council Bill Number: 22688**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33905**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 45th Avenue Northeast, 46th Avenue Northeast, 47th Avenue Northeast, 48th Avenue Northeast and 49th Avenue Northeast, each from East 45th Street to East 50th Street; East 45th Street, from the West margin of Town of Yesler Addition to 50th Avenue Northeast; 50th Avenue Northeast, from East 40th Street to East 45th Street; 51st Avenue Northeast, from East 41st Street to Lake Washington, and from East 41st Street to East 42nd Street; 45th Avenue Northeast, from East 41st Street to East 45th Street; East 41st Street, from 45th Avenue Northeast to 55th Avenue Northeast; East 43rd Street, from 55th Avenue Northeast to Lake Washington, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4582, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
