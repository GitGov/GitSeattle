**Council Bill Number: 22953**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34112**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Stevens, from California Avenue to Forty-fifth Avenue Southwest, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, constructing concrete sidewalks, together with all necessary cross-walks and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4524 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
