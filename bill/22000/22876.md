**Council Bill Number: 22876**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34055**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2776 for the improvement of Fairview Avenue, from Denny Way to Virginia Street as extended and established by Ordinance No. 21766, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs; by the alteration, constriction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks; by such grading as may be necessary.
