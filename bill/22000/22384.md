**Ordinance Number: 33633**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Fairview Avenue North, from the south margin of East Galer Street to the north margin of Roanoke Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a bridge roadway thereon; by grading and planking the same where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4441, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
