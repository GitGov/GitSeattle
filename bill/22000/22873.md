**Ordinance Number: 34052**

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2770 for the improvement of East Seventy-second Street, from East Green Lake Boulevard to Woodlawn Avenue, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by paving with asphalt or other suitable material on proper base; concrete or armored concrete curbs, brick or asphalt gutters; constructing cast iron watermains, concrete sidewalks, providing for the necessary surface drainage, under Ordinance No. 33107, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
