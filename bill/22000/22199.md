**Council Bill Number: 22199**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33473**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Sixth Avenue and Seventh Avenue, from Westlake Avenue to Pine Street and Eighth Avenue from Stewart Street to Westlake Avenue, Olive Street from Westlake Avenue to the alley between Seventh and Eighth Avenues; Stewart Street from Westlake Avenue to alley between Eighth and Ninth Avenues, and Virginia Street from Westlake Avenue to Ninth Avenue, by paving the same with brick or other suitable material, all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 55731 of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
