**Council Bill Number: 22200**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33474**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Etruria Street, from Nickerson Street to a point one hundred eighty (180) feet west of Warren Avenue; Warren Avenue, from Florentia Street to Nickerson Street; The Southwesterly side of Nickerson Street from Florentia Street to Warren Avenue; by the construction of concrete walks, with all necessary cross walks, all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 56317, of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
