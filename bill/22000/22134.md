**Ordinance Number: 33408**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2621 for the improvement of Sixth Avenue from Cherry Street to Madison Street and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same and paving with brick and brick blocks or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation with granite and armored concrete curbs and by the construction of concrete walks, the construction and reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, watermains and their appurtenances that may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district.
