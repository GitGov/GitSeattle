**Council Bill Number: 22927**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34101**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2721 for the improvement of Pike Place, from Virginia Street to Pike Street, and Pike Street, from Pike Place to First Avenue, and from the alley between Western Avenue and First Avenue to First Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with armored concrete or granite curbing, and armored concrete or granite corners; by the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary.
