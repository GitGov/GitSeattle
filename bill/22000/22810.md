**Council Bill Number: 22810**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34012**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-third Avenue North, between East Highland Drive and East Galer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4535 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
