**Council Bill Number: 22012**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33290**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of California Avenue, from West Atlantic Street to West Myrtle Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with concrete, brick or asphalt gutters, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4194 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
