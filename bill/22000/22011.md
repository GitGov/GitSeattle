**Council Bill Number: 22011**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33289**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Forty-second Avenue Southwest, from Ferry Avenue to West Lander Street, Forty-first Avenue Southwest from Three Hundred (300) feet north of West Holgate Street to West Lander Street; West Holgate Street from Walnut Avenue to Forty-first Avenue Southwest, Walnut Avenue from Arch Avenue to West Holgate Street; Arch Avenue from West Massachusetts Street to West Holgate Street; West Massachusetts Street from Ferry Avenue to Arch Avenue; Ferry Avenue from Victoria Avenue to California Avenue; Victoria Avenue from Ferry Avenue to One Hundred-fifth (150) feet south of West Holgate Street; Edgewood Avenue from Ferry Avenue to Palm Avenue; alley East of Forty-first Avenue Southwest from West Holgate Street to West Hill Street; Georgia Street from the north line of Seattle Tide Lands Plat to intersection of Victoria Avenue and Ferry Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all wyes, opening, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and other appurtenances, and including as a part of this improvement a temporary wooden box outfall sewer in Georgia Street extending from the northerly line of the Seattle Tide Lands Plat to the intersection of Victoria Avenue and Ferry Avenue, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4366 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
