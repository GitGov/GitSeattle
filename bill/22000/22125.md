**Ordinance Number: 33405**
********

 An Ordinance ratifying and confirming a loan of The City of Seattle in the aggregate sum of Eighth Hundred Twenty-nine Thousand Five Hundred ($829,500.00) Dollars, for the purpose of providing money to construct a suitable bridge at Fifteenth Avenue Northwest (across Salmon Bay Waterway) and a bridge at Fremont Avenue (across Lake Washington Canal), authorized at a special election held in The City of Seattle June 30, 1914, under and pursuant to Ordinance No. 33133, and directing the issuance of such bonds in the sum of Eight Hundred Twenty-nine Thousand Five Hundred ($829,500.00) Dollars, and the levy and collection of taxes for the payment of the principal and interest thereof.
