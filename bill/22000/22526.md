**Council Bill Number: 22526**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35207**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing Puget Sound Traction, Light & Power Company to discontinue operation of its street railway system within the following location: From California Avenue and Donald Street along California Avenue and Ferry Avenue to the intersection of California Way and California Place; and granting to Puget Sound Traction, Light & Power Company, its successors and assigns, the right, privilege, authority and franchise to construct, maintain and operate a railway line, with all necessary tracks, poles, wires and appurtenances, upon, over and along California Way from its intersection with California Avenue and Donald Street, thence northerly, easterly and southerly to its intersection with Ferry Avenue near California Place.

**Status:** Passed
