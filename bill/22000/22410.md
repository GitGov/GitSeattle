**Ordinance Number: 33656**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2767, for the improvement of Queen Anne Boulevard, as established by Ordinance No. 16790, from the intersection of McGraw Street and Bigelow Avenue (now Queen Anne Boulevard) to a point one hundred twenty (120) feet west of the west margin of Nob Hill Avenue, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks, together with all necessary cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, under Ordinance No. 33081 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
