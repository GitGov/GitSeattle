**Council Bill Number: 22995**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34185**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2704 for the improvement of West Lee Street, from Seventh Avenue West to Eighth Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with stone, or other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with armored concrete curbs and armored or granite corners, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the alteration, construction or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks, where necessary, and by such grading and regrading as may be necessary.
