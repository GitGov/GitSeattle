**Council Bill Number: 22785**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33985**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Shoreland Drive, from Lake Park Drive to a connection with the existing pavement at Mount Claire Drive, all in the City of Seattle, by paving with suitable material, the construction of concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4586, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
