**Council Bill Number: 35033**
   
**Ordinance Number: 45323**
********

 An ordinance providing for and establishing of the curb grades of 9th Avenue South, from Massachusetts Street to Diagonal Avenue; Diagonal Avenue, from 9th Avenue South to Spokane Street; 6th Avenue South, from Holgate Street to Lander Street; Holgate Street, from 6th Avenue South to the alley east of 9th Avenue South; Lander Street from 6th Avenue South to 9th Avenue South; Bay View Street, from 9th Avenue South to 10th Avenue South; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property and property rights necessary for the grading and regrading of said avenues and streets in conformity with the grades established herein, and for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenues and streets; and specifying the manner of payment of the compensation to be made therefor.
