**Ordinance Number: 47115**

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, extending and establishing of public easement for street purposes from East Prospect Street to East Park Place; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, and damaging of property necessary therefor; providing for the establishing of the grade of said easement from East Prospect Street to East Park Place; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law, or from such fund as the City Council may direct.
