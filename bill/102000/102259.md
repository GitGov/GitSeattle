**Ordinance Number: 109932**

 An Ordinance amending Title 10 of the Seattle Municipal Code, relating to Health and Safety, to add a new Chapter 10.54, relating to requiring permits for fumigators, exterminators and their apprentices; regulating the manner of fumigation and the use of certain dangerous substances therefor; defining offenses and prescribing penalties; and repealing Ordinances 73223, 75771, 85744, 88838 and 102625 relating to licensing and regulating fumigators and extgerminators.
