**Council Bill Number: 102937**
   
**Ordinance Number: 110504**
********

 AN ORDINANCE amending the Consultant Selection Ordinance (Seattle Municipal Code s 6 and 13) to forbid simultaneous contracts for management and auditing services, to require rotation of retainer contracts after five years, and to vest the City Comptroller with the authority to participate in the selection of consultants for accounting and auditing services.
