**Council Bill Number: 102618**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the license or occupation taxes for persons engaged in or carrying on a telephone business; amending SMC 5.48.020 to include the definitions of "telephone business" and "competitive telephone services"; amending SMC 5.48.050 to exclude from the license and occupation tax certain income of persons engaged in or carrying on a telephone business, and to increase the rate of tax applied to the gross income of persons so engaged.
