**Council Bill Number: 102823**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Investment Committee, amending Seattle Municipal Code Chapter 5.04 (Ordinance 94190 s 1) to increase its membership, to authorize investments by the City Treasurer, rather than the City Comptroller and to add the City Attorney as advisor to the committee, providing for its meetings; delegating authority to apportion earnings on pooled investments and oversee interfund loans; directing additional reports; and establishing certain investment policies.
