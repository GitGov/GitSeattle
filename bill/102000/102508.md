 AN ORDINANCE amending Chapter 6 of the Seattle Municipal Code, relating to used goods, amending Sections 6.288.010, 6.288.030, 6.288.070, 6.288.080 and 6.288.100 and adding a new Section 6.\_\_\_.045, to define scrap metal, scrap metal hauler, and non-metal junk, and add clarifying language to exempt recording of scrap metal transactions less than Five Dollars ($5.00), to make discretionary recording of certain transactions, to exempt certain scrap metal from a holding period requirement, to exempt used book dealers from regulation, to impose special regulations for scrap metal haulers; and amending Section 4 of Ordinance 109818 to correct a clerical error.
