**Ordinance Number: 109763**

 ORDINANCE repealing Sections 376, 377, 378, 379, 380, 381 and 382 of the License Code (Ordinance 48022), (Seattle Municipal Code SS 6.30.010 -- 6.30.070) relating to the licensing and regulation of rental (housing) agencies; and amending the New Seattle License Code (Ordinance 108934), to include a new Chapter 11 to license and regulate the business of collecting and selling information as to residential rental property, providing for surety bonds to be filed by rental housing agencies, and requiring licensure only if a rental housing agency contracts with prospective tenants; and amending the License Fee Ordinance (Ordinance 109081), to add a new Section 7 setting a fee of $250 for rental (housing) agencies.
