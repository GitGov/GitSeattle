 AN ORDINANCE relating to licenses and occupation taxes; imposing a tax upon persons engaging in or carrying on a telephone business; amending SMC 5.48.020 to define "telephone business" and "competitive telephone service"; amending SMC 5.48.050 to exclude from the tax certain income of persons engaged in carrying on a telephone business, and increasing the rate of tax applied to the gross income of persons so engaged; and amending the rate of taxation of certain utilities.
