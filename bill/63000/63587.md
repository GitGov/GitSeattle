 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, Mt. Baker Parker Addition, being the alley between 30th Avenue South and 31st Avenue South, from Bayview Street to Lander Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13434 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
