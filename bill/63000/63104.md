**Council Bill Number: 63104**
   
**Ordinance Number: 71904**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the southeasterly 130 feet, more or less, of the Alley in Block 11, Heirs of Sarah A. Bell Addition, being the Alley between 6th Avenue and 7th Avenue, from Stewart Street to a connection with the existing pavement 130 feet, more or less, northwesterly of Stewart Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 13234 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
