 An ordinance relating to and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal light and power plant and system; declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be; authorizing the preparation of plans and specifications therefor, and the construction and acquisition of a transmission line communication system; and providing the funds therefor by appropriation of $54,530.00 from the surplus in the Light Fund as an advance to Capital Account, to be repaid from the proceeds of future bond sales.
