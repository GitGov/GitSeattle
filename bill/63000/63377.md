**Ordinance Number: 72176**

 An ordinance calling a special election for November 3rd, 1942; providing for the submission thereat of a proposition authorizing a tax levy at the rate of three mills in excess of the rate specified in Chapter 83, Laws of Washington, 1939 (Forty Mill Tax Limit) for civilian defense emergencies due to war; requiring the City Comptroller and ex-officio City Clerk to certify to the Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title; and declaring an emergency.
