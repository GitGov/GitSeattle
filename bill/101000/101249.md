

********

**Council Bill Number: 101249**
********

 AN ORDINANCE establishing a Department of Water and Waste Management; prescribing the duties of the Superintendent thereof; abolishing the Water Department and transferring responsibilities, positions and staff to the Department of Water and Waste Management; transferring the municipal sewage utility, the municipal solid waste utility and related engineering and support responsibilities from the Department of Engineering, together with the resource recovery project of the Energy Office, to the Department of Water and Waste Management, and repealing Ordinance 107788.

**Status:** DNP
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** June 9, 1980
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** April 21, 1980
   
**Committee:** Transportation
   
**Sponsor:** EXECUTIVE REQUEST
   
   
**Index Terms:** GOVERNMENTAL-REORGANIZATION, WATER-DEPARTMENT, PUBLIC-UTILITIES
