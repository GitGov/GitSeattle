**Ordinance Number: 109631**

 AN ORDINANCE amending Section 2, 3, 4, and 5 of Ordinance 108656 to transfer data processing functions and fixed assets from the Executive Department, Office of Management and Budget, Management Information Services (OMB/MIS) to the Department of Administrative Services, various related responsibilities from the Budget Director to the Director of Administrative Services, and certain liabilities from the General Fund to the Administrative Services Fund; abolishing the Data Processing Operating Fund; and repealing Section 1 of Ordinance 108656.
