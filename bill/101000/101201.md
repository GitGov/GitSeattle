**Ordinance Number: 108997**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Department of Community Development and the Pike Place Project, authorizing an agreement with the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development providing for an Urban Development Action Grant (UDAG) under Section 119 of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974; accepting UDAG funds to assist in rehabilitation of the Sanitary Market; depositing such funds in the Housing and Community Development Revenue Sharing Fund and appropriating such funds for the purposes set forth in the UDAG Grant Agreement; authorizing agreements as a may be necessary for implementation of the program; ratifying and confirming prior acts.
