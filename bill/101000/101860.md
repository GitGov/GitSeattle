**Council Bill Number: 101860**
   
**Ordinance Number: 109523**
********

 AN ORDINANCE amending Sections 2, 3, 12, 13 and 20 of Ordinance 72630, respectively to revise definitions; increase the rate of tax upon services, and establish the business of furnishing transient lodging through hotels, rooming houses, tourist courts, motels or trailer camps as a separate classification and establish separate rates for that classification; increase the Small Business License fee and the applicable gross income qualifications; increase the application fee; and increase the rate of interest on underpaid taxes.
