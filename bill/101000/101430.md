**Council Bill Number: 101430**
********

 AN ORDINANCE creating a Committee on Investment and Management, authorizing said committee to invest such City moneys as may be in excess of current needs, to provide advice in matters pertaining to investment and borrowing of money, designating the Treasure and Comptroller as responsible for daily transactions necessary for effective financial management, and repealing Ordinance 94190 and 103588.

**Status:** DNP
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** February 22, 1988
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** June 16, 1980
   
**Committee:** Finance
   
**Sponsor:** EXECUTIVE REQUEST
