**Ordinance Number: 105556**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to and authorizing an agreement with the United States Energy Research and Development Administration (ERDA) for financial assistance for preliminary engineering and design and analysis of pyrolysis gas and required processing for ammonia synthesis in connection with the City's Solid Waste/Ammonia Project; designating and selecting the Union Carbide Corporation's PUROK process for said Project and authorizing a letter of intent with said Corporation for purchase of necessary pyrolysis equipment; increasing an expenditure allowance in the 1976 Budget of the Executive Department-Office of Management and Budget for such purpose; and making a reimbursable appropriation from the Emergency Fund and declaring the emergency therefor.
