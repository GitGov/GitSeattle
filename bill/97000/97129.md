 AN ORDINANCE relating to and providing for a transfer of responsibilities for administration and operation of the Data Processing Center from the General Services Department to the Executive Department - Office of Management and Budget transferring certain positions and establishing certain additional positions in connection therewith; establishing certain expenditure allowances and a program category in the 1976 Budget of the Executive Department and increasing certain other expenditure allowances therein and in the 1976 Budgets of certain other departments; directing the Mayor to prepare annually a three-year City Data Processing Plan; making reimbursable appropriations from the Emergency Fund and declaring the emergency therefor, and superseding Ordinance 99554 to the extent inconsistent.
