**Council Bill Number: 65870**
   
**Ordinance Number: 74603**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of the city at the general municipal election to be held therein on March 12, 1946, the proposition of creating an indebtedness in the sum of Five Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($5,500,000.00), and the issuance and sale therefor of general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds of said city to pay the cost of acquiring a site for and constructing, equipping and furnishing a fireproof building of approximately fourteen stories, to include all facilities for the Health and Police Departments; a City Jail and Municipal Courts; and an underground garage, and to pay the principal and interest on such bonds by an annual tax levy in excess of the limitation contained in Constitution Art. VII, Sec. 2, during the term thereof; requiring the City Comptroller and Ex-officio City Clerk to certify to the Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, the said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title; and declaring an emergency.
