 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 3 and 4, Niesz Plat of West Seattle, being the alley between California Avenue and 42nd Avenue Southwest, from Admiral Way to West College Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13883 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
