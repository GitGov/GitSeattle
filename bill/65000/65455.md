**Ordinance Number: 74197**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 11, First Plat of West Seattle, being the alley between California Avenue and 42nd Avenue Southwest, from West Seattle Street to West Atlantic Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13918 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
