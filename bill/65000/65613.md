**Ordinance Number: 74354**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alleys in Block 3, Midland Addition, unrecorded, as established by Ordinance No. 60348, being the alleys in the block bounded by North 76th Street, North 77th Street, Aurora Avenue, and Winona Avenue; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13976 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
