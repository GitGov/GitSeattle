**Council Bill Number: 65561**
   
**Ordinance Number: 74303**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alleys in Blocks 2 and 5, University Place Addition, and in Block 2, College Addition, being the alleys between 21st Avenue Northeast and 22nd Avenue Northeast, from East 62nd Street to East 65th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13978 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
