**Council Bill Number: 65716**
   
**Ordinance Number: 74452**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of East 45th Street (both side), from Sand Point Way to 43rd Avenue Northeast, and from 50th Avenue Northeast to 51st Avenue Northeast; and East 45th Street (South Side), from 48th Avenue Northeast to 50th Avenue Northeast; by constructing concrete sidewalks and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13987 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
