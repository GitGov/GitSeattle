**Ordinance Number: 74123**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, J. W. Hart's Addition and Block 2, Dio Richardson's Addition, being the alley between 18th Avenue Northeast and 19th Avenue Northeast, from East 75th Street to East 77th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13889 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
