**Ordinance Number: 74138**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 3, Queen Anne 2nd Addition, being the alley between 8th Avenue West and 9th Avenue West, from West Crockett Street to West McGraw Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13935 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
