**Ordinance Number: 74348**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 19, Replat of a portion of Pleasant Valley Addition, being the alley between 29th Avenue West and 30th Avenue West, from West Dravus Street to West Bertona Street, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13929 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
