 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 5, J. B. Johnson Company's 1st Addition, being the alley between 18th Avenue South and 19th Avenue South, from Spokane Street to Hinds Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13956 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
