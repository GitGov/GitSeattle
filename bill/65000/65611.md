 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 14, Woodlawn Terrace Addition, being the alley between North 50th Street and North 51st Street, from Greenwood Avenue to 1st Avenue Northwest, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13936 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
