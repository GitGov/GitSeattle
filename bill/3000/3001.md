**Council Bill Number: 3001**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16234**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 1576, entitled "An Ordinance to prevent any person or persons from improperly retaining all books, papers and other property belonging to the City and all books, records, accounts, statements, abstracts, returns, reports, papers, plats, profiles, maps, charts, plans specifications, estimates, drawings, or other written or printed data made or kept by any city official or other person in the employee of the City, or which shall be produced or procured by the labor or any one while in the service of the City, shall, for the purpose of this ordinance, be deemed and taken to be the property of the City," approved December 31, 1890.
