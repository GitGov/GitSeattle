**Council Bill Number: 3997**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17203**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 19, A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, being the alley between Pike Street and Westlake Avenue, 4th Avenue and 5th Avenue, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and constructing all necessary catch basins, sewers, water mains, and retaining walls, all in accordance with Resolution No. 896 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
