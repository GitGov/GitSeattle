**Council Bill Number: 3273**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16610**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1398, for the improvement of Twenty-ninth Avenue, from Yesler Way to East Cherry Street; Thirtieth Avenue, from Yesler Way to East Cherry Street and East Spruce Street, from Twenty-ninth Avenue to Thirtieth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite corners (except, however, East Spruce Street from Twenty-ninth Avenue to Thirtieth Avenue, which shall be paved with sandstone blocks) under Ordinance No. 15256 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
