**Ordinance Number: 16786**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Hiawatha Place, from a point one hundred twenty (120) feet south of Dearborn Street to the alley between Block Eight (8) Rainier Boulevard Addition to the City of Seattle, and Block Five (5), of Twenty-second Street Addition to the City of Seattle; thence following said alley in a southerly direction to a connection with the existing sewer in Rainier Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a sewer therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, lampholes, flush tanks and other appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 723 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
