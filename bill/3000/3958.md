**Ordinance Number: 17127**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1408, for the improvement of Fairview Avenue from Denny Way to Aloha Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle by grading and re-grading the same, in accordance with the elevations established by Ordinance No. 12823, and by Ordinance No. 13699, and by constructing wooden curbs and gutters, and including as part of said work the construction of all necessary slopes or retaining walls, the construction of approaches where necessary and the reconstruction, alteration and extension of the existing sewer system, under Ordinance No. 15321 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
