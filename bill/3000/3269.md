**Ordinance Number: 16842**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North and West 80th Street, North and West 81st Street, North and West 82nd Street, North and West 83rd Street, North and West 84th Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to 3rd Avenue Northwest; and Greenwood Avenue from North 80th Street to North 85th Street; by constructing water mains therein of proper dimensions and materials with all gates, tees, crosses fire hydrants, and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 32309 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
