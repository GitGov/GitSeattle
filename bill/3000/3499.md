**Council Bill Number: 3499**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16769**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fortieth Avenue North, from East Ward Street to East Garfield Street; Forty-first Avenue North, from East Prospect Street to East Blaine Street; Forty-second Avenue North, from East Lee Street to a point one hundred twenty (120) feet north of East Blaine Street, East Lee Street, from Fortieth Avenue North to an outfall, into Lake Washington; and also in the alley in Block Eleven (11), of John J. McGilvra's Second Addition to the City of Seattle, beginning at East Highland Drive and running north to a connection with the sewer in East Lee Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; there shall also be included as a necessary part of said system the construction of a proper outfall sewer; the whole system to be constructed in accordance with Resolution No. 586 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
