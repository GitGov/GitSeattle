 An Ordinance amending Section One (1) of Ordinance No. 15902, entitled "An Ordinance ratifying and confirming a loan of five hundred thousand ($500,000.00) Dollars, authorized by election held on the 6th day of March, 1906, under and in pursuance of Ordinance No. 13188, entitled "An Ordinance submitting to the qualified electors of the City of Seattle at the general election to be held in said city on Tuesday, the 6th day of March, 1906, the question of the issuance and sale of negotiable bonds of said city in the amount of five hundred thousand ($500,000.00) dollars to provide money for the purchase of land for parks, parkways and playgrounds, the improvement thereof and the payment of existing indebtedness incurred in the purchase of property for parks, parkways and play grounds', passed January 8, 1906, and approved January 10, 1906, directing the issuance of bonds therefor, providing the form of such bonds, and providing for the payment of the principal and interest thereof", approved May 1, 1907, and published May 3, 1907.
