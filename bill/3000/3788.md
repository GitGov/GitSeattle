**Council Bill Number: 3788**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16977**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Jackson Street, in the City of Seattle, from 2nd Avenue South to a point 52 feet vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation with granite curbs and by constructing the necessary bulkheads and retaining walls, and by making such extensions to the existing sewer and water main systems as may be necessary fro the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 835 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
