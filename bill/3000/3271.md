**Council Bill Number: 3271**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16768**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fortieth Avenue, from East Howell Street to the north line of Denny Blaine Lake Park; Whitman Place, from Fortieth Avenue to Lake Washington; Stixrud Drive from Thirty-ninth Avenue to Fortieth Avenue; Evergreen Place, from East Olive Street to East Howell Street; Thirty-ninth Avenue, from East Olive Street to East Thomas; Dorfel Drive, from East Howell Street to East Thomas Street; Maiden Lane, from East Denny Way to East Thomas Street; Madrona Place North, from East Denny Way to East John Street; East Howell Street, from East Denny Way to Thirty-ninth Avenue North, on the north, East Denny Way, from the west line of Thirty-eighth Avenue to East Howell Street; East John Street, from Thirty-seventh Avenue North to Thirty-ninth Avenue; and High Lane, from East John Street to Thirty-seventh Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite corners, and including as a necessary part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration, or extension of concrete sidewalks; the construction of all sewers and water mains, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Majority Petition, being File No. 32387, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
