**Council Bill Number: 3671**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16973**
********

 An ordinance authorizing and directing the levying and collection of a new assessment or re-assessment to pay the cost and expense of and for the improvement of Harvard Avenue and other streets and avenues, named in Ordinance No. 10710 by assessing the property benefited thereby, and directing the city treasurer, upon receipt of the approved re-assessment roll, to credit the property therein specified with any payments already made for said property on account of the assessment heretofore levied to pay for said improvement and to charge the property therein specified with interest on the amount found due in the re-assessment roll herein provided for.
