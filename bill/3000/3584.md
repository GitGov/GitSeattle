**Ordinance Number: 16798**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Elliott Avenue, from a point 120 feet south of Clay Street to Eagle Street; Clay Street, from Western Avenue to Elliott Avenue; Broad Street, from Western Avenue to Railroad Avenue, and Eagle Street, from Elliott Avenue to an outfall in Elliott Bay, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and other appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 786 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
