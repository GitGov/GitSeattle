 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1376, for the improvement of the alley in Block Five (5), A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, same being the alley between Second Avenue and Third Avenue, from University Street to Seneca Street, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, including such grading and regrading as may be necessary, and constructing of sewer and watermains with their appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 15106 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
