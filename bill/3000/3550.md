**Council Bill Number: 3550**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21331**
********

 An ordinance authorizing the Grant Smith and Company and Stillwell to construct a 20 inch cast iron water main on Harrison Street and East Harrison Street; directing the Superintendent of Light and Water to furnish Grant Smith and Company and Stillwell with surplus water for sluicing; fixing the price at which surplus water shall be furnished; providing for the acquisition of the water main by the City of Seattle; and specifying the manner in which the water main is to be constructed.
