**Ordinance Number: 16670**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 15327, entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-second Avenue, from Yesler Way to East Columbia Street; East Spruce Street, from the east line of Thirty-second Avenue on the south, to the west line of Thirty-second Avenue on the north; Thirty-third Avenue from a point one hundred eighty (180) feet south of East James Street to East Columbia Street; Thirty-fourth Avenue, from the south line of Summit Park Supplemental Addition to the City of Seattle, to the north line of East Pike Street; Thirty-sixth Avenue, from East Union Street to East Olive Street; Thirty-seventh Avenue, from East Union Street to East Howell Street; East Howell Street, from Thirty-seventh Avenue to Thirty-eighth Avenue; Thirty-eighth Avenue, from East Howell Street to East Denny Way; East James Street and East Columbia Street, each from Thirty-second Avenue to Thirty-fifth Street; East Marion Street, East Spring Street and East Union Street each from Fourth-fourth Avenue to Fifth-fifth Avenue; East Pike Street, from Thrifty-fourth Avenue to Thirty-seventh Avenue, and East Pike Street, from Thirty-fifth Avenue to Thirty-seventh Avenue, all in the City of Seattle by grading, sub-grading and by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite corners (except, however, East Pike Street and East Pike Street, each from Thirty-fifth Avenue to Thirty-seventh Avenue, which shall be paved with sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation), all in accordance with Resolution No. 421, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
