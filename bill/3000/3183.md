**Ordinance Number: 16413**

 An Ordinance to promote and maintain the health of and prevent the spread of contagious diseases among school children, and to promote and maintain sanitary conditions in schools and school buildings, providing for the medical inspection of school children, the appointment of medical school inspectors and school nurses, defining their powers and duties, and providing penalties for violations of this ordinance.
