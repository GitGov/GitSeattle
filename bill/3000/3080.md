 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Corliss Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from the alley in Block four (4), replat of blocks three (3), four (4), five (5), six (6) and thirteen (13), Green Lake Home Addition to the City of Seattle, to North Eighty-fifth Street, by constructing water mains therein of proper dimensions, together with such gates, tees, crosses and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the reasonable water service of said portion of said avenue, in accordance with Resolution No. 599 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
