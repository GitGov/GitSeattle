**Council Bill Number: 3657**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16920**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of certain streets, avenues, alleys, public lands and places, from a point beginning at the intersection of the outer harbor line and Connecticut Street to 6th Avenue South; thence north along 6th Avenue South to Seattle Boulevard; thence northwest along Seattle Boulevard to 6th Avenue South, from the north by construction of a main trunk sewer therein and along, and by the construction of a branch sewer from Connecticut Street and 4th Avenue South, and running north along 4th Avenue South to Main Street; all in the City of Seattle and providing for the establishing of a main trunk sewer or branch district to be known as the "Connecticut Street et al Trunk Sewer District" and providing for the creation of Local Improvement District No. 1591; all in accordance with Resolution No. 651 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
