**Council Bill Number: 3398**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16599**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1424, for the improvement of East Cherry Street, from Twentieth Avenue to Thirty-fifth Avenue, in the City of Seattle, by sub-grading and by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite corners, and all sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; except certain portions of certain Streets, therein named which shall be paved with sandstone blocks, under Ordinance No. 15520 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
