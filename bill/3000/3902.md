**Council Bill Number: 3902**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17186**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1310 the improvement of 4th Avenue and 4th Avenue South, from Union Street to Washington Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle by grading and re-grading the same to conform to the respective elevations above city datum as established by Ordinance No. 13074; and including as a necessary part of said work the construction of a bridge to carry Yesler Way and Terrace Street over existing sewer system, together with such temporary adjustment of the existing sewers as may be necessary during the progress of said work, and the construction of the necessary slopes and retaining walls for cuts and fills.
