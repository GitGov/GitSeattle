**Council Bill Number: 3426**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16698**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Block Ten (10) of Boren & Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, same being the alley between Second Avenue and Third Avenue, between Madison Street and Marion Street, by grading and paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and including such sewers and watermains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Majority Petition, being File No. 32535 and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
