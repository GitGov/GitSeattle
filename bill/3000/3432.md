**Council Bill Number: 3432**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16781**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Melrose Avenue North, from East Denny Way to East Roy Street; Bellevue Place, from East Roy Street to Belmont Avenue North; Lakeview Avenue, from Eastlake Avenue to East Roy Street; East Mercer Street, East Republican Street and East Thomas Street, each from Eastlake Avenue to Bellevue Avenue North, and East Harrison Street, from Eastlake Avenue to the alley between Eastlake Avenue and Melrose Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the same, and by constructing wooden bulkheads, concrete sidewalks and concrete stairways where necessary; by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; together with brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, providing sandstone pavement may be substituted for asphalt where the grades exceed ten per cent (10%), together with such extensions to the existing sewer and water systems, with all appurtenances as may be necessary, in accordance with Resolution No. 753 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
