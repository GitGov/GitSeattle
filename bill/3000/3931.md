**Council Bill Number: 3931**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17125**
********

 An ordinance authorizing and directing the payment of the compensation of the Board of Eminent Domain Commissioners from the general fund, and authorizing and directing the drawing of warrants upon condemnation funds in favor of the general fund as a reimbursement of such payments and for the other expenses incurred in condemnation proceedings, upon the filling with the City Comptroller a property attested court order setting forth the amount of said expenses and appropriating therefor the sum of $4,000.00 or so much thereof as may be necessary.
