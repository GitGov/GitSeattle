**Ordinance Number: 84726**

 An ordinance relating to the general municipal election of March 13, 1956; providing for the submission thereat of Ordinance 84390 to a referendum vote pursuant to petition therefor, which ordinance relates to the financing, maintenance and operation of the municipal sewerage system and additions and betterments thereto as a public utility and provides for rates and charges; requiring the City Clerk to certify to the County Auditor as Supervisor of Elections, as required by law, the proposition for the ratification or rejection of said ordinance in the form of a specified ballot title and said ordinance in full, and superseding Ordinance 84648.
