**Council Bill Number: 76236**
   
**Ordinance Number: 84815**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 40, as laid off by D. T. Denny, Guardian of the estate of J. H. Nagle (commonly known as Nagle's Addition), as established by Ordinance No. 5236, being the alley between 14th Avenue North and 15th Avenue North from East Denny Way to East Howell Street; and certain other streets, avenues, and alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16699 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
