**Ordinance Number: 85150**

 An ordinance relating to the municipal light and power plant and system; authorizing the Superintendent of Lighting to complete regotiations with PUD No. 2 of Grant County, Washington whereby the District will agree to sell and the City will agree to buy a percentage share of the proposed Priest Rapids Development output of electric energy and pay for the same out of the gross revenues of said plant and system; and to execute a letter of escros instructions and contract in connection therewith.
