**Ordinance Number: 85376**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 5, Sparkman & McLean's Second Addition to West Seattle, being the alley between 45th Avenue Southwest and 46th Avenue Southwest from West Charlestown Street to West Spokane Street; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17003 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
