**Ordinance Number: 81313**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 1, 2 and 3, B. F. Day's 1st Addition, being a continuous alley between Fremont Avenue and Evanston Avenue, from Bowdoin Place to North 42nd Street; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15750 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
