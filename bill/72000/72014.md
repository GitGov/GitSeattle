**Ordinance Number: 80642**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Northlake Avenue, from approximately 45 feet west of the west margin of Waterway No. 22 to approximately 420 feet west thereof; by constructing a retaining wall and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15933 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
