**Ordinance Number: 81139**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 12th Avenue Northeast from East 88th Street to approximately 145 feet north of East 92nd Street; and certain other streets and avenues; by constructing concrete sidewalks and others improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 15804 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
