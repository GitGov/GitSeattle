**Council Bill Number: 84458**
   
**Ordinance Number: 92957**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 19th Ave. Southwest from Southwest Othello St. to approximately 110 feet north of Southwest Webster St. and from approximately 75 feet south of Southwest Webster St. to approximately 40 feet north to Southwest Holden St.; and certain other streets, avenues and easements; by constructing sanitary sewers and otherwise improving same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 19742 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
