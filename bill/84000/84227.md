**Council Bill Number: 84227**
   
**Ordinance Number: 92726**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening and establishing of Northeast 75th St. between 8th Ave. Northeast and 20th Ave. Northeast; of Roosevelt Way Northeast between the north margin of Northeast 75th St. and a point approximately 71 feet north thereof, all at existing grade; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; providing for the acquisition of the right to construct slopes for cuts and fills on certain abutting properties; and providing for payment from the City Street Fund or the Arterial City Street Fund or both in such amounts as the City Council may direct.
