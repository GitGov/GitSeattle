**Ordinance Number: 91371**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley between Block 1 and Block 2, Carleton Park Terrace-Second Division Addition, being the alley between 35th Ave. West and 36th Ave. West from West Armour Street to West Raye Street; and a certain other street and alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with the Resolution No. 18626 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
