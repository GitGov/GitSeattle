**Council Bill Number: 82064**
   
**Ordinance Number: 90625**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Thirtieth Ave. Addition, being the alley between 29th Ave. on 30th Ave. from East Alder Street to approximately 310 feet north thereof; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18526 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
