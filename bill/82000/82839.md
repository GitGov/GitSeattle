 An ordinance providing for the improvement of alley in Block 3, Frances R. Day's La Grande Addition, as established by King County Court Cause No. 393405, being the alley between Whitman Ave. North and Woodland Park Ave. North from North 39th Street to North 40th Street; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18625 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
