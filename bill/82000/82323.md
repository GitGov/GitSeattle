**Council Bill Number: 82323**
   
**Ordinance Number: 90850**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of an elevated structure, and approaches thereto, on Southwest Spokane Street; providing for the widening of Southwest Spokane Street, Chelan Avenue Southwest, Delridge Way Southwest and Admiral Way Southwest as surface roadways and approaches to said elevates structure; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land another property necessary therefor; providing for the changing and establishing of the necessary grades; providing for the acquisition of the right to construct slopes for cuts and fills on certain abutting properties; providing for the taking of land and other property in fee simple for general municipal purposes; and providing that the cost of such improvement shall be paid from the Seattle General Street Improvement Bonds 1960 Funds; except as herein otherwise provided.
