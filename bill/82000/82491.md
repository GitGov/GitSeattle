**Ordinance Number: 90997**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment rol of Local Improvement District No. 6229 for the improvement of East 60th Street from 65th Ave. Northeast to approximately 585 feet southeasterly of Windermere Road; and certain other streets, avenues and easements; (all streets and avenues designated in this ordinance are in accordance with the street nomenclature in effect at the time said local improvement district was created, and not as subsequently modified by Ordinance No. 89910.) by constructing a sanitary sewer system and otherwise improving the same; under Ordinance No. 89268, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
