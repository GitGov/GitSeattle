**Ordinance Number: 100444**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Engineering Department; authorizing the preparation of plans, specifications and cost estimates for the improvement of 10th Avenue East from East Republican Street to East Mercer Street and of East Mercer Street from Broadway Avenue East to the vicinity of 10th Avenue East by widening, including necessary sidewalk, fire hydrant, storm drain, and utility relocation, local utility service reconnections, private driveway adjustments, and the planting of street trees, all within the existing right of way, and making a reimbursable appropriation from the City Street Fund therefor.
