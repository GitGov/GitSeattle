**Council Bill Number: 92171**
********

 Providing for the submission to the qualified electors (voters) of the City of Seattle at a special municipal election to be held therein on Feb. 8, 1972 of the initiative measure contemplated by voters' petition (C.F. 271203) prohibiting the construction of an aquarium in Golden Gardens Park; providing for certification of such initiative measure to the Director of the Dept. of Records and Elections of King County and prescribing the ballot title therefor.
