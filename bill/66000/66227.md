**Ordinance Number: 74957**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Laurelhurst Addition, being the alley between 43rd Avenue Northeast and 45th Avenue Northeast, from East 44th Street to East 45th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14023 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
