**Ordinance Number: 74958**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 8, D. T. Denny's Water Front Addition, being the Alley between 3rd Avenue West and 4th Avenue West, from West Harrison Street to West Republican Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14022 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
