**Ordinance Number: 75394**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Green Lake Circle Railroad Addition, being the alley between Greenwood Avenue and Palatine Avenue from North 65th Street to North 67th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14115 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
