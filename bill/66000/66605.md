**Council Bill Number: 66605**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75329**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of the City at the special municipal election to be held therein on November 5, 1946, the proposition of creating an indebtedness in the sum of Three Million Five Hundered Thousand Dollars ($3,500,000.00) and the issuance and sale therefor of general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds of said City, for the purpose of constructing extensions, improvements and betterments of the municipal street lighting system, and to pay the principal and interest on such bonds by an annual tax levy in excess of the limitation contained in Construction Article VII, Section 2 during the term of such bonds; requiring the City Comptroller and ex-officio City Clerk to certify to the Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, the said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title; and declaring an emergency.
