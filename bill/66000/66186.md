**Council Bill Number: 66186**
   
**Ordinance Number: 74917**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of California Avenue, from West Kenyon Street to West Sullivan Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing concrete sidewalks and concrete stairways; by doing such grading or regarding and graveling as may be necessary; by providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 13905 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
