 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 6, University Scenic Addition, being the alley between East 62nd Street and East 63rd Street, from 15th Avenue Northeast to 17th Avenue Northeast; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14300 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
