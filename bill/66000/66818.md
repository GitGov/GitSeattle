**Council Bill Number: 66818**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75539**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 32, 33 and 34, Woodlawn Addition to Green Lake, being the alley between East 72nd Street, and East 73rd Street, from Woodlawn Avenue to 5th Avenue Northeast; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14346 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
