**Ordinance Number: 75716**

 An ordinance providing for the proper maintenance under the supervision of the Board of Park Commissioners of the Center Parking Strip in 43rd Avenue Northeast, from East 38th Street to East 45th Street, for a period of five (5) years only; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14515 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
