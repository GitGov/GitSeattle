**Council Bill Number: 66362**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75087**

 An ordinance relating to the licensing and regulation of certain occupations, businesses, vocations, trades, places of amusement, boats, places and establishments under the License Code (Ordinance No. 48022); amending Sections 13, 22, 23, 111, 112, 131 and 164 of said Code to adjust fees, fix new expiration dates for Amusement Licenses and Steam Engineers' Licenses, make the Junk Wagon License fee annual only, abolish semi-annual license for theatres, and increase annual renewal fee for For-Hire Drivers and Steam Engineers.
