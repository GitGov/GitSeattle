**Council Bill Number: 66789**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75511**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 4, Washington Heights Addition; and in Block 6, Randell's Addition, being the alley between 35th Avenue and 36th Avenue, from East Marion Street to East Spring Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14335 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
