**Ordinance Number: 75288**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 7, Magnolia Terrace Addition and in Block 6, Buena Vista Addition No. 2, being the alley between 28th Avenue West and 29th Avenue West from West Armour Street to West Barrett Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14174 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
