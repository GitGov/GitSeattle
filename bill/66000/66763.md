**Council Bill Number: 66763**
   
**Ordinance Number: 75488**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 22nd Avenue Northeast, from East 68th Street to the south line of Ravenna Park Heights Addition; and from East 65th Street to approximately 50 feet south thereof; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing sewers and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14167 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
