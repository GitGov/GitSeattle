**Ordinance Number: 74712**

 An ordinance relating to the acute housing problem in Seattle; declaring the public health and welfare involved; and that the operation of a Housing Information Center sponsored by a public social agency will alleviated the problem; designating the City's agent to authorize and approve certain expenditures in aid of such operation; appropriating $10,000.00 from the Emergency Fund for such purpose, subject to audit, and declaring the emergency therefor.
