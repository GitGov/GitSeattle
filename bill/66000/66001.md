**Council Bill Number: 66001**
   
**Ordinance Number: 74736**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alleys in Block 5, Beacon Heights Addition, being the north and south alley between Beacon Avenue and 17th Avenue South, from Stevens Street to the east and west alley in said block, and said east and west alley between Hanford Street and Stevens Street, from Beacon Avenue to 17th Avenue South; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13983 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
