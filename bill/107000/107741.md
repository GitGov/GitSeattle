**Council Bill Number: 107741**
   
**Ordinance Number: 114901**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to an exchange of properties between the City of Seattle and Seattle University; authorizing a contract for the exchange of properties; authorizing conveyance of City real property to Seattle University; providing for reimbursement of the Emergency Fund for prior appropriation; crediting proceeds of the exchange to the Transit Fund; authorizing the Department of Community Development to enter into leases for acquired properties; and appropriating funds from the Transit Fund for property management expenses.

**Status:** Passed
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** December 11, 1989
   
**Committee:** Transportation
   
**Sponsor:** WILLIAMS
