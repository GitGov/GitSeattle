**Council Bill Number: 107650**

 AN ORDINANCE making an appropriation from the Emergency Fund to provide for lobbying the United States Congress to amend the Passenger Service Act of 1886 in order to allow foreign cruise ships to provide service between Seattle and Alaska; increasing an expenditure allowance in the 1989 Budget of the Department of Community Development; and declaring the emergency therefor.

**Status:** DNP
   
**Vote:** 8-0
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** October 23, 1989
   
**Committee:** Finance and Personnel
