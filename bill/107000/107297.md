**Ordinance Number: 114517**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the admission Tax; amending Seattle Municipal Code (SMC) Section 5.40.070 to change the penalty date from 15 days after the end of the month in which the tax was collected to 25 days after the end of the month in which the tax was collected; and adding a new Section to SMC Chapter 5.40 to establish a method of computing delinquency days.
