**Ordinance Number: 114820**
********

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing the Director of Administrative Services and the Purchasing Agent to acquire, by lease of purchase, heating, ventilating and air conditioning systems and associated equipment and services authorizing the necessary agreements for such acquisition; conditionally authorizing alternative financing for such acquisition by issuance of general obligation bonds of the City; providing the form, terms and conditions on the issuance of such obligations; designating the City Comptroller to sell and deliver the obligations and to fix the dates, maximum thereof, interest rates and other details consistent with the ordinance; and providing payment therefor.
