**Council Bill Number: 47657**
   
**Ordinance Number: 57329**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alleys in blocks 9, 10, 11, and 12, Pettit's University Addition, and the alley in block 5, Interlake Addition, and the alleys deeded under Ordinance No. 17736 and Ordinance No. 24247, being the alleys from East 45th Street to East 56th Street, between 10th Avenue Northeast and 11th Avenue Northeast, by paving and otherwise improving the same in accordance with Resolution No. 9714 of the City Council of the Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
