**Council Bill Number: 47621**
   
**Ordinance Number: 57281**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Boston Street from 14th Avenue North on the south to a point approximately 137 feet east of the east margin of 15th Avenue North produced from the south; by paving and otherwise improving the same in accordance with Resolution No. 9816 of the City Council of the Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
