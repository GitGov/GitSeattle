**Council Bill Number: 47677**
   
**Ordinance Number: 57355**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal street railway system; providing for the construction, reconstruction and acquisition of, and payment for, an elevated structure and certain street railway lines, property and equipment on Spokane and West Spokane Streets, track reconstruction incidental thereto; declaring the estimated cost therof, as near as may be; providing for the issuance and sale of warrants to provide funds therefor; creating and establishing a special fund to pay the principal and interest of said warrants.
