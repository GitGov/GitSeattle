**Council Bill Number: 47290**
   
**Ordinance Number: 56978**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 15, Hall's Addition, and the same extended to East Madison Street; and the alley in Block 1, Witt's Addition, and the same extended to the alley in said Block 15, Hall's Addition; being the alleys in the block bounded by 20th Avenue, East Denny Way and East Madison Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 116309 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
