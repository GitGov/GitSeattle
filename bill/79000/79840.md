**Ordinance Number: 88381**

 An ordinance authorizing the summary abatement of a public nuisance caused by an excavation for a proposed apartment building on private property in Block 78, Public Benefit Suppl. Plat Addition, which excavation has impaired the lateral support to an adjacent public street and sewer and water utility located therein; authorizing the City Engineer to summarily abate such nuisance; making an appropriation from the Emergency Fund in connection therewith and declaring the emergency therefor.
