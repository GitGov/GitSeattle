**Council Bill Number: 79634**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88221**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Lockmore Addition Number 3, being the alley between 27th Avenue South and 28th Avenue South from Hudson Street to Dawson Street; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17773 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
