 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 8, Westholme Addition, being the alley between Avalon Way and 32nd Avenue Southwest from West Andover Street to West Genesee Street; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17807 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment of Bonds".
