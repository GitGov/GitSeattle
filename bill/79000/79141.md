 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 5, Gilman's Addition, being the alley between 23rd Avenue West and 24th Avenue West, from West Dravus Street to West Bertona Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 17696 of the City Council of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
