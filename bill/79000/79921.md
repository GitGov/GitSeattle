**Ordinance Number: 88462**

 An ordinance relating to motor vehicle traffic, travel and transportation and incidents thereof upon the streets and other ways open to the public and the regulation thereof by the Traffic Code; adding a new Section 50.03 and amending Sections 50.03 through 50.08 of said Code (Ordinance 80998) as amended by Ordinance 82670, to increase the speed limit on Aurora Avenue between the Battery Street tunnel and Winona Place to 40 miles per hour.
