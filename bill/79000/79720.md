**Council Bill Number: 79720**
   
**Ordinance Number: 88261**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 35th Avenue West from Thurman Street to approximately 250 feet north thereof; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 17672 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
