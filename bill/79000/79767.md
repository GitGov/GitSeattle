 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West Kenyon Street from 45th Avenue Southwest to 46th Avenue Southwest; and a certain other street, by constructing sewers and otherwise imporving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17837 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a Local Improvement District therefor, providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds", and declaring such improvement necessary for the protection of the public health and safety.
