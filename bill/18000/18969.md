**Council Bill Number: 18969**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30531**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 12th Avenue South from the north line of Judkins Street to the south line of Holgate Street; Judkins Street, Atlantic Street, Massachusetts Street and Holgate Street, each from 12th Avenue South to 13th Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete sidewalks where necessary; by providing proper sewerage, water service and fire protection, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3754, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
