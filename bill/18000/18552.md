**Council Bill Number: 18552**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30577**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 27782, entitled: "An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of a public street and highway from West Spokane Street and 28th Avenue Southwest to Alaska Street near 39th Avenue Southwest, all in the City of Seattle,; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of said street and highway and approaches thereto; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of such public street and highway and approaches thereto; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law", approved August 21, 1911, and authorizing the dismissal of the action in the Superior Court based upon said ordinance and the payment of all taxable costs.
