**Ordinance Number: 30486**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-fourth Avenue Northwest from West Sixty-second Street to West Sixty-eight Street, Thirty-sixth Avenue Northeast, from West Sixty-second Street to West Sixty-fifth street; West Sixty-fifth Street from Thirty-sixth Avenue Northwest to Thirty-second Avenue Northwest; West Sixty-seventh Street and West Sixty-eighth Street, each from Thirty-fifth Avenue Northwest to Thirty-second Avenue Northwest, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3681 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
