**Ordinance Number: 30063**

 An Ordinance accepting a deed from Frank D. Black and Kate H. Black, his wife, to The City of Seattle, executed on the Sixty Day of September, 1912 for all that portion of Columbia Park lying within the following described limits, viz. Beginning at the southwest corner of lot 1536 block 55, Columbia Supplemental No. 1, as recorded in Vol. 8 of Plats, p. 12, Records of King Co., Washington; thence in a northwesterly direction along a line parallel with the westerly marginal line of Rainier Avenue, as platted in Columbia Terrace, a plat recorded in Vol. 10 of Plats, p. 48, of said records of King County, 200 feet; thence northeasterly at right angles 75 feet more or less to the westerly marginal line of Rainier Avenue; thence southeasterly along the northwesterly marginal line of said Lot A and southerly along the westerly marginal line of said Lot 1536 to the point of beginning.
