**Council Bill Number: 18783**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30344**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2530 for the improvement of Eleventh Avenue West, from the south margin of West Galer Street to the north margin of West Howe Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete, foundation, together with concrete curbs, and armored concrete corners, and including as a part of said work, the construction and reconstruction of all sewers and side sewers, and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, and by the construction and reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary, and by such grading and regrading as may be necessary under Ordinance No. 29412, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
