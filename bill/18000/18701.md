**Council Bill Number: 18701**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30281**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2542 for the improvement of Dose Terrace, from the Easterly Margin of Thirty-first Avenue South to the easterly margin of Thirty-third Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs, and by the constriction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewreage, water service and fire protection of said district, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, under Ordinance No. 29457, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund, and fixing a date for the issuance of bonds.
