**Ordinance Number: 30479**
********

 An Ordinance requesting Puget Sound Electric Railway to change and remove certain of its tracks between the southerly line of John Street, as platted in Corgiat Addition, and Director Avenue, in the plat of Fair Grounds Addition, originally located within the limits of a county road and now being within the limits of the City of Seattle on such road, to a location in such road to be approved by the Board of Public Works of the city, ramifying and approving such change of location, and obligating the city not to interfere with such tracks and railway in such new location, and the operation thereof.
