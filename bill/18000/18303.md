**Ordinance Number: 29901**

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 29446, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Charles Street and Norman Street, each from 23rd Avenue South to 26th Avenue South; 23rd Avenue South, 24th Avenue South, and 25th Avenue South, each from Dearborn Street to Norman Street; 26th Avenue South, from Dearborn Street to Judkins Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3397, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds'" approved May 22, 1912, and published May 25, 1912.
