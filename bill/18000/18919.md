**Council Bill Number: 18919**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30485**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Wheeler Street from Queen Anne Boulevard to the westerly margin of Tract No. 2 as platted in Hunter's Lake union Addition, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, by constructing concrete sidewalks and concrete retaining walls where necessary, by providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3527 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
