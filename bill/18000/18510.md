**Council Bill Number: 18510**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30210**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Seventh Avenue West, from West McGraw Street to West Blaine Street, West Blaine Street, from Seventh Avenue West to Eighth Avenue West, Eighth Avenue West, from West Blaine Street to West Highland Drive, all of said streets being part of the Queen Anne Boulevard in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers and side sewers therein, together with all necessary appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3577 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
