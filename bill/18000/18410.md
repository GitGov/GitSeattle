**Council Bill Number: 18410**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30026**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Eighth Avenue West, from West Wheeler Street to West Fulton Street; Ninth Avenue West, from West Halladay Street to West Barrett Street; Tenth Avenue West and Queen Anne Boulevard, each from West Halladay Street to West Fulton Street; and Tenth Avenue West from West Armour Street to West Barrett Street; Eleventh Avenue West, from West Ray Street to Grand Boulevard; West Halladay Street, from Seventh Avenue West to Ninth Avenue West; West Ray Street, from Seventh Avenue West to Eighth Avenue West and from Ninth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; West Newell Street, from Eighth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; Both well Street, from Eighth Avenue West to Ninth Avenue West and from Tenth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; West Armour Street, from Eighth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; West Fulton Street, from Eighth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West and West Barrett Street from Ninth Avenue West to Eleventh avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, together with the necessary wooden crosswalks and wood or concrete stairways, providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3596 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
