**Council Bill Number: 18809**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30378**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2491 for the improvement of First Avenue South from Oxbow Bridge to Barton Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading or constructing a plank trestle as may be necessary; provided that where a plank trestle is necessary it shall not be less than 18 feet in width, and where an earth roadway is necessary it shall not be more than forty-two feet in width; all in accordance with Majority Petition being File No. 43950 under Ordinance No. 27288, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund, and fixing a date for the issuance of bonds.
