**Ordinance Number: 30005**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2515 for the improvement of Harvard Avenue North from East Boston Street to Lakeview Boulevard, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material, together with brick or asphalt gutters, concrete or armoured concrete curbs, the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, together with all the appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 28807, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund, and fixing a date for the issuance of bonds.
