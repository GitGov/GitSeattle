**Council Bill Number: 18367**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30002**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Post Street, from the north margin of Marion Street to the North margin of Seneca Street; Spring Street, from Western Avenue to First Avenue; and Seneca Street, by paving the same with vitrified brick or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick gutters, granite or reinforced concrete curbs, and including the necessary earth filling, the construction of necessary plank roadways, wooden bulkheads and the construction or reconstruction of all necessary concrete sidewalks, sewers, side sewers and watermains, together with the appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 3430 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
