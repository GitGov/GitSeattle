**Council Bill Number: 18506**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30104**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Bella Vista Avenue, from Court Street to Horton Street; Horton Street, and Hinds Street, each from Hunter Boulevard to Cascade Avenue; and Spokane Street, from Hunter Boulevard to Bella Vista, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, or some other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with concrete and armored concrete curbs and proper gutters; by the constructinon of reconstruction of all sewers, side-sewers, and watermains and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, water service, and fire protection of said district, by the construction or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary.
