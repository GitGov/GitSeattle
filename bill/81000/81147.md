**Ordinance Number: 89682**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 11th Ave. Southwest from West Henderson Street to West Roxbury Street; and certain other streets and avenues, by constructing sanitary sewers and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18150 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a Local Improvement District therefor, providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds", and declaring such improvement necessary for the protection of the public health and safety.
