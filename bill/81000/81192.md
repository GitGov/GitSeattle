**Council Bill Number: 81192**
   
**Ordinance Number: 89726**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Playfield known as Soundview Playfield, as established by Ordinance No. 82669, situated west of 15th Ave. Northwest and north of West 90th Street; and certain streets and avenues; by grading and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18427 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Immediate Payment."
