 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West 57th Street from Seaview Ave. to approximately 100 feet west thereof; and certain other streets and avenues; by constructing sanitary sewers and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 18505 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds."
