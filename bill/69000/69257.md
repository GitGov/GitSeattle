**Council Bill Number: 69257**
   
**Ordinance Number: 77933**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 52, John H. Nagle's Second Addition, being the alley between 10th Avenue North and 11th Avenue North, from East Thomas Street to East John Street; and certain other alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14995 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
