**Council Bill Number: 69400**
   
**Ordinance Number: 78071**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 13, Summit Heights 2nd Addition, being the alley between West 74th Street and West 75th Street, from 32nd Avenue Northwest to approximately 370 feet west thereof, and certain other alley and streets, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15012 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
