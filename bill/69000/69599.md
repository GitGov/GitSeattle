**Council Bill Number: 69599**
   
**Ordinance Number: 78271**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 25, 2nd Plat of West Seattle by The West Seattle Land and Improvement Company, being the alley between California Avenue and 44th Avenue Southwest, from West Massachusetts Street to West Holgate Street, and certain other alleys and streets, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15025 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
