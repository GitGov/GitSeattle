**Ordinance Number: 77916**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 29 and 30, Carleton Park Addition, and in Block 5, Briarcliff Addition No. 2, being the alley in block bounded by 42nd Avenue West, West Viewmont Way, and West Armour Street, and certain other alleys and streets, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 14698 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
