**Council Bill Number: 69202**
   
**Ordinance Number: 77879**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Block 6, Hillman's Lake Front Addition, Division No. 3, and Block 1, Hillman's Lake Front Addition, Division No. 4, being the alley between North 71st Street and North 72nd Street, from Dayton Avenue to Greenwood Avenue; and certain other alleys and streets; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 14868 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
