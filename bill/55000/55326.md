**Council Bill Number: 55326**
   
**Ordinance Number: 65781**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 57588, entitled: "An Ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Ryan Street, between 55th Avenue South and 62nd Avenue South; of Norfolk Street and Roxbury Street, each between Renton Avenue as now established and 59th Avenue South; of 57th Avenue South, between Ryan Street and Renton Avenue as now established; and of 59th Avenue South, between Bangor Street and Roxbury Street; providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of said streets and avenues, and of Victor Street, between 57th Avenue South, between Renton Avenue as now established and Norfolk Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and for the making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting on said streets and avenues; and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property especially benefited in the manner provided by law", approved the 29th day of May, 1929, and directing the discontinuance of condemnation proceedings heretofor instituted thereunder.
