**Council Bill Number: 55645**
   
**Ordinance Number: 64832**
********

 An ordinance relating to the Police and Fire Departments; modifying the conditions of employment therein by requiring certain members (Classes "C" and "D") to lay off one (1) day in each eight (8) day period without pay from July 1, 1934 to January 1, 1935 in order to reduce the expenses and balance of budgets of said Departments and suspending all ordinances or parts thereof in conflict herewith.
