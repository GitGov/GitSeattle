**Ordinance Number: 55653**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 60, West Seattle Land & Improvement Company's 2nd Plat, and in Block 2, Aldine Heights Addition, being the alley between California Avenue and 42nd Avenue Southwest, from West Walker Street to West Hill Street, by grading and paving the same; by providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 115202 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
