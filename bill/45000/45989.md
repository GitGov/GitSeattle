**Ordinance Number: 55753**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in blocks 88 to 91, inclusive, Woodlawn Addition to Green Lake, being the alley between Latona Avenue and 4th Avenue Northeast, from Woodlawn Avenue to East 65th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 9474 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of " Payment in Bonds".
