**Ordinance Number: 55167**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Admiral Way (southerly parking strip), from 49th Avenue Southwest to the alley between 48th Avenue Southwest and 47th Avenue Southwest; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing sewers and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 9323 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
