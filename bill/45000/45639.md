**Council Bill Number: 45639**
   
**Ordinance Number: 55442**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 15th Avenue North from East Howell Street to a point approximately one hundred and ninety and no one- hundredths (190.00) feet north of East Galer Street, and of East Thomas Street and East John Street at 20th Avenue North; providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of said avenues and streets; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking an damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; for the making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting on said avenue and streets; and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
