**Council Bill Number: 45004**
   
**Ordinance Number: 54818**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of 14th Avenue South from Director Street to Dallas Avenue at the City Limits south of the Duwamish Waterway; 16th Avenue South from the City Limits north of the Duwamish Waterway to East Marginal Way; and East Marginal Way, from 16th Avenue South to a point approximately Four Hundred Four (404) feet northwesterly therefrom; providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of the easterly roadway on said 14th Avenue South and on 16th Avenue South and on East Marginal Way; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and for the making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting said avenues and way; providing for the condemnation of land and other property in fee simple, for general municipal purposes; and providing that the entire cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law; except as herein otherwise designated.
