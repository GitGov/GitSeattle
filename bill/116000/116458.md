 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of $12,407,797.86 on Summit mechanical warrants numbered 4001500308- 4001503092 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of $214,859,985.79 are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of $33,247,752.49 on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 51056030 - 51057472 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 70001 - 80962 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Feb.3, 2009 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Feb. 12, 2009 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 17th day of Feb. 2009 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Feb. 17, 2009.
