 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Ten Million, Eight Hundred Thirty Five Thousand, Five Hundred Ninety Four and 62/100 dollars ($10,835,594.62) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001291269- 4001293708, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Twenty Million, Seven Hundred Forty Six Thousand, Three Hundred Forty Nine and 01/100 dollars, ($20,746,349.01) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Nine Million, Six Hundred Thirty Eight Thousand, Nine Hundred Ninety Three and 82/100 dollars ($29,638,993.82) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50996974 - 50998635 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 470001 - 480400 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Nov. 13, 2007 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on November 26, 2007 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 26th day of Nov. 2007 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Nov. 26, 2007. ___________________________________ President ___________ of the City Council
