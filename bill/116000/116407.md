 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Nineteen Million, Seventy Three Thousand, Eight Hundred Ninety Three and03/100 dollars ($19,073,893.03) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001464776- 4001468361 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, plus Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) of Seven Million, Two Hundred Fourteen Thousand, Nine Hundred Ninety Three and 41/100 dollars ($7,214,993.41) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW.

 SECTION 2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 1st day of Dec. 2008 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Dec. 1, 2008.
