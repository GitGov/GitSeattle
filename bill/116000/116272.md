 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Eleven Million, Nine Hundred Fifty Three Thousand, Four Hundred Two and 33/100 dollars ($11,953,402.33) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001395695- 4001399055, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Eleven Million, Seven Hundred Twenty Five Thousand, Sixty One and 12/100 dollars, ($11,725,061.12) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Thirty Two Million, Thirty Thousand, Six Hundred Eighty and 14/100 dollars ($32,030,680.14) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 51028751- 51030936 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 290001 - 300841 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date July 8,2008 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on July 17, 2008 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 21st day of July 2008 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this July 21, 2008.
