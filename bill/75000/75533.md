**Council Bill Number: 75533**
   
**Ordinance Number: 84141**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 25th Avenue Northeast from approximately 175 feet north of East 45th Street to approximately 320 feet south of East 54th Street; and certain other streets, avenues and easements, by constructing sanitary and storm sewer systems therein, and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16668 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
