 An ordinance providing for the improvement of; Mayflower Street from 54th Avenue South to Wilson Avenue; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving with portland cement concrete and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16591 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
