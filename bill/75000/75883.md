**Council Bill Number: 75883**
   
**Ordinance Number: 84461**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, May Addition, being the alley between 15th Avenue Northeast and University Way from East 56th Street to Ravenna Boulevard; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 16731 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
