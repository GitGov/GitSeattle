**Council Bill Number: 75800**
   
**Ordinance Number: 84378**
********

 An ordinance authorizing the execution of a contract between the City and Water District No. 3 to provide for the transfer to the City of certain property of said District, the assumption by the City of all existing liabilities of the District, the furnishing by the City of water to that portion of the District outside of the City, and the continued existence of the District for certain purposes; and for the operation and maintenance by the City of a street lighting system in the District and the payment therefor by the District; and creating special "Seattle Water District #3 Fund".
