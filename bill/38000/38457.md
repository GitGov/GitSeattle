**Council Bill Number: 38457**
   
**Ordinance Number: 48516**
********

 An ordinance relating to a certain action at law instituted by the City of Seattle against the Great Northern Railway Company, John Davidson and C. Baker, for damages by reason of the loss, destruction and damaging of the West Wheeler Street Bridge in the City of Seattle by fire, and ratifying and confirming the action heretofore taken thereon by the Corporation Counsel in joining John Davidson and C. Baker as parties defendant therein, and further authorizing the Corporation Counsel to bring suit against any other parties necessary to a complete determination of the matters involved in said action at law.
