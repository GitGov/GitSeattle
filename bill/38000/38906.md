**Ordinance Number: 49003**

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal street railway system; providing for the construction and acquisition of, and payment for, certain street railway lines, property and equipment on 15th avenue Northwest and across Cowen Park; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; providing for the issuance and sale of warrants to provide funds therefor; and creating and establishing a construction fund, and creating a special fund to pay the principal and interest of said warrants.
