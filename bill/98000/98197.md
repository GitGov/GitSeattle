 AN ORDINANCE designating as a Landmark, the snagboat W.T. preston, United States Army, Corps of Engineers; specifying the particular features to be preserved in accordance with the Landmarks Preservation Ordinance (102229) while such vessel is within the City limits and subject to its jurisdiction, exempting such Landmark from the payment of certain fees, and requiring a written assurance of responsibility for salvage while such Landmark is in any submerged street area or waterway in this City.
