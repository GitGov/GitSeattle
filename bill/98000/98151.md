**Ordinance Number: 106236**

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 2, Buckius Addition, being the alley in the block bounded by 27th Ave., E. Pike St., Empire Way and E. Union St.; by paving the same; by doing such grading or regarding as may be necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 24993 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
