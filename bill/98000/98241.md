**Ordinance Number: 106330**

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of 36th Ave. N.E. from approximately 100 feet south of N.E. 57th St. to approximately 110 feet north of N.E. 60th St.; and certain other streets and an avenue; by constructing underground utilities lines and a residential street lighting system; all in accordance with Resolution No. 25425 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
