 AN ORDINANCE providing for submission to the qualified electors of the City at a special election to be held therein on September 20, 1977, of a proposition authorizing the City to levy an excess tax for one year only in the amount of $4,500,000, to be utilized for rehabilitation, maintenance and repairs of the Public Safety Building and contiguous plazas and facilities.
