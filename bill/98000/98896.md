**Council Bill Number: 98896**
   
**Ordinance Number: 106900**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of South Washington Street from Alaskan Way South to 1st Avenue South; by constructing and/or Reconstructing decorative sidewalks, concrete curbs and brick gutters, and appurtenances, reconstructing the street base and paving; constructing and/or reconstructing the support for the concrete walk to be constructed or reconstructed in the above named street; planting trees and installing tree guards and other miscellaneous construction connected therewith; providing for the necessary surface drainage and acquisition of any necessary right-of-way; all in accordance with Resolution No. 25611 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a Local Improvement District therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
