 AN ORDINANCE accepting the offer of Sunrise House/New Haven, a non-profit corporation, to convey to the City as a site for a Central Area Senior Center Lots 1, 2, 3, 4, 11, 12, 13 and 14, Block 21, Burke's Second Addition, for a cash consideration consisting of two-thirds of the appraised fair cash market value thereof; accepting a gift by the seller of one-third of the fair cash market value thereof; authorizing the preparation of plans, specifications and cost estimates for the renovation of said property, making an appropriation from the Emergency Fund and declaring the emergency therefor.
