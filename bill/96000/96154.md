**Council Bill Number: 96154**
   
**Ordinance Number: 104371**
********

 AN ORDINANCE ratifying and confirming execution of an agreement with the Municipality of Metropolitan Seattle in connection with provision by METRO of improved transit service to and from Seattle Central Freeway park, the Central Freeway Municipal Parking Garage, the Central Business District and other appropriate routes and points and providing for the construction by the City of Seattle of a pedestrian stairway from the park and garage to the 8th Avenue bridge and for payment by METRO to the City for the costs of preliminary engineering, project administration and construction costs, in a total amount not to exceed a maximum total amount of Twenty Thousand Dollars ($20,000).
