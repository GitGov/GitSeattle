**Council Bill Number: 96354**
   
**Ordinance Number: 104906**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the submission to the qualified electors (voters) of The City of Seattle at the general municipal election to be held therein on November 4, 1975 of the initiative measure contemplated by voters' petition (C.F. 281123) directing the construction of an eighteen hole golf course on certain property at Fort Lawton which is expected to be released to the City as surplus property by the United States of America; providing for certification of such initiative measure to the Director of the Department of Records and Elections of King County and prescribing the ballot title therefor.
