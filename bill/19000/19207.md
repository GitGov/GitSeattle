**Council Bill Number: 19207**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30760**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Bayview Street, from 31st Avenue South to 34th Avenue South; Lander Street from 31st Avenue South to 34th Avenue South; Mount Baker Circle, from Mount Baker Boulevard to 30th Avenue South; Hanford Street from Mount Baker Circle to 35th Avenue South; 31st Avenue South from McClellan Street to Mount Baker Boulevard; 32nd Avenue South from College Street to Mount Baker Boulevard; 33rd Avenue South and 34th Avenue South, each from Mount Baker Boulevard to Hanford Street; 35th Avenue South, from Mount Baker Boulevard to Hanford Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with concrete and armored concrete curbs, the construction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, and the construction of all necessary concrete walks, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3632, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
