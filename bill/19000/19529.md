**Ordinance Number: 31035**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Division Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to West 70th Street; 8th Avenue Northwest from West 70th Street to West 73rd Street; Dibble Avenue Northwest from West 65th Street to West 75th Street; 9th Avenue Northwest and 10th Avenue Northwest, each from West 70th Street to West 75th Street; 11th Avenue Northwest from West 65th Street to West 70th Street; 13th Avenue Northwest from West 70th Street to West 73rd Street; West 65th Street, from 8th Avenue Northwest to 15th Avenue Northwest; West 75th Street from 8th Avenue Northwest to 11th Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3709, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
