**Council Bill Number: 19875**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31376**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Dayton Avenue, from North 77th Street to North 85th Street; Woodland Park Avenue, from North 76th Street to North 79th Street; North 77th Street and North 77th Street produced easterly from three hundred fifty (350) feet west of Greenwood Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard; North 78th Street, from three hundred fifty (350) feet west of Greenwood Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; North 79th Street from Three hundred Eighty (380) feet west of Greenwood Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; North 80th Street, from three hundred feet (300) west of Greenwood Avenue to Aurora Avenue; North 81st Street, from three hundred feet (300) west of Greenwood Avenue to Aurora Avenue; North 82nd Street, from three hundred forty feet (340) west of Greenwood Avenue to three hundred feet (300) west of Aurora Avenue; North 83rd Street, from two hundred sixty (260) feet west of Aurora Avenue; North 84th Street, from Three hundred twenty (320) feet west of 1st Avenue Northwest, to three hundred feet (300) west of Aurora Street; all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 3913, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
