**Ordinance Number: 30796**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 1st Avenue Northwest and 3rd Avenue Northwest, each from West 80th Street to West 85th Street; 8th Avenue Northwest, from West 75th Street to West 85th Street; North and West 80th Street, North and West 81st Street, North and West 82nd Street, North and West 83rd Street, North and West 84th Street, North and West 85th Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to 8th Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3775, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
