**Council Bill Number: 19706**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31208**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2556, for the improvement of Charles Street and Norman Street, each from 23rd Avenue South to 26th Avenue South, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; with concrete and armored concrete curbs, brick gutters, and the construction and reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains, together with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection and providing for the necessary surface drainage.
