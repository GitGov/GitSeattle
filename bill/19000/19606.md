**Council Bill Number: 19606**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31113**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of College Street, from 31st Avenue South to 34th Avenue South; Dose Terrace from 33rd Avenue South to 34th Avenue South; Plum Street from 31st Avenue South to 34th Avenue South; and 32nd Avenue South, 33rd Avenue South and 34th Avenue South, each from Plum Street to College Street; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the same where necessary, by the construction and reconstruction of all water mains with their appurtenances, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3882, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
