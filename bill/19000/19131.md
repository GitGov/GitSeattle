**Council Bill Number: 19131**
   
**Ordinance Number: 30695**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 6th Avenue from Cherry Street to Madison Street; 7th Avenue from Columbia Street to Madison Street; Columbia Street from 5th Avenue to 7th Avenue; Marion Street from 5th Avenue to 8th Avenue all in the City of Seattle, by regrading the same and paving with brick or brick blocks or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation with granite or armored concrete curbs, and by constructing concrete sidewalks, providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3563, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
