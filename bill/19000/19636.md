**Ordinance Number: 31322**
********

 An ordinance authorizing and directing the Mayor and City Comptroller and ex-officio City Clerk in the name and on behalf of the City of Seattle to make, execute and deliver to the Pacific Coast Company, a corporation, a quit claim deed to the following described property: that part of the "Mackintosh" strip lying between lot 5, block 4, Maynard's Plat in the town (now city) of Seattle and the Government Meander Line more particular described as follows: beginning at the Southwest corner of said lot 5, thence south 16.801 feet to the Government Meander Line; thence easterly along said Meander Line 111 feet to the west marginal line of 1st Avenue South; thence north along said westerly marginal line of 1st Avenue South 17.971 feet to the south line of said lot 5; thence west along said south line of lot 3 111 feet to the place of beginning, all of which property is located and situated in Seattle, King County, State of Washington.
