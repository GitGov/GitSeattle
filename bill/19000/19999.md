**Council Bill Number: 19999**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31490**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2105, for the improvement of 13th Avenue South and 14th Avenue South, each from Lander Street to Lyons Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; and including as part of said improvement the construction of a wooden box out fall sewer along Hinds Street, from 13th Avenue South to 9th Avenue South and 9th Avenue South, from Hinds Street to Lander Street.
