**Council Bill Number: 13687**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25769**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-sixth Avenue Northwest, from West Fifty-sixth Street to West Sixty-second Street; and West Fifty-sixth Street, from Twenty-sixth Avenue Northwest to Thirty-second Avenue Northwest, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden crosswalks thereon and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2851 of the City Council of the City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
