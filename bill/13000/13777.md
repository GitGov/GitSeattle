**Council Bill Number: 13777**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25842**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Meridian Avenue and Kirkwood Place from East Green Lake Boulevard to Lawrence Street; Woodlawn Avenue from a point two hundred fifty (250) feet west of Kirkwood Place to Meridian Avenue; McKinley Place from North Fifty-seventh Street to Meridian Avenue; Keystone Place, from McKinley Pace to North Fifty-first Street; North Sixty-fifth Street from East Green Lake Boulevard to Sunnyside Avenue; North Sixty-fourth Street, North Sixty-third Street, North Sixty-second Street and North Sixty-first Street, each from First Avenue Northeast to Meridian Avenue; North and East Sixtieth Street and North and East Fifty-ninth Street, each from Latona Avenue to Meridian Avenue; North and East Fifty-eighth Street; North and East Fifty-seventh Street; North and East Fifty-sixth Street; North and East Fifty-fifth Street, and North and East Fifty-fourth Street, each from Latona Avenue to Kensington Place; East Fifty-third Street from Latona Avenue to First Avenue Northeast and Kensington Place and First Avenue Northeast from East Fifty-second Street to North Fifty-ninth Street, all in The City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials together with all wyes, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances for the proper drainage and sewerage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2969 of the City Council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
