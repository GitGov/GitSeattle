**Ordinance Number: 25897**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North and West Seventy-sixth Street, from Woodland Park Avenue to Eighth Avenue Northwest; North Seventy-seventh Street, from Woodland Park Avenue to Greenwood Avenue; Dayton Avenue, from North Seventy-fifth Street to North Seventy-seventh Street; Fremont Avenue, from North Seventy-fifth Street to North Seventy-eighth Street, all in The City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden crosswalks thereon, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2953 of the City Council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
