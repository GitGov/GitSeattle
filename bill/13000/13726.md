**Ordinance Number: 25838**

 An Ordinance approving the amended assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2282 for the improvement of Dexter Avenue, from the South margin of Roy Street Fourth Avenue North, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the same to the elevations established by Ordinance No. 17628, by making approaches where necessary to existing streets, by constructing curbs and gutters, by providing for the necessary surface drainage, and by constructing bulkheads where necessary; under Ordinance No. 23456, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
