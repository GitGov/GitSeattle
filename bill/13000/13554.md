**Council Bill Number: 13554**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25647**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2300 for the improvement of Lakewood Avenue, from Terminal Court to Cascadia Avenue, in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing brick, asphalt or concrete gutters and granite, armoured or concrete curbs, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 23748,and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
