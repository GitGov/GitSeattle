**Ordinance Number: 25703**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2284 for the improvement of Westlake Avenue North, from Mercer Street to Fremont Avenue, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in The City of Seattle, by the construction of grades, slopes, excavations and embankments; retaining walls, bulk heads and bridges; curbs and gutters; planking and replanking with necessary sidewalks and crosswalks; under Ordinance No. 23458, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, declaring the same to be a first lien thereon, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
