**Council Bill Number: 13628**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25704**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Ninth Avenue Northeast, from East Forty-fifth Street to four hundred (400) feet north of East Fiftieth Street; Tenth Avenue Northeast, from East Forty-fifth Street to East Fifty-second Street, all in The City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all wyes, openings, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2965 of the City Council of The City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
