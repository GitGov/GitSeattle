Michaelis, a deed to the following described property, to wit: Commencing at the Northwest corner of the West half (W 1/2) of the South 2/5 of the East Half (E 1/2) of the Southeast quarter (SE 1/4 of the Southwest quarter (SW 1/4) of Section 21, Township 25 North, Range 4 East, W. Michaelis of Record in Vol. 737, Page 598 Records of King County, Washington. Thence North 40o 57' 45" West 81.11 feet along said east boundary to the point and place of commencement.
