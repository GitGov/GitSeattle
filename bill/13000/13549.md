**Council Bill Number: 13549**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25642**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1981 for the improvement of Howe Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to Fourth Avenue North, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation and by constructing brick, asphalt or concrete gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, the construction of all sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; provided however, that where the grades exceed eight (8) per cent, the pavement may be of stone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation, under Ordinance No. 20386, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
