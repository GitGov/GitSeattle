**Council Bill Number: 13997**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26028**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2366, for the improvement of East Johns Street, in the City of Seattle, from Summit Avenue North to Bellevue Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick or asphalt gutters, armored concrete curbs, and including as part of said work, the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and by constructing concrete sidewalks, that where the grade exceeds 8% stone block pavement may be laid; under Ordinance No. 24674 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
