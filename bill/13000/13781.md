**Council Bill Number: 13781**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25844**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2290 for the improvement of East Pike Street, in The City of Seattle, from Melrose Avenue to Broadway, by widening the pavement so that the roadway shall have a total width of forty-eight (48) feet, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the necessary removal of all curbs, catch basins, trees, bulkheads, retaining walls and concrete sidewalks; and by constructing and reconstructing all pavements, curbs, gutters, catch basins, concrete sidewalks, side sewers and fire hydrants, in a manner to make a complete and finished improvement; under Ordinance No. 23595, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
