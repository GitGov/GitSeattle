**Council Bill Number: 13032**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25363**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 32nd Avenue North from East John Street to East Harrison Street, and from East Republican Street to East Mercer Street; 33rd Avenue North from East Thomas Street to Washington Boulevard; 34th Avenue North from East Thomas Street to East Harrison Street; 36th Avenue North, from a point 150 feet south of the south margin of East Thomas Street to East Republican Street; and from Washington Boulevard to East Mercer Street; 37th Avenue North, from East Thomas Street to East Harrison Street; East Harrison Street, from 31st Avenue North to 32nd Avenue North, and from 33rd Avenue North to 37th Avenue North; East Republican Street from 31st Avenue North to 36th Avenue North; Ford Place, from 36th Avenue North to Washington Boulevard; and Washington Boulevard from East Harrison Street to East Mercer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2844, of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
