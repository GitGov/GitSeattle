**Council Bill Number: 13287**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26257**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Beacon Avenue from 14th Avenue South to Hanford Street; Hanford Street from Beacon Avenue to 16th Avenue South form the north, 13th Avenue South from Norman Street to College Street; 14th Avenue South, Judkins Street, Atlantic Street and Massachusetts Street, each from 13th Avenue South to 14th Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt and other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with concrete or armored concrete curbs; including as part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2934 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
