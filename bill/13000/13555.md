**Ordinance Number: 26260**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 8th Avenue Northeast, from East 55th Street to East 65th Street; the north half of East 55th Street form 5th Avenue Northeast to 9th Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, by constructing cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2954 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
