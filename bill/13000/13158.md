**Council Bill Number: 13158**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25372**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2299, for the improvement of West 59th Street, form 24th Avenue Northwest to 32nd Avenue Northwest; and 32nd Avenue Northwest, from West 59th Street to West 65th Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners, providing for the necessary surface drainage,, including all grading, the altering, repairing and extending of concrete walks where necessary, together with the construction of all sewers with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 23747 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
