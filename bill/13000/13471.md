**Council Bill Number: 13471**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25560**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2278 for the improvement of James Street, from Sixth Avenue to Ninth Avenue, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in The City of Seattle., by paving the same with brick and stone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing granite curbs and concrete sidewalks, and including as a part of said work, all necessary sewer and side sewer connections, the construction, alteration and extension of water mains, fire hydrants and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 23445, and levying and assessing the amount thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
