**Ordinance Number: 25981**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2305 for the improvement of Howard Avenue North and Minor Avenue North as condemned by Ordinance No. 18905, each from Roanoke Street, to the North line of Green's Addition, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, by constructing such sewers and watermains and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 23887, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
