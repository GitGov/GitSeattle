**Ordinance Number: 25847**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2374 for the improvement of Fifteenth Avenue West, from the South margin of Ruffner Street to Thorndyke Avenue, and Thorndyke Avenue, from the westerly margin of Fifteenth Avenue West produced, to the easterly margin of Thirteenth Avenue West on the north, all in The City of Seattle, by planking and replanking the same to a width of eighteenth (18) feet, by doing such grading as may be necessary to property lay such planking and the necessary temporary wood walks; and by making necessary provisions to care for the surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 24682 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
