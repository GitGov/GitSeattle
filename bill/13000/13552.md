**Council Bill Number: 13552**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25645**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1952 for the improvement of Pine Street, in The City of Seattle, from First Avenue to Westlake Avenue, in the following manner, to wit, by the alteration, extension and reconstruction of the asphalt pavement, changing curbs, the alteration and extension of concrete walks, the relaying of brick gutters, and the moving of all catch basins and inlets, where necessary, to conform to the new width of said street as widened by Ordinance No. 14500; under Ordinance No. 20173, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
