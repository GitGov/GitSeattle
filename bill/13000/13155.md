**Ordinance Number: 25369**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2271, for the improvement of 20th Avenue South, from Yesler Way to Charles Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with asphalt or brick gutters with armored concrete curbs, armored concrete or granite corners and the construction of all sewers, side sewers, all water mains, gates, hydrants, crosses and tees, under Ordinance No. 23438 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
