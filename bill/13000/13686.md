**Council Bill Number: 13686**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25768**
********

 An Ordinance approving the amended assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2304 for the improvement of Ninth Avenue and Ninth Avenue North, from Westlake Avenue to Westlake Avenue North, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the same to the elevations established by Ordinance No. 25158 and making approaches where necessary to existing streets, said approaches not to exceed a distance of two hundred fifty-six (256) feet, by making all necessary provisions to care for sewers and watermains and surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 23886, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
