**Council Bill Number: 36667**
   
**Ordinance Number: 46859**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of a Public Alley, over and across Block Nineteen (19), Laurelhurst Heights, an Addition to The City of Seattle, from the northeasterly line of Lot One (1), said block, to the south line of Lot Eighteen (18), said block, providing for the establishing of the grades of said alley; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of the above described alley and for the establishing of the grades, and necessary for the making of the slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said alley; and providing that the entire cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
