**Council Bill Number: 36244**
   
**Ordinance Number: 46794**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 30th Avenue Northeast from Blakely Street to East 55th Street; of East 53rd Street, between 29th Avenue Northeast and 30th Avenue Northeast, and of the alley between East 53rd Street and East 54th Street, from 30th Avenue Northeast to a point approximately 100 feet west thereof; providing for the establishing of the curb grades of said 30th Avenue Northeast and of East 53rd Street from 27th Avenue Northeast to 30th Avenue Northeast; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described avenue, street and alley, and for the establishing of the grades and for making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenue and street, and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property especially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
