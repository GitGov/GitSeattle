**Council Bill Number: 36019**
   
**Ordinance Number: 46251**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Duwamish Waterway, from 8th Avenue South to West Spokane Street, by removing sand bars therefrom and doing such other work incidental thereto as may be necessary; declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be; creating a special trust fund for the purpose of paying the cost thereof; and authorizing the City Comptroller to collect from King County its proportion of the cost of said work, and declaring an emergency.
