**Council Bill Number: 36390**
   
**Ordinance Number: 46592**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 8th Avenue West, from West Fulton Street to West Barrett Street; providing for the establishing of the curb grades of said 8th Avenue West from West Fulton Street to West Barrett Street; and of West Barrett Street from 7th Avenue West to 8th Avenue West; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described avenue and necessary for the establishing of the grades and for the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting on said 8th Avenue West and said West Barrett Street; and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
