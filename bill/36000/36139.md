**Council Bill Number: 36139**
   
**Ordinance Number: 46352**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Fidalgo Street, from East Marginal Way, as established by Ordinance No. 32881, westerly to an intersection with the Government meander line; and of a public street and highway from Fidalgo Street to an intersection with East Marginal Way, as established by Ordinance No. 32881, near the intersection of said East Marginal Way with a public street and alley established by Ordinance No. 43273; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property therefor; providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
