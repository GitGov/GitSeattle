**Ordinance Number: 46316**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Dayton Avenue, from North 70th Street as established by Ordinance No. 42799, to North 68th Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described Dayton Avenue and for the establishing of grades and for the making of necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting on said Dayton Avenue; providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
