**Council Bill Number: 37841**
   
**Ordinance Number: 47942**
********

 An ordinance relating to the municipal light and power plant and system; providing for the purchase, pursuant to Ordinances Nos. 39069 and 44154, of the North Seattle and Fremont Substations of the Puget Sound Power and Light Company, as additions and betterments thereto, and extensions thereof, as contemplated by Ordinance No. 42495; providing for the payment therefor by an appropriation from Municipal Light Extension Bond, 1921, Construction Fund; directing the Light Department to furnish five thousand (5000) kilowatts of power through said substations to the municipal street railway system at the same rates as specified in contract executed pursuant to Ordinance No. 39069; and declaring an emergency.
