**Council Bill Number: 37762**
   
**Ordinance Number: 47881**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for, the collection and removal of tin cans from the garbage dumps and sanitary fills maintained by the City of Seattle; authorizing and directing the Board of Public Works to enter into a contract selling all tin cans which may be deposited upon the city garbage dumps and sanitary fills for a period of ten (10) years from the effective date of this ordinance; authorizing the Commissioner of Health to prescribe rules and regulations with reference thereto; defining offenses; providing penalties and repealing all ordinances, or parts of ordinances, in conflict herewith.
