**Council Bill Number: 37514**
   
**Ordinance Number: 47646**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 33rd Avenue South, between Brandon Street and Dawson Street; and Dawson Street, from 32nd Avenue South to Empire Way; providing for the establishing of the curb grades of 33rd Avenue South, from Brandon Street to Dawson Street, and of Dawson Street, from 32nd Avenue South to Empire Way; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of the above described avenues and street; and for the the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting said street and avenue, and providing that the entire cost of improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
