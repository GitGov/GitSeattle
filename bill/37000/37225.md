**Ordinance Number: 47380**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the purchase of certain real estate for park play and recreation grounds; providing for the payment therefor by an appropriation from the General Fund; and (such purpose not having been specified in any tax levy or official estimate) declaring the emergency making necessary such appropriation; appropriation $30,000.00 from the Water Bonds Sinking Fund to the General Fund as a temporary loan in aid of said purchase; and providing for the repayment thereof.
