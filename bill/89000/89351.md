 AN ORDINANCE providing for and authorizing the issuance and sale of general negotiable interest bearing serial coupon bonds in the amount of not to exceed $8,000,000 for the purpose of acquisition of any necessary proerty and property rights for and construction of a parking garage facility or facilities, or other capital and permanent municipal improvements on or adjacent to the Seattle Center site; providing for the levy and collection of taxes for the payment of the principal of and interest on said bonds; creating and establishing a construction fund and pledging a specified portion of the permissible property tax levy in connection therewith.
