**Ordinance Number: 98146**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Engineering Department; providing for the improvement of Iowa Avenue Southwest from Southwest Spokane Street to Southwest Dakota Street, of West Marginal Way Southwest from Southwest Dakota Street to Southwest Hudson Street, West Marginal Way Southwest from Highland Park Way Southwest to First Avenue South and of approaches to West Marginal Way Southwest over Highland Park Way Southwest and Southwest Michigan Street, by widening the paved section, acquisition of the necessary additional right of way, construction of necessary drainage and drainage out-falls, revision of street channelization, the traffic signal system, and the street lighting system, and landscaping, and making an appropriation from the City Street Fund and a reimbursable appropriation from the Arterial City Street Fund.
