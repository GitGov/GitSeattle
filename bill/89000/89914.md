**Council Bill Number: 89914**
   
**Ordinance Number: 98349**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to and extensions of the existing municipal light and power system, declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be and providing for the issuance and sale of utility revenue bonds to provide funds therefor in the amount of not to exceed $17,000,000 to finance in part such plan or system; designating the bond redemption fund for the payment of the principal of and interest on such bonds and revenue bonds hereafter issued on a parity therewith; and providing for the issuance of said bonds on a parity with those issued under Ordinance 86865, Ordinance 92939, Ordinance 96510, Ordinance 98286, and like bonds hereafter authorized, as contemplated by Chapter 35.92, Revised Code of Washington.
