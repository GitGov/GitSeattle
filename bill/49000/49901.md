**Council Bill Number: 49901**
   
**Ordinance Number: 59502**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for, the separation of grades for travel over Duwamish Avenue at Albro Place; directing certain railroad companies at their own cost and expense to construct at said point an overhead bridge or viaduct over the tracks of said railroad companies, with necessary approaches and landings to accommodate said travel, or in the alternative to contribute the sum of One Hundred Thousand ($100,000.00) Dollars toward the cost of construction thereof, and appropriation said cost or said contribution among said railroad companies.
