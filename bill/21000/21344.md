**Council Bill Number: 21344**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32678**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2690, for the improvement of Westlake Avenue North from Mercer Street to Florentia Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving a 20-foot roadway along the westerly portion thereof with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; together with armored concrete curbs; providing for the necessary surface drainage; by laying such temporary planking as may be necessary between Mercer Street and Galer Street; and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith.
