**Ordinance Number: 33119**
********

 An Ordinance submitting to the qualified voters of The City of Seattle at a special election to be held in said city on the 30th day of June, 1914, the proposition of the ratification and adoption of the Charter prepared and proposed by the Freeholders elected March 3, 1914, and filed with the City Clerk May 2, 1914, and also submi9tting for ratification and adopting at said election an alternate article and proposition to be voted on separately relating to granting franchise for street car extensions in the residence districts when not upon trunk lines or canyon routes, without including in such franchises certain provisions required in the Freeholders Charter for granting franchises.
