**Council Bill Number: 21460**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32804**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2617, for the improvement of Rainier Avenue from Angeline Street to Mead Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, including as part of said improvement a wooden box sewer from 37th Avenue South and Angeline Street to a temporary outfall in Lake Washington.
