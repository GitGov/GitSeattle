**Council Bill Number: 21033**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32369**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified voters of the City of Seattle, at the general election to be held in such city on Tuesday, the 3rd day of March, 1914, of the question or proposition of incurring an indebtedness of Two Hundred Fifty Thousand ($250,000) Dollars, and of authorizing the issuance and sale of negotiable bonds of said city in the amount of Two Hundred Fifty Thousand ($250,000) Dollars for the purpose of acquiring, by purchase or by condemnation and purchase, lands and other property for a storage reservoir and a site for the development of water power and other power derived therefrom at Lake Cushman, Mason County, Washington, and for all necessary rights of way, as provided for and specified in Ordinance No. 32321, and of authorizing said Two Hundred Fifty Thousand ($250,000) Dollars of bonds, or so much thereof as may be necessary, to take the place of a like amount of bonds of the unsold portion of the One Million ($1,000,000) Dollars bond issue for the "Cedar River Water Supply System" authorized by Ordinance No. 27499, and by the vote of the people at the special election held on the 5th day of September, 1911, and providing for the for of ballot to be used at said election, and for the payment of the principal and interest of said bonds.
