**Council Bill Number: 21967**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33256**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fairview Avenue, from Denny Way to Virginia Street as extended and established by Ordinance No. 21766, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs; by the alteration, construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks; by such grading as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4365 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
