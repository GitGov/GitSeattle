**Council Bill Number: 21563**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32852**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 25394, creating Local Improvement District No. 2425, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 34, Pontius' Addition to the City of Seattle, being the alley from Eat Thomas Street to East Harrison Street, between Melrose Avenue North and Bellevue Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and providing for the construction of a sewers and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2906, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds', as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle", approved October 20, 1910, and published October 25, 1910.
