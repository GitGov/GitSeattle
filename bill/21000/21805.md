**Council Bill Number: 21805**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33110**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Waverly Place, from the north line of Westlake Boulevard Addition to Newton Street; Eloise Terrace, from McGraw Street to the south line of Westlake Boulevard Addition; Newton Street, from Eloise Terrace to Dexter Avenue; Dexter Avenue from Fourth Avenue North to Galer Street; Howe Street produced, from Dexter Avenue to Rex Street, Rex Street; from Howe Street produced to Seventh Avenue North, Seventh Avenue North, from Rex Street to the north line of Crockett Street; the alley between Blocks Seven (7) and Eight (8), Union Lake Supplemental Addition, from the north line of said addition to Garfield Street; and Garfield Street, from the alley between Blocks Seven (7) and Eight (8), Union Lake Supplemental Addition to Dexter Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein sewers by the proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and all other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4247 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
