**Council Bill Number: 21049**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32383**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2681, for the improvement of 34th Avenue North, from East Denny Way to the south line of Coulter's Addition; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation under, together with brick gutters and concrete or armored concrete curbs, and the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains, together with their appurtenances, as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, Ordinance No. 31558 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
