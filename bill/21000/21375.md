**Council Bill Number: 21375**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32703**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2711, for the improvement of Queen Anne Boulevard, as established by Ordinance No. 16790, from Galer Street to a connection with the existing sewer in 4th Avenue North, between Wheeler Street and Smith Street, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, by constructing side sewers, under Ordinance No. 31894 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
