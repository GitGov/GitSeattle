**Council Bill Number: 21789**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33083**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 31106, being "An Ordinance providing for the laying off, widening, extending, altering and establishing of West Spokane Street, from Alki Avenue to Iowa Street; of Alki Avenue, from West Spokane Street to West Hinds Street; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of said West Spokane Street, between First Avenue South and Alki Avenue; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said street and approaches thereto, in conformity with such established grades for cuts or fills on the property abutting upon said street and approaches thereto; and providing that the entire cost of said improvement be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law," passed by the City Council April 7th, 1913, approved April 11th, 1913 and published April 15th, 1913.
