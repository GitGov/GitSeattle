**Ordinance Number: 32913**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Leary Avenue, from 3rd Avenue Northwest to 6th Avenue Northwest; 6th Avenue Northwest from Leary Avenue to West 43rd Street; West 41st Street, from Leary Avenue to 3rd Avenue Northwest; 3rd Avenue Northwest, from West Kilbourne Street to West 39th Street, West Kilbourne Street, and Leary Avenue, from 3rd Avenue Northwest to 1st Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers and reconstructing existing sewers where necessary, by constructing a connection with the North Trunk Sewer, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4266, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
