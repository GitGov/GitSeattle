**Ordinance Number: 33033**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 30585, creating Local Improvement District No. 2618, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Boylston Avenue North, from Lakeview Boulevard to East Newton Street; East Blaine Street, and East Howe Street, each from Franklin Avenue to Lakeview Boulevard, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage; by constructing wooden stairways and concrete retaining walls where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3540 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds", approved December 31st, 1912, and published January 6th, 1913.
