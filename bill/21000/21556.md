**Council Bill Number: 21556**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32845**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 26516, creating Local Improvement District No. 1461, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Railroad Avenue from the south margin of University Street from the west to the south margin of Wet Massachusetts Street, all in the City of Seattle, in the following manner, the improvement of the westerly portion of this street by filling, planking and replanking, by the construction of wooden curbs and gutters, wood walks and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3075, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds', as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle", approved February 28, 1911, and published March 7, 1911.
