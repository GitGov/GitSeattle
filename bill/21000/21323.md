**Council Bill Number: 21323**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32673**
********

 An ordinance relating to weighing, measuring and inspecting all commodities sold or offered for sale by weight or measure within the City of Seattle; to enforce the keeping of proper legal weights and measures by all vendors in the City; to provide for the inspection thereof, and the issuance of certificates therefor; and providing penalties for violation thereof, and repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
