**Ordinance Number: 33031**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 31641, creating Local Improvement District No. 2692, entitled: "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Courtland Place from Dakota Street to Andover Street; Thirty-seventh Avenue South from Genesee Street to Court Street; Thirty-ninth Avenue South and Fortieth Avenue South, each from Dakota Street to Andover Street; Forty-first Avenue South from Genesee Street to Andover Street; and Dakota Street from Rainier Avenue to Forty-third Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3949 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a Local Improvement District therefor and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district, payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," approved August 5, 1913, and published August 12, 1913.
