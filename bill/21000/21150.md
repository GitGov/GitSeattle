**Ordinance Number: 32470**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 30th Avenue South, from Norman Street to Dearborn Street; and Dearborn Street, from 30th Avenue South on the south to 30th Avenue South on the north, all in the City of Seattle, by planking the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4218, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
