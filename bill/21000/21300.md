**Council Bill Number: 21300**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32631**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2283, for the improvement of Alki Avenue, from the south margin of West Spokane Street to the south margin of West Atlantic Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and filling the same to a width not less than 30 feet, making all necessary slopes for excavations and embankments, by making all necessary approaches thereto, by constructing all necessary bulkheads and retaining walls, by the construction of curbs and gutters where necessary, by planking the roadway and by constructing wooden sidewalks thereon, and providing for the necessary surface drainage.
