**Ordinance Number: 32801**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2697, for the improvement of the alleys in block 18, Capitol Hill Addition, Division No. 2, and blocks 27, and 30, Capitol Hill Addition, Division No. 4, being the alleys between 16th Avenue North and 17th Avenue North, from East Aloha Street to East Galer Street; by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, providing for the necessary surface drainage, by doing such grading and regrading as may be necessary under Ordinance No. 31667 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
