**Council Bill Number: 21567**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32856**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 21369, creating Local Improvement District No. 2109, entitled; "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 3, Capitol Hill Addition to the City of Seattle, Division No. 1, being the alley from East Roy Street to East Aloha Street, and between 12th Avenue North and 13th Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by grading the same, providing for the necessary surface drainage, and by making the necessary connection to the existing improvements, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2179, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds' as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle", approved July 10, 1909 and published July 16, 1909.
