**Council Bill Number: 21564**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32853**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 23598, creating Local Improvement District No. 2293, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alleys in blocks 34, 33 and 23, Pontius' Addition to the City of Seattle; being the alleys from Eat Thomas Street to East Mercer Street, between Bellevue Avenue North and Melrose Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same where necessary and paving with concrete and asphalt; all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 39276; and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds' as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle", approved March 18, 1910 and published March 26, 1910.
