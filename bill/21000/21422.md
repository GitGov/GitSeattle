**Ordinance Number: 32730**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 6th Avenue South, from Holgate Street to Charlestown Street and Charlestown Street, from 6th Avenue South to 8th Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by construction of an asphalt top on existing bridge roadway, and including the relaying and repairing of such of the existing planking and sub-structure as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4170, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
