**Council Bill Number: 21130**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32471**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified voters of the City of Seattle, at the general election to be held in such city on Tuesday, March 3, 1914, the proposition of the issuance and sale by said city, of its general bonds in the sum of one million, eight hundred thirty thousand ($1,830,000) Dollars for the purpose of providing money for the construction of a system of bridges for the City of Seattle across the Government canal, Lake Union, Salmon Bay Waterway, West Waterway and the Duwamish River.

**Status:** Passed
