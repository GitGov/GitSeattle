**Council Bill Number: 21580**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32914**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley between 11th Avenue South and 12th Avenue South, from Atlantic Street to Massachusetts Street, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the necessary grading and providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 53138, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
