**Ordinance Number: 32578**

 An ordinance relating to the construction and maintenance of an area way under or beneath any public place, providing for a permit so to do, providing penalties for the violation thereof, and repealing Sections 6, 6 and 7, of Ordinance No. 16081, entitled: "An ordinance regulating the use and occupation of and the conduct of persons in or upon streets, avenues, ways, boulevards, park drives, park paths, and public places and wharves, station grounds and rights of way, open to the use of the public, and the space above or beneath the surface of the same, and providing for the control of the same, and for the safety, comfort, and convenience of the public in the use of the same and providing penalties for violations thereof", approved May 31, 1907, and Ordinance No. 26560 amendatory thereto.
