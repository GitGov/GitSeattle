**Council Bill Number: 21803**
   
**Ordinance Number: 33088**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Howe Street, from East Lynn Street to East Blaine Street, and Twenty-second Avenue North, from East Howe Street to East Newton Street, by paving the same with concrete pavement for the full width of East Howe Street and for a width of eighteen (18) feet on Twenty-second Avenue North; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by providing for the necessary surface drainage; by constructing sewers and side-sewers where necessary, and by and by making all addition and alterations necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; by constructing concrete sidewalks on the easterly side of East Blaine Street, from the intersection of East Howe Street to Boyer Avenue, and on the northeasterly side of Boyer Avenue, from Twenty-third Avenue North to Nineteenth Avenue North, together with such wooden cross-walks as may be necessary, and that such other work be done as may be necessary in connection therewith, all in accordance with plans to be prepared by the City Engineer; all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 53469, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
