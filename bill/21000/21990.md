**Ordinance Number: 33286**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 30864 entitled: "An Ordinance providing for the laying off, widening, altering, extending and establishing of Western Avenue form Eagle Street to Fourth Avenue West, and Elliott Avenue; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of said Western Avenue as widened herein from Eagle Street to Fourth Avenue West and Elliott Avenue; of Denny Way and West Denny Way, from First Avenue North to Western Avenue; of Bay Street, from First Avenue to Western Avenue; of Queen Anne Avenue, from Jacobs Street to Denny Way; of First Avenue West, from West Thomas Street to Western Avenue; of Second Avenue West, from West Thomas Street to Western Avenue, to Third Avenue West, from West Harrison Street to Western Avenue; West Johns Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to Western Avenue, of West Thomas Street, from Second Avenue West to Western Avenue, providing for the grading and regrading of said streets and avenues, and approaches thereto, in conformity with such established grades, and providing that the entire cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provide by law.", approved February 26, 1913.
