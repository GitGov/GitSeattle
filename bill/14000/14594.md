**Council Bill Number: 14594**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26518**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Maynard Avenue from Weller Street to Plummer Street; Dearborn Street from Sixth Avenue South to Maynard Avenue; Charles Street from Seattle Boulevard to Maynard Avenue; Seattle Boulevard from Sixth Avenue South from the south to Maynard Avenue and approaches thereto, all in The City of Seattle; by relaying all existing planking that is suitable; by relaying wood walks; by constructing wood curbs and gutters; by furnishing any new lumber necessary and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3083 of the city council of The City of Seattle, and declaring an emergency; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by law of the State of Washington and the charter and ordinances of the City of Seattle.
