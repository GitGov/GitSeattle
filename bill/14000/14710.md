**Council Bill Number: 14710**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26814**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-sixth Avenue North from Helen Street to East Galer Street; Twenty-seventh Avenue North from the south line of Bard-Foster Washington Park Addition to Bard Avenue, and from East Highland Drive to East Galer Street; Twenty-eighth Avenue North from Helen Street to East Highland Drive; Foster Place from South line of Bard-Foster Washington Park Addition to the East Highland Drive; Bard Avenue from Twenty-sixth Avenue North to Twenty-eighth Avenue North; Moran Place from Twenty-eighth Avenue North to Foster Place; East Prospect Street from Twenty-fifth Avenue North to Twenty-sixth Avenue North; East Highland Drive from Twenty-fifth Avenue North to Foster Place; East Lee Street from Twenty-fifth Avenue North to Twenty-seventh Avenue North; East Galer Street from Twenty-sixth Avenue North to Twenty-seventh Avenue North, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 3095 of the city council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
