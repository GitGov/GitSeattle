 An ordinance declaring the advisability of the city electric railway on Rainier Avenue and other streets, avenues, and ways, and providing for the same, specifying and adopting the system of plan proposed, declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be, and providing for the submission of such system or plan and the incurring of an indebtedness therefor to the qualified voters of the City of Seattle, for their adoption and assent thereto, or for the rejection thereof, at a special election to be held on the day of the general city election on the 7th day of March, 1911.
