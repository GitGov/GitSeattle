**Council Bill Number: 14425**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26440**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 5th Avenue and 5th Avenue South, from Main Street to Madison Street; Washington Street from 4th Avenue South to 5th Avenue South; Terrace Street, Jefferson Street, Columbia Street and Marion Street, each from 4th Avenue to 5th Avenue; James Street from 4th Avenue to 6th Avenue; Cherry Street, from 4th Avenue to 7th Avenue; 6th Avenue from Cherry Street to James Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with concrete or granite curbs; by constructing concrete sidewalks, together with all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3076, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
