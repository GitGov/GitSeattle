**Council Bill Number: 14707**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26705**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2424 for the improvement of the Alley in Block Forty-two (42), D. S. Maynard's Plat, being the alley between Jackson Street and Main Street and Maynard Avenue and Sixth Avenue South, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with the construction of such sewers and side sewers as may be necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; under Ordinance No. 25393, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
