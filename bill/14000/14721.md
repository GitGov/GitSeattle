**Ordinance Number: 26831**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Sixth Avenue West, from West Dravus Street to Nickerson Street; West Dravus Street and West Dravus Street and Fifth Avenue West as widened and extended under Ordinance No. 25451, from Fifth Avenue West to Seventh Avenue West; West Cremona Street from Sixth Avenue West to Fifth Avenue West; and West Bertona Street from Sixth Avenue West to Fifth Avenue West; all in The City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and by providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2997 of the city council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
