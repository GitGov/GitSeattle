**Ordinance Number: 26603**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2385 for the improvement of Walnut Avenue, from West Charlestown Street to West Lander Street; Fortieth Avenue Southwest; Forty-fist Avenue Southwest and Forty-second Avenue Southwest, each from West Charlestown Street to West Hanford Street; Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest and Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest; each from West Hanford Street to West Lander Street; Manning Street, from California Avenue to Forty-first Avenue Southwest, and from Walnut Avenue to Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest; West Hanford Street, from Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest to Thirty-ninth Avenue Southwest; and West Stevens Street, from Thirty-sixth Avenue Southwest to California Avenue, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 24845, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
