**Council Bill Number: 14702**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26702**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2285 for the improvement of Thirty-first Avenue, from East Cherry Street to Yesler Way, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with asphalt, brick or stone block gutters, with concrete or armoured concrete curbs, armoured concrete or granite corners, concrete walks, and the construction of all sewers, side sewers, water mains, gates, hydrants, crosses and tees necessary; under Ordinance No. 23482, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
