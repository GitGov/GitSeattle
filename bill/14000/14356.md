**Council Bill Number: 14356**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26812**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Lakeview Boulevard from Nelson Place to East Newton Street, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with brick or asphalt gutters, concrete or armoured concrete curbs, and providing for the necessary grading, and the construction of concrete walks; for the necessary sewerage and drainage of said district, and including such alterations of watermains with their appurtenances, as may be necessary for proper water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3073 of the City Council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.

**Status:** Passed
