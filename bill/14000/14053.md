**Council Bill Number: 14053**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26067**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2327, for the improvement of 5th Avenue and 5th Avenue South, from Main Street to Madison Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same and making all necessary approaches to streets and alleys; by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, by the construction of a steel and concrete bridge on Yesler Way over 5th Avenue South, by the construction of retaining walls and abutments, concrete walks and stairways; by the construction of temporary wood walks, stairways and foot bridges; by planking and replanking; under Ordinance No. 24037 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
