**Council Bill Number: 14132**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26158**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2368, for the improvement of the westerly side of 4th Avenue, from Pike Street to Pine Street, all in the City of Seattle,, by widening the same to the width established by Ordinance No. 13776, by doing all necessary grading, by reconstructing and adjusting granite curbs and brick gutters; by extending the concrete walks and the asphalt pavement to conform to the new width of street; by all necessary adjustment of the drainage and water systems; under Ordinance No. 24676 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
