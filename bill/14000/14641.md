**Council Bill Number: 14641**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26602**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2270 for the improvement of East Jefferson Street, from Twenty-third Avenue to Twenty-seventh Avenue; Twenty-fourth, from East Alder Street to East Jefferson Street; Twenty-fifth Avenue and Twenty-sixth Avenue, each from East Alder Street to East Cherry Street, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with asphalt or brick gutters, with armoured concrete or concrete curbs, armoured concrete or granite corners, also concrete walks where necessary, and the construction of all sewers, side sewers, all water mains, gates, hydrants, crosses and tees necessary; under Ordinance No. 23437, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and other part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
