**Council Bill Number: 14700**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26700**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2355 for the improvement of Fourth Avenue, from Virginia Street to Wall Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by construction brick gutters and granite curbs, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration of extension of all sewers and watermains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and by the construction of concrete sidewalks; under Ordinance No. 24405, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
