**Council Bill Number: 14728**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26724**
********

 An Ordinance directing the Superintendent of The Lighting Department and the Superintendent of the Water Department of the City of Seattle to issue and mail to all light and water consumers monthly bill, having attached a perforated stub each for the City Comptroller and City Treasurer, for light and water consumed, and authorizing the City Treasurer to receipt for payment on such bills accordingly for the purpose of eliminating congestion in the Treasurer's Collection Department, and expending the method of payment.
