**Council Bill Number: 14593**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26515**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2241 for the improvement of East Highland Drive, from Twenty-second Avenue North to Twenty-third Avenue North, and Twenty-third Avenue North from East Prospect Street to the alley between Blocks Fifty-seven and Fifty-eight; Capitol Hill Addition Number five Supplemental, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction alteration or extension of concrete walks and of sewers and watermains with all their necessary appurtenances; provided, however, that where the grade exceeds eight (8) per cent, stone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation may be substituted for asphalt; under Ordinance No. 23078, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
