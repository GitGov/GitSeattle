**Council Bill Number: 14596**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26828**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Grand Boulevard from Twenty-ninth Avenue West to a point one hundred (100) feet west of Thirty-eighth Avenue West; Thirty-fourth Avenue West and Thirty-fifth Avenue West, each from Grand Boulevard to Elmore Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing watermains therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all gates, tees, crosses, and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3092 of the city council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinance of the City of Seattle.
