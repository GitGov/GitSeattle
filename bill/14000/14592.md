 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No.2340 for the improvement of Stewart Street, from First Avenue to Westlake Avenue; Olive Street, from Fourth Avenue to Westlake Avenue; Virginia Street, from the Alley between Third Avenue and Fourth Avenue to Westlake Avenue; Fourth Avenue, from Pine Street to Virginia Street; Firth Avenue and Sixth Avenue, each from Westlake Avenue to Virginia Street, all in The City of Seattle, by the installation of a system of cluster lights along the curb line of each of said streets and avenues, together with the installation of such poles and conduits and wires as may be necessary for the proper installation and equipment of said system; under Ordinance No. 24282, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land apart thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
