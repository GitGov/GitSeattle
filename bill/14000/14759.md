**Council Bill Number: 14759**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26747**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance Number 24851, entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Forty-fifth Street, from Fifteenth Avenue Northeast to Twentieth Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, by paying the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by construction concrete curbs with armoured concrete or granite corners, by doing all necessary grading, and by constructing concrete walks where necessary, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of such sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances, as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2833 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle," approved August 17th, 1910.
