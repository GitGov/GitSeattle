**Council Bill Number: 14709**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26707**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2280 for the improvement of Western Avenue, from Yesler Way to Seneca Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick or granite blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with granite curbs, together with all necessary sewers, side sewers, watermains and appurtenances necessary for the proper fire protection; the construction of all concrete walks and concrete walls necessary; under Ordinance No. 23447, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
