**Council Bill Number: 14131**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26157**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2337, for the improvement of 9th Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Howell Street to Virginia Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing granite curbs and side sewer connections where necessary, together with such sewers and water mains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, under Ordinance No. 24263 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
