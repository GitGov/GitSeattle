**Council Bill Number: 14873**
   
**Ordinance Number: 26892**
********

 An Ordinance submitting to the qualified voters of the City of Seattle, at a special election to be held in said city on Tuesday, the 5th day of September, 1911, the proposition of the issuance and sale, by said city, of its negotiable bonds in the sum of Five Hundred Thousand ($500,000) Dollars for the purpose of acquiring, by purchase or condemnation, a site for a museum of arts and sciences, and for clearing, grading and preparing said site for the construction thereon of a building to be known as "Seattle Museum of Arts and Science", and providing for the payment of the principal and interest of said bonds.
