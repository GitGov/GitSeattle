**Council Bill Number: 100716**
   
**Ordinance Number: 108568**
********

 AN ORDINANCE provides for the improvement of Lawton Wood Sanitary Sewer System to be constructed in easement to be acquired in Government Lot 7, Section 10, Township 25 North, Range 3 East, W.M. generally located in area north of Discovery Park, between 47th Ave. W. and Bay Terrace Addition; by constructing a sanitary sewer system together with the necessary appurtenances; all in accordance with Resolution 26137 of The City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
