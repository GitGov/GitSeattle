 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of portion of the East-West Alley in Block 83, Gilman's Addition, being that portion of the East-West alley between W. Elmore St. and W. Jameson St. from 27th Ave. W. to the north-South alley in said Block; by paving the same; by doing such grading or regrading as may be necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 25991 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds."q
