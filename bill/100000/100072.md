**Council Bill Number: 100072**
   
**Ordinance Number: 108000**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Forward Thrust Sewer Plan and Program; making certain determinations pursuant to Ordinance 96327 that certain projects and the method of accomplishing certain projects set forth in Section 3 of said Ordinance have become inpractical and that certain named projects have been substantially completed or duly provided for; abandoning certain provided for; abandoning certain projects which have become impractical to substantially accomplish; authorizing allocation of balances remaining in the Sewer Improvement Fund from such abandonments, interest earnings, cost savings, and bond proceeds made available through accomplishment of projects, in whole or in part, with supplemental and matching moneys, to the accomplishment of other projects set forth or authorized in Section 3 or projects determined by the Legislative Authority to be necessary, desirable and consistent with the purposes of Ordinance 96327.
