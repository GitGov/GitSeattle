

********

**Council Bill Number: 100765**
********

 AN ORDINANCE repealing Ordinances 16117 (Judgement Fund), 100501 (Advance Payment Claims Fund), 103167 (Claims Payment Fund), 104526 (Officers' and Employees' Claims Payment Fund); creating the Judgement/Claims Fund prescribing the method for the issuance of Warrants thereon; authorizing The City Attorney to make advance payments in partial settlement of Claims against the City in certain circumstances, to make payment of claims against The City in amount of not more than Two Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($2,500.00)or as authorized by Ordinance; to represent and defend City Officers and Employees in connection with any claims for damages and/or Litigation arising in the course of employment; and to make payment of Litigation/Claims related expenditures; and to establish a special fund in such connection; and to transfer the assets and liabilities of the "Judgment Fund", "Advance Payment Claims Fund", "Claims Payment Fund" and "Officers and Employees Claims Payment Fund" thereto.

**Status:** DNP

**Date introduced/referred to committee:** October 15, 1979
   
**Committee:** Budget
