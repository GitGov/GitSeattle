**Council Bill Number: 100468**
   
**Ordinance Number: 108345**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of Lake City Way N.E. from N.E. 123rd St. to N.E. 127th St.; by the construction/and or reconstruction and widening of concrete and/or decorative sidewalks, planting and installation of street trees, relocation of overhead and underground utilities and appurtenances; and by providing for the necessary surface drainage and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith; all in accordance with Resolution No. 26077 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".

**Vote:** 8-0
