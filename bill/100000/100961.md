 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of the Alley in Block 7, plat of Liberty Division No. 1, being the alley between 20th Avenue S.W. and 18th Avenue S.W. from S.W. Cloverdale St. to S.W. Thistle Street; and a certain other alley; by paving the same; by doing such grading or regrading as may be necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 26166 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of installment notes.
