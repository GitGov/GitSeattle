 AN ORDINANCE relating to license or occupation taxes for cable television systems, and for garbage collection and disposal businesses; amending Section 5 of Ordinance 62662 to provide for a license or occupation tax upon everyone engaged in the business of collection and disposal of garbage, rubbish, trade and other waste, and to extend the applicability of license or occupation taxation upon those engaged in the business of operating or conducting a cable television system.
