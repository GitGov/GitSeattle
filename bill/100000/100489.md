**Ordinance Number: 108364**

 AN ORDINANCE approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 6694 for the improvement of S. Washington St. from Alaskan Way S. to 1st Ave. S.; by constructing and/or reconstructing decorative sidewalks, concrete curbs and brick gutters, and appurtenances, reconstructing the street base and paving; constructing and/or reconstructing the support for the concrete walk to be constructed or reconstructed in the above named street; planting trees and installing tree guards and other miscellaneous construction connected therewith; providing for the necessary surface drainage and acquisition of any necessary right-of-way; under Ordinance No. 106900, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
