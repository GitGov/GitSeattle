**Council Bill Number: 100634**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Westlake Project; ordering the acquisition, construction and equipping of public improvements and the acquisition of property and property rights for the Westlake Project within the Central Business District; authorizing the issuance and sale of limited tax levy general obligation bonds in the principal amount of not to exceed $10,800,000 for such purposes; creating a construction fund and a bond redemption fund; providing for the annual levy and collection of taxes, along with other available money, to pay the principal of and interest of the bonds; providing for leases of property and property rights in certain areas of the Westlake Project; and conditionally providing for the transfer of bond proceeds, property and property rights to a public corporation and repealing Ordinance 106434.

**Status:** DNP
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** October 22, 1979
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** August 20, 1979
   
**Committee:** Special Committee on Westlake Project
   
**Sponsor:** EXECUTIVE REQUEST
   
   
**Index Terms:** WESTLAKE-PARK, WESTLAKE-MALL, LAND-ACQUISITION, IMPROVEMENT, CENTRAL-BUSINESS-DISTRICT, DOWNTOWN, TAXES, FINANCE, CONSTRUCTION
