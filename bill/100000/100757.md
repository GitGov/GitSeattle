**Council Bill Number: 100757**

 AN ORDINANCE creating an internal service fund designated the "Data Processing Operating Fund" for expenditures relating to Central Data Processing Services; providing for the deposit into said Fund of any Revenues in excess of total costs resulting from 1980 Central Data Processing Operations; providing for the operation of the Central Data processing services program out of the Data Processing Operating Fund effective January 1, 1981; and superseding Ordinance 107840 to the extent inconsistent.
