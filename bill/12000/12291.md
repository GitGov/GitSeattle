**Council Bill Number: 12291**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24454**
********

 An Ordinance creating and establishing in the City Treasury of The City of Seattle a separate fund to be known as "City Property Assessment Redemption Fund", authorized and directing the City Comptroller to draw, and the City Treasurer to pay, a warrant on the General Fund in favor of said special fund in the sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand Five Hundred Thirty-nine and five one-hundredths Dollars ($150,539.05), and providing for the payment into said special fund of a certain proportion of General Fund moneys collected under the tax levy made in 1909 for the year 1910.
