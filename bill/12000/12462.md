**Council Bill Number: 12462**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24682**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 15th Avenue West, from the south margin of Ruffner Street to Thorndyke Avenue; and Thorndyke Avenue, from the westerly margin of 15th Avenue Northwest produced, to the easterly margin of 13th Avenue West on the north, all in the City of Seattle, by planking and replanking the same to a width of 18 feet; by doing such grading as may be necessary to properly lay such planking and the necessary temporary wood walks; and making necessary provisions to care for surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2807 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
