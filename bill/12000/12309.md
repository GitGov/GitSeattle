**Council Bill Number: 12309**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24532**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2286, for the improvement of the alley in block 41, Capitol Hill Addition, Division No. 6, all in the City of Seattle, being the alley from East Prospect Street to East Aloha Street, and between 21st Avenue North and 22nd Avenue North, by grading the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, and constructing all necessary bulkheads and retaining walls; provided, however, that the grade be a vertical curve the entire length of the block, and that the cut at the center of the block shall not exceed three feet, under Ordinance No. 23483 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
