**Council Bill Number: 12511**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24685**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2229, for the improvement of Hunter Boulevard from Hanford Street to the south line of Spokane Street, by paving the same on either side of the parking strip (which is 40 feet in width in the center of the street) with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with the exception of a strip 8 feet wide on either side of the parking strip, which is to be occupied by street railway tracks and improved by the street railway company; under Ordinance No. 22753 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
