**Ordinance Number: 24723**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the northerly half of Grand Boulevard, from 15th Avenue West to 20th Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by planking and replanking the same from 15th Avenue West to 17th Avenue West; and by constructing a wooden trestle bridge with the necessary spans, from 17th Avenue West to 20th Avenue West; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2820 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
