**Council Bill Number: 12621**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24851**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East 45th Street, from 15th Avenue Northeast to 20th Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete curbs with armored concrete or granite corners; by doing all necessary grading and by constructing concrete sidewalks where necessary; and including as part of said work, the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2833 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
