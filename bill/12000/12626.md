**Council Bill Number: 12626**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24777**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2176, for the improvement of 5th Avenue, from Denny Way to the south margin of Vine Street, from 5th Avenue to 4th Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing granite curbs; and including as part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration and extension of concrete sidewalks; the construction, alteration and extension of all sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 21735 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
