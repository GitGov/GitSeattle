**Ordinance Number: 25048**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Waters Avenue, from 57th Avenue South to Bangor Street; 57th Avenue South, from Rainier Avenue to Roxbury Street; 58th Avenue South, and 59th Avenue South, each from Waters Avenue to Roxbury Street; 60th Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Cooper Street; 61st Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Ryan Street; 62nd Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Renton Avenue; 63rd Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Prentice Street; 64th Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Bangor Street; 65th Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Bangor Street; 66th Avenue South, from Waters Avenue to Bangor Street; 67th Avenue South, from Prentice Street to Bangor Street; Fletcher Street, from 57th Avenue South to Waters Avenue; Carver Street, Redwing Street, Keppler Street, Perry Street, and Thayer Street, each from Waters Avenue to the south margin of Rainier Avenue; Roxbury Street, from 59th Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; Pilgrim Street, from 59th Avenue South to 61st Avenue South and from Waters Avenue to Rainier Avenue; Norfolk Street, from 59th Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; Cooper Street from 59th Avenue South to 65th Avenue South; and from Wasters Avenue to Arrowsmith Avenue; Ryan Street, from 61st Avenue South to Waters Avenue; Prentice Street, from Renton Avenue to Waters Avenue; Bangor Street, from 64th Avenue South to Waters Avenue; and Arrowsmith Avenue, from Perry Street to Thayer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing wooden curbs and gutters; by constructing and relaying wooden sidewalks, and providing all the necessary grading and surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2837 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
