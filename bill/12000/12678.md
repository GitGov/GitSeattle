**Council Bill Number: 12678**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24857**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2249, for the improvement of Montlake Boulevard in Montlake Park Addition, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing asphalt gutters and concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, and including as part of said work all surface and sub-drainage, together with underground conduits for street lighting and the erection of iron poles with one light each, at such places as directed by the City Engineer, under Ordinance No. 23086 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
