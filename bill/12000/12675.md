**Council Bill Number: 12675**
   
**Ordinance Number: 24854**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming amended assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2100, for the improvement of Melrose Avenue North, from East Denny Way to East Roy Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing asphalt, concrete or brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners; and including as part of said work the construction, alteration, or extension of concrete walks and such sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; provided, that where the grades exceed 8% stone block pavement may be used; and provided further that Republican Street, from Eastlake Avenue to Bellevue Avenue North, may be improved at the discretion of the city engineer, by constructing stairways, concrete walks and parks, under Ordinance No. 21194 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
