**Ordinance Number: 24536**

 An ordinance accepting a deed from Emily L . Tull and Victor E. Tull, her husband, to the City of Seattle, dated May 2, 1910, to all that certain tract or lot or parcel of land, situate, lying and being in the County of King, State of Washington, particularly bounded and described as follows to-wit: that portion of the Northwest quarter of the Southwest quarter of the Southeast quarter of section 11, township 24 north, range 3 east, W.M., described as follows to-wit: beginning at the Northwest corner of lot 1, block 1, Tull's 2nd Addition to the City of West Seattle (now the City of Seattle); thence north along a line which is parallel to and distant 80 fee east from the west margin of California Avenue as platted, a distance of 507369 feet to a point on a line which is parallel to and distant 60 feet south from the north margin of West Lander Street as platted; thence east along said line a distance of 619.70 feet to a point on the west line of Banner Tract, an addition to the City of West Seattle (now the City of Seattle); thence north along said west line a distance of 30 feet, more or less, to a point on the south margin of West Lander Street as plated; thence west along said south margin a distance of 659.70 feet to a point on the east margin of California Avenue as platted; thence south along said east margin a distance of 537.48 feet; thence east a distance of 40 feet to a point of beginning all in the City of Seattle, for street purposes.
