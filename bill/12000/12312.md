**Ordinance Number: 24498**

 An Ordinance approving the new assessment or reassessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1417, for the improvement of Sixth Avenue South, from Seattle Boulevard at Charles Street to its intersection with Charlestown Street; thence easterly along Charlestown Street to Eighth Avenue South, by constructing a plank roadway thirty (30) feet in width thereon, with wooden sidewalks six (6) feet in width along one side thereof, as provided for and specified in Ordinance No. 15463, under Ordinance No. 21849, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
