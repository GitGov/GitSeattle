**Council Bill Number: 12975**
   
**Ordinance Number: 25106**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2208, for the improvement of Westlake Avenue, from Virginia Street to Mercer Street, by paving the same as follows: by paving with asphalt from Virginia Street to Harrison Street and by paving with brick from Harrison Street to Mercer Street, all to be laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing granite curbs, curbed granite corners and brick gutters, and providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 22196 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
