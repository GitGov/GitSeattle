**Ordinance Number: 24720**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 418 of the City of Georgetown, now City of Seattle, entitled: "An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of Fidalgo Street, in the City of Georgetown, over and across Tract "A" of Georgetown Riverfront Addition in Seattle; also over and across an unplatted land lying between the east line of the Eli B. Maple Donation Claim No. 39 and the westerly line of Charleston Avenue, and providing for the taking or damaging of land and other property necessary therefor and for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation to be made for the private property to be taken or damaged for said purpose, and for an assessment upon the property benefited, and for the purpose of making such compensation" passed and approved November 16, 1908, and authorizing the dismissal of the action in the Superior Court based upon said ordinance, and authorizing payment of all taxable costs in said action.
