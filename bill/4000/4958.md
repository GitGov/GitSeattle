**Ordinance Number: 18114**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Bellevue Place, form East Roy Street to Bellevue Avenue North, and Bellevue Court, from Bellevue Place to Belmont Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1104 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
