**Ordinance Number: 17543**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fuhrman Avenue, form Allison Street to Eastlake Avenue; Allison Street, from a point one hundred twenty (120) feet east of Harvard Avenue North to Fuhrman Avenue; Barton Place, from a point one hundred twenty (120) feet northeasterly of Allison Street, to Fuhrman Avenue; Franklin Avenue, from a point one hundred twenty (120) feet northeasterly of Shelby Street to Fuhrman Avenue; Eastlake Avenue, from Fuhrman Avenue to Hamlin Street, and Hamlin Street, from Eastlake Avenue to and connecting with existing sewer in the alley west of Eastlake Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein or proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and other appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1001 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
