**Council Bill Number: 4885**
   
**Ordinance Number: 18138**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North 37th Street, from Sunnyside Avenue to Northlake Avenue; thence across private property to Water Way Number 17, and Sunnyside Avenue from North 42nd Street to North 37th Street; all in the City of Seattle by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1153 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
