**Ordinance Number: 17544**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Main Street, in the City of Seattle, from the west margin of the alley between First Avenue South and Railroad Avenue, to the east margin of Railroad Avenue, by paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing granite curbs and laying such sewers and water mains as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 997 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
