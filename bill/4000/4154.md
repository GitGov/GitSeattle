**Council Bill Number: 4154**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17293**
********

 An ordinance providing for the rebating and refunding of the excess portions of all assessments levied for the improvement of East Thomas Street, by grading under Ordinance No. 6488 approved January 18, 1901, creating Local Improvement District No. 329, and authorizing the City Treasurer to prepare a tabulated statement showing the amounts of refunds due on the lots and parcels of land in said assessments and appropriating out of the special fund of said local improvement district the amounts necessary to pay such refunds and authorizing and directing the Mayor and City Comptroller to draw warrants on such special fund in favor of such payors, as shown by said tabulated statement and authorizing the City Treasurer to pay said warrants out of such special fund and repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
