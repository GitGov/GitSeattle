**Council Bill Number: 4550**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17763**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Blocks "E" and "F" of an Addition to the Town (now City) of Seattle as laid off by A. A. Denny, being the alleys between Western Avenue and First Avenue, and from University Street to Pike Street; and Post Street, from the north line of University Street to the south line of Seneca Street, by grading and paving the same, together with such retaining walls, sewers, catch basins, and other appurtenances, as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1057 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
