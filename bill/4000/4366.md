**Council Bill Number: 4366**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17541**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the alleys in blocks Fourteen (14), Fifteen (15) and Sixteen (16), David S. Maynard's plat of the town (now city) of Seattle, by paving and planking the same, and by laying such sewers, of proper dimensions and materials, as may be necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said alleys; provided that the alley in said Block Fifteen (15) be paved with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, the alley in said Block Sixteen (16) be paved with sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and the alley in said Block Fourteen (14) be improved by filling and planking the same, in accordance with Resolution No. 1007 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
