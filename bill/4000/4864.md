**Ordinance Number: 17957**

 An Ordinance granting to E. C. Lewis, and others, the right, privilege and authority to improve the alley in Block Ten (10), Capitol Hill Addition to the City of Seattle, Division No. 3, being the alley from East Aloha Street to East Valley Street, between Fourteenth Avenue North and Fifteenth Avenue North, by paving the same with vitrified brick, or other suitable paving material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, in accordance with plans to be furnished by the City Engineer, at their own cost and expense.
