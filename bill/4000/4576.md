**Council Bill Number: 4576**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17717**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1583, for the improvement of Fremont Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from a point one hundred and twenty (120) feet south of the south margin of North Thirty-ninth Street to Kilbourne Street, by constructing therein sewers of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary openings, wyes, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and other appurtenances, and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
