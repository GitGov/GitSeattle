**Ordinance Number: 17426**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 1, D. S. Maynard's Plat of the Town (now City) of Seattle, being the alley from Yesler Way to Washington Street, between 1st Avenue South and Railroad Avenue; by constructing concrete bulkhead thereon and paving the same with vitrified brick, all in accordance with Resolution No. 952 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
