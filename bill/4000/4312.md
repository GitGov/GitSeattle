**Ordinance Number: 17508**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Pike Street, from First Avenue to Third Avenue; University Street, Madison Street, and Columbia Street, each from the west marginal line of Railroad Avenue to Third Avenue; Cherry Street, from First Avenue to Third Avenue; Yesler Way, from Railroad Avenue to Third Avenue; Washington Street, from a point about two hundred (200) feet west of the west marginal line of Railroad Avenue to Third Avenue South; Jackson Street, from a point about three hundred (300) feet west of the west marginal line of Railroad Avenue to Third Avenue South; Railroad Avenue, from Columbia Street to Yesler Way; First Avenue and First Avenue South, from Pike Street to King Street; and Third Avenue and Third Avenue South, from Pike Street to Jackson Street, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of water mains, fire hydrants, valves and other appurtenances, and the necessary pumping station for the purpose of an auxillary fire protection system, in accordance with Resolution No. 982 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
