**Ordinance Number: 17939**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Mayfair Avenue, Second Avenue North, and Warren Avenue, each from Ray Street to Florentia Street; Third Avenue North, from Florentia Street to Newell Street; Nob Hill Avenue, from Fulton Street to a point One Hundred Eighty-six and Forty-three One-Hundredths (186.43) feet north of the south margin of Newell Street; Fulton Street and Newell Street, each from First Avenue North to the east line of the Mayfair Addition to the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete walks and alley crossings, and all necessary crosswalks and box drains, in accordance with Resolution No. 1072 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
