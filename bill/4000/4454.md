**Council Bill Number: 4454**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17610**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1541, for the improvement of the alley in Block ten (10) of Boren & Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, same being the alley between Section Avenue and Third Avenue, between Madison Street and Marion Street, by grading and paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and including such sewers and water mains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 16698, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
