**Ordinance Number: 17699**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Block One Hundred and Seven (107), A. A. Denny's Broadway Addition to the City of Seattle, being the alley between Eighth Avenue and Ninth Avenue, from Union Street to Pike Street, by grading and paving the same with vitrified brick, or other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with such sewers, catch basins, and other appurtenances as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1055 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
