 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 12, D. S. Maynard's Plat of the Town (now City) of Seattle, being the alley from King Street to Jackson Street, between 2nd Avenue South and Occidental Avenue, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, all in accordance with Resolution No. 957 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
