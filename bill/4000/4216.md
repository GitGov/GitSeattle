**Council Bill Number: 4216**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17430**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alleys in blocks 9 and 10, of D. S. Maynard's Plat of the Town (now City) of Seattle, being the alleys from Yesler Way to Main Street, between Occidental Avenue to 2nd Avenue South, by filling and planking the alley in block 9 and paving the alley in block 10, with vitrified brick and constructing a concrete bulkhead therein, all in accordance with Resolution No. 956 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
