**Ordinance Number: 17445**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Fremont Avenue, from Kilbourne Street to North 45th Street; Linden Avenue, Winslow Place, and Woodland Park Avenue, each from North 42nd Street to North 45th Street; Aurora Avenue, from Motor Place to North 45th Street; Motor Place, North 45th Street, North 41st Street, and North 38th Street, each from Fremont Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; Allen Place, from Fremont Avenue to Aurora Avenue; and from Woodland Park Avenue to Interlake Avenue, all in the City of Seattle by constructing concrete sidewalks and wood cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 964 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
