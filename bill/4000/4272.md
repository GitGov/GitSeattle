**Council Bill Number: 4272**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17453**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 37th Avenue South in the City of Seattle from the south margin of Hanford Street to the south margin of Court Street, by grading and curbing the same, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden cross walks, together with water mains of proper dimensions and materials, and such other appurtenances as may be necessary, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 33279 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
