**Council Bill Number: 4886**
   
**Ordinance Number: 17980**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1243, for the improvement of Pine Street and East Pine Street, from Westlake Avenue to Belmont Avenue; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the same and including as a part of said grading and regrading the construction of all necessary bulkheads and retaining walls, the reconstruction of all sewers where necessary and the regrading, residewalking and repaving where necessary, a approaches of the streets and avenues named therein, under Ordinance No. 13614 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, declaring the same to be a first lien thereon, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
