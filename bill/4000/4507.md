**Ordinance Number: 17648**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1566, for the improvement of Hiawatha Place, from a point one hundred twenty (120) feet south of Dearborn Street to the alley between Block Eight (8), Rainier Boulevard Addition to the City of Seattle, and Block Five (5), of Twenty-second Street Addition to the City of Seattle; thence following said alley in a southerly direction to a connection with the existing sewer in Rainier Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a sewer therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, lampholes, flush tanks and other appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 16786, and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
