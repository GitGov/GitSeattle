 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Phinney Avenue, from North Fiftieth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street; Greenwood Avenue, from North Sixty-fifth Street to North Eightieth Street; Woodland Park Avenue, from Aurora Avenue to North Eighty-second Street; West Green Lake Boulevard, from North Sixty-sixth Street to North Seventy-second Street; Linden Avenue, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street; Palatine Avenue, from North Sixtieth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street; North Sixtieth Street and North Sixty-fifth Street, each from Palatine Avenue to Phinney Avenue; North Sixty-fist Street and North Sixty-fourth Street ,each from Phinney Avenue to Linden Avenue; North Sixty-sixth Street, from Aurora Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard; North Sixty-eighth Street and North Seventy-second Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard; North Seventieth Street and North Seventy-fifth Street, each from Fremont Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard; North Seventy-third Street, North Seventy-fifth Street, North Seventy-sixth Street, North Seventy-seventh Street and North Eightieth Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; North Seventy-fourth Street, from Fremont Avenue to Winona Avenue; Winona Avenue, from North Seventy-fourth Street to Woodland Park Avenue; North Seventy-eighth Street, North Seventy-ninth Street, North Eighty-first Street and North Eighty-second Street, each from Fremont Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue all in the City of Seattle, by attaching hydrants to the existing water mains therein, together with such gates, tees, connecting pipes and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the reasonable fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1031 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
