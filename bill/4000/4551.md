**Ordinance Number: 17701**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alleys in blocks 26 and 27, of Additions to the town (now City) of Seattle, as laid off by A. A. Denny, being the alleys between 1st Avenue and 2nd Avenue, from Pike Street to Stewart Street, by grading and paving the same vitrified brick or other suitable paving material, laid upon a concrete foundation, together with such sewers, catch basins, and other appurtenances, as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1061, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
