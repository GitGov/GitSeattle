**Ordinance Number: 85750**

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of 15th Avenue Northwest from Ballard Way to West 51st Street; providing for surface roads in said portion of 15th Avenue Northwest; and for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor and necessary for a grade separation with limited access to be constructed along 15th Avenue Northwest from West 46th Street to West 51st Street; for the changing and establishing of the necessary grades; for acquiring the right to construct slopes for cuts and fills along the surface roads herein; and providing for payment from the Seattle General Arterial Improvement Bond 1954 Fund.
