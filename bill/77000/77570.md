 An ordinance relating to "The Municipal Court of Seattle" created by Chapter 290, Laws of Washington 1955; transferring certain budget items, salaries, and positions for the operation of, and establishing places for said court; providing for a chief clerk thereof, fixing his compensation and providing for his bond; providing for salaries and bonds for the judges of said court, for the compensation of judges pro tempore; creating positions of bailiff and of probation officer and fixing their salaries, and superseding Ordinance 84122.
