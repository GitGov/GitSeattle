 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2594 for the improvement of West Wheeler Street, from Fifteenth Avenue West to Thorndyke Avenue; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing bridge roadways thereon, together with such sidewalks as may be necessary; by paving the roadways with brick or other suitable material; by providing for the necessary surface drainage; by providing for the necessary lighting system; by providing for the necessary fire protection, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith.
