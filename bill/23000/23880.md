**Council Bill Number: 23880**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34967**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of easterly side of Franklin Avenue, from East Howe Street to East Newton Street; southerly side of East Newton Street, from Franklin Avenue to Franklin Place; westerly side of Franklin Place from East Newton Street to East Howe Street; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, together with such wooden crosswalks as may be necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith. all in accordance with Resolution No. 4812, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
