**Council Bill Number: 23116**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34280**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Forty-first Street, from Forty-fifth Avenue Northeast to Fifty-fifth Avenue Northeast; Fiftieth Avenue Northeast, from the south margin of East Fortieth Street to East Forty-fifth Street; Fifty-first Avenue Northeast and Fifty-first Avenue Northeast Produced from Lake Washington to East Forty-first Street and from East Forty-first Street to East Forty-second Street; Fifty-fifth Avenue Northeast, from East Forty-first Street to East Forty-fifth Street; East Forty-third Street from Fifty-fifth Avenue Northeast to Lake Washington, all in the City of Seattle , by constructing concrete sidewalks, concrete stairways, concrete and armored concrete curbs, and by providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4437 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
