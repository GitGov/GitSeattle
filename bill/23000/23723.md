**Council Bill Number: 23723**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34840**
********

 An ordinance providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of 43rd Avenue Northeast from East 44th Street to East 45th Street, and of East 45th Street from 42nd Avenue Northeast to 45th Avenue Northeast; and approaches thereto; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said avenue and street, and approaches thereto, in conformity with such established grades, and for the cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenue, street and approaches thereto, and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
