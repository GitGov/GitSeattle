**Council Bill Number: 23114**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34278**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2804 for the improvement of Densmore Avenue, from North Forty-fourth Street to North Forty-fifth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by the alteration, construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side-sewers and watermains, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the alteration, and reconstruction of concrete sidewalks; by such grading as may be necessary.
