**Council Bill Number: 23695**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34783**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Eighth Avenue West from West Prospect Street to West Howe Street; West Galer Street, from Eighth Avenue West to the westerly margin of the alley between Eighth Avenue West and Ninth Avenue West; West Garfield Street, from Eighth Avenue West to the stairway between Eighth Avenue West and Ninth Avenue West; West Blaine Street, from Queen Anne Boulevard and Seventh Avenue West to Ninth Avenue West; and West Howe Street, from Queen Anne Avenue and Seventh Avenue West to Eighth Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt together with brick gutters where the grades of the streets are less than eight per cent (8%); by paving the same with brick blocks where the grades of the streets are in excess of eight per cent (8%) and less than thirteen per cent (13%); by paving the same with sandstone blocks where the grades exceed thirteen per cent (13%); all pavement laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction of armored concrete curbs; by the construction of side sewers and water mains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction and reconstruction of concrete stairways, together with such concrete sidewalks as may be necessary on West Howe Street; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4800 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing the payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
