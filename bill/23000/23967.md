**Council Bill Number: 23967**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35061**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2816 for the improvement of Eighty Avenue, from Olive Street to Stewart Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing brick gutters, armored concrete curbs with granite corners and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 33797, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
