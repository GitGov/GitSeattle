**Council Bill Number: 23910**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34989**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Utah Avenue, from Stacy Street; 1st Avenue South from Stacy Street to Horton Street; Stacy Street, from 1st Avenue South to Utah Avenue; and Lander Street, from 1st Avenue South to Colorado Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction of granite curbs and concrete sidewalks where necessary; by the construction of sewers, side sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; and by necessary filling; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4806, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
