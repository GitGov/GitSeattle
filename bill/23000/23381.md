**Council Bill Number: 23381**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34513**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 37th Place, from Mount Rainier Drive to Hanford Street; 37th Avenue South, from Mount Rainier Drive to Court Street; Hinds Street, Horton Street and Spokane Street each from 37th Avenue South to Hunter Boulevard; Hanford Street from 37th Place to Hunter Boulevard; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, by grading and sub-grading the same, together with the construction and reconstruction of concrete walks where necessary, and the construction and reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4732 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing the payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
