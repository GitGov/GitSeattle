**Council Bill Number: 23583**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34942**
********

 An ordinance relating to the maintenance and inspection of dairies, milk plants, creameries, ice cream factories, stores and restaurants and the production, pasteurization, keeping, transportation, sale, labeling, and distribution of milk and milk derivatives; testing for tuberculosis in cows, prohibiting the transportation into and sale within the City of Seattle of milk drawn from cows infected with tuberculosis, and providing for the branding of such diseased cows; describing the conditions under which certain cows may be sold within the City of Seattle; and relating to the supply of milk and milk derivatives for the inhabitants of the City of Seattle; and repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
