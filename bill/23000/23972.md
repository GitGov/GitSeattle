**Council Bill Number: 23972**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35066**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Stroud Avenue, from East Green Lake Way to the north margin of North Eighty-second Street; North Eightieth Street, from Stroud Avenue to Corliss Avenue; and North Eighty-second Street, from Stroud Avenue to Meridian Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, constructing concrete sidewalks with the necessary wooden cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; by the reconstruction of the existing watermains where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4808 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
