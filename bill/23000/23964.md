**Council Bill Number: 23964**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35058**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Mary Street, West Dakota Street and Yancy Street, each from Twenty-eighth Avenue Southwest to Thirtieth Avenue Southwest; Twenty-eighth Avenue Southwest, from Mary Street to Yancy Street; and Thirtieth Avenue Southwest, from Mary Street to Avalon Way, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same; by providing for the necessary surface drainage; by constructing temporary wood walks where necessary; by planking and replanking; by the readjustment of existing watermains where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4834 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
