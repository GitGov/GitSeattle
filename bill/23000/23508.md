**Council Bill Number: 23508**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34640**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2801, for the improvement of West Mercer Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to Beach Drive, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by the construction, alteration, or reconstruction of all sewers, water mains and side sewers, necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary.
