**Council Bill Number: 23974**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35068**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Mill Street, from Railroad Avenue to Centennial Place, all in the City of Seattle, by filling; paving with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, watermains and their appurtenances that may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4854 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".

**Status:** Passed
