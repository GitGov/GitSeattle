**Council Bill Number: 23478**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34633**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2792, for the improvement of 47th Avenue Northeast (formerly Ridge Road), from the south margin of East 35th Street (formerly Northside Place) to the north margin of East 39th Street (formerly East 40th Street), and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by the alteration, construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with such appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction, reconstruction or alteration of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary.
