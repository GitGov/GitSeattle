**Ordinance Number: 34530**
********

 An ordinance ratifying and confirming a loan of the City of Seattle in the aggregate sum of Eighty Thousand ($80,000) Dollars, for the purpose of providing funds for additions and betterments to the existing water supp0ly system of the city, by the acquisition by purchase or by condemnation and purchase, of lands, properties, rights and privileges, within and near the watershed of Cedar River, necessary to be had to protect the said water supply system, commonly known as "Cedar River Water Supply System", from pollution, being a part of $1,000,000) bonds of the City of Seattle authorized at an election held September 5, 1911; and directing the issuance of such bonds in the sum of Eighty Thousand ($80,000) Dollars, and the levy and collection of taxes for the payment of the principal and interest thereof.
