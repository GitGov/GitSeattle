**Council Bill Number: 23733**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34845**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Massachusetts Street, from 1st Avenue South to Colorado Avenue; Utah Avenue, from Massachusetts Street to Atlantic Street; by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction of granite curbs; filling where necessary; the construction of sewers, side sewers and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4747, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
