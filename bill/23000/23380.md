**Council Bill Number: 23380**
   
**Ordinance Number: 34638**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Brooklyn Avenue form the south margin of East 38th Street to Ravenna Boulevard; East 41st Street, East 42nd Street, East 43rd Street, East 47th Street, East 50th Street, East 52nd Street and East 55th Street, each from Brooklyn Avenue to 14th Avenue Northeast; also the alley in block 2, Brooklyn Addition, being the alley between Brooklyn Avenue and 14th Avenue Northeast, from East 43rd Street to East 45th Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same to a width of 25 feet with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4569, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
