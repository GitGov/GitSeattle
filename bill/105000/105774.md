**Council Bill Number: 105774**
   
**Ordinance Number: 113098**

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the laying off, opening, widening and establishing of portions of North 85th Street at existing grade, all as a part of the North 85th Street UAB project; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking an damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, including property and property rights necessary for cut and fill slopes on certain abutting properties; and, providing for payment from the Arterial City Street Fund or from such other general funds of the City of Seattle as may be provided by law, which funds shall be partially reimbursed by the maximum amount allowable by law from funds received from the urban Arterial Board.
