**Ordinance Number: 112944**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Solid Waste Utility; providing for the issuance of Solid Waste Utility Revenue Bonds, 1986, of the City for the purpose of paying a part of the cost of carrying out the system or plan of additions to and betterments and extensions of the Solid Waste Utility specified, adopted and ordered to be carried out by this ordinances, to fund a reserve and to pay the costs of issuance and sale of those bonds, and for certain other costs; creating a special construction account and bond redemption account; directing the City Comptroller to negotiate the sale and delivery of those bonds and to recommend their principal amount, interest rates, and other details consistent with this ordinance, and providing for a resolution of the City Council to specify those details; and amending SMC 21.40.130.
