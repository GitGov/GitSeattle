**Council Bill Number: 105078**
   
**Ordinance Number: 112471**
********

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing the Director of Administrative Services and the Purchasing Agent to acquire, by lease or purchase, a telecommunications network and associated equipment and services; authorizing the necessary agreements for such acquisition; conditionally authorizing alternative financing for such acquisition by issuance of obligations of the City; providing the form, terms and conditions on the issuance of such obligations; designating the City Comptroller to sell and deliver the obligations and to fix the dates, maximum term, interest rates and other details consistent with the ordinance; and providing payment therefor.
