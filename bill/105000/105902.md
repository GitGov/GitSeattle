

********

**Council Bill Number: 105902**
********

 Relating to the Low Income Elderly and Handicapped Housing Program; authorizing the issuance and sale of up to $58,000,000 principal amount of general obligation refunding bonds of the City for the purpose of providing part of the funds necessary to refund all or a part of the City's outstanding General Obligation Bonds, 1982; authorizing the purchase of certain obligations with the proceeds of the sale of such refunding bonds and for the use and application of the funds to be derived from investments of such bonds; providing for the redemption of certain oustanding bonds to be refunded and for a refunding agent; providing the the adoption of a resolution fixing the date, form, terms and covenants of said refunding bonds to be issued; providing for unlimited tax levies to pay the principal thereof and interest on the bonds authorized to be issued by such resolution; authorizing the approval of the sale of the Bonds by the Comptroller and the adopting of a Resolution authorizing the sale.

**Status:** DNP
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** December 1, 1986
   
**Committee:** Finance and Personnel
