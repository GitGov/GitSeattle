**Council Bill Number: 105576**
********

 Amending Seattle Municipal Code Sections 5.48.050 and 5.48.070 (Ordinance 62662 SS 5 and 9) to respectively increase the utility tax on garbage collection and apply the tax to garbage transfer stations, and to establish a deduction to prohibit "pyramiding" of taxes to the retail consumer; and adding a new section dedicating all revenues from the increased rate for landfill closure costs.

**Status:** DNP
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** June 16, 1986
