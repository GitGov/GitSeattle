

********

**Council Bill Number: 105882**
********

 Relating to the Solid Waste Utility; authorizing the Mayor to execute an agreement with King County for the City of Seattle's use of King County disposal facilities; designating the King County solid waste disposal system as the disposal system for Seattle solid waste during the term of the agreement between the City of Seattle and King County; and increasing the expenditure allowances in the 1986 budget of the Solid Waste utility, making an appropriation from the Engineering Department Operating Fund and Solid Waste Fund and authorizing the Mayor to pay to King County the up-front costs specified in the agreement.

**Status:** DNP
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** November 24, 1986
   
**Sponsor:** FULL COUNCIL
