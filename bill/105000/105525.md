**Ordinance Number: 112879**

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing the Mayor to execute an agreement with the Municipality of Metropolitan Seattle (Metro) for reimbursement of City of Seattle costs for design, design review and construction activities on the Renton Effluent Transfer System (RETS) project and payment to Metro for designing and constructing two water distribution feeders and upgrading other water mains as part of Metro's construction; authorizing the acceptance of funds when received; authorizing the retention of consultants; establishing positions; increasing the 1986 expenditure allowance for the Department of Parks and Recreation; and making reimbursable appropriations from the Parks and Recreation and Parks Acquisition and Development Funds therefor.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** June 9, 1986
   
**Vote:** 7-0
   
**Date of Mayor's signature:** June 13, 1986
