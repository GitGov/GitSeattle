

********

**Council Bill Number: 105855**
********

 Relating to the Solid Waste Utility; authorizing the Mayor to execute an agreement with King County for the City of Seattle's use of King County disposal facilities; designating King County as the receptor disposal system for Seattle solid waste during the term of the agreement between the City of Seattle and King County; and increasing the expenditure allowances in the 1986 budget of the Solid Waste Utility, making an appropriation from the Engineering Department Operating Fund and Solid Waste Fund;, and authorizing the Mayor to pay to King County the up-front costs specified in the agreement; and declaring the emergency therefor.

**Status:** DNP
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** October 27, 1986
   
**Sponsor:** FULL COUNCIL
