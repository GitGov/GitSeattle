**Council Bill Number: 105249**
********

 Appropriating General Fund monies, equal to the amount due the City of Seattle for right-of-way vacations associated with the Washington State Convention and Trade Center project, into an account within the General Trust Fund, to be used for low-income housing in downtown or First Hill.
