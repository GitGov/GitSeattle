

********

**Council Bill Number: 105598**
********

 Providing for submission to the qualified electors of the City at a special election to be held in conjunction with the municipal primary election on September 16, 1986, a proposition authorizing the City to levy regular property taxes in excess of the 106 percent limitation for eight years to provide housing low income households in Seattle; establishing and funding City housing development programs; providing assistance to the Seattle Housing Authority for development and maintenance of low-income housing and authorizing a Housing Cooperation Agreement in such connection; creating a Levy Oversight Committee; and providing for an annual levy and the collection of taxes.

**Status:** In Committee
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** June 30, 1986
   
**Committee:** Housing and Human Services
   
**Sponsor:** NOLAND
