**Ordinance Number: 113060**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to land use and zoning; amending plats 22E and 29E, pages 76 and 90 of the Official Land Use Map, and pages 30 and 45 of Exhibit E-2 (Ordinance 106200) to relocate the Seattle Construction Limit Line on the north side of Lake Union between Waterways 20 and 21; to rezone the property between the old and new construction limit lines and in front of Waterway 21 from Single Family Low Density (SF 9600) to Manufacturing (M); to reclassify shoreline environmental designations of said lands from Conservancy from Conservancy Management (CM) and Conservancy Natural (CN) to Urban Stable/Lake Union (US/LU); and accepting a Property Use and Development Agreement in connection therewith. (Petition of Marine Power and Equipment, C.F. Nos. 293041 and 293646).
