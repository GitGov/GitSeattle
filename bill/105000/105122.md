**Council Bill Number: 105122**
********

 Relating to the Seattle Arts Festival commonly known as "BUMBERSHOOT" '86'; authorizing the Seattle Center Department to enclose and charge temporary, daily admission fees to the Seattle Center grounds and Arts Festival events and activities thereon and install temporary food service, crafts and souvenir sales on site; authorizing receipt and expenditure of grants, contributions and business support for the Festival; abandoning the Bumbershoot Subaccount and repealing Ordinance 111143 in connection therewith.
