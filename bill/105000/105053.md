 AN ORDINANCE relating to a countywide additional regular property tax levy for the provision of emergency medical services; giving conditional City of Seattle approval for a King County ballot proposition requesting an additional regular property tax levy for emergency medical services; authorizing an Interlocal Agreement between the City of Seattle and King County regarding allocation of tax revenues collected from the taxable property of the City of Seattle and King County Fire Protection District Number 5 pursuant to said levy; and creating an account for deposit of said tax revenues if said proposition is approved by the voters.
