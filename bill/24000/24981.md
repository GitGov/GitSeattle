**Ordinance Number: 36261**

 An ordinance appropriating the sum of seven hundred ($700.00) dollars out of the Water Fund of the City of Seattle in settlement of claim of Peter Madson and Mary Madson, his wife, for damages to their property on account of water from the Cedar River Pipe Line flowing over said property and causing said property to be covered with sand to a depth of from five inches to two feet, which damage occurred on the 11th and 12th days of January, 1916, and authorizing the drawing of a warrant therefor.
