**Council Bill Number: 24136**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35212**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Hamlin Street and Shelby Street, each from Montlake Boulevard to West Park Drive; West Park Drive, from Hamlin Street to Shelby Street; all in the City of Seattle; by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; constructing armored concrete curbs and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading or regrading as may be necessary; by the installation of a system of cluster lights along the curb line on each side of said streets and avenues, together with the installation of such poles, conduits, wires and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper installation and equipment of said system, all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 59654 creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
