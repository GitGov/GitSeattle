**Council Bill Number: 24811**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35852**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 9th Avenue West, from Queen Anne Boulevard (formerly West Wheeler Street) to West Ray Street; and West Halladay Street, from Queen Anne Boulevard to 8th Avenue West; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction, reconstruction or alteration of all sewers, side sewers and water mains necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service, and fire protection of said district; by the construction or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5014, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
