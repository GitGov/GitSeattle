**Council Bill Number: 24873**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35898**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 20th Avenue South, from Charles Street to Atlantic Street; and Atlantic Street, from 20th Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; all in the City of Seattle; by grading and regrading the same where necessary; by the construction of wooden curbs and gutters and cross walks; by the construction of concrete walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4981, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
