**Council Bill Number: 24332**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35388**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2813 for the improvement of Bellevue Place, from Roy Street to Bellevue Avenue North, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same; by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs; by constructing concrete sidewalks and concrete stairways where necessary; by constructing wood or concrete retaining walls where necessary; by providing for the necessary sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 33767, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
