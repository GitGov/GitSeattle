**Ordinance Number: 35430**
********

 An Ordinance appropriating the sum of Seventy-five ($75.) Dollars out of the General Fund of the City of Seattle to pay the claim of A. M. Bains for personal injuries received by him on the 5th day of October, 1915, at about 7:30 P. M., near the Seattle Sawmill Company's plant, by reason of his horse stepping into a hole in the planking on the bridge on which said Bains was driving, in said city, and authorizing the drawing of a warrant therefor.
