**Council Bill Number: 24313**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35371**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirteenth Avenue Northwest, from West Seventieth Street to West Seventy-fifth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers and side sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4923 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
