**Council Bill Number: 24314**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35382**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-third Avenue West, from West Garfield Street to West Lee Street; West Lee Street, from Twenty-third Avenue West to Logan Avenue; Logan Avenue, from West Lee Street to Third-second Avenue West; and Third-second Avenue West, from Logan Avenue to high tide line, all in the City of Seattle, by repiling the existing bridge structure and replacing sway braces where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4838 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
