**Council Bill Number: 24674**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35730**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2860, for the improvement of 10th Avenue from East Spring Street to East Pine Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick, asphalt or wood block; all pavement laid upon a proper concrete foundation with armored concrete curbs; the construction of concrete walks; construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, and water mains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and the construction of the necessary cluster light system, under Ordinance No. 34718 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
