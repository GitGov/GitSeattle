**Council Bill Number: 24911**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35936**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2889, for the improvement of 20th Avenue Northeast and 21st Avenue Northeast, each from East 45th Street to the north margin of East 50th Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving with asphalt, together with brick gutters, laid upon a proper concrete foundation where the grades are less than seven per cent (7%) and by paving the same with brick blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation where the grades exceed seven per cent (7%); by constructing concrete curbs; by providing for surface drainage; by constructing all side sewers necessary for the proper sewerage of said district; by planking where necessary; under Ordinance No. 35087 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
