**Council Bill Number: 24316**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35373**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Western Avenue, from the south margin of University Street to Virginia Street; Union Street and Pike Street, each from Western Avenue to the Alley between Western Avenue and First Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick blocks, provided that Union Street shall be paved with sandstone block; the construction of granite curbs, concrete sidewalks, concrete sideways; the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewer and watermains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4908 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
