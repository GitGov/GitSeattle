**Ordinance Number: 36001**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2886, for the improvement of Mill Street, from Railroad Avenue to Centennial Place, all in the City of Seattle, by filling; by paving with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, water mains and their appurtenances that may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 35068 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
