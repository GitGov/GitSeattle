 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of East 50th Street, from the west line of University Annex, an addition to the City of Seattle, to 35th Avenue Northeast; of East 48th Street, from the west line of University Annex, an addition to the City of Seattle, to 36th Avenue Northeast; and of East 47th Street and Railroad Avenue Northeast, each from the west line of University Annex, an addition to the City of Seattle, to 35th Avenue Northeast; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
