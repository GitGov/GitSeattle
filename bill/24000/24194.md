**Council Bill Number: 24194**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35261**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2850 for the improvement of Thirty-seventh Place, from Mount Rainier Drive to Hanford Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, by grading and sub-grading the same, together with the construction and reconstruction of concrete walks where necessary, and the construction and reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district and providing for the necessary surface drainage under Ordinance No. 34513, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
