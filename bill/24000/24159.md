**Council Bill Number: 24159**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35229**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fiftieth Avenue Northeast, from East Thirty-ninth Street to East Forty-first Street; East Forty-first Street, from Fiftieth Avenue Northeast to Fifty-fifth Avenue Northeast; and Fifty-fifth Avenue Northeast, from East Forty-fifth Street to East Forty-fifth Street; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4906 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
