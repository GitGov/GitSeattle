**Council Bill Number: 24225**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35299**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North and West Forty-eighth Street, from Eleventh Avenue Northwest to Phinney Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper materials and dimensions, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4913 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
