**Ordinance Number: 35845**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 43rd Avenue Northeast, from East 45th Street to East 33rd Street; 44th Avenue Northeast, from East 41st Street to East 38th Street and from 45th Avenue Northeast to East 35th Street; 45th Avenue Northeast, from East 38th Street to East 35th Street and from 44th Avenue Northeast to the most southerly point of block 26, Laurelhurst Addition; East 33rd Street, from 47th Avenue Northeast its westerly terminus in block 17, Laurelhurst Addition; East 38th Street, from 43rd Avenue Northeast to 47th Avenue Northeast; East 41st Street, East 42nd Street, East 43rd Street and East 44th Street, each from 43rd Avenue Northeast to 45th Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4934, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
