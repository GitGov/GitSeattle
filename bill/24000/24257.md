**Council Bill Number: 24257**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35320**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2864 for the improvement of 16th Avenue South, from Beacon Avenue to Hanford Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, all in the City of Seattle, by subgrading, the construction of concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, concrete sidewalks, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, watermains, and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 34793, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
