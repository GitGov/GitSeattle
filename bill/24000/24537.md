**Council Bill Number: 24537**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35585**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2875, for the improvement of 5th Avenue North, from the north margin of Harrison Street to the north margin of Aloha Street, all in the City of Seattle,, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by construction armored concrete curbs; by the construction, reconstruction or alteration of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by the construction or reconstruction of sewers, side sewers and water mains, with such appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; by doing such grading and regrading as may be necessary, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith, under Ordinance No. 34966 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
