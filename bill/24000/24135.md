**Council Bill Number: 24135**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35211**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Utah Avenue, from Massachusetts Street to Stacy Street, all in the City of Seattle, by filling and planking the same and including as part of said work the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and their appurtenances, and the adjustment of water mains and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water services and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4811 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
