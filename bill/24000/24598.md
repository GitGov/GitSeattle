**Ordinance Number: 35642**

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2907, for the improvement of a strip of land across lots 16 to 22, inclusive, in block 69, Mount Baker Park Addition as described by easement granted by Hunter Tract Improvement Company and accepted by Ordinance No. 35020; and Lake Washington Park and Parkway, form a point near the Southeast corner of lot 22, block 69, Mount Baker Park Addition, to a connection with existing sewer outfall in Landing Parkway, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers, of proper dimension and materials together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 35185 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
