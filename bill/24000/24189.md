**Council Bill Number: 24189**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35256**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Meridian Avenue, from North Forty-fifth Street to North Fifty-fifth Street; North Fifty-fifth Street from the westerly margin of Meridian Avenue to Keystone Place; all in the City of Seattle; by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs, and brick gutters; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of sewers, side sewers, and watermains where necessary, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the alteration, construction and reconstruction of concrete side walks where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4863 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
