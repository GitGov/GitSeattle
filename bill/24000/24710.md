**Council Bill Number: 24710**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35750**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 2nd Avenue West, from West Thomas Street to West Republican Street; 3rd Avenue West, from West Harrison Street to West Roy Street; and 4th Avenue West, from West Republican Street to West Roy Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein concrete sidewalks with wooden cross walks; providing for the necessary surface drainage, by doing such grading and regrading as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5023, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
