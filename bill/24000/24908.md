**Ordinance Number: 35933**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 15, J. H. Nagle's Addition, and block 2, Sackman's Home Addition, being the alley between Harvard Avenue and Broadway, from East Pike Street to East Pine Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 55803, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
