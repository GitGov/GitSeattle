**Council Bill Number: 24191**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35258**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2819 for the improvement of Ewing Street, from Woodland Park Avenue to Wallingford Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving with brick or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing armored concrete curbs, together with such sewers and water mains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 33836, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
