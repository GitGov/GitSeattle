**Council Bill Number: 24315**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35372**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-eighth Avenue Northeast from East Fifty-fifth Street to East Sixtieth Street; East Fifty-fifth Street, from Twenty-seventh Avenue Northeast to Twenty-ninth Avenue Northeast; and East Sixtieth Street from Twenty-seventh Avenue Northeast to Twenty-eighth Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle; by grading and curbing the same, providing for the necessary surface drainage and by the construction of concrete walks all in accordance with Resolution No. 4874 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
