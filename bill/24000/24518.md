**Council Bill Number: 24518**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35570**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2867, for the improvement of Massachusetts Street, from 1st Avenue South to Colorado Avenue, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction of granite curbs; filling where necessary; the construction of sewers, side sewers and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith; under Ordinance No. 34845 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
