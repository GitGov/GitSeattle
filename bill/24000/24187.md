**Ordinance Number: 35254**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Ferdinand Street, from Forty-second Avenue South to Forty-ninth Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing and constructing concrete sidewalks, together with all necessary cross-walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 60811, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
