**Council Bill Number: 24137**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35213**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of East Sixty-ninth Street, from the easterly line of James' Division of Green Lake Addition, to a point approximately three hundred ten (310) feet easterly therefrom, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers and side sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 61418, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
