**Council Bill Number: 24476**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35524**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West 55th Street from 6th Avenue Northwest to the west margin of 8th Avenue Northwest; West 59th Street, from 8th Avenue Northwest to 14th Avenue Northwest; and 8th Avenue Northwest, from West 58th Street to West 60th Street; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks with necessary wooden cross walks; by doing such resurfacing and recurbing as may be necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4915, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
