**Council Bill Number: 24331**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35387**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Valley Street and East Ward Street, each from Thirty-fourth Avenue North to Thirty-sixth Avenue North; and Thirty-sixth Avenue North from East Madison Street to East Mercer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of concrete sidewalks, combined concrete curbs and gutters, and including as a part of said work the improvement of East Valley Street from Thirty-fourth Avenue North to Thirty-sixth Avenue North, by grading and by paving the same with asphalt and with asphaltic concrete all in accordance with Resolution No. 4930 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
