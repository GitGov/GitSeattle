**Council Bill Number: 24497**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35548**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 29th Avenue and 29th Avenue North, from East John Street to East Union Street; 30th Avenue from Denny Way to East Union Street; 31st Avenue from East Denny Way to East Pike Street; East Howell Street and East Pine Street, each from 28th Avenue to 32nd Avenue; East Olive Street, from 28th Avenue to 32nd Avenue; East Pike Street, from 28th Avenue to 32nd Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by construction of concrete walks on the west side of 29th Avenue and 29th Avenue North, 30th Avenue and 31st Avenue; on both sides of East Howell street and East Pine Street, and on the south side of East Olive Street and East Pike Street; together with the construction of concrete walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4983, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
