**Council Bill Number: 24909**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35934**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 22nd Avenue North and 24th Avenue North, each from East Lynn Street to East Montlake Place; East McGraw Street, Calhoun Street, Miller Street and Louisa Street, each from 22nd Avenue North to 24th Avenue North; Roanoke Street, from 22nd Avenue North to East Montlake Place; and East Montlake Place, from 24th Avenue North to Montlake Boulevard; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sidewalks with the necessary wood cross walks; by constructing wooden curbs and gutters and temporary wooden walks where necessary; and providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 5049, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
