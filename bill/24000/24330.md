**Council Bill Number: 24330**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35386**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Alonzo Avenue Northwest from West Seventieth Street to West Sixty-seventh Street; and West Sixty-seventh Street from Alonzo Avenue Northwest to a connection with the existing sewer on Fourteenth Avenue Northwest, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein a sewer of proper materials and dimensions together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 4900 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".

**Status:** Passed
