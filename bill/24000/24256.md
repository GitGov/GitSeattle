**Council Bill Number: 24256**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35319**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2855 for the improvement of 14th Avenue, from Yesler Way to East Alder Street and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with brick gutters, concrete curbs with armored concrete or granite corners, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers, watermains, and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 34639, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
