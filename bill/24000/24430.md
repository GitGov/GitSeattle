**Council Bill Number: 24430**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35482**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2863 for the improvement of Eighty Avenue West from West Prospect Street to West Howe Street and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt together with brick gutters where the grades of the streets are less than eight per cent(8%); by paving the same with brick blocks where the grades of the streets are in excess of eight per cent (8%) and less than thirteen per cent (13%); by paving the same with sandstone blocks where the grades exceed thirteen per cent (13%); all pavement laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by the construction of armored concrete curbs; by the construction of side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the property sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the construction and reconstruction of concrete stairways, together with such concrete sidewalks as may be necessary on West Howe Street, under Ordinance No. 34783, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
