**Council Bill Number: 24410**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35462**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-ninth Avenue Northeast, from the north line of Shay's Ravenna Addition to the City of Seattle to East Sixty-fifth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same; by constructing wooden curbs and gutters; by constructing concrete sidewalks with the necessary wooden cross walks; by providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4937 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
