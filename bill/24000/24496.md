**Council Bill Number: 24496**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35547**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2869, for the improvement of Horton Street, from 1st Avenue South to a connection with existing pavement on Whatcom Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing brick gutters, granite curbs and side sewer connections where necessary; grading and filling where necessary, together with such sewers and water mains, together with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith; under Ordinance No. 34847 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
