**Council Bill Number: 24188**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35255**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of The North Side of West Dravus Street from Third Avenue West to Fourth Avenue West; The South Side of West Dravus Street from Third Avenue West to Humes Place; The East Side of Fourth Avenue West from a point one hundred seventy (170) feet north of West Barrett Street to a point ninety (90) feet north of West Dravus Street; and The West Side of Fourth Avenue West from Humes Place to West Dravus Street; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein concrete sidewalks with the necessary wooden cross walks, all in accordance with Majority Petition (File No. 61133), creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
