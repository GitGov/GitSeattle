**Council Bill Number: 24480**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35528**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2854, for the improvement of Brooklyn Avenue from the south margin of East 38th Street to Ravenna Boulevard; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same to a width of 25 feet with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs; by the construction, alteration, or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by the construction, alteration or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 34638 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
