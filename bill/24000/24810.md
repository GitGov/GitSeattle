**Council Bill Number: 24810**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35851**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Bailey Street, from 13th Avenue South to Corson Avenue; 12th Avenue South, from Bailey Street to Vale Street; Vale Street, form 12th Avenue South to Duwamish Avenue; 13th Avenue South, from Duwamish Avenue to 14th Avenue South; Stanley Avenue from 13th Avenue South to 14th Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; constructing concrete curbs and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage., drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary; by such grading and regrading as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4942, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
