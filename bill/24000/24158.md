**Council Bill Number: 24158**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35233**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Alley in Block 28, Capitol Hill Addition, Division No. 4, being the alley between Fifteenth Avenue North and Sixteenth Avenue North, from East Prospect Street to East Highland Drive, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with concrete; by providing for the necessary surface drainage; by doing such grading as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4839 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
