**Council Bill Number: 24519**
   
**Ordinance Number: 35571**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2848, for the improvement of the property within the following described area to-wit: the real property situated in the City of Seattle, County of King, and State of Washington, bounded and described as follows to-wit: beginning at the intersection of the city limits in 12th Avenue South and the center line of Dallas Avenue; thence Northwest along said cetner line to the center line of 10th Avenue South; thence north along said center line of 10th Avenue South to the Southwest margin of Duwamish Waterway; thence Southeast along said Southwest margin to the east line of River Park Addition, which is the city limits; thence south and east along said city limits to a point beginning also, beginning at the intersection of the Northeast margin of the Duwamish Waterway, and the Southeast band of the Duwamish River; thence Northeast along said Southeast bank to the Northeast margin of East Marginal Way as established by Ordinance No. 32881; thence Southeast along said Northeast margin to the city limits, which is the south line of Section 28, Township 24 North, Range 4 East; W.M.; thence west along said city limits to the Northeast margin of the Duwamish Waterway; thence Northwest along said Northeast margin to a point beginning; by filling as a sanitary measure, together with bulkheading thereof, or of filling of the streets abutting on said private property with earth to serve as a bulkheading for the fill upon such private property; under Ordinance No. 34442 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
