**Council Bill Number: 110428**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Birthplace of Seattle Log House Museum, authorizing expenditure from the Community Improvement Fund established by Ordinance 115496 of $200,000 for the acquisition by the City of easements, convenants and other rights in the Old Log Cabin House in West Seattle in order to ensure its preservation and operation as a community museum; authorizing an agreement with the Southwest Seattle Historical Society and the acceptance of a deed; and ratifying and confirming prior acts.
