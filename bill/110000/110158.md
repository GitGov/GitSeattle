

********

**Council Bill Number: 110158**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Criminal Code; changing the definition of the crime of Stalking to encompass repeated harassment in addition to repeated following, changing the crime of Obstructing a Public Servant to expand the definition of "public servant", upgrading Obstructing a Public Servant from a misdemeanor to a gross misdemeanor, and amending Sections 12A.06.035 and 12A.16.010 of the Seattle Municipal Code.

**Status:** Do Not Pass
   
**Vote:** 8-0
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** May 31, 1994
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** May 9, 1994
   
**Committee:** Public Safety
   
**Sponsor:** PAGELER
   
   
**Index Terms:** MISDEMEANORS, CRIMINAL-OFFENSES
