**Council Bill Number: 110288**
   
**Ordinance Number: 117281**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the City Light Department; authorizing City Light to sign the "Cooperative Agreement Between United States National Park Service, North Cascades National Park Service Complex, And, City of Seattle, City Light Department," to build a new boathouse and maintenance facility on Ross Lake which both agencies will own and operate; authorizing City Light to accept funding from the National Park Service to pay for the National Park Service's portion of the project; and increasing the expenditure allowances in the 1994 Budget of the City Light Department by the amount of funding provided by the National Park Service for the Ross Lake Boathouse and Maintenance Facility.

**Date passed by Full Council:** August 22, 1994
   
**Vote:** 7-0
