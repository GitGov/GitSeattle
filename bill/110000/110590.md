**Ordinance Number: 117535**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the transfer of Seattle water, drainage and wastewater facilities located in the city of Tukwila to the City of Tukwila; authorizing the Mayor to enter into agreements with the City of Tukwila to transfer responsibility, customers, and utility assets for providing water and wastewater service to customers in areas outside the City of Seattle now annexed to Tukwila, to cooperate on the Norfolk Drainage Project and other shared utility facilities, and to accept payments received under those agreements.
