

********

**Council Bill Number: 110530**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to historic preservation; imposing controls upon the Paramount Theater, a Landmark designated by the Landmarks Preservation Board, and adding it to the Table of Historical Landmarks contained in Chapter 25.32 of the Seattle Municipal Code; making an appropriation from the Development Rights Fund to purchase development rights from the Paramount Theater; authorizing related agreements and acceptance of a deed; and ratifying and confirming prior acts, all by a three-fourths vote.

**Status:** Retired 97
   
**Vote:** 9-0
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** August 5, 1996
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** January 23, 1995
   
**Committee:** Parks, Public Grounds and Recreation
   
**Sponsor:** DONALDSON
   
   
**Index Terms:** DOWNTOWN, THEATERS, LANDMARKS
