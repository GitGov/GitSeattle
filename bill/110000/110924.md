 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 Be it ordained by the City of Seattle as follows:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Thirty Three Million Sixty Hundred Ninety Seven Thousand Five Hundred Twenty and 20 ($33,697,520.47) dollars on SFMS Warrant Issue mechanical warrants numbered 40607373- 40609983 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Sept. 28,1995 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2 . Payment of the sum of Fourteen Million Seven Hundred forty-three Thousand Six Hundred Fifty Five 19/100 ($14,743,655.19) dollars on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 30945907- 30949295 manual and agency issues representing Gross Payroll for payroll ending date Sept. 19, 1995 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Sept. 28,1995 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 2 day of Oct., 1995, and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this-2 day of Oct., 1995 ____________________________________ _ President______________of the City Council
