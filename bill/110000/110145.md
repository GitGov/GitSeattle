**Ordinance Number: 117136**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to summer youth employment; authorizing the Superintendent of Parks and Recreation to utilize approximately 100 youth from the Seattle Youth Volunteer Corps to increase summer program staffing; reducing an expenditure allowance in the 1994 budget of the Department of Housing and Human Services; and reappropriating that amount to the 1994 budget of the Department of Parks and Recreation to be used for that summer youth staffing.
