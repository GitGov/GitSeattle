**Council Bill Number: 110584**
   
**Ordinance Number: 117531**
********

 AN ORDINANCE regarding development of a City sabbatical leave policy, authorizing the Personnel Department to develop a Sabbatical Leave Policy and implement a Sabbatical Leave Pilot Project consistent with the principles contained in this ordinance, authorizing the Director of the Personnel Department to suspend the application of Personnel Rules which are inconsistent with this ordinance or with a Sabbatical Leave Policy developed pursuant to this ordinance and directing the Director of the Personnel Department to develop a report evaluating the effectiveness of the Sabbatical Leave Pilot Project.
