**Council Bill Number: 110671**
   
**Ordinance Number: 117620**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the City's directory assistance service transferring the function of providing City directory assistance from the Department of Administrative Services to the Department of Neighborhoods; transferring two positions in connection with that function; transferring funds from the Administrative Services Operating Fund to the General Fund; reducing the expenditure allowance in the 1995 Budget of the Department of Administrative Services; increasing the expenditure allowance in the 1995 Budget for the Department of Neighborhoods accordingly; and amending Sections 3.35.030 and 3.35.040 of the Seattle Municipal Code.
