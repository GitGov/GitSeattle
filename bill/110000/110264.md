**Council Bill Number: 110264**
   
**Ordinance Number: 117248**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to library facilities; providing for the submission to the voters of the City at a special election to be held therein on November 8, 1994, in conjunction with the state general election to be held on the same date, of a proposition authorizing the City to incur indebtedness by issuing its general obligation bonds, payable by annual property tax levies to be made in excess of regular property tax levies, for the purpose of paying all or a part of the cost of constructing, acquiring, renovating and equipping central, branch, satellite and other library facilities and to levy those excess property taxes; finding an emergency and requesting a special election.
