 AN ORDINANCE relating to Seattle Center; authorizing the execution of an agreement with Seattle Monorail Services Joint Venture regarding the operation, maintenance, and administration of the Monorail System and the use and occupancy of certain related portions of the Seattle Center and Westlake Center for a period of years and certain related Memoranda of Understanding; repealing Seattle Municipal Code Sections 17.19.020 and -.030; amending Seattle Municipal Code Sections 17.19.040, -.050 and -.060 to eliminate references to Monorail transportation and fares; and, upon a three-fourths vote of the City Council, increasing the budget of the Seattle Center Department.
