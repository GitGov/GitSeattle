**Ordinance Number: 117125**

 AN ORDINANCE Relating to the City Light Department; authorizing the Superintendent of City Light to undertake studies, to initiate discussions with other interested parties and to undertake other efforts in anticipation of eventually submitting an application to the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) for a new license for the City's Boundary Hydroelectric Project (FERC Project No. 2144 - Washington); and, authorizing the Superintendent to purchase 95 acres of land in preparation for relicensing the Project.
