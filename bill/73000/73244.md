**Council Bill Number: 73244**
   
**Ordinance Number: 81858**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Magnolia Boulevard from West Dravus Street to West Howe Street at 34th Avenue West, and from West Howe Street at Clise Place to West Galer Street at 29th Avenue West; and certain other streets and avenues; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 15562 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
