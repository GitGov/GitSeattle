**Ordinance Number: 81856**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Haglund's First Addition, being the alley between 59th Avenue Southwest and 60th Avenue Southwest from Admiral Way to West Stevens Street; and certain other avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15925 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
