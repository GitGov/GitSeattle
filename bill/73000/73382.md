**Council Bill Number: 73382**
   
**Ordinance Number: 81992**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 2, Garland's First Addition, being the alley between North 74th Street and North 75th Street from Fremont Avenue to Dayton Avenue; and certain other streets and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15923 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
