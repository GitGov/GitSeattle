**Council Bill Number: 73705**
   
**Ordinance Number: 82316**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 7, Renton's Addition, being the Alley between 15th Avenue and 16th Avenue from East Olive Street to East Howell Street, and certain other streets, avenues, and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16037 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
