**Council Bill Number: 73243**
   
**Ordinance Number: 81857**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Holman Addition to Ballard, being the alley between West 69th Street and West 70th Street from 32nd Avenue Northwest to approximately 385 feet west thereof; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15924 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
