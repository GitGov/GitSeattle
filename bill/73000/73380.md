**Ordinance Number: 81990**

 An ordinance relating to buildings and structures and applications for building permits therefor, and amending the Building Code by adding a new section (Section 108-1) requiring such applications for buildings which are to be serviced by a cesspool or septic tank to be submitted to the Director of Public Health for approval before being granted by the Superintendent of Buildings; and superseding the Plumbing Code (Ordinance No. 80242) insofar as in conflict herewith.
