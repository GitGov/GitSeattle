**Council Bill Number: 73715**
   
**Ordinance Number: 82328**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 23, Comstock Supplemental Addition as established by Ordinance No. 79603, being the alley between West Prospect Street and West Highland Drive from Sixth Avenue West to Willard Avenue; and certain other avenues and alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16016 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
