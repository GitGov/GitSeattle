**Council Bill Number: 73295**
   
**Ordinance Number: 81908**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West Stevens Street from 45th Avenue Southwest to approximately 350 feet west of 52nd Avenue Southwest; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 15856 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
