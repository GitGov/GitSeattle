**Ordinance Number: 43497**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 44th Avenue Southwest, from West Dakota Street to West Hanford Street; 45th Avenue Southwest, from West Andover Street to West Charlestown Street; West Charlestown Street and West Andover Street, each from California Avenue to 44th Avenue Southwest; West Andover Street (north side), from 44th Avenue Southwest to 46th Avenue Southwest; West Charlestown Street (south side), from 44th Avenue Southwest to 46th Avenue Southwest, all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6990, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
