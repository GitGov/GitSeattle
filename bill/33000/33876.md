 An Ordinance providing for the construction of a trunk sewer and subsewers along East Marginal Way, from Dawson Street to Fidalgo Street, and along certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; the construction of branch or lateral sewers along certain streets and avenues and providing for the necessary outfall, and in The City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 7132 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds", adopting the maps, plans and specifications for such improvement.
