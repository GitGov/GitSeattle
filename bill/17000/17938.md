 An ordinance authorizing the dismissal of the suit to condemn, appropriate and damage private property for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of North and East 80th Street, from Woodland Park Avenue to 15th Avenue Northeast in the City of Seattle, under Ordinance No. 19426, Cause No. 64757, in so far as the same affects that portion of the south 30 feet of the south half of the Southeast quarter of the Northeast quarter of the Northeast quarter of Section 6, Township 25 North, Range 4 East, W.M., lying east of the west margin, produced north, of Wallingford Avenue from the north; also that portion of the Southeast quarter of the Northeast quarter of the Northeast quarter of Section 6, described as follows, to-wit: beginning at a point which is distant 8 feet west from the Southwest corner of lot 1, block 8, in H.
