**Council Bill Number: 17876**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29582**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 22830, creating Local Improvement District No. 2232, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 23rd Avenue Southwest from north line of Star Addition to the north margin of West Genesee Street; 24th Avenue Southwest and 25th Avenue Southwest, from the north line of Faegre's 2nd Addition to the City of Seattle to the north margin of West Genesee Street; West Dakota Street, from the east margin of 30th Avenue Southwest to the west margin of 23rd Avenue Southwest; West Andover Street from the east margin of 26th Avenue Southwest to the west margin of 22nd Avenue Southwest; by clearing and grubbing and temporary grading, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2480, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said  district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds' as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved December 28, 1909 and published January 3, 1910.
