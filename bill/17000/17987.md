**Council Bill Number: 17987**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29641**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 14th Avenue Northeast, from East 50th Street to Meadow Drive, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt and other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, providing for the necessary surface drainage,; and that such other work be done as may be necessary in connection therewith; all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 47773, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
