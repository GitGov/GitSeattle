**Ordinance Number: 29491**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Ninth Avenue South, from Yesler Way to Washington Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading, construction of wooden curbs and gutters, planking, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3387 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
