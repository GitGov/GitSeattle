**Council Bill Number: 17693**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29409**
********

 An Ordinance approving assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2320 for the improvement of Twenty-sixth Avenue North and Twenty-sixth Avenue North Produced, from the north boundary of Interlaken Addition to the south line of the Lake Washington Canal right of way and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein names, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of the proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, and including as a part of said improvement, the construction of a proper outfall to Lake Washington, under Ordinance No. 24162, and levying and assessing the amount thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
