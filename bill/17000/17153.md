**Council Bill Number: 17153**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28890**
********

 An ordinance relating to the maintenance of dairies for the production of certified milk and certified cream, to the keeping, production, handling, transportation, sale and disposition of certified milk and certified cream, providing for the inspection, regulations, and control of all dairies, utensils and general equipment used therein and such products thereof, of the manner of keeping, producing, handling, transporting, selling and disposing of certified milk and certified cream, the issuance of certificates, the appointment of a certified milk commission, providing penalties for the violation thereof and the repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
