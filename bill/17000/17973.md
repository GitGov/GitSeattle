**Ordinance Number: 29639**

 An ordinance amending section 195 of Ordinance No. 17240 (building ordinance), entitled: "An ordinance relating to and regulating the construction, erection, alteration, arrangement, equipment, repair, removal, demolition, use and occupation of buildings and structures, and the use and occupation of lands and premises by and in connection with buildings and structures within the limits of the City of Seattle, and within certain specified districts and localities therein, providing for the issuance of permits therefor, providing for the condemnation, closing and vacation of buildings and structures dangerous to persons and property, defining powers and duties of certain officers in relation thereto, providing penalties for violations thereof, declaring certain things done in violation of this ordinance to be nuisances, and providing for the abatement thereof", approved October 26, 1907, as amended by Ordinance No. 17602, which became a law pursuant to the provisions of the city charter December 18, 1907, as amended by Ordinance No. 22107, approved October 18, 1909, for the purpose of permitting certain changes as to requirements for minor alterations in Class "C" buildings.
