**Council Bill Number: 17896**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29561**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2496, for the improvement of 25th Avenue Southwest from the north margin of West Genesee Street north, to the south margin of West Andover Street, all in the City of Seattle, by clearing, grubbing, and grading to a permanent elevation, full width of same, constructing wooden curbs, wooden gutters, wooden cross walks, and wooden sidewalks, four feet in width on both sides of the street; under Ordinance No. 27361 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
