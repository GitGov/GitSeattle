**Ordinance Number: 29777**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 6498, entitled; "An ordinance granting to John S. McGroaty and George C. Blanker, their successors and assigns, the right and privilege to construct and maintain conduits for an underground telephone and telegraph system, to erect and maintain telephone and telephone poles, and to run, suspend, maintain and operate wires for a telephone and telegraph system, for a period of 25 years, in, over, upon and under the streets, alleys and public highways of the City of Seattle", which became law under the provision of the City Charter, January 21, 1901; and requiring the grantees, successors and assigns thereof to remove from the streets, alleys and public places of the city within 90 days from the taking effect of this ordinance, all property therein or thereon belonging to them and placed there pursuant to said Ordinance No. 6498.
