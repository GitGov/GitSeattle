**Council Bill Number: 17738**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29446**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Charles Street and Norman Street, each from Twenty-third Avenue South to Twenty-sixth Avenue South; Twenty-third Avenue South, Twenty-fourth Avenue South and Twenty-fifth Avenue, from Dearborn Street to Norman Street; and Twenty-sixth Avenue South, from Dearborn Street to Judkins Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper foundation, and providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Resolution No. 3397 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
