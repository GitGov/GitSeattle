**Council Bill Number: 17822**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29492**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Nickerson Street, from Third Avenue West to the westerly margin of Sixth Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the same, by constructing curbs and gutters; by constructing concrete sidewalks, and by making all necessary provisions to care for the surface drainage, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 47468, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of'Payment by Bonds'.
