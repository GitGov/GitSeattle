**Council Bill Number: 17697**
   
**Ordinance Number: 29412**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Eleventh Avenue West, from the south margin of West Galer Street to the north margin of West Howe Street; West Galer Street, West Garfield Street, West Blaine Street and West Howe Street, each from Tenth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with concrete curbs, and armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work, the constructing and reconstruction of all sewers and side sewers, and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, and by the construction and reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary, and by such grading and regrading as may be necessary; all in accordance with Resolution No. 3400 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
