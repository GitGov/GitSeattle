**Council Bill Number: 9154**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21762**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1956, for the improvement of Occidental Avenue from the south margin of Connecticut Street to a connection with the existing bridge on Holgate Street; Atlantic Street and Massachusetts Street, each from First Avenue South to Occidental Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by planking the same with a plank roadway twenty (20) feet in width, to be placed in the center of the street; under Ordinance No. 20318, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
