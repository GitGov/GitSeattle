**Council Bill Number: 9607**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22293**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2127, for the improvement of East Forty-fifth Street, in the City of Seattle, from Fifteenth Avenue Northeast to Nineteenth Avenue Northeast, by grading and curbing the same and constructing the necessary catch basins, surface and subdrains and by constructing a concrete sidewalk on the south side of the street, and by connecting the concrete walks with the curbs on the north side of the street, and by constructing wooden crosswalks thereon, under Ordinance No. 21371, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
