 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 15078 entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Spruce Street, from Thirty-second Avenue to Lake Dell Avenue; Lake Dell Avenue, from East Spruce Street to East Alder Street; East Alder Street, from lake Dell Avenue to Lakeside Avenue; Lakeside Avenue, from the south line of Yesler's Third Addition to the Hugo Place; Hugo Place, from Lakeside Avenue to Grand Avenue; Grand Avenue, from Hugo Place to East Howell Street; East Olive Street, from Thirty-seventh Avenue to Fortieth Avenue; Fortieth Avenue, from East Pine Street to East Olive Street; Thirty-ninth Avenue, from East Pine Street to East Howell Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing water mains therein, or proper dimensions and material together with all tees, crosses, gates, fire hydrants and other appurtenances necessary for the reasonable water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 328, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled' An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, provided for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899 and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899."
