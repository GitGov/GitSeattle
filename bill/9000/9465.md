**Council Bill Number: 9465**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22114**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Howell Street, from 13th Avenue to 18th Avenue; East Olive Street from 13th Avenue to 15th Avenue; and 13th Avenue from East Olive Street to East Howell Street; all in the City of Seattle by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon where necessary and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2382 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
