**Council Bill Number: 9836**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22513**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Beach Drive and West Mercer Street from Elliott Avenue to Fifth Avenue West, and the Alley in Block "E", Kinnear Park Addition, from Beach Drive to a point one hundred twenty (120) feet west of Fifth Avenue West, by constructing sewers therein of the proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, including a suitable wooden box outfall to Elliott Bay, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2471 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
