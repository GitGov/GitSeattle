**Ordinance Number: 23075**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Meridian Avenue, Bagley Avenue and Corliss Avenue, each from North Forty-fifth Street to the north line of the Baltimore Addition, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all wyes, openings, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2361 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
