**Council Bill Number: 9477**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22120**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2008, for the improvement of Boylston Avenue North from East Newton Street to Roanoke Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation and by constructing asphalt, concrete or brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners, and including as a part of said work the construction, alteration or extension of concrete walks and such sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage and water service and fire protection of said district; provided that where grades exceed 8% stone block pavement on a suitable concrete foundation may be used, under Ordinance No. 20413 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
