**Council Bill Number: 9123**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22106**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 19826 entitled: "An ordinance providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of Queen Anne Boulevard as established by Ordinance No. 16790, of the City of Seattle, and approaches thereto, all in the City of Seattle, providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regarding of said Boulevard and approaches thereto, and for the construction of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said Boulevard and approaches thereto; all in the City of Seattle; providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law; and repealing all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith" approved December 30, 1908.
