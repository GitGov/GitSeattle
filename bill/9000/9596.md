**Council Bill Number: 9596**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23078**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Highland Drive from Twenty-second Avenue North to Twenty-third Avenue North, and Twenty-third Avenue North from East Prospect Street to the alley between Blocks Fifty-seven and Fifty-eighth, Capitol Hill Addition Number Five Supplemental, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regarding, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete walks and of sewers and water mains with all their necessary appurtenances, provided, however, that where the grades exceed eight (8%) per cent, stone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation may be substituted for asphalt; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2429 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
