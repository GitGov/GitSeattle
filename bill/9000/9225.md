**Council Bill Number: 9225**
   
**Ordinance Number: 21959**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1987, for the improvement of Federal Avenue in the City of Seattle, from the north line of the Lincoln Pontius Addition to East Prospect Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing brick, concrete or asphalt gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regarding, the constructing of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 20392, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
