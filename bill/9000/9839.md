**Council Bill Number: 9839**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23086**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Montlake Blvd, et al., all in Montlake Park Addition to the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing asphalt gutters and concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, and including as a necessary art of said work all surface and sub drainage, together with underground conduits for street lighting and the erection of iron poles with one light each, as such places as directed by the City Engineer, all in accordance with Majority Petition, being File No. 38075; prescribing the rate of interest thereon and authorizing their delivery to the contractor constructing the improvement.
