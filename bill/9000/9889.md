**Ordinance Number: 22728**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Alki Avenue, from Fifty-third Avenue Southwest to a point about two hundred fifty (250) feet north of the northwest corner of Section Fifteen (15), Township Twenty-four (24) North, Range Three (3) East, W. M., and from West Spokane Street to West Juneau Street; Sixty-third Avenue Southwest, from a point about eight hundred eighteen (818) feet north of West Hanford Street to a point about twelve hundred twenty (1220) feet south of West Hanford Street; West Andover Street, from Alki Avenue to Fifty-fifth Avenue Southwest; Fifty-fifth Avenue Southwest, from West Andover Street to West Dakota Street; West Dakota Street, from Fifty-fifth Avenue Southwest to Fifty-second Avenue Southwest; all in the City of Seattle, by laying water mains therein of proper dimensions and materials together with such gates tees, crosses, fire hydrants, and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2460 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
