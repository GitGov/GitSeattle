**Council Bill Number: 9771**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22221**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 15252 entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Melrose Avenue and Bellevue Avenue, each from East Pike Street to East Denny Way; Summit Avenue and Summit Avenue North, from East Pike Street to East Thomas Street; Belmont Avenue, from East Pike Street to East Howell Street; East Pine Street, from Melrose Avenue to Boylston Avenue; East Olive Street, from Melrose Avenue to Boylston Avenue; East Howell Street, from Bellevue Avenue to Boylston Avenue; East Denny Way, from Eastlake Avenue to Belmont Avenue North; East John Street, from Summit Avenue North to Belmont Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing concrete curbs, with granite corners and brick gutters, including all grading and regarding; the repairing, alteration and extension of concrete sidewalks where necessary, together with the construction of all watermains and sewers, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, water service and fire protection, all in accordance with Resolution No. 373 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle."

**Status:** Passed
