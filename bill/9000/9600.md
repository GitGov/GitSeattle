**Council Bill Number: 9600**
   
**Ordinance Number: 23082**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of University View Place from East Fiftieth Street to Thirty-fourth Avenue Northeast; Thirty-third Avenue Northeast and Thirty-fourth Avenue Northeast, each from University View Place to East Fiftieth Street; East Fiftieth Street and East Fiftieth Street produced from University View Place to Railroad Avenue Northeast; Railroad Avenue Northeast from Thirty-fifth Avenue Northeast to the west boundary of Exposition Heights Addition; thence along a right of way to be obtained across private property from the west boundary of Exposition Heights Addition to Twenty-sixth Avenue Northeast; Twenty-sixth Avenue Northeast from the right of way of the Northern Pacific Railway to East Fifty-fourth Street, by constructing sewers therein of the proper dimensions and materials, together with all wyes, openings, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; all in accordance with Resolution No. 2424 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
