**Council Bill Number: 9844**
   
**Ordinance Number: 22475**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1986, for the improvement of 15th Avenue Northeast in the City of Seattle, between East 50th Street and East 55th Street; by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with stone, brick, concrete or granite corners, and by the construction of all water mains, sewers and side sewer connection necessary, and by the construction of necessary surface and sub-drains, and by the construction of a concrete sidewalk along the west side of 15th Avenue Northeast; between East 50th Street and East 52nd Street; under Ordinance No.20391 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
