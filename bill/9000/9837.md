**Ordinance Number: 23085**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West 48th Street, from Sixth Avenue North to 17th Avenue North; West 49th Street, from 6th Avenue Northwest to Eleventh Avenue Northwest; West 49th Street, from Fourteenth Avenue Northwest to Seventeenth Avenue Northwest ; and Fourteenth Avenue Northwest from West 48th Street to West 49th Street. all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2468 of the City Council of the City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City Of Seattle.
