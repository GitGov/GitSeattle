**Ordinance Number: 21614**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North Seventy-third Street, from Greenwood Avenue to First Avenue Northwest; West Seventy-third Street, from Third Avenue Northwest to Eighth Avenue Northwest; Palatine Avenue and First Avenue Northwest, each from North Sixty-fifth Street to North Seventy-third Street; Murphy Place and Second Avenue Northwest, each from West Sixty-fifth Street to West Seventy-third Street; Third Avenue Northwest, Fourth Avenue Northwest, Fifth Avenue Northwest, and Sixth Avenue Northwest, each from West Sixty-seventh Street to West Seventy-third Street; Seventh Avenue Northwest, from West Fifty-fifth Street to a point three hundred thirty (330) feet north of the north line of West Seventieth Street; Eighth Avenue Northwest, from West Sixty-fifth Street to West Seventieth Street; and West Sixty-fifth Street, from Sixth Avenue Northwest to Eighth Avenue Northwest, all in the City of Seattle, by laying water mains therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 2251 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
