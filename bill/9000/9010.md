**Ordinance Number: 21541**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-first Avenue Southwest, from West Andover Street to Colby Street; Twentieth Avenue Southwest and Nineteenth Avenue Southwest, each from the south line of Hasslocher's Addition to the north line of White Bros.' Addition; Colby street, from Twenty-second Avenue Southwest to Iowa Street; and West Andover Street, from Twenty-first Avenue Southwest to Nineteenth Avenue Southwest, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing thereon wood walks and stairways; and the temporary grading of Twenty-first Avenue Southwest from West Andover Street to Colby Street, and Colby Street, from Twenty-first Avenue Southwest to Twentieth Avenue Southwest, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2188 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
