 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 15322 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West and North 50th Street, from 3rd Avenue Northwest to Fremont Avenue; Bowdoin Place, from Phinney Avenue to Dayton Avenue; North 39th Street, from Phinney Avenue to Fremont Avenue; Evanston Avenue, from North 50th Street to Bowdoin Place; Dayton Avenue, from North 50th Street to Kilbourne Street; Sunset Place from North 45th Street to North 42nd Street; and from North 39th Street to Kilbourne Street; Phinney Avenue from North 50th Street to Ewing Street; Palatine Avenue, from North 45th Street to the south line of Palatine Hill Addition; 1st Avenue Northwest form North 50th Street to the south line of Palatine Hill Addition; 2nd Avenue Northwest from West 50th Street to West Kilbourne Street; Baker Avenue, from West 47th Street to Ross Place; 3rd Avenue Northwest from West 50th Street to West 45th Street; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and by constructing necessary catch basins and inlets and other appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 414, of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
