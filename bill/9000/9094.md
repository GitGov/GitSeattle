 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1825, for the improvement of Ninth Avenue Northwest, from West Fifty-eighth Street to West Sixty-fifth Street; Eighth Avenue Northwest and Eleventh Avenue Northwest, each from West Sixtieth Street to West Sixty-fifth Street; and West Sixtieth Street, West Sixty-first Street and West Sixty-second Street, each from Eighth Avenue Northwest to Fourteenth Avenue Northwest, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden crosswalks thereon, together with all necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage; under Ordinance No. 19017, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
