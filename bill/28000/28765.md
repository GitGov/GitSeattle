**Council Bill Number: 28765**
   
**Ordinance Number: 39505**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the payment of the cost and expense of the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of a public street and highway to be known as West Marginal Way, from the intersection of Iowa Avenue and West Dakota Street to a point in First Avenue South (said point being near the intersection of First Avenue South and the center line produced west of Webster Street, as platted in River Park Addition); providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of West Marginal Way, as laid off, opened, extended and established herein, from West Dakota Street to First Avenue South; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of West Marginal Way, and for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of said West Marginal Way and for the grading and regrading of said way and approaches thereto; and providing that the entire cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessments upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law, as provided in Ordinance No. 38205 of the City of Seattle, by the issuance and sale of bonds of the district to pay therefor.
