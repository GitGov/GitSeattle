**Ordinance Number: 38897**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Orcas Street, from Sixth Avenue South to Corson Avenue; Homer Street, from Maynard Avenue to Corson Avenue; Findlay Street, from Sixth Avenue South to Seventh Avenue South; Lucile Street, from Sixth Avenue Soth to Seventh Avenue South; Lucile Street (South Side), from Seventh Avenue South to Duwamish Avenue; Brandon Street (South Side), from Sixth Avenue South to Colorado Avenue; Colorado Avenue (Southwesterly Side), from Bennett Street to Lucile Street; Maynard Avenue, from Colorado Avenue to Orcas Street; Seventh Avenue South, from Lucile Street to Fidalgo Street; Eight Avenue South (West Side), from Lucile Street to Homer Street; Padilla Place, from Homer Street to Fidalgo Street; and Corson Avenue (West Side), from Duwamish Avenue to Michigan Street; all in the City of Seattle. All in accordance with Resolution No. 5903 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
