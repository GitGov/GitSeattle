**Ordinance Number: 39143**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Leary Avenue, from Twentieth Avenue Northwest to Seventeenth Avenue Northwest; West Forty-eighth Street, from Seventeenth Avenue Northwest to Ninth Avenue Northwest; Leary Way, from Ninth Avenue Northwest to Second Avenue Northwest; West Thirty-sixth Street, from Second Avenue Northwest to First Avenue Northwest; North Thirty-sixth Street, from First Avenue Northwest to Evanston Avenue; Fremont Place from Evanston Avenue to Fremont Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5997, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
