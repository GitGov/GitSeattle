**Ordinance Number: 39527**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fiftieth Avenue South, from Brandon Street to Andover Street; Forty-nine Avenue South, from Hudson Street to Dawson Street; Dawson Street and Mayflower Street, each from Forty-eighth Avenue South to Fiftieth Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle; all in accordance with Resolution No. 6035 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
