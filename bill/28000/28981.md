**Ordinance Number: 39679**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Wilson Avenue, from Hudson Street to Gratton Street; Graton Street, from Wilson Avenue to Rainier Avenue; Genesee Street, from Rainier Avenue to 50th Avenue South; 50th Avenue South, from Genesee Street to Wilson Avenue, Dawson Street, from Wilson Avenue to 54th Avenue South; Seward Park Avenue, from 54th Avenue South to Wilson Avenue; Juneau Street, from Seward Park Avenue to Lakeside Avenue; 57th Avenue South, from Seward Park Avenue to Holly Street; and Holly Street, from 57th Avenue South to a point 384 feet west therefrom; all in the City of Seattle. All in accordance with Resolution No. 6108 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds", and declaring an emergency.
