**Ordinance Number: 39675**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twelfth Avenue Northeast, from East Fiftieth Street to Ravenna Boulevard; East Fifty-second Street, East Fifty-fifth Street and East Fifty-eight Street, each from Twelfth Avenue Northeast to Brooklyn Avenue; and The South side of Ravenna Boulevard, from Eleventh Avenue Northeast to Twelfth Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle; all in accordance with Resolution No. 6164 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds", and declaring an emergency.
