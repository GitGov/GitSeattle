**Council Bill Number: 104400**
   
**Ordinance Number: 111907**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 13, plat of Central Seattle (commonly known as McKinney's Plat of Central Seattle), being the alley between 13th Avenue South and 14th Avenue South from South Walker Street to South Hill Street; by paving the same, by doing such grading or regrading as may be necessary, providing for the necessary surface drainage; in accord with Resolution 27125 of the City Council of the City of Seattle; creating Local Improvement District No. 6743 and providing for payment of the cost and expense of the improvements by special assessments upon property within the district by payment by bonds.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** September 17, 1984
   
**Vote:** 9-0
   
**Date of Mayor's signature:** September 21, 1984
