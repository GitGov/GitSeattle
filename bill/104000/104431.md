**Ordinance Number: 111840**

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 10, Plat of Dumar, Division No. 2, being the alley between 18th Avenue Southwest and 17th Avenue Southwest from Southwest Kenyon Street to Southwest Holden Street; by paving the same, by doing such grading or regarding as may be necessary, providing for the necessary surface drainage; in accord with Resolution 27128 of the City Council of the City of Seattle; creating Local Improvement District No. 6742 and providing for payment of the cost and expense of the improvement from the Housing and Community Development Revenue Sharing Fund and by special assessments upon property within the district.
