**Council Bill Number: 104585**
   
**Ordinance Number: 112033**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Municipal Water System; providing for the defeasance or release by anticipatory marshalling of the claim of lien of all of the outstanding $1,300,000 par value of "Seattle Municipal Water Revenue Bonds, 1961"; authorizing the transfer of money and investments and investing or reinvesting the same to accomplish the defeasance; authorizing and appointing the City Treasurer to serve as trustee and authorizing the executiion of an agreement with an alternate trustee; creating a Bond Defeasance Fund of the City and a 1961 Water Revenue Bonds Defeasance Subfund; abandoning certain of the additions and betterments to and extensions of the Municipal Water System authorized by and amending Section 3 of Ordinance 90505, accordingly.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** November 26, 1984
   
**Vote:** 7-0
