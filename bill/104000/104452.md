**Ordinance Number: 111860**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Criminal Code; repealing Section 12A.06.080 (sexual abuse); Section 12A.10.030 (promoting prostitution); Section 12A.20.020 (unlawful to distribute or possess controlled substances); Section 12A.20.040 (affirmative defenses, controlled substances); amending Section 12A.20.010 (definitions, controlled substances); Section 12A.20.030 (penalties, controlled substances) to bring the Criminal Code into conformity with State law by repealing ordinances in conflict with State penalty provisions; and amending Chapter 12A.12 (offenses against public Order) by adding a new Section 12A.12.040 thereto, relating to conduct on public buses; defining crimes and prescribing penalties.
