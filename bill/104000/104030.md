 AN ORDINANCE relating to the City Employees' Retirement System, amending Seattle Municipal Code subsection 4.36.130 F to authorize the wider range of investment authorized by RCW 35.39.060; amending SMC subsections 4.36.130 G and H, to authorize registration and custodial services for securities contemplated by RCW 35.39.070; amending SMC 4.36.140 A to provide for an investment advisory committee as contemplated by RCW 35.39.080-090; and adding a new section to authorize hypothecation of retirement system securities for a fee.
