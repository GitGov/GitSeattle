 AN ORDINANCE relating to the municipal light and power system and authorizing an agreement between the City of Seattle and Pend Oreille County to provide Pend Oreille County, the municipalities of Metaline, Metaline Falls and Ione, and Mt. Linton Hospital District with certain assistance as compensation for the loss of revenues and increased financial burden occasioned by the City's construction and installation of two new water turbines and electrical generators in the Boundary hydroelectric project located within Pend Oreille County.
