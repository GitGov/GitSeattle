**Ordinance Number: 40990**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 15th Avenue Northwest, from the north margin of West 47th Street to the north margin of West 73rd Street' 19th Avenue Northwest and 21st Avenue Northwest, each from the south margin of Sloop Place to the north margin of Canoe Place; 20th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to Sloop Place; 24th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to the north margin of West 73rd Street; 26th Avenue Northwest and 28th Avenue Northwest, each from West 59th Street to the north margin of West 62nd Street; West 60th Street and West 62nd Street, each from 26th Avenue Northwest, to 28th Avenue Northwest; West 61st Street, from 15th Avenue Northwest to 26th Avenue Northwest and from 28th Avenue Northwest to 32nd Avenue Northwest; West 70th Street, from 7th Avenue Northwest to 19th Avenue Northwest and from 21st Avenue Northwest to 24th Avenue Northwest; Sloop Place and Canoe Place, each from 19th Avenue Northwest to 21st Avenue Northwest; West 73rd Street, from 15th Avenue Northwest to 24th Avenue Northwest; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6442, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
