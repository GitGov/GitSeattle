**Ordinance Number: 41533**

 An ordinance authorizing the dismissal of a suit for the condemnation, appropriation, taking of certain real estate, as provided for by Ordinance No. 36755, being Cause No. 120973, insofar as the same affects certain property in Block One (1), First Plat of West Seattle by the West Seattle Land and Improvement Company, and in Block Sixty-four (64), in the Replat of the West Seattle Land and Improvement Company's Third Plat; and in Blocks Four Hundred Fifty (450), Four Hundred Fifty-four (454), Four Hundred Fifty-five (455) and Four Hundred Fifty-eight (458), Seattle Tidelands; and certain unplatted property between Sunset Place and Alki Avenue; and appropriating money to pay all taxable costs incurred by the parties to said condemnation suit whose property is dismissed by this ordinance.
