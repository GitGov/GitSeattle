**Ordinance Number: 40924**

 An ordinance appropriating the sum of Three Hundred Fifty ($350.00) Dollars or so much thereof as may be necessary, from the General Fund of the City of Seattle to the State of Washington, for the purpose of paying the judgements, with interest, in favor of the State of Washington in the condemnation of Burien Way, instituted under Ordinance No. 38654; authorizing the drawing of a warrant therefor; providing for the repayment of said appropriation to the General Fund from the first moneys coming into Local Improvement Condemnation Award, District No. 3240; appropriating the sum of Three Hundred Fifty ($350.00) Dollars from the Local Improvement Condemnation Award, District No. 3240, of he City of Seattle to the General Fund of the City of Seattle; authorizing the drawing of a warrant therefor; and declaring an emergency.
