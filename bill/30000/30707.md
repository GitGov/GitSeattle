**Ordinance Number: 41306**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Northlake Avenue and East Northlake Avenue, from the west margin of Stone Way to the east margin of Pasadena Place; Northlake Place, from Northlake Avenue to North 34th Street; and 6th Avenue Northeast, from East Northlake Avenue to a connection with the existing pavement at East 40th Street; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6471 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds" and declaring an emergency.
