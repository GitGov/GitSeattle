**Ordinance Number: 41083**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Yale Avenue North, from Yale Place North to Roanoke Street; Yale Place North, from Eastlake Avenue to Yale Avenue North; Minor Avenue North, from East Newton Street to Roanoke Street; East Newton Street, East Boston Street, East Lynn Street, and Roanoke Street, each from Eastlake Avenue to Fairview Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6423 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds" and declaring an emergency.
