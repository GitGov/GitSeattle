**Council Bill Number: 30666**
   
**Ordinance Number: 41280**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Shilshole Avenue, from 24th Avenue Northwest to the east margin of 14th Avenue Northwest; 14th Avenue Northwest, from Shilshole Avenue to West 48th Street; 20th Avenue Northwest, Vernon Place and 22nd Avenue Northwest, each from Shilshole Avenue to Ballard Avenue; 24th Avenue Northwest, from Market Street to a point 95-feet, more or less, south from the southerly margin of Shilshole Avenue; all in the City of Seattle; all in accordance with Resolution No. 6481 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds" and declaring an emergency.
