**Ordinance Number: 41003**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of 1st Avenue North, from McGraw Street to Smith Street; Warren Avenue, from Boston Street to Smith Street; 2nd Avenue North, from Boston Street to McGraw Place; McGraw Street, from 1st Avenue North to 2nd Avenue North; Lynn Street, from 1st Avenue North to 2nd Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6436 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
