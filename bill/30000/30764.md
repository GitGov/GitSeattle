 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Westlake Avenue, from Denny Way to 7th Avenue; 6th Avenue and 7th Avenue each from Westlake Avenue to Pine Street; 8th Avenue, from Westlake Avenue to Olive Street; 9th Avenue, from Virginia Street to Denny Way; Olive Street, from Westlake Avenue to 8th Avenue Stewart Street, from Westlake Avenue to Alley between 8th Avenue and 9th Avenue; Virginia Street, from Westlake Avenue to 9th Avenue; Lenora Street, from Westlake Avenue to 9th Avenue; and Denny Way, from Westlake Avenue to 9th Avenue; all in The City of Seattle; all in accordance with Resolution No. 6271 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds" and declaring an emergency.
