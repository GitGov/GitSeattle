**Council Bill Number: 1948**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15251**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Madison Street from 19th Avenue to 120 feet east of 29th Avenue from the south, by paving the same with sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite corners (except however, those portions of East Madison Street lying from 160 feet east of the east margin of 20th Avenue on the north to 140 feet east of 22nd Avenue on the north; from 120 feet east of 29th Avenue on the north to 120 feet east of 29th Avenue on the south, which portions shall be paved with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation) and by the construction of sewers, and water mains, together with appurtenances as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 358 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
