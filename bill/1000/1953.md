**Council Bill Number: 1953**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15256**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 29th Avenue from Yesler Way to East Cherry Street; 30th Avenue from Yesler Way to East Cherry Street; and East Spruce Street from 29th Avenue to 30th Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs with granite corners (except however, East Spruce Street from 29th Avenue to 30th Avenue, which shall be paved with sandstone blocks laid upon a proper concrete foundation), all in accordance with Resolution No. 352 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
