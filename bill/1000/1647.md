**Ordinance Number: 14929**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 7415, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Queen Anne Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Roy Street to Comstock Street, by curbing and planking the same, curbs on the west side of the street between Prospect and Comstock Streets, to be placed nine feet from the property margin; on the remaining portions of the street to be placed twelve feet from the property margins; all that portion of the roadway between said curbs, excepting that required to be maintained by virtue of a double track street railway franchise to be planked; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment", under the provisions of Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899."
