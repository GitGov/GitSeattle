**Council Bill Number: 1709**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15075**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 2nd Avenue Northeast from East 45th Street to Pacific Place; and Pacific Place, from 2nd Avenue Northeast to a connection with the existing sewer in Latona Avenue, all in the City of Seattle by constructing therein sewers of proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary manholes, wyes, openings, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 321 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
