**Ordinance Number: 14864**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 13984, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fortieth Avenue North, from East Garfield Street to East Aloha Street; Forty-first Avenue North, from East Madison Street to East Prospect Street; Forty-second Avenue North, from one hundred twenty (120) feet north of East Blaine Street to Lake Washington; and East Lee Street, from Fortieth Avenue North to Lake Washington, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein sewers of proper dimensions, together with all necessary wyes, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; there shall be also be included as a necessary part of said system the construction of a proper outfall sewer; the whole system to be constructed in accordance with Resolution No. 113 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899."
