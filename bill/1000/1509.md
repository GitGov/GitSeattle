 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 7880, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of that portion of Seattle Boulevard, in the City of Seattle, from Fourth Avenue South to Oriental Avenue, and Oriental Avenue, in said City of Seattle, from Seattle Boulevard to the north line of Weller Street, by constructing therein a plank roadway with pilling supports, in accordance with the following plan: said roadway to be twenty-four (24) feet wide, with a sidewalk on one side thereof four (4) feet wide, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," under the provisions of Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899."
