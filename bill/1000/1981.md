**Council Bill Number: 1981**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15850**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the refunding of the excess portions of all special assessments levied for the improvement of alley in block 65 A. A. Denny's Addition, by grading same under Ordinance No. 7099, approved December 18, 1901, creating Local Improvement District No. 464, and authorizing the City Treasurer to prepare a tabulated statement showing the amounts of the refunds due on the lots and parcels of land in said assessment district, and the names of the persons paying such ascertainment and appropriating out of the special fund of said local improvement district the amounts necessary to pay such refunds, and authorizing and directing the Mayor and City Comptroller to draw warrants on such special fund in favor of the persons paying such assessments for the several amounts due such payors, as shown by said tabulated statement and authorizing the City Treasurer to pay said warrants out of the special fund, and repealing all ordinances of parts of ordinances in conflict herewith.
