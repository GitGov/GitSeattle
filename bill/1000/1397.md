**Ordinance Number: 14703**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 9109, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the following named streets and avenues, in the City of Seattle, to-wit: East Lake Avenue, from East Boston Street to Twelfth Avenue North; Franklin Avenue from East Boston Street to Twelfth Avenue North; Harvard Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Millard Street; North Broadway, form Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Tenth Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Twelfth Avenue North, from Shelby Street to Franklin Avenue; East Boston Street, from East Lake Avenue to Franklin Avenue; East Lynn Street, from East Lake Avenue to Boylston Avenue; Roanoke Street, from East Lake Avenue to Tenth Avenue North; Shelby Street from East Lake Avenue to Twelfth Avenue North; Millard Street, from Harvard Avenue North to Twelfth Avenue North, and Allison Street from Franklin Avenue to Twelfth Avenue North, by laying water mains therein, together with all necessary appurtenances, under Majority Petition, being Clerk's File No. 15275, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 said city, approved December 6, 1899."; and repealing Ordinances No. 8391 and No. 8911."
