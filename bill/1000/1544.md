**Ordinance Number: 14851**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 11468, entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of University Street, in the City of Seattle, from Ninth Avenue to a point one hundred twenty (120) feet west of Sixth Avenue, by grading the same and by constructing concrete sidewalks on both side thereof, together with wooden curbs, gutters and crosswalks; provided, that the roadway between Eighth Avenue and Ninth Avenue shall be graded to connect with the existing roadway on Ninth Avenue north of University Street; the sidewalks along the south side of University Street to be connected with the existing sidewalk at University Street and Ninth Avenue with a concrete stairway, in accordance with Resolution No. 241 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
