**Council Bill Number: 1954**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15257**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 30th Avenue South from Dearborn Street to Yesler Way; 29th Avenue South, from Jackson Street to Yesler Way; 28th Avenue South from Jackson Street to Yesler Way; Washington Street, from 28th Avenue South to 30th Avenue South, Main Street from the west line of 28th Avenue South to the north, all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, all in accordance with Resolution No. 363 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
