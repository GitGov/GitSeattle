**Council Bill Number: 1619**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14901**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 4838, entitled, "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Republican Street and East Republican Street, in the City of Seattle, from Minor Avenue North to the alley between Melrose Avenue North and Bellevue Avenue North, by the constructing of an ironstone pipe sewer of proper dimensions, through said portions of said streets, with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, lampholes, ventilators, catch basins and flushing tanks, and providing for the payment to therefor by the mode of immediate payment, and establishing of a fund for such payment by an assessment upon all lots and parcels of land benefited by such improvement, under the provisions of Ordinance No. 2085, approved April 29th, 1892, as amended by Ordinance No. 3350, approved May 8th, 1894, and Ordinance No. 4492, approved May 5th, 1897, and the City Charter as now in force."
