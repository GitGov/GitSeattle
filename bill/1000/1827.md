**Council Bill Number: 1827**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15107**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 14, Capitol Hill Addition to the City of Seattle, Division No. 2, same being the alley between 15th Avenue North and 16th Avenue North; from East Roy Street to East Aloha Street, all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, including such grading as may be necessary and the construction of all water mains and sewers, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, and drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 372 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
