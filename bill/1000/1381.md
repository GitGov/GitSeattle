**Council Bill Number: 1381**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14687**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1230, for the improvement of Fifth Avenue North, from Denny Way to the North line of Mercer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters and concrete curbs, with granite corners, and by constructing all necessary sewers, with appurtenances, for the proper drainage and sewerage system of said district, and by constructing all necessary water mains and necessary appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 13463 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
