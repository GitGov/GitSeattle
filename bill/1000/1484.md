**Council Bill Number: 1484**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14784**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fourth Avenue and Fourth Avenue South, from Union Street to Washington Street; Washington Street, from Fourth Avenue South to Fifth Avenue South; Yesler Way, from a point one hundred thirty-six and eight tenths (136.8) feet East of the West line of Third Avenue South to Fifth Avenue South; Terrace Street, from Fourth Avenue to Fifth Avenue; Jefferson Street, James Street, Cherry Street, Columbia Street, Marion Street and Madison Street, each from Third Avenue to Fifth Avenue; Spring Street and Seneca Street, each from Third Avenue to Seventh Avenue; Fifth Avenue, from Seneca Street to Madison Street, and Sixth Avenue, from University Street to Madison Street, all in the City of Seattle, and of the approaches to the different alleys now located in the several blocks bounded by said streets and avenues, or any of them, and of the approaches to said streets or avenues, to such distance back from such streets, avenues and alleys as may be necessary to make proper and suitable approaches thereto, by grading and regrading the same to conform to the respective elevation above city datum as established by Ordinance No. 13074; and including, as a necessary part of said work, the construction of a bridge to carry Yesler Way and Terrace Street over Fourth Avenue; the reconstruction, alteration and extensions of the existing sewer system, together with such temporary adjustment of the existing sewers as may be necessary during the progress of said work and the construction of the necessary slopes and retaining walls for cuts and fills, in accordance with Resolution No. 308 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
