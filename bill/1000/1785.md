**Council Bill Number: 1785**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15103**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Burke Avenue from North 44th Street to Blewett Street; Wallingford Avenue from North 43rd Street to Ewing Street; Densmore Avenue, from 120 feet south of North 45th Street south of North 45th Street to Ewing Street; Woodlawn Avenue, from North 43rd Street to Ewing Street; Ashworth Avenue, from North 39th Street to Blewett Street; Interlake Avenue from North 39th Street to Blewett Street; Blewett Street from Burket Avenue to Walingford Avenue and from Carr Place to Interlake Avenue; and Ewing Street from existing sewer in Ewing Street; all in the City of Seattle by constructing therein sewers of such dimensions and materials as may be required, together with all necessary wyes, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other necessary appurtenances for the proper drainage and sewerage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 335 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
