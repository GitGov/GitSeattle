**Council Bill Number: 1379**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14787**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Westlake Avenue from Pike Street to Denny Way, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sidewalks thereon, on both sides thereof, together with all necessary grading, bulkheads and retaining walls, as follows: From Pike Street to Seventh Avenue, sidewalks to be constructed of concrete; from Seventh Avenue to Denny Way, sidewalks to be constructed of wood.
