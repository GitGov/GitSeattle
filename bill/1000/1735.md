 An ordinance authorizing and directing the Board of Park Commissioners of the City of Seattle, to purchase all lands, interest, and rights whatsoever between Green Lake Boulevard, and the center of Green Lake and comprise in the lots, blocks and descriptions above referred to more particularly described as follows: all the unplatted tracts in government lot 1, section 6, township 25 north, range 4 east, W.M., lying between the south margin of Green Lake Boulevard and the center of Green Lake, and between a southerly production of Wallingford Avenue and a Southeast production of the boundary line separating the lands of S. Levinson, and W. D. Wood, for use as a park and parkway, and providing for method of paying the same.
