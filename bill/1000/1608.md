**Council Bill Number: 1608**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14892**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1177, for the improvement of Prefontaine Place, from Washington Street to Yesler Way, and Third Avenue, from Yesler Way to Pike Street, by paving with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation; Third Avenue South, from Washington Street to Yesler Way; Yesler Way, from Second Avenue to a point one hundred thirty-six (136) feet east of the east line of Third Avenue South; Jefferson Street, from Yesler Way to Third Avenue; James Street, Cherry Street, Columbia Street, Marion Street, Madison Street, Spring Street, Seneca Street and University Street, all from Second Avenue to Third Avenue, by paving with sandstone blocks laid upon proper concrete foundation, and by constructing sewers of proper dimensions, together with all necessary appurtenances, and water mains, together with all necessary appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 12993, and levying and assessing the amount thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof, shown on said roll, declaring the same to be a first lien thereon and providing for the collection of said assessment.
