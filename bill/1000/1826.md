**Council Bill Number: 1826**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15106**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of the alley in block 5, A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, same being the alley between 2nd Avenue and 3rd Avenue; from University Street to Seneca Street; by paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, including such grading and re-grading as may be necessary for the construction of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 349 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
