**Council Bill Number: 1058**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14882**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, widening, extending and establishing of Olive Street, between Westlake Avenue and Eighth Avenue; and Howell Street, between Eighth Avenue and Denny Way, all in the City of Seattle, as public streets and highways, over and across certain lots, blocks, tracks and parcels of land, and providing for the taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation to be made for the private property to be taken or damaged for said purpose, and for an assessment upon the property benefited for the purpose of making such compensation.
