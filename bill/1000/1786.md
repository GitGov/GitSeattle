**Ordinance Number: 15104**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Temple Place from Washington Street to Main Street; Main Street, form Temple Place to 28th Avenue South; 28th Avenue South, from Main Street to Yesler Way, all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, and by constructing concrete sidewalks, all in accordance with Resolution No. 337 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
