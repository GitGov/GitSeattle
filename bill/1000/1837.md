**Ordinance Number: 15117**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of, all in accordance with Resolution No. 13th Avenue South, from Grand Street to College Street; 15th Avenue South, from Grand Street to Beacon Avenue; 16th Avenue South, from Holgate Street to Bayview Street; Holgate Street, from 12th Avenue South to 14th Avenue South; Plum Street, from 12th Avenue South to 14th Avenue South; Plum Street and Hill Street, each from 13th Avenue South to 16th Avenue South; Walker Street and College Street, each from 13th Avenue South to 17th Avenue South; Bayview Street, from 14th Avenue South to 17th Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, in accordance with Resolution No. 360 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
