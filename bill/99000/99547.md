**Ordinance Number: 107494**

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of South Bond St. from Beacon Ave. S. to approximately 220 feet east thereof; and from approximately 305 feet east of Beacon Ave. S. to the westerly line of the City of Seattle Transmission Line Right of Way; and a certain other street, avenues, an alley, and easements; by constructing sanitary sewers and otherwise providing the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 25788 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a Local Improvement District therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by Special Assessments upon property in said District payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
