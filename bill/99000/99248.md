**Council Bill Number: 99248**
   
**Ordinance Number: 107259**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the improvement of the alley in Block 4, Fern Hill Addition and in Block 1, Wabash Addition, being a continuous alley between 47th Avenue Southwest and 46th Avenue Southwest from S.W. Heinze Way to S.W. Hudson St.; by paving the same; by doing such grading or regrading as may be necessary; providing for the necessary surface drainage; all in accordance with Res. No. 25427 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
