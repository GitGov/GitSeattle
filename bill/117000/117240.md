 ORDINANCE _________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of $18,359,678.55 on Summit mechanical warrants numbered 4001895379-- 4001898647 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, E- Payables of $146,964.38 on Summit 9000003769 -- 9000003833 and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of $14,878,198.06 are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of $34,024,105.04 on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 51139301-- 51140680 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 300001 -- 307979 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date July 19, 2011 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council July 28, 2011 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 4. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force 30 days after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Seattle Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
