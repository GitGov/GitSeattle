 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of $8,096,538.53 on Summit mechanical warrants numbered 4001796207 -- 4001798078 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, E- Payables of $262,118.18 on Summit 9000001021 - 9000001068 and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of $22,684,992.57 are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of $32,918,066.08 on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 51123156 -- 51124221 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 480001 -- 490840 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Nov. 23, 2010 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Dec. 2, 2010 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 6th day of Dec. 2010 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Dec. 6, 2010.
