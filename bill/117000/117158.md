 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of $12,315,130.90 on Summit mechanical warrants numbered 4001853322 -- 4001856277 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, E- Payables of $291,008.61 on Summit 9000002653-- 9000002732 and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of $31,285,493.17 are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of $33,248,623.64 on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 51132373 -- 51133246 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 160001 -- 170591 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date April 12, 2011 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council April 21, 2011 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 25th day of April 2011 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this April 25, 2011.
