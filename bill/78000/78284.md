**Council Bill Number: 78284**
   
**Ordinance Number: 86840**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of the City of Seattle at a special election in conjunction with the general municipal election to be held therein on March 11, 1958 the proposition of creating and indebtedness in the sum of $2,000,000 and the issuance and sale therefor of general obligation bonds of said City for the purpose of providing funds for the advance acquisition of land and other property for corporate purposes, including the establishing, widening and extending of streets and highways of an arterial nature and for parkways, and for municipal service sites, and to pay the principal and interest on such bonds by an annual tax levy in excess of the millage limitation contained in the State Constitution and the state law during the term of such bonds; and requiring the City Clerk to certify to the Auditor of King County as required by law, the said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title; and declaring an emergency.
