**Ordinance Number: 87188**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of East 135th Street from 35th Avenue Northeast to approximately 215 feet east of 40th Avenue Northeast; and certain other streets and avenues; by constructing watermains and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17729 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
