**Council Bill Number: 78778**
   
**Ordinance Number: 87329**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 10, Halls Addition, and in Block 4, Jansen's Addition, being the alley between 20th Avenue North and 21st Avenue North, from East Denny Way to Jensen Court; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17500 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
