**Ordinance Number: 87026**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of Fremont Avenue from North 125th Street to approximately 360 feet south of North 115th Street; and certain other streets, avenues and easements, by constructing sanitary sewers, and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17591 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
