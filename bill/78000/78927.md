**Ordinance Number: 87478**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 5, Ross Second Addition, being the alley in the block bounded by Argand Street, 8th Avenue West, Emerson Street, 6th Avenue West and West Bertona Street; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 17502 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
