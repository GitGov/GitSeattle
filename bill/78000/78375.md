**Council Bill Number: 78375**
   
**Ordinance Number: 87034**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alleys in Block 10, Greenwood Park Addition, being the alleys bounded by North 84th Street, North 85th Street, 1st Avenue Northwest and Greenwood Avenue; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance in with Resolution No. 17272 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
