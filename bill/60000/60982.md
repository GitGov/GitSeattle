**Council Bill Number: 60982**
   
**Ordinance Number: 69854**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 47, 48 and 49, Carleton Park Addition, being the alley between Montavista Place and Morley Place and 36th Avenue West, from West McGraw Street to Montavista Place; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12749 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
