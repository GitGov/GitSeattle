 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 5, Ferry's Addition, being the alley between 9th Avenue West and 10th Place West, from West Fulton Street, to West Barrett Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12623 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
