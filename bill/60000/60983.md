**Ordinance Number: 69855**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 60th Avenue Southwest, from West Hinds Street to a point 120 feet, more or less, south of West Spokane Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12401 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
