**Ordinance Number: 49082**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in blocks 45 and 46, Law's 2nd Addition, being the alley between Queen Anne Avenue and 1st Avenue West, from West Galer Street to West Garfield Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 97077 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
