**Ordinance Number: 50066**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for, an audible and visible grade crossing danger warning signal at the intersection of Duwamish Avenue and Chicago Street; directing the Puget Sound Electric Railway, a corporation, at its own cost and expense, to construct and install at said point, and thereafter maintain and operate, and replace, such a signal conforming to plans and specifications approved by the Board of Public Works; and authorizing legal actions to compel compliance herewith.
