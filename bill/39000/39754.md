**Council Bill Number: 39754**
   
**Ordinance Number: 49828**
********

 An ordinance relating to the general municipal election to be held March 9, 1926; calling an election for, and providing for the election thereat, by the qualified electors of the City, of fifteen (15) freeholders whose duty it shall be to prepare and propose a charter for the City of Seattle; providing for the filling of nominations of candidates therefor; certifying the offices of said freeholders to the Election Board of King County, Washington, as provided by law; and requiring the City Comptroller and ex-officio City Clerk to certify to such Board the names of all duly qualified persons who have filed their nominations for said offices.
