**Council Bill Number: 39944**
   
**Ordinance Number: 49975**
********

 An ordinance relating to the municipal primary and general elections of 1926; certifying the Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, a list of offices to be filled at said general municipal election, providing for the submission thereat of the proposed charter amendment contemplated by Resolution No. 8360, and requiring the City Comptroller and ex-officio City Clerk to certify to said Election Board of King County, Washington, as required by law, said proposed amendment in the form of a specified ballot title, and said amendment in full, and the names of all persons filing declarations of candidacy for nomination at such primary election.
