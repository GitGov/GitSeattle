**Council Bill Number: 74342**
   
**Ordinance Number: 82940**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 13, Ladd's Second Addition to South Seattle, being the alley between 12th Avenue South and 13th Avenue South from Nevada Street to Oregon Street; and certain other streets and avenues; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16256 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
