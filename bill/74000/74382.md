**Ordinance Number: 82987**

 An ordinance relating to the municipal light and power plant and system; authorizing participation with Tacoma City Light, Puget Sound Power & Light Company, Snohomish County P. U. D., and Chelan County P. U. D. in studies toward a coordinated development of their respective and combined power resources; authorizing the employment of a competent consulting engineer; and making an appropriation from the surplus in the Light Fund in connection therewith.
