**Council Bill Number: 74824**
   
**Ordinance Number: 83424**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of the City at the special municipal election to be held therein on November 2, 1954 the proposition to increase the tax imposed by Ordinance No. 72630 on certain businesses and occupations from the present rate of one-tenth of one percent to the rate of one-fifth of one percent, of the gross proceeds of the sale, or gross income of the business, as the case mey be, to increase firemen's and policemen's pay and to augment presently inadequate revenues for maintenance; requiring the City Clerk to certify to the Auditor of King County as required by law, the said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title; requesting said Auditor to call such an election; and declaring an emergency.
