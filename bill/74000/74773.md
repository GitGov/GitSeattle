**Council Bill Number: 74773**
   
**Ordinance Number: 83374**
********

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified electors (voters) of the City at the special municipal election to be held therein on November 2, 1954 the proposition to levy as permitted by law an additional tax of one-half (1/2) mill upon real and personal property in the city for civil defense purposes over and above the millage limitation contained in the Constitution and the state law for a period of one year; and requiring the City Clerk to certify to the Auditor of King County as required by law, the said proposition in the form of a specified ballot title; requesting said Auditor to call such an election; and declaring an emergency.
