 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Hansen View Addition, being the alley between 35th Avenue Southwest and 36th Avenue Southwest from West Hudson Street to West Dawson Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16305 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
