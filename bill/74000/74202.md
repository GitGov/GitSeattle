**Council Bill Number: 74202**
   
**Ordinance Number: 82808**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 41, Gilman's Addition, being the alley between 12th Avenue West and 13th Avenue West from Ruffner Street to West Bertona Street; and certain other streets, avenues and alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 16172 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
