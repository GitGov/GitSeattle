**Ordinance Number: 15320**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Aurora Avenue, from North 66th Street to North 85th Street; Linden Avenue, from North 61st Street to North 65th Street; Woodstock Place, from North 64th Street to North 65th Street; Fremont Avenue, from North 61st Street to North 66th Street; North 61st Street, from Phinney Avenue to the east line of Whitman Avenue; North 64th Street from Phinney Avenue to the east line of Aurora Avenue; North 65th Street, from Fremont Avenue to Linden Avenue; North 68th Street, from Fremont Avenue to Aurora Avenue; North 68th Street, from Greenwood Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard; North 72nd Street, and North 73rd Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to Aurora Avenue, all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 384 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
