**Council Bill Number: 2204**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15496**
********

 An ordinance granting to the Mayfair Land Company, Calhoun, Denny & Ewing, Inc., The Orient Trust Company, a corporation, Commercial Security Company, a corporation, all organized under the laws of the State of Washington, and D. E. Ferguson and J. C. Gosnell, authority and permission to improve, by grading, Warren Avenue from Ray Street to Florentia Street; 2nd Avenue North, from Ray Street to Florentia Street; Mayfair Avenue, from Ray Street to Florentia Street; 3rd Avenue North, from Ray Street to Florentia Street; Fulton Street and Newell Street from 1st Avenue North to the eastern boundary of 3rd Avenue North; and the south 33 feet of Florentia Street from 1st Avenue North to the eastern boundary line of Mayfair Avenue; all of said streets lying and being the an adjoining the Mayfair Addition to the City of Seattle, as shown by the recorded plat, said grading to be done at the petitioner's above named proper cost and expense.
