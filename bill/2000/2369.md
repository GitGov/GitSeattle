**Ordinance Number: 15642**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, extending and establishing of a public street and highway over and across certain portions of Block Fifteen (15), Ravenna Springs Park Supplemental Addition to the City of Seattle, and over and across certain unplatted portions of Section Nine (9), Township Twenty-five (25) North, Range Four (4) East, W. M., and over and across that part of the Plan of Union City shown on the plat thereof as "Pike's Reserve for Canal 200 feet width;" and providing for the taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation to be made for the private property to be taken or damaged for said purpose, and for an assessment upon the property benefited for the purpose of making such compensation.
