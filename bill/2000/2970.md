**Ordinance Number: 13416**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Louisa Street, from Eastlake Avenue to Boylston Avenue North; Miller Street from Boylston Avenue North to Federal Avenue; East Lynn Street from Federal Avenue to Lake Union; East Boston Street from Federal Avenue to Lake Union; East Newton Street, from Federal Avenue to North Broadway; North Broadway from Roanoke Street to the north line of Oakland Addition to the City of Seattle; Harvard Avenue North from East Lynn Street to the north line of Oakland Addition to the City of Seattle; Boylston Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to East Newton Street; Franklin Avenue from Louisa Street to East Newton Street; Howard Avenue North, from East Newton Street to the north line of Greene's Addition to the City of Seattle; and Minor Avenue North from East Newton Street to the north line of Greene's Addition to the City of Seattle, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks, in accordance with Resolution No. 837 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
