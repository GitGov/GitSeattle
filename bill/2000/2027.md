**Ordinance Number: 15323**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Fremont Avenue from Kilbourne Street to North 50th Street; Linden Avenue from North 42nd Street to North 50th Street; Aurora Avenue from Motor Place to North 50th Street; Winslow Place, from North 42nd Street to North 45th Street; Whitman Avenue, from North 45th Street to North 50th Street; North 38th Street from Fremont Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; North 41st Street, from Fremont Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; Motor Place from Fremont Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; North 44th Street, from Fremont Avenue to Aurora Avenue and from Woodland Park Avenue to Interlake Avenue; Allen Place, from Fremont Avenue to Woodlawn Place; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, and constructing all necessary catch basins, inlets and other appurtenances necessary to secure the proper surface drainage of said districts, all in accordance with Resolution No. 336 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
