**Council Bill Number: 2346**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15814**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1309, for the improvement of Westlake Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Pike Street to Denny Way, by constructing sidewalks thereon, on both sides thereof, together with all necessary grading, bulkheads and retaining walls, as follows: from Pike Street to Seventh Avenue, sidewalks to be constructed of concrete, from Seventh Avenue to Denny Way, sidewalks to be constructed of wood, under Ordinance No. 14787 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
