**Ordinance Number: 16040**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Fortieth Street, from Latona Avenue to Tenth Avenue Northeast; and Sixth Avenue Northeast, from Northlake Avenue to East Fortieth Street, by grading and curbing the same; provided, however, that East Fortieth Street, from Latona Avenue to Seventh Avenue Northeast, shall be improved by constructing a double roadway, including the necessary bulkheads and stairways, in accordance with Resolution No. 548 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
