**Ordinance Number: 15324**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 15th Avenue Northeast, from East 40th Street to Northlake Avenue; Northlake Avenue, from 15th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast; and Brintnall Place (on both sides of the right of way of the Northern Pacific Railway) from Brooklyn Avenue to 15th Avenue Northeast all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and by constructing concrete walks thereon, together with catch basins and other necessary appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 416 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
