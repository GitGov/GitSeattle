**Council Bill Number: 2576**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15924**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, widening, extending and establishing of Meadow Place, University Boulevard, and East Seventieth Street, as public streets, highways, boulevards and park ways in the City of Seattle, between East Green Lake Boulevard and Fifteenth Avenue Northeast, over and across certain lots, blocks, tracts and parcels of land in said City, and providing for the condemnation and appropriation to the public use as a park of certain other lands and premises adjoining and proximate thereto, and providing for the taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation to be made for the private property to be taken or damaged for said purpose, and for the assessment of a just and equitable proportion of said compensation and of the necessary expenses upon the private property benefited, and providing for the payment of the remainder of said compensation and other necessary expenses from the General Fund of the City of Seattle.
