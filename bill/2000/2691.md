**Ordinance Number: 15952**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Stone Way, from Ewing Street to North Forty-fifth Street; North Forty-fifth Street from Stone Way to Burke Avenue; Allen Place, from Stone Way to Woodlawn Place; North Forty-fourth Street, from Stone Way to Interlake Avenue; Woodlawn Avenue, from North Forty-third Street to North Forty-fifth Street; Wallingford Avenue, from North Forty-third Street to North Forty-fifth Street; Burke Avenue, from North Forty-fourth Street to North Forty-fifth Street; and Blewett Street and Blewett Street produced from Interlake Avenue to Stone Way, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and other appurtenance necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 578 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
