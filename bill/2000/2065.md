**Council Bill Number: 2065**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15520**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Cherry Street, from Twentieth Avenue to Thirty-fifth Avenue, in the City of Seattle, by sub-grading and by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite corners, and all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, except that East Cherry Street, from Twenty-first Avenue to Twenty-third Avenue and from Twenty-ninth Avenue to Thirty-third Avenue, shall be paved with sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, all in accordance with Resolution No. 356 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
