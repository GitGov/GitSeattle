**Council Bill Number: 2066**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15358**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 4th Avenue, from Union Street to Pike Street, all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation and constructing granite curbs, together with all sewers, and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 427 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
