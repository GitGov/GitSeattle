**Council Bill Number: 2403**
   
**Ordinance Number: 12912**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-seventh Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from a point fifty (50) feet north of the north line of East Spring Street to the north line of Rosedale Addition, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, with all necessary wooden curbs, gutters and crosswalks, together with such surface drainage as may be necessary, in accordance with Resolution No. 663 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
