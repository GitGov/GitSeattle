**Ordinance Number: 16253**

 An Ordinance authorizing and directing the Board of Public Works to remove buildings situated in streets of Irondale Additions to the City of Seattle, according to the replat, and appropriating Three Hundred Dollars ($300.00) from the General Fund to be paid to the owners of Lots ten (10), Eleven (11), and Twelve (12), in Block eight (8), of said Irondale Addition to the City of Seattle, for the purpose of defraying the cost of removing said buildings from that portion of said lots that will be included in the street, according to the replat.
