**Council Bill Number: 2766**
   
**Ordinance Number: 16032**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing the City of Seattle to convey unto the Oregon & Washington Railroad Company, a corporation formed under the news of the State of Oregon and lawfully doing business in the State of Washington, and to its successors and assigns, a right of way forty-five (45) feet in width, beneath the surface, in block thirty-ninth (39) of C. D. Boren's Addition to the City of Seattle, for the purpose of constructing, by said Railroad Company, a double track tunnel for railway purposes under the surface or said block.
