**Council Bill Number: 2219**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15527**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Tenth Avenue North, from East Prospect Street to Roanoke Street; Roanoke Street, from the east line of Tenth Avenue North, as laid out in the Denny- Fuhrman Addition to the City of Seattle, to Eastlake Avenue; Franklin Avenue, Boylston Avenue North, North Broadway and Tenth Avenue North, each from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Harvard Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Eastlake Avenue; Shelby Street, from Franklin Avenue to Tenth Avenue North; Edgar Street and Hamlin Street, each from Eastlake Avenue to Tenth Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs, with granite corners and including as a necessary part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, the construction of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 460, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
