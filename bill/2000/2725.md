 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twentieth Avenue North, from East Harrison Street to East Roy Street; East Mercer Street, from Nineteenth Avenue North to Twenty-first Avenue North; and East Republican Street, from Nineteenth Avenue North to Twenty-first Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same, and constructing wooden curbs and gutters, and concrete sidewalks, and by constructing sewers, of property dimensions and material, and water mains, together with all necessary appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 591 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
