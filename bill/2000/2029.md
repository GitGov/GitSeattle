**Council Bill Number: 2029**
   
**Ordinance Number: 15325**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Marion Street in the City of Seattle, from the east margin of 3rd Avenue to the intersection of 4th Avenue, by paving the same with sandstone blocks, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with granite curbs, and brick gutters, by constructing all necessary water mains, with appurtenances and sewers with appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 431 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
