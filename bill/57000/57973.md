**Ordinance Number: 66977**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The North 410 feet of The Alley in Block 6, H. S. Turner Park Addition, being the north 410 feet of the alley between 25th Avenue North and 26th Avenue North, from East Newton Street to the north line of Interlaken Addition; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 11909 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
