**Ordinance Number: 66618**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 5, Laurelhurst Heights, being the alley between 46th Avenue Northeast and 47th Avenue Northeast, from East 38th Street to East 40th Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 11841 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
