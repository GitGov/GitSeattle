**Ordinance Number: 66066**

 An ordinance relating to and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal waterworks system; providing for the construction of a watermain of Cloverdale Street from Meyers Way to 50th Avenue South; declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be; providing the funds therefor by appropriation of $3,000 from the surplus in the Water Fund; authorizing the Board of Public Works to prepare plans and specifications for, and to proceed to lay the same; authorizing the expenditures and appropriating funds therefor.
