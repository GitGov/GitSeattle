**Council Bill Number: 57029**
   
**Ordinance Number: 66080**
********

 An ordinance relating to and creating and establishing a special fund to be known as "Railway Snow and Ice Removal Fund" to be used for the payment of emergency expenses of the Division of Municipal Railways in clearing its tracks of snow and ice; appropriating the sum of Thirty Three Thousand Dollars ($33,000.00), or so much thereof as may be necessary, from the City Railway Fund therefor; and declaring an emergency.
