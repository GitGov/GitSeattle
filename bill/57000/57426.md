**Council Bill Number: 57426**
   
**Ordinance Number: 66439**
********

 An ordinance relating to the Department of Police; temporarily modifying the conditions of employment therein by restoration of certain members to full time and full pay; making an appropriation from the Emergency Fund therefor; declaring the emergency making necessary such appropriation; transferring the necessary funds therefor to the Emergency Fund from the General Fund as a loan; and suspending all ordinances or parts of Ordinances in conflict herewith.
