**Council Bill Number: 57598**
   
**Ordinance Number: 66599**
********

 An ordinance relating to and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal water works system; providing for the acquisition of a franchise for, and the construction of pipelines in, the vicinity of Fremont Avenue and North 180th and 185th Streets, and making an appropriation from the surplus in the Water Fund therefor, upon condition that the owners of property in the vicinity thereof obligate themselves to the payment of the cost thereof.
