**Council Bill Number: 27196**
   
**Ordinance Number: 38056**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 36686, entitled: "An ordinance providing for the improvement of Roy Street from Fifth Avenue North to Dexter Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, together with brick gutters, constructing armored concrete curbs, including as part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, the construction, alteration or reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary, by such grading or regrading as may be necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5326, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds'", approved November 20, 1916.
