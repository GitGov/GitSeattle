**Council Bill Number: 27303**
   
**Ordinance Number: 38375**
********

 An ordinance providing for the separation of grades for pedestrian travel over Railroad Avenue at Yesler Way and directing railway companies owning franchises in Railroad Avenue at said point to construct, at their own cost and expense, a bridge or viaduct, together with necessary approaches, over the tracks of said street, or to pay the cost of same, said bridge or viaduct to be constructed in accordance with plans provided for in Ordinance No. 34994, approved July 19, 1915, as amended, and the judgement of the Superior Court of King County in Cause No. 114115, condemning the right to construct and maintain such bridge, viaduct and approaches, pursuant to said ordinance.
