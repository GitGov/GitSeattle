**Council Bill Number: 27538**
   
**Ordinance Number: 38377**
********

 An ordinance relating to and creating an indebtedness of the City of Seattle in the sum of Two hundred seventy thousand dollars ($270,000) and providing for a loan of said city in said sum by the issuance and sale of general negotiable coupon bonds, for the purpose of paying certain awards heretofore made in condemnation proceedings prosecuted by said city, and certain condemnation fund warrants heretofore issued in payment of a portion of said awards, together with interest of said bonds in said amount, and providing for the levy of taxes for the payment of the principal and interest thereof.
