**Ordinance Number: 38786**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Juneau Street, from the Park Boulevard to Seward Park Avenue; Seward Park Avenue, from Juneau Street to Fifty-seventh Avenue South, (formerly Meadow Avenue); Fifth-seventh Avenue South, from Seward Park to Holly Street; Holly Street, from Fifty-seventh Avenue South to the west line of Lot Three (3), Block Thirteen (13), Plat of Brighton Beach; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Majority Petition File No. 68875, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
