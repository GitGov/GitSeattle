**Council Bill Number: 27135**
   
**Ordinance Number: 38225**
********

 An ordinance granting permission to the Puget Mill Company, to improve 32nd Avenue Southwest, from West Yancy Street to West Genesee Street; 33rd Avenue Southwest, from West Spokane Street to West Andover Street; 34th Avenue Southwest, from West Manning Street to West Dakota Street; West Yancy Street, from Avalon Way to Fauntleroy Avenue; West Genesee Street, from Avalon Way to 35th Avenue Southwest; West Dakota Street, from 34th Avenue Southwest to 35th Avenue Southwest; West Andover Street, from Fauntleroy Avenue to 35th Avenue Southwest; West Charlestown Street, from 33rd Avenue Southwest to 35th Avenue Southwest; West Manning Street, from 34th Avenue Southwest to 35th Avenue Southwest; and Fauntleroy Avenue from 35th Avenue Southwest to a point approximately 300 feet north of the north margin of Andover Street; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same; constructing concrete sidewalks; constructing watermains therein of proper dimensions and material, together with fire hydrants and appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith, at its own cost and expense, under certain terms and conditions.
