**Council Bill Number: 27661**
   
**Ordinance Number: 38486**
********

 An ordinance providing for the establishing of curb grades of Northlake Avenue, from the southwesterly margin of Ewing Street, as platted between blocks seven (7) and eight (8), Bagley's Addition to the City of Seattle, to a point one hundred and fifty-two one- hundredths(100.52) feet southwesterly therefrom; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading of said avenue in conformity with the grades established herein and for fills upon property abutting on said avenue; and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law, and declaring an emergency.
