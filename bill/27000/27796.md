**Ordinance Number: 38617**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement Yale Avenue North and Minor Avenue North, each from the north line of Green's Addition to Roanoke Street; and Roanoke Street, from Fairview Avenue North to Eastlake Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 5849 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
