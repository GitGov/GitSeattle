**Ordinance Number: 28275**
********

 An ordinance amending section 5 of Ordinance No. 25899, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North 80th Street and North 81st Street, each from Woodland Park Avenue to Greenwood Avenue; North; North 78th Street and North 79th Street each from Woodland Park Avenue to Fremont Avenue; Dayton Avenue, from North 79th Street to North 81st Street; and Fremont Avenue, from North 78th Street to North 81st Street; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wooden cross walks thereon, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2964 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" passed over mayor's veto December 12, 1910 and published December 16, 1910, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years and by changing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
