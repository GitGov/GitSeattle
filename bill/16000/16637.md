**Council Bill Number: 16637**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28382**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2297, for the improvement of 21st Avenue North, 20th Avenue North, 22nd Avenue North, each from East Madison Street to East Roy Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle,, by paving the same with any suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; provided, that where the grade does not exceed 7%, asphalt shall be used, and that brick or stone block shall be used where grades exceed 7%, and by constructing curbs and asphalt, brick or stone gutters thereon, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the construction, alteration, or extension of concrete walks and of such sewers and side sewers and watermains and their appurtenances necessary for the proper drainage, sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 23745 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
