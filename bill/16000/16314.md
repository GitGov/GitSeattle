**Council Bill Number: 16314**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28141**
********

 An ordinance amending section 5 of Ordinance No. 27286, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 22nd Avenue Northeast, between East 45th Street and East 54th Street; East 54th Street, from 22nd Avenue Northeast to Ravenna Place; Ravenna Place, from East 54th Street, from Blakely Avenue; Blakely Avenue,. From 24th Avenue Northeast to 30th Avenue Northeast; 20th Avenue Northeast, from East 45th Street to East 50th Street; East 45th Street, from 20th Avenue Northeast to 22nd Avenue Northeast; and East 47th Street, from 20th Avenue Northeast to 22nd Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 43899, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved May 31, 1911 and published June 6, 1911, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years, and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
