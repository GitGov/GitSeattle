**Council Bill Number: 16644**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28437**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Dearborn Street, from Rainier Avenue to 7th Avenue South, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, and doing such other work as may be necessary in connection therewith, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3310, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
