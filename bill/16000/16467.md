**Ordinance Number: 28280**

 An ordinance amending section 5 of Ordinance No. 27508 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 9th Avenue West, from West Halliday Street to West Barrett Street; 8th Avenue West from West Wheeler Street to West Ray Street; 10th Avenue West from West Armour Street to West Barrett Street; 11th Avenue West from West Ray Street to Grand Boulevard; West Halliday Street, from 7th Avenue West to 9th Avenue West; Bothwell Street, from 8th Avenue West to 9th Avenue West; and from 10th Avenue West to 11th Avenue West; West Newell Street, from 8th Avenue West to 11th Avenue West; West Armour Street from 8th Avenue West to 11th Avenue West; West Fulton Street from 9th Avenue West to 11th Avenue West; and West Barrett Street from 9th Avenue West to 11th Avenue West all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3209, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved July 6, 1911, and published July 10, 1911, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
