**Ordinance Number: 27905**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2431 for the improvement of Ninth Avenue Northeast form East Forty-fifth Street to four hundred (400) feet north of East Fiftieth Street; Tenth Avenue Northeast, from East Forty-fifth Street to East Fifty-second Street, all in The City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all wyes, openings, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; under Ordinance No. 25704, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
