**Council Bill Number: 16346**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28154**
********

 An ordinance amending Section 5 of Ordinance No. 18675 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 1st Avenue West, and 2nd Avenue West, each from West McGraw Street to West Ray Street; and West Smith Street, from 2nd Avenue West to Queen Anne Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks and wood cross walks, and grading and curbing the same where necessary, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1345, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved June 24, 1908, and published June 26, 1908' by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years, and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
