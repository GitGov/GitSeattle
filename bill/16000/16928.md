 An ordinance approving amended assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2488, for the improvement of East Pine Street from Belmont Avenue to the east margin of Nagle Place, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and regrading the widened portion of the street, by constructing ore reconstructing concrete curbs, sidewalks, plank roadway and asphalt pavement where necessary, including in said work the necessary removal of all bulkheads, retaining walls and concrete sidewalks; under Ordinance No. 27241 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
