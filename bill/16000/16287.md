**Council Bill Number: 16287**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28107**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2471, for the improvement of Lakeview Boulevard, from Nelson Place to East Newton Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation with brick or asphalt gutters, concrete or armored concrete curbs, and providing for the necessary grading and the construction of concrete sidewalks; for the necessary sewerage and drainage of said district, and including such alterations of water mains and their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district.
