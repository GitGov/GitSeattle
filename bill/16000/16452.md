**Council Bill Number: 16452**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28211**
********

 An ordinance ratifying and confirming a loan of the City of Seattle in the aggregate sum of $500,000 for the purpose of providing funds for additions and betterments to the existing water supply system of the City by the acquisition by purchase, or by condemnation and purchase, of lands, properties, rights and privileges, within and near the watershed of the Cedar River, necessary to be had to protect the said water supply system, commonly known as "Cedar River Water Supply System" from pollution, being a part of $1,000,000 bonds of the City of Seattle, authorized at an election held September 5, 1911; and directing the issuance of bonds in the sum of $5000,000 and the levy and collection of taxes for the payment of the principal and interest thereof.
