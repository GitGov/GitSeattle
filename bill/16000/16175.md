**Ordinance Number: 27996**
********

 An Ordinance appropriating the sum of Forty Nine and 70/100 ($49.70) Dollars out of the Light Fund of the City of Seattle, in favor of Ed. L. Terry, as City Treasurer, for the purpose of reimbursing said Treasurer on account of worthless checks issued to him in payment of electric current furnished to Minnie Eisbert, Fred B. Harvey, and the Ever-Ready Company, Inc., upon which payment has been refused by the several banks in question, chiefly for lack of funds.
