**Council Bill Number: 16890**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28667**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Boyer Avenue North from 24th Avenue North to Washington Park; Graham Place from 24th Avenue North to 25th Avenue North; Madrona Drive from 20th Avenue North to East Galer Street; East Galer Street from the westerly margin of Madrona Drive to the easterly margin of 26th Avenue North from the north; East Newton Street from 24th Avenue North to Washington Park; East Lynn Street from 24th Avenue North to the westerly margin of 26th Avenue North from the north; 20th Avenue North from Crescent Drive to Madrona Drive; Pine Court, Crescent Drive to Madrona Drive; 21st Avenue North from Crescent Drive to Interlaken Parkway; 22nd Avenue North from East Galer to Madrona Drive; 25th Avenue North from Boyer Avenue to East Lynn Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick or asphalt gutters, concrete or armored concrete curbs and the providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sidewalks, sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 45774, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
