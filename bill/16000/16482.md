**Council Bill Number: 16482**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28328**
********

 An ordinance amending section 5 of Ordinance No. 25235 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Court Place in the City of Seattle from Denny Way to Howell Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with brick or asphalt gutters and concrete curbs with armored concrete corners, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2898, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds', as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle", approved October 6, 1910, and published October 11, 1910, by changing the time of payment from 5 years to 10 years, and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6 %.
