**Council Bill Number: 16236**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28025**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2387, for the improvement of 8th Avenue South, from Edmunds Street to Duwamish Avenue; and Duwamish venue from 8th Avenue South to Juneau Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with armored concrete curbs, by constructing concrete sidewalks, and including in said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 24847 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
