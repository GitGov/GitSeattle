**Council Bill Number: 16678**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28477**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 28277 entitled: "An ordinance amending Section 5 of Ordinance No. 26292 entitled: `An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Highland Drive from Dexter Avenue to Westlake Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a bridge roadway thereof 22 feet in width, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3028, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds' as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle' approved February 4, 1911, and published February 11, 1911, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years and reducing the rate of interest from 7 % to 6 %."
