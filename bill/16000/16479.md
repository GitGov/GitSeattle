**Ordinance Number: 28283**

 An ordinance amending Section 5 of Ordinance No. 20433, entitled: "An ordinance providing for the improvement of West 51st Street, from 3rd Avenue Northwest to 8th Avenue Northwest; West 49th Street and West 50th Street, each from 1st Avenue Northwest to 9th Avenue Northwest; 2nd Avenue Northwest, from West 48th Street tot West 50th Street; Maple Grove Place, from West 50th Street to West 52nd Street; 3rd Avenue Northwest and 8th Avenue Northwest, each from West 48th Street to West 52nd Street; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and providing for necessary surface drainage, in accordance with Resolution No. 1825 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of 'Payment in Bonds'as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved March 22, 1909 and published March 25, 1909, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years and by reducing the rate of interest from 7 percent to 6 percent.
