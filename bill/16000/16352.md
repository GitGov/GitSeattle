**Ordinance Number: 28160**

 An ordinance amending Ordinance No. 24844 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Oregon Street, from west margin of California Avenue to 49th Avenue Southwest; 44th Avenue Southwest, from West Edmunds Street to West Spokane Street; 46th Avenue Southwest from West Edmunds Street to West Spokane Street; 47th Avenue Southwest from West Edmunds Street to West Charlestown Street; 48th Avenue Southwest from West Alaska Street to West Genesee Street, and 49th Avenue Southwest from West Alaska Street to West Oregon Street; all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2775, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved August 17, 1910 and published August 19, 1910, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years, and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
