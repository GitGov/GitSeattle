**Council Bill Number: 16420**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28233**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2469, for the improvement of Elliott Avenue from Western Avenue to Bay Street, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick or stone blocks or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation together with brick or stone gutters, and granite curbs with granite corners, and including as part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district , under Ordinance No. 26800 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
