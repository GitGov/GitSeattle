**Council Bill Number: 16193**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28006**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2460, for the improvement of University Street, from 6th Avenue to the easterly margin of University Tract, Fifth and " Street or the alley lying west of 6th Avenue between Union Street and University Street; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified block or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with granite or armored concrete curb, and providing for the grading and the construction of concrete walks; for the necessary sewerage and drainage of said district, and including such alterations of water mains with their appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 26441 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
