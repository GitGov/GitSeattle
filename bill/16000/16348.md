**Council Bill Number: 16348**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28156**
********

 An ordinance amending section 5 of Ordinance No. 23457, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Alki Avenue, from south margin of West Spokane Street to the south margin of West Atlantic Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and filling the same to a width of not less than 30 feet, making all necessary slopes for excavation and embankments, by making all necessary approaches thereto, by constructing all necessary bulkheads and retaining walls, by the construction of curbs and gutters where necessary, by planking the roadway and by constructing wooden sidewalks therein and making all necessary provisions to care for the surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2588, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved March 1, 1910, published March 4, 1910 by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years, and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
