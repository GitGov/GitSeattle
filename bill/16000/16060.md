**Council Bill Number: 16060**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27906**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2266 for the improvement of Thirty-six Avenue, from the North margin of East Spring Street to East Terrace Street and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues therein named, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with asphalt, brick or stone gutters, with armoured concrete or concrete curbs, armoured concrete or granite corners, concrete walks and concrete retaining walls where necessary, and the construction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains, together with all appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; and East Terrace Street, from Thirty-sixth Avenue to the west line of Yesler's Third Addition to the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete stairways and concrete walks thereon; under Ordinance No. 23103 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and parts thereon shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
