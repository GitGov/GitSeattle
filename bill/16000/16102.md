**Ordinance Number: 27942**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2468 for the improvement of the east side of Twenty-third Avenue North from a point eighty-four and forty-two hundredths (84.42) feet south of East Highland Drive from the east to East Galer Street; and East Lee Street from Twenty-third Avenue North to Twenty-fourth Avenue North, all in The City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, constructing concrete sidewalks, together with all necessary crosswalks and providing for the necessary surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 26787, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
