**Ordinance Number: 28029**
********

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 16975 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 11th Avenue West, from West Blaine Street to West Galer Street; West Galer Street, from 11th Avenue West to the alley between 11th Avenue West and 10th Avenue West; the alley between 11th Avenue West and 10th Avenue West, from West Galer Street to West Lee Street; and West Lee Street, from the alley between 11th Avenue West and 10th Avenue West; to a connection with the existing sewer in 10th Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 798, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved September 20, 1907.
