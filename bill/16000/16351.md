**Council Bill Number: 16351**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28158**
********

 An ordinance amending Ordinance No. 24363, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 18th Avenue South and 19th Avenue South, each from Hanford Street to Atlantic Street; 20th Avenue South, from Hanford Street to Massachusetts Street; Valentine Place, from Grand Street to Atlantic Street; Massachusetts Street and State Street, each from 17th Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; Holgate Street, Plum Street, Hill Street, Walker Street, College Street, Bayview Street, Waite Street, McClellan Street and Forest Street, each from 17th Avenue South to 20th Avenue South; Stevens Street, from Beacon Avenue to 20th Avenue South and Hanford Street from 19th Avenue South to 20th Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks, concrete stairways, and necessary cross walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2732 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved June 14, 1910 and published June 17, 1910, by changing the time of payment from five years to ten years, and by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6%.
