**Ordinance Number: 28223**

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 26354 entitled: "An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, widening, extending and establishing of West Oregon Street from 35th Avenue Southwest to the alley between 37th Avenue Southwest and 38th Avenue Southwest, and of a public street and highway from West Oregon Street and the alley between 37th Avenue Southwest and 38th Avenue Southwest to 39th Avenue Southwest; and West Alaska Street, all in the City of Seattle, and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and providing for the changing and establishing of curb grades of West Oregon Street from 35th Avenue Southwest to the alley between 37th Avenue Southwest and 38th Avenue Southwest; and of the public street and highway provided herein, extending from West Oregon Street and alley between 37th Avenue Southwest and 38th Avenue Southwest to 39th Avenue Southwest and West Alaska Street, and approaches thereto and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said streets and avenues and providing for the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenues and streets, and providing that the cost of said improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law" approved March 10, 1911.
