**Council Bill Number: 16483**
   
**Ordinance Number: 28286**
********

 An ordinance amending Section 5 of Ordinance No. 25359, entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Newport Avenue, from Grant Avenue to 38th Avenue; 38th Avenue, from a point 155 feet south of East Union Street to East Spring Street and from East Spring Street to Norwood Place; Grand Avenue, from Newport Avenue to East Spring Street; East Spring Street from 38th Avenue from the south to Lakeside Avenue; Randolph Avenue from 38th Avenue to East Marion Street, and from East Columbia Street to Norwood Place; East Marion Street from 37th Avenue to 38th Avenue; East Columbia Street from 37th Avenue to Lakeside Avenue; 39th Avenue from East Columbia Street to Wellington Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks, concrete stairway, cross walks, temporary wood walks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2839, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of `Payment in Bonds', as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved October 14, 1910 and published October 18, 1910, by reducing the rate of interest from 7% to 6% and changing the time of payment from five years to ten years.
