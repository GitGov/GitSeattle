 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 25365 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North 50th Street from Phinney Avenue to 1st Avenue Northwest; North 51st Street and West 51st Street, from Greenwood Avenue to 2nd Avenue Northwest; North 54th Street from Greenwood Avenue to 1st Avenue Northwest; North 55th Street, from Greenwood Avenue to 1st Avenue Northwest; North 55th Street and West 55th Street, from Greenwood Avenue to Scenic Place; Woodlawn Circle, from 1st Avenue Northwest to Terrace Drive; West 54t Street from 1st Avenue Northwest to Terrace Drive; West 52nd Street, from 2nd Avenue Northwest to 3rd Avenue Northwest; 1st Avenue Northwest from North 51st Street to West 56th Street; Trinity Place from North 51st Street to North 54th Street; Terrace Drive from Scenic Place to 1st Avenue Northwest; Scenic Place form Terrace Drive to West 55th Street; Argyle Place, from Greenwood Avenue to 1st Avenue Northwest; 2nd Avenue Northwest, from West 55th Street to West 56th Street; West 56th Street, from 1st Avenue Northwest to 2nd Avenue Northwest; Palatine Avenue from West 56th Street to North 58th Street; Ballard Place from 1st Avenue Northwest to Palatine Avenue; North 57th Street from Palatine Avenue to Greenwood Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing watermains therein of proper dimensions and materials with all gates, tees, crosses fire hydrants, and other appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2904, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved October 14, 1910.
