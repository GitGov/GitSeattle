**Ordinance Number: 115863**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Engineering Department; authorizing the Mayor or the Director of Engineering to enter into an agreement with the Washington State Interagency Committee for Outdoor Recreation to receive a grant to purchase right-of-way for the Ship Canal Trail; accepting and depositing funds from the Washington State Interagency Committee for Outdoor Recreation into the Open Spaces and Trails Bond Fund; authorizing negotiations for and acquisition of portions of the Burlington Northern Railroad Company right-of-way located on the South Side of the Ship Canal between 3rd Avenue West and the Fremont Bridge; and making an appropriation of funds from the Open Spaces and Trails Bond Fund to purchase the right-of-way.
