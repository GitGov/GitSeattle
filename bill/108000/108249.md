**Council Bill Number: 108249**
   
**Ordinance Number: 115449**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to and providing for disposition of moneys seized and forfeited and proceeds of the sale of goods and materials seized and forfeited pursuant to the Uniform Controlled Substance Act; accepting such forfeitures; appropriating such moneys to reimburse expenses incurred by the Police and Law Departments and the Office of the Hearing Examiner and to expand programs of the Police and Law Departments to enforce narcotic and other drug enforcement laws; and increasing certain expenditure allowances in the 1991 Budget of the Police and Law Departments and the Office of the Hearing Examiner by reimbursable appropriation from the General Fund.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** November 26, 1990
   
**Vote:** 8-0
