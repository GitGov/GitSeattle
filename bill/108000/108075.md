 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Center; amending SMC 17.19.010 to increase maximum parking rates between July 20 and August 6, 1990; creating a new position of employment and authorizing the filling of the same using intermittent Parking Attendants; authorizing implementation of an off-site parking and shuttle program during the Goodwill Games; and, on a three-fourths vote of the City Council, making an appropriation from the Seattle Center Fund to provide for such implementation and declaring the emergency therefor.
