

********

**Council Bill Number: 108200**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the submission to the qualified electors of the City at a special election on November 6, 1990 of a proposition authorizing the City to levy regular property taxes in excess of the 106 percent limitation on levies for the purpose of providing assistance for educational and developmental services, such as early childhood development, school-based student/family services, comprehensive student health services, and out-of-school youth activities; providing for the annual levy; establishing the funding thereof, creating an oversight committee, and authorizing implementing agreements.
