**Council Bill Number: 108344**
   
**Ordinance Number: 115531**
********

 AN ORDINANCE regulating the sale of tobacco products; adding a new chapter to the Seattle Municipal Code ("SMC") regulating the retail sale of tobacco products, requiring licenses for tobacco product retailing and the operation of tobacco vending machines, defining civil infractions, and prescribing penalties therefor; and repealing SMC Chapter 6.60 and 6.84.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** February 11, 1991
   
**Vote:** 7-0
