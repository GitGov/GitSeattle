**Council Bill Number: 108293**
   
**Ordinance Number: 115464**
********

 AN ORDINANCE to encourage reporting of improper governmental action to the City Comptroller, the State Auditor and other auditing officials; providing protection to "whistleblowers", forbidding retaliation, and providing for investigation of such reports; adding a new subchapter III to Seattle Municipal Code Chapter 4.20 and new sections 4.20.600 through 4.20.650; and amending SMC 4.04.070 and 4.20.650.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** December 10, 1990
   
**Vote:** 9-0
