**Council Bill Number: 108556**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Personnel System; redefining the terms "appointing authority", "employee", "employing unit", "interim employee", "probationary employee", "regular employee", and "temporary worker" by the amendment of Seattle Municipal Code 4.04.030 to eliminate the implication that City employment may be permanent and to otherwise clarify the meanings of such terms; adding a new subsection to SMC 4.04.030 to define "staff"; amending SMC 4.04.225 A.3 and 4, B.1.a and c, 3.b, 4.a and b C, E.1 and 2, and F, to clarify which personnel are affected by legislation regulating smoking in City work environments and make other technical corrections; and amending SMC 4.04.230 and adding new code sections (SMC 4.04.227 and SMC 4.08.165) to implement federal requirements to provide a drug-free workplace, making unlawful drug-related activities at a City workplace a cause for discharge and requiring City staff to inform their ultimate supervisor regarding convictions for drug-related offenses at the worksite and to participate in rehabilitation programs.

**Status:** Retired 97
   
**Vote:** 9-0
   
**Date filed with the City Clerk:** August 5, 1996
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** May 6, 1991
