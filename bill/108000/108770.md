**Council Bill Number: 108770**
   
**Ordinance Number: 115851**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to sick leave for City employees; amending SMC 4.24.100 by deleting the "sunset" clause and reference to "pilot" program; implementing a sick leave transfer program by rule; reducing qualifications for receiving donated sick leave or donating sick leave; reducing the number of donated sick leave hours an employee may receive; and, adding a reporting requirement to the Personnel Director.
