**Council Bill Number: 108493**
   
**Ordinance Number: 115593**
********

 AN ORDINANCE providing for the submission to the qualified electors of the City at a special election to be held therein on May 28, 1991, a proposition authorizing the City to levy regular property taxes for eight (8) years in excess of the 106% limitation on increases in regular property tax levies for civic and community center purposes; providing for construction, reconstruction, acquisition, installation, development, equipping, repair and betterment of certain facilities at Seattle Center and certain community centers; providing for the annual levy and collection of taxes; establishing the funding therefor; establishing certain administrative and financial procedures, including certain borrowings; authorizing implementing agreements; increasing an expenditure allowance in an account in the Department of Finance General for election expenses by appropriation from the Emergency Fund and declaring the emergency therefor.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** March 25, 1991
   
**Vote:** 8-0
   
**Date of Mayor's signature:** April 1, 1991
