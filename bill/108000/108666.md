**Council Bill Number: 108666**
   
**Ordinance Number: 115797**
********

 AN ORDINANCE amending Seattle Municipal Code 4.20.360 and 4.34.055 to allow employees to elect to use their accumulated sick and vacation leaves during the elimination period for long term disability benefits, and, after the elimination period, choose between receiving long term disability benefits and remaining on sick leave (or vacation pay with departmental approval).
