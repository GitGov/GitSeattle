**Council Bill Number: 103728**

 AN ORDINANCE amending Seattle Municipal Code Subsections 25.08.520 D&E (Ordinance 108442, S 2) to substitute a fee for noise monitoring services in place of a deposit to insure compliance with the sound limits, and to authorize withholding of permission to amplify sound to ordinance violators for a period of two years; adding a subsection F and amending SMC Subsection 25.08.540A to restrict an exemption to unamplified sound.
