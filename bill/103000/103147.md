**Council Bill Number: 103147**
   
**Ordinance Number: 110701**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Criminal Code and to offenses against governmental order; amending Section 12A.16.010 of the Seattle Criminal Code to make it unlawful for a person to intentionally conceal or alter any material which he knows a public officer is attempting to obtain, secure, or preserve during a lawful investigation, search, or arrest, to require a judge to determine if a public officer was acting lawfully in a governmental function, and to define public officer.
