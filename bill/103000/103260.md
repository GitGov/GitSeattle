**Ordinance Number: 110791**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Low Income Elderly and Handicapped Housing Program; authorizing and providing for the issuance and sale of its unlimited tax levy general obligation bonds of the City in the principal amount of $48,170,000 for the purpose of providing funds to pay the costs to develop, acquire, construct and rehabilitate additional housing pursuant to a Housing Cooperation Agreement, for low-income elderly and handicapped persons in the City, authorized by the qualified electors of the City at a special election held therein on November 3, 1981; providing the form, terms and maturities of said bonds; providing for the annual levy and collection of ad valorem taxes to pay the principal of and interest on said bonds; and authorizing the Comptroller to set the sale date and fix the date of and redemption schedule for said bonds.
