**Ordinance Number: 111478**

 AN ORDINANCE declaring all nonguaranteed Local Improvement bonds and coupons obsolete, cancelling the same, terminating all accounting thereon, clearing such bonds and coupons off the City's records, and transferring all unclaimed monies held in the respective local improvement funds to the General Fund.
