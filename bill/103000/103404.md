**Council Bill Number: 103404**
   
**Ordinance Number: 110934**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Fire Department; establishing a program supported by donations for the purchase and installation of smoke detectors by volunteers free of charge in City residences of low income elderly; authorizing the Fire Chief to accept donations for the cost of such program; establishing a "Low Income Elderly Smoke Detector Installation" account in the General Donations and Gift Trust Fund for the deposit of such donations; and authorizing the Fire Chief to incur necessary expenses in connection with such program.
