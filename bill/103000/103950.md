**Ordinance Number: 111443**

 AN ORDINANCE amending Seattle Municipal Code Section 21.24.040 (Ordinance 82583, Section 2-B) to establish an equitable cost basis for special sewer connection charges on sewer construction projects by assessing those charges in constant dollar terms as measured by the relevant Consumer Price Index for all urban consumers; and to allow certain special sewer connection charges to be deferred under certain conditions for economically disadvantaged individuals.
