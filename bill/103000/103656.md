**Ordinance Number: 111144**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the City's 1983 Seattle Arts Festival commonly known as "BUMBERSHOOT" '83"; authorizing the Seattle Center Department to contract for the production of "BUMBERSHOOT '83", to charge temporary, daily admission fees to the Seattle Center grounds and arts festival events and activities thereon, and install temporary food service, crafts and souvenir sales on site; authorizing receipt and expenditure of certain grants, corporate sponsorships, and contributions; increasing expenditure allowances in the 1983 Budget of the Department; requiring certain reports to the City Council; authorizing the Seattle Center Director to contract for planning of the "BUMBERSHOOT '84) FESTIVAL; and making a reimbursable appropriation from the Emergency Fund and declaring the emergency therefor.

**Status:** Passed
   
**Date passed by Full Council:** May 23, 1983
   
**Vote:** 7-0
   
**Date of Mayor's signature:** May 27, 1983
