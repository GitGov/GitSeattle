**Council Bill Number: 20638**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32036**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Shoreland Drive, from Mt. Claire Drive to lake Park Drive, and a sewer right of way across a strip of land on lots 2 to 18 inclusive, block 70, Replat of portions of blocks 33, 34 and 70, Mt. Baker Park Addition, as described in the easement granted to the city by the Hunter Tract Improvement Company, for such sewer right of way, and accepted by Ordinance No. 31444; all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of a sewer and outfall of the proper dimensions, together with all wyes, flush tanks, manholes, catch basins and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4131, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
