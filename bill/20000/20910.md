**Council Bill Number: 20910**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32280**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2637, for the improvement of Pine Street, from Westlake Avenue to Melrose Avenue; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with armored concrete curbs with granite corners; by construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and water mains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, by construction or reconstructing concrete sidewalks where necessary.
