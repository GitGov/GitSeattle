**Council Bill Number: 20370**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31832**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Sixty-eighth Street, from Twenty-eighth Avenue Northwest to Thirty-second Avenue Northwest; and Thirtieth Avenue Northwest, from West Sixty-fifth Street to West Seventieth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4005 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
