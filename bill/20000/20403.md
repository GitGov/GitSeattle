**Council Bill Number: 20403**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31849**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2608 for the improvement of Leary Avenue, from Market Street to Southeast line of Ione Place; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; all in the City of Seattle, by paving with brick and by the construction of permanent sidewalks and granite curbs; and that the remainder of said streets be improved by the construction of temporary sidewalks and planking, said planking to be sixteen feet wide on Ione Place and eighteen feet on Leary Avenue east of 20th Avenue, and that aforesaid pavement provide for planking in center of sufficient width to place double street railway tracks thereon, and that such other work be done as may be necessary in connection therewith.
