**Council Bill Number: 20590**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32002**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Avalon Way, from West Spokane Street to 36th Avenue Southwest; 36th Avenue Southwest, from West Andover Street to West Snoqualmie Street; 37th Avenue Southwest, from West Andover Street to West Dakota Street; West Andover Street from 35th Avenue Southwest to 38th Avenue Southwest; West Dakota Street, from 35th Avenue Southwest to 37th Avenue Southwest; all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete curbs thereon; and Avalon Way, from West Spokane Street to 36th Avenue Southwest and 36th Avenue Southwest, from Avalon Way to West Alaska Street; by planking the same, and including as part of said improvement the construction and adjustment of a trestle approach to Avalon Way on West Spokane Street, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4125, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
