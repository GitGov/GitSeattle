 An Ordinance authorizing the Board of Public Works to purchase the timber on all those portions of Government Lot Two (2), Section Twenty (20); the Southwest One-quarter of the Southwest One-quarter (SW 1/4 of SW 1/4) of Section Twenty-two (22); Government Lots One (10, Two (2), Three (3), and Four (4), and the Southwest One-quarter of the Southeast One-quarter (SW 1/4 of SE 1/4) of Section Twenty-six (26), all in Township Twenty-two (22) North, Range Nine (9) East, W.
