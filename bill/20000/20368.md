**Council Bill Number: 20368**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31830**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North Seventh-eighth Street, from East Greenlake Boulevard to Meridian Avenue and Meridian Avenue, from North Seventy-seventh Street to North Eightieth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing concrete sidewalks thereon, together with crosswalks, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 4084 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
