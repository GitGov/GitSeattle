**Council Bill Number: 20713**
   
**Ordinance Number: 32110**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2573, for the improvement of 5th Avenue from Virginia Street to Vine Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation; together with armored concrete curbs and corners, and including as part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; by construction and reconstruction of concrete sidewalks where necessary and by such grading and regrading as may be necessary.
