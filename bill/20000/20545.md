**Council Bill Number: 20545**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31977**
********

 An Ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2572 for the improvement of Post Street from the north margin of Marion Street to the North margin of Marion Street to the North Margin of Seneca Street; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick or other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick gutters granite or reinforced concrete curbs, and including the necessary earth filling, the construction of necessary plank roadways, wooden bulkheads and the construction or reconstruction of all necessary concrete sidewalks, sewers, side sewers and watermains, together with the appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district.
