**Council Bill Number: 20331**
   
**Ordinance Number: 31788**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Lee Street, from Seventh Avenue West to Eighth Avenue West, all in The City of Seattle, by paving the same with stone, or other suitable material, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with armored concrete curbs and armored or granite corners, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains with such appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by the alteration, construction or reconstruction of concrete side-walks, where necessary and by such grading and regrading as may be necessary, all in accordance with plans prepared by the City Engineer, all in accordance with Majority Petition, File No. 50684, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
