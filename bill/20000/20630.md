**Ordinance Number: 32087**
********

 An ordinance ratifying and confirming the purchase of the City of Seattle under the provisions of Ordinance No. 32086, of said City, of certain timber within the existing water supply system commonly known as "Cedar River Water Supply System" from pollution; providing for the payment thereof by the issuance, execution and delivery of $75,000 general bonds of the City of Seattle, being a part of $1,000,000 bonds of said City, authorized at an election held September 6, 1911, and directing the issuance of such bonds in the sum of $75,000 and the levy and collection of taxes for the payment of the principal and interest thereof.
