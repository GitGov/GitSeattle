**Ordinance Number: 50416**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of 9th Avenue Northwest, between West 52nd Street and West 54th Street, and of West 53rd Street, from 8th Avenue Northwest to 9th Avenue Northwest; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of the above avenue and street, and for the establishing of the grades, and for the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenue and on West 53rd Street, from 8th Avenue Northwest to 11th Avenue Northwest; and providing that the entire cost of such improvement shall be paid by special assessment on the property specially benefited in the manner provided by law.
