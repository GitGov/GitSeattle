**Ordinance Number: 50952**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East 42nd Street from 48th Avenue Northeast to 50th Avenue Northeast, and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues, by grading and graveling; constructing concrete sidewalks, sewers watermains and fire hydrants and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 103595 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds".
