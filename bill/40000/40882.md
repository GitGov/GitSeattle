**Ordinance Number: 50920**
********

 An ordinance relating to, and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to, and extensions of, the existing municipal street railway system; providing for the construction and acquisition of, and payment for, track payment, property and equipment upon certain portions of said system on California Avenue and West Alaska Street, and the necessary track reconstruction incidental thereto; declaring the estimated cost thereof, as near as may be; providing for the issuance and sale of warrants to provide funds therefor; and creating and establishing a special fund to pay the principal and interest of said warrants.
