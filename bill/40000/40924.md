**Ordinance Number: 50914**

 An ordinance relating to and regulating the use, furnishing and disposition of premium stamps, commonly called trading stamps, stamps, cash discount stamps, certificates, cards, checks, tickets, coupons and other similar devices; licensing their use, furnishing and disposition for, with and in connection with the sale of goods, wares and merchandise and contracts therefor; prohibiting the use, furnishing or disposition of certain kinds of the same; defining offenses, providing penalties and declaring an emergency.
