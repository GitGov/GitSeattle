**Council Bill Number: 40652**
   
**Ordinance Number: 50867**
********

 An ordinance providing for the laying off, opening, extending and establishing of 2nd Avenue from Yesler Way to 4th Avenue South; providing for the changing and establishing of the curb grades of said avenue, and of 2nd Avenue South form 2nd Avenue as extended herein to Jackson Street, Yesler Way from 2nd Avenue South to a point approximately one hundred fifty-four (154) easterly therefrom, and Washington Street from the alley between Occidental Avenue and 2nd Avenue South to 3rd Avenue South; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for acquiring of the right to maintain the lateral support of said avenues, way and street, by making of the necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting property, and providing that the entire cost of improvement shall be paid by special assessment upon the property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.
