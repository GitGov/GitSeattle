**Council Bill Number: 71658**
   
**Ordinance Number: 80288**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 15th Avenue Northeast from East Pacific Street to approximately 158 feet south of East Northlake Avenue; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15845 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
