 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 51, Extension of Terry's First Addition, being the alley between 6th Avenue and 7th Avenue from Spring Street to Seneca Street; and certain other streets alleys; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15586 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
