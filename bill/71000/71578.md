 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 25th Avenue West from West Smith Street to approximately 170 feet south of West Ray Street; and certain other streets and avenues; by sewers and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 15774 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
