**Council Bill Number: 71190**
   
**Ordinance Number: 79832**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 10, Brygger's Second Home Addition to Ballard, being the alley between Market Street and West 56th Street, from 32nd Avenue Northwest to 34th Avenue Northwest; and certain other alleys, streets and avenues, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15298 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
