**Ordinance Number: 80116**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 4, Loyal View Addition, being the alley between 25th Avenue Northwest and 26th Avenue Northwest from West 83rd Street to West 85th Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15599 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
