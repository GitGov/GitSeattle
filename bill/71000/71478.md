**Council Bill Number: 71478**
   
**Ordinance Number: 80117**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 1, Wilcox's Addition, being the alley between North 78th Street and North 79th Street, from Ashworth Avenue to approximately 305 feet west thereof; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15642 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
