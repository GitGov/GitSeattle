**Council Bill Number: 71320**
   
**Ordinance Number: 79954**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 26, 27, 28 and 29, Woodlawn Addition to Green Lake, being the alley between 4th Avenue Northeast and Maple Leaf Place from Woodlawn Avenue to the northeasterly line of Block 29, said addition; and certain other alleys, streets and avenues, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15710 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
