**Ordinance Number: 70824**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 3, Admiralty Heights Addition, being the alley between Campbell Place Southwest and Admiral Way, from West Lander Street to the production northwesterly of the southwesterly line of the alley in said block established by Ordinance No. 58860; and the alley in said block established by Ordinance No. 58860; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 13007 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
