**Ordinance Number: 70035**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West Myrtle Street, from 36th Avenue Southwest to 39th Avenue Southwest; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by constructing concrete sidewalks, concrete stairways, grading or regrading, and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12438 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
