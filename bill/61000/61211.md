**Ordinance Number: 70063**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 21 and 22, Law's 2nd Addition, being the alley between 1st Avenue West and 2nd Avenue West, from West Garfield Street to West Blaine Street; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12773 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
