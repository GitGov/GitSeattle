 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley between Blocks 1 and 2, Queen Anne Central Addition, being the alley between 4th Avenue West and 5th Avenue West, from West McGraw Street to West Smith Street, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12934 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
