**Ordinance Number: 70286**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of that portion of the Alley in Block 6, Hunter's Lake Union Addition, lying between Wheeler Street and McGraw Street, from 4th Avenue North to Bigelow Avenue North; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12841 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
