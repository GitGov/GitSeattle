**Council Bill Number: 61851**
   
**Ordinance Number: 70701**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of West Webster Street, from California Avenue to a point approximately 155 feet west thereof; and certain parts of certain other streets and avenues; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12988 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
