**Council Bill Number: 61765**
   
**Ordinance Number: 70611**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 14, First Plat of West Seattle by the West Seattle Land and Improvement Co., being the alley between 42nd Avenue Southwest and Palm Avenue, from Palm Avenue on the north to Palm Avenue on the south; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 12966 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
