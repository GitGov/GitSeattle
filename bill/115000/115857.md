 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Thirteen Million, Twelve Thousand, Four Hundred Fourteen and 92/100 dollars ($13,012,414.92) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001193304 - 4001195894, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Sixteen Million, Forty Nine Thousand, Eight Hundred Fifty Nine and 32/100 dollars, ($16,049,859.32) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Eight Million, Seven Hundred Seventy One Thousand, Seven Hundred Seventy Four and 87/100 dollars ($28,771,774.87) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50968355 - 50969938 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 130001 - 140178 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date March 20, 2007 in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on March 29, 2007 with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 2nd day of April 2007 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this April 2, 2007.
