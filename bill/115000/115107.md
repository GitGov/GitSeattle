 ORDINANCE _________________

 AN ORDINANCE related to the City Light Department increasing appropriations to the Light Fund in the 2004 Budget for Purchased Power.

 WHEREAS, City Light has developed a new strategy to guide its power market trading with the intent of balancing the exposure to the forward and spot markets; and

 WHEREAS, this risk management strategy requires more power marketing activity (both purchases and sales) than City Light's previous risk management strategy; and

 WHEREAS, additional budget authority is required to fund the power purchases that City Light must make to stay at an optimum risk position; NOW, THEREFORE,

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. In order to pay for necessary costs and expenses incurred or to be incurred in 2004, but for which insufficient appropriations were made, the appropriation for the following in the 2004 Budget is increased from the fund shown as follows:

 Fund Name and Number Department Budget Control Level Amount

 Light Fund City Light SCL700 $28,530,264 41000

 Section 2. Any act consistent with the authority of this ordinance taken after Council passage of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
