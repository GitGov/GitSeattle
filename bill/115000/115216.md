 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Eleven Million, Four Hundred Ninety Nine Thousand, Four Hundred Sixteen and 35/100 dollars ($11,499,416.35) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000924795- 4000927009 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, plus Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) of Twenty Two Million, Six Hundred Twenty Eight Thousand, Eight Hundred Three and 15/100 dollars ($22,628,803.15) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Five Million, Three Hundred Eighty Six Thousand, Seven Hundred Twelve and 33/100 dollars ($25,386,712.33) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50877376 - 50879083 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 130001 - 139687 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date March 22, 2005 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on March 31, 2005 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 4th day of April, 2005 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this April 4, 2005.
