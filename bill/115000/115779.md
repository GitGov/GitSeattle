 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twenty Million, Four Hundred Seventy Three Thousand, Seven Hundred Sixty Seven and 12/100 dollars ($20,473,767.12) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001141929- 4001144617, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Sixty Nine Million, Four Hundred Ninety One Thousand, Eight Hundred Nineteen and 84/100 dollars,(69,491,819.84) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Eight Million, Two Hundred Ten Thousand, Three Hundred Ninety Seven and 13/100 dollars ($28,210,397.13) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50952691 - 50954273 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 450001 - 460096 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Oct. 31, 2006 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Nov. 9, 2006 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 13th day of Nov. 2006 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Nov. 13, 2006.
