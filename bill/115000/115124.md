 ORDINANCE _________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twelve Million, Two Hundred Eighty Seven Thousand, Seven Hundred Four and 57/100 dollars ($12,287,704.57) on Summit mechanical checks numbered, 4000880117- 4000882671, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Sixteen Million, Seven Hundred Forty Three Thousand, Three Hundred Forty Three and 50/100 dollars, ($16,743,343.50) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million, Six Hundred Forty One Thousand, Five Hundred Seventeen and 58/100 dollars ($24,641,517.58) on City General Salary Fund mechanical checks numbered 50861291- 50863158 plus manual checks, agencies checks, and direct deposits numbered 480001- 489648 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Nov. 16, 2004 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Nov. 24, 2004 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.
