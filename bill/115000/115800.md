 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Sixteen Million, Two Hundred Eighty Six Thousand, Nine Hundred Forty Eight and 80/100 dollars ($16,286,948.80) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001151285 - 4001153748, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Sixty Nine Million, Seventy Five Thousand, Four Hundred Sixty Four and 78/100 dollars, ($69,075,464.78) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Seven Million, Seven Hundred Twenty Four Thousand, Six Hundred Seventy Seven and 35/100 dollars ($27,724,677.35) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50955833 - 50957422 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 490001 - 5000084 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Nov. 28, 2006 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Dec. 7, 2006 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 11th day of Dec. 2006 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this Dec. 11, 2006.
