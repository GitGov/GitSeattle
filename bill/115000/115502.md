 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Eight Million, Four Hundred Forty Eight Thousand, Seven Hundred Seventy Six and 00/100 dollars ($8,448,776.00) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4001050542 - 4001053388 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, plus Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) of Seventy Million, Two Hundred Sixty Three Thousand, Two Hundred Seventy Eight and 31/100 dollars ($70,263,278.31) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCW.

 SECTION 2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 13th day of March 2006 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this March 13, 2006.
