 ORDINANCE __________________

 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Ten Million, Six Hundred Twenty Four Thousand, Three Hundred Six and 33/100 dollars ($10,624,306.33) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000945582- 4000947650, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Thirteen Million, Eight Hundred Fifteen Thousand, Two Hundred Twenty Four and 09/100 dollars, ($13,815,224.09) are presented for ratification by the City Council per Chapter 42.23RCW.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Twenty Five Million, One Hundred Eighty Six Thousand, Three Hundred Seventy and 38/100 dollars ($25,186,370.38) on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50884392 - 50886182 plus manual warrants, agencies warrants, and direct deposits numbered 210001 - 219736 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date May 17,2005 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on May 26, 2005 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 31st day of May, 2005 and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this May 31, 2005.
