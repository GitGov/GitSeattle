**Council Bill Number: 70630**
   
**Ordinance Number: 79282**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 32nd Avenue West from Government Way to West Barrett Street; and certain other streets and avenues, by paving with portland cement concrete and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15521 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
