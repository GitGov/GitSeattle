**Council Bill Number: 70349**
   
**Ordinance Number: 79010**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 3, Laurelhurst Crest, being the alley bounded by West Laurel Drive, East Laurel Drive, East 45th Street, 53rd Avenue Northeast and 54th Avenue Northeast; by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 15353 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
