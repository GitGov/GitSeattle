**Council Bill Number: 70551**
   
**Ordinance Number: 79205**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 22, North Seattle Addition, being the alley between 1st Avenue West and Queen Anne Avenue, from West Harrison Street to West Republican Street; and certain other alleys, streets and avenues, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15000 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
