**Ordinance Number: 79414**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 56th Avenue Southwest from approximately 106 feet north of West Dakota Street to Hillcrest Avenue and certain other streets, avenues, alleys and easements, by constructing sewers and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15555 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
