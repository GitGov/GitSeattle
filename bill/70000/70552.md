**Ordinance Number: 79206**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Blocks 1 and 2, Replat of Green Lake Home Addition, being the alley in the block bounded by East Green Lake Way, Latona Avenue, Sunnyside Avenue, North 75th Street and Woodlawn Avenue, by paving and otherwise improving the same; all in accordance with Resolution No. 15571 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
