**Council Bill Number: 70550**
   
**Ordinance Number: 79204**
********

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 6, Barron Adddition, being the alley between 19th Avenue Northwest, and 20th Avenue Northwest, from West 80th Street to West 83rd Street; and certain other alleys and streets; by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15263 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
