**Ordinance Number: 78934**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of The Alley in Block 39, A. A. Denny's 6th Addition, being the alley between 1st Avenue and Western Avenue, from Lenora Street to Blanchard Street; and certain other alleys, by paving and otherwise improving the same, all in accordance with Resolution No. 15325 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment by Bonds".
