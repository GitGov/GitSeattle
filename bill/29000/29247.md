**Ordinance Number: 39949**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East 60th Street, from 5th Avenue Northeast to Ravenna Boulevard; East 63rd Street, from Latona Avenue to Ravenna Boulevard; 6th Avenue Northeast, 7th Avenue Northeast and Eight Avenue Northeast, each from East 60th Street to Ravenna Boulevard; all in the City of Seattle; all in accordance with Resolution No. 6093 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
