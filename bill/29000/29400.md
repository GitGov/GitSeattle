**Ordinance Number: 40089**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Fullerton Avenue, from Lakeside Avenue to 38th Avenue; 38th Avenue, from East James Street to East Columbia Street; Norwood Place, from 38th Avenue to Randolph Avenue; Randolph Avenue, from Norwood Place to East James Sreet; 37th Avenue, from East Cherry Street to East James Street; Wellington Avenue, from the west line of Lot 4, Block 34, Yesler's 3rd Addition to 39th Avenue; 39th Avenue, from Wellington Avenue to East Columbia Street, and East James Street from Wellington Avenue to 37th Avenue; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Majority Petition No. 73982, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
