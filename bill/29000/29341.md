**Ordinance Number: 40031**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North and West 80th Street, North and West 81st Street, North and West 82nd Street, North and West 83rd Street, North and West 84th Street, each from Greenwood Avenue to 8th Avenue Northwest; 1st Avenue Northwest, from North 80th Street to North 85th Street; 3rd Avenue Northwest, from Wet 80th Street to West 85th Street; and 8th Avenue Northwest, from West 79th Street to West 85th Street; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6176, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
