**Ordinance Number: 39729**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Fifty-sixth Street, East-Seventh Street and East Fifty-eighth Street, each from Fifth Avenue Northeast to Latona Avenue; and Fifth Avenue Northeast, from East Fifty-fifth Street to East Fifty-ninth Street; all in the City of Seattle, all in accordance with Resolution No. 6104 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment of said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district payable by mode of "Payment by Bonds".
