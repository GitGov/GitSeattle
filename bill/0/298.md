**Council Bill Number: 298**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13770**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of East Prospect Street, from Eleventh Avenue North to Fiftieth Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite corners, together with all water mains and sewers, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper water service, fire protection and sewerage and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 31 of the City Council of the Seattle, provided, however that fifteen (15) percent of the total cost of this improvement shall be paid from the general fund of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment therefor, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
