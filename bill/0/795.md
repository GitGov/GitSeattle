**Council Bill Number: 795**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14171**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the laying off, extending and establishing of the North one-half (N. 1/2) of East Forty-seventh Street, in the City of Seattle, as a public Street and highway, over and across unplatted land between Pettit's University Addition and University Heights Addition, and providing for the taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor, and for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation to be made for the private property to be taken or damaged for said purpose, and for an assessment upon the property benefited, for the purpose of making such compensation.
