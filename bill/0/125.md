**Council Bill Number: 125**
   
**Ordinance Number: 10928**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Linden Avenue from Kilbourne Street to North 42nd Street; Aurora Avenue from North 39th Street to Motor Place; Whitman Avenue from North 39th Street to North 42nd Street; and North 39th Street from Fremont Avenue to Woodland Park Avenue; all in the City of Seattle by constructing wooden sidewalks, curbs and gutters, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefore, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
