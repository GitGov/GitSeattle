**Ordinance Number: 14108**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1214, for the improvement of Thackery Place, from East Forty-fifth Street to East Fiftieth Street, East Fiftieth Street, from Thackery Place to Latona Avenue; Latona Avenue, from East Fiftieth Street to East Sixty-fifth Street; East Sixty-fifth Street, from Latona Avenue to Fourth Avenue Northeast; Fourth Avenue Northeast, from East Sixty-fifth Street to Woodlawn Avenue; Woodlawn Avenue, from Fourth Avenue Northeast to Maple Leaf Place; Maple Leaf Place, from Woodlawn Avenue to Fifth Avenue Northeast; and Fifth Avenue Northeast, from Maple Leaf Place to East Eighty-second Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein water mains of proper dimensions, together with such gates, tees, crosses and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the reasonable water service of said district, under Ordinance No. 13311 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
