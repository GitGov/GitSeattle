**Council Bill Number: 557**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13978**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twenty-first Avenue, from Yesler Way to East Jefferson Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein water mains of proper dimensions, together with such gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the reasonable water service and fire protection of said portion of said street, in accordance with Resolution No. 96 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
