**Council Bill Number: 248**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13699**
********

 An Ordinance changing and establishing the grades of Westlake Avenue, from Denny Way to Mercer Street; Harrison Street, from Eighth Avenue North to Minor Avenue North; Thomas Street, from Fairview Avenue to Minor Avenue North; and Ninth Avenue North, from Thomas Street to Republican Street, all in the City of Seattle, and providing for the taking and damaging of lands and other property necessary in the grading and regrading of said Westlake Avenue and other streets and avenues, and approaches thereto, in conformity with such established grades, and in the construction of the necessary slopes and retaining walls for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenues, and providing for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation to be made for the property taken or damaged by reason of such changes of grade and the grading and regrading of said Westlake Avenue and approaches thereto in conformity with such established grades, and for an assessment upon the property specially benefited, for the purpose of making such compensation, and repealing all Ordinances or parts of Ordinance in conflict herewith.
