**Council Bill Number: 21**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13508**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Fifth Avenue North from Prospect Street to a point one hundred twenty (120) feet south of Galer Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein a sewer of property dimensions, together with all necessary wyes, man holes, catch basins, lamp holes, flush tanks, openings and other necessary appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 916 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
