**Council Bill Number: 297**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13769**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-first Avenue North, from East John Street to East Republican Street; Thirtieth Avenue North, from East Denny Way to East Republican Street; Twenty-ninth Avenue North, from East Denny Way to East Republican Street; Twentieth-eighth Avenue and Twenty-eighth Avenue North, from East Union Street to East Harrison Street; Twenty-seventh Avenue and Twenty-seventh Avenue North, from East Union Street to East Madison Street; Arthur Place, from Twenty-seventh Avenue North to Twenty-ninth Avenue North, East Harrison Street, from Twenty-eighth Avenue North to Arthur Place; East Republican Street from East Madison Street to Thirty-first Avenue North and East Denny Way from Twenty-fifth Avenue to Thirtieth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing water mains of proper dimensions, together with such gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances may be necessary for the reasonable water service and fire protection, all in accordance with Resolution No. 30 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
