**Council Bill Number: 24**
   
**Ordinance Number: 10926**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 10th Avenue Northeast, 11th Avenue Northeast, 12th Avenue Northeast, Brooklyn Avenue and 14th Avenue Northeast, all form Railroad Avenue to Northlake Avenue; and Northlake Avenue from 14th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle by constructing sewers of proper dimensions, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1014 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefore, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
