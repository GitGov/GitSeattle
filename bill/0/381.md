**Council Bill Number: 381**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13841**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the following named streets and avenues as one connected improvement, by constructing wooden sidewalks as follows, to-wit: Eighth Avenue South, from Charlestown Street to Adams Street, the sidewalks thereon to be of wood, eight feet in width and placed on both sides of the Avenue; Eighth Avenue South, from Adams Street to the south boundary of the City of Seattle; Seventh Avenue South, from Andover Street to Henrietta Street; Maynard Avenue, from Andover Street to Henrietta Street; Sixth Avenue South, from Andover Street to Henrietta Street; and Ninth Avenue South, from Dakota Street to Adams Street, the sidewalks thereon to be of wood, six feet in width, placed on both side of said streets and avenues; Andover Street from Eighth Avenue South to Seventh Avenue South; Dakota Street, from Ninth Avenue South to Maynard Avenue; Adams Street, from Seventh Avenue South to Fifth Avenue South; and Snoqualmie Street, from Eighth Avenue South to Sixth Avenue South, the sidewalks thereon to be of wood, six feet in width, and placed on the south side of said streets, all in the City of Seattle, according to Resolution No. 41 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," under provisions of Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
