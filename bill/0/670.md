**Council Bill Number: 670**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14067**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the refunding of the excess portions of all assessments levied for the improvement of Roy Street, et al, by constructing a sewer thereon, under Ordinance No. 5655, approved November 16, 1899, creating Local Improvement District No. 238, and authorizing the City Treasurer to prepare a tabulated statement showing the amounts of refunds due on the lots and parcels of land in said assessment district, and the names of the persons paying such assessments, and appropriating out of the special fund of said Local Improvement District the amounts necessary to pay such refunds, and authorizing and directing the Mayor and City Comptroller to draw warrants on such special fund in favor of the persons paying such assessments for the several amounts due such payors, as shown by said tabulated statement, and authorizing the City Treasurer to pay said warrants our of such special fund, and repealing all ordinances and parts of Ordinances in conflict herewith.
