**Council Bill Number: 559**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13979**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Thirty-ninth Avenue, from East Olive Street to Rose Avenue; Rose Avenue, from Thirty-ninth Avenue to Newport Way; Newport Way; from Rose Avenue to Lake Washington; Fortieth Avenue, from East Olive Street to East Pine Street; and East Pine Street, from Fortieth Avenue to Thirty-ninth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein sewers of proper dimensions, together with all necessary wyes, manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; there shall also be included as a necessary part of said system, the construction of a proper outfall sewer, the whole system to be constructed in accordance with Resolution No. 114 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
