**Council Bill Number: 914**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14965**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of West Forty-first Street and West Forty-second Street, from Third Avenue Northwest to Sixth Avenue Northwest; Sixth Avenue Northwest, from West Forty-first Street to West Forty-fifth Street; Fourth Avenue Northwest and Fifth Avenue Northwest, each from West Forty-second Street to West Forty-fourth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by laying water mains therein of proper dimensions, together with all gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances necessary for the reasonable water service and fire protection of said portions of said streets and avenues, in accordance with Resolution No. 209 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
