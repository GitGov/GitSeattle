**Council Bill Number: 421**
   
**Ordinance Number: 11239**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Corliss Avenue, from North Forty-second Street to Kilbourne Street; Meridian Avenue, from North Forty-second Street to Kilbourne Street, and Kilbourne Street, from Wallingford Avenue to Corliss Avenue by constructing water mains of proper dimensions in said portions of streets and avenues, together with such gates, tees, crosses fire hydrants and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the reasonable water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 97 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
