**Council Bill Number: 833**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14189**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1216, for the improvement of Twentieth Avenue North and Twentieth Avenue North produced, from East Aloha Street to East Republican Street; Twenty-first Avenue North, from East Aloha Street to East Thomas Street; Twenty-second Avenue North, from East Aloha Street to East Mercer Street; East Mercer Street, from Twenty-second Avenue North to Twenty-first Avenue North; and East Thomas Street, from Twenty-first Avenue North to Twentieth Avenue North, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and material, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and other appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 13313 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
