**Ordinance Number: 13977**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Twelfth Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Eleventh Avenue North, from Roanoke Street to Shelby Street; Tenth Avenue North, from East Newton Street to Shelby Street; North Broadway, from East Howe Street to Shelby Street; Harvard Avenue North, from East Howe Street to Roanoke Street; Franklin Avenue, from East Newton Street to Shelby Street, East Howe Street, from Harvard Avenue North to North Broadway; East Newton Street, from Franklin Avenue to Boylston Avenue North, East Lynn Street, from Boylston Avenue North to Federal Avenue; Roanoke Street, from Boylston Avenue North to Harvard Avenue North, and also from Eleventh Avenue North to Twelfth Avenue North; Shelby Street, from North Broadway to Twelfth Avenue North; Millard Street, from Harvard Avenue North to Fuhrman Avenue, Allison Street, from Franklin Avenue to Fuhrman Street; Barton Place, from Allison Street to Fuhrman Avenue; and Fuhrman Avenue, from Shelby Street to Franklin Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein water mains of proper dimensions, together with gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 86 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
