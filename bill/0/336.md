**Council Bill Number: 336**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13780**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing and directing the City Comptroller to advertise for sale the negotiable bond of the City of Seattle, in the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000.00) as voted at the general election held in the City of Seattle on the 6th day of March, 1906, under and virtue of Ordinance No. 13188, of the City of Seattle, entitled "An Ordinance submitting to the qualified electors of the City of Seattle at the general election to be held in said city on Tuesday, the 6th day of March, 1906, the question of the issuance and sale of negotiable bond of said city in the amount of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000.00) to provide money for the purchase of land for parks, parkways and playgrounds, the improvement thereof and the payment of existing indebtedness incurred in the purchase of property for parks, parkways and playgrounds", approved the 10th day of January, 1906.
