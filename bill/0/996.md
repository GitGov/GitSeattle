**Council Bill Number: 996**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14370**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1219 for the improvement of Pike Street, from Fourth Avenue to Minor Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, in the manner following, that is to pay: Between Fourth Avenue and Seventh Avenue, by widening the pavement so that the roadway shall have a total width of fifty-four (54) feet; there to be included in the necessary work the removal of the curbing, catch basins, and the levying and refurnishing of all necessary brick; also, by constructing, reconstructing and repairing sidewalks on both side of said Pike Street, from said Fourth Avenue to said Minor Avenue, under Ordinance No. 13380, and levying and assessing the amounts therefor against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown on said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
