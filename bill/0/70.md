**Council Bill Number: 70**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13529**
********

 An Ordinance establishing the grades of Olympic Way, in the City of Seattle, from Prospect Street to Tenth Avenue West, and providing for the taking and damaging of land and other property necessary in the grading of said Olympic Way and of approaches thereto in conformity with such established grades, and in the construction of the necessary slopes or retaining walls for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said way, and providing for the ascertainment and payment of the just compensation on to be made for the property taken or damaged by reason of the grading of said Olympic Way and approaches thereto, in conformity with such grades, and for an assessment upon the property specially benefited for the purpose of making such compensation.
