**Council Bill Number: 505**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13965**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Grand Boulevard from Thirteenth Avenue West to Twentieth Avenue West; Thirteenth Avenue West, from Grand Boulevard to Bertona Street (formerly Orchard Street); Fourteenth Avenue West; from Barret Street to Bertona Street (formerly Orchard Street); Sixteenth Avenue West; from Gilman Avenue to Thorndyke Avenue; Seventeenth Avenue West, from Gilman Avenue to Thorndyke Avenue; Thorndyke Avenue, from Ruffner Street to Bertona Street (formerly Orchard Street), and from Twentieth Avenue West to Smith Street; Twentieth Avenue West, from Thorndyke Avenue to Gilman Avenue; Gilman Avenue, from Twentieth Avenue West to Commodore Way; Twenty-first Avenue West, from Thorndyke Avenue to Gilman Avenue; Twenty-second Avenue West, from Thorndyke Avenue to Gilman Avenue; Twenty-third Avenue West, from Barret Street to Gilman Avenue; Twenty-fourth Avenue West, from Emerson Street to Commodore Way; and Commodore Way, from Twenty-third Avenue West to Twenty-fourth Avenue West, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing therein watermains of proper dimension, together with all necessary gates, tees, crosses, fire hydrants and other appurtenances, in accordance with Resolution No. 84 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
