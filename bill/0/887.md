**Council Bill Number: 887**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14241**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Block Seventeen (17), A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, being the alley between Fifth Avenue and Sixth Avenue, from Pike Street to Union Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading the same and by paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing sewers and water mains, with appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and water service of said alley, in accordance with Resolution No. 185 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," under the provisions of Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
