**Council Bill Number: 354**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13801**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1128, for the improvement of Westlake Avenue, from Pike Street to Denny Way, all in the City of Seattle, in the following manner: That portion of said avenue lying south of the north marginal line of Virginia Street to be improved with granite curbs, and by paving with asphalt and by constructing brick gutters, laid on proper concrete foundations; that portion of said avenue lying north of the north margin of Virginia Street to be improved by constructing wooden curbs and gutters, and by planking the roadway with five inch (5") planking; provided that no payment shall be laid on the central eighteen (18) feet of said roadway, and that where said pavement is omitted the intersections shall be planked, under Ordinance No. 12821 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
