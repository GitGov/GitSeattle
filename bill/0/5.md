**Council Bill Number: 5**
   
**Ordinance Number: 13475**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1162, for the improvement of Fourteenth Avenue from East Union Street to East Columbia Street; Fifteenth Avenue from East Union Street to East Columbia Street; East Spring Street from Fourteenth to Sixteenth Avenue; East Marion Street from Fourteenth to Sixteenth Avenue; and East Columbia Street from Fourteenth to Sixteenth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving same with asphalt, with brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite corners, with all necessary sewers and catch basins, with appurtenances, and by constructing such water mains, together with appurtenances, including hydrants therein under Ordinance No. 12931 and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
