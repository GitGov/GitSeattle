**Ordinance Number: 13971**

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North Forty-third Street and East Forty-third Street, from Stone Avenue to Brooklyn Avenue; North Forty-fifth Street and East Forty-fifth Street, from Woodland Park Avenue to Fifteenth Avenue Northeast; and Interlake Avenue, from North Forty-second Street to North Fiftieth Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same and constructing the necessary catch basins and inlets, in accordance with Resolution No. 98 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by an act of the Legislature of the State of Washington, entitled "An act authorizing the issuance and sale of bonds by cities, to pay for local improvements, providing for the payment thereof, and declaring an emergency," approved March 14, 1899, and Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
