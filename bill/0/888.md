**Council Bill Number: 888**
   
**Ordinance Number: 14242**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of the alley in Blocks Twenty-one (21) and Twenty-two (22) of A. A. Denny's Addition to the City of Seattle, being the alley between Third Avenue and Fourth Avenue, from Pine Street to Union Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, including all water mains and sewers necessary for the proper water service and sewerage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 193 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," under the provisions of Ordinance No. 5693 of said city, approved December 6, 1899.
