**Council Bill Number: 15669**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27545**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2404 for the improvement of Terry Avenue, from Howell Street to the South margin of Olive Street; and Olive Street, from Terry Avenue to Ninth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with vitrified brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with brick gutters, granite or armoured concrete curbs, and by the construction of concrete sidewalks; providing for the necessary surface drainage, and including as a part of said work, the construction or alteration of such sewers and water mains, with appurtenances, as may be necessary for the proper water service and fire protection of said district; under Ordinance No. 25236, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
