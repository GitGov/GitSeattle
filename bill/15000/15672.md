**Council Bill Number: 15672**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27548**
********

 An Ordinance approving the amended assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2378 for the improvement of the Alley in Block Seventeen (17), Boren, Denny and Yesler Addition to the Town (now City of Seattle), from Spring Street to Seneca Street, and between Fifth Avenue and Sixth Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by doing any necessary grading, and by the construction of a sewer, with appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district; under Ordinance No. 24803, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the each parcel and lot of land and part thereof, shown on said roll, declaring the same to be a first lien thereon, and providing for the collection of said assessment.
