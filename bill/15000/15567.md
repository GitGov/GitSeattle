**Council Bill Number: 15567**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27460**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West 65th Street from 28th Avenue Northwest to a point 280 feet east of 34th Avenue Northwest; West 66th Street, from 28th Avenue Northwest to 32nd Avenue Northwest; and West 68th Street form 28th Avenue Northwest to 32nd Avenue Northwest; and West 68th Street, from 28th Avenue Northwest to a point 345 feet west of 32nd Avenue Northwest; and 32nd Avenue Northwest from West 66th Street to West 67th Street, all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3217 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
