**Ordinance Number: 27014**

 An ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 25392 entitled: "An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of5th and " Avenue, in the City of Seattle, from Union Street to University Street, by grading and paving the same with brick laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with brick gutters and granite curbs, providing for the necessary surface drainage, and including as part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2916, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds" as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle" approved October 20, 1910.
