**Ordinance Number: 27508**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Ninth Avenue West from West Halliday Street to West Barrett Street; Eighth Avenue West, from West Wheeler Street to West Ray Street; Tenth Avenue West, from West Armour Street to West Barrett Street; Eleventh Avenue West, from West Ray Street to Grand Boulevard; West Halliday Street, from Seventh Avenue West to Ninth Avenue West; Bothwell Street, from Eighth Avenue West to Ninth Avenue West, and from Tenth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; West Newell Street, from Eighth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; West Armour Street from Eighth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; West Fulton Street, from Ninth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West; and West Barrett Street, from Ninth Avenue West to Eleventh Avenue West, all in The City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, together with all necessary adjustment of the existing sewer and water systems with their appurtenances, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3209 of the City Council of The City of Seattle; and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
