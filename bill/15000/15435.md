**Ordinance Number: 27363**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Brandon Street, Lucile Street and Findlay Street, each from 42nd Avenue South to 46th Avenue South; Orcas Street from 42nd Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; Mead Street and Juneau Street, each from 42nd Avenue South to 46th Avenue South; Kenny Street from 42nd Avenue South to 44th Avenue South; 42nd Avenue South, from Rainier Avenue to Juneau Street; 44th Avenue South from Juneau Street to Graham Street; 46th Avenue South, from Orcas Street to Juneau Street; Rainier Avenue from 42nd Avenue South to Kenny Street, all in the City of Seattle, by grading and curbing the same, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 3184, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
