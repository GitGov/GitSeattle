**Council Bill Number: 15376**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27285**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2453, for the improvement of 3rd Avenue from Pike Street to Pine Street, all in the City of Seattle, by widening the same to a width established by Ordinance No. 13776, by doing all necessary grading, by reconstructing and adjusting the granite curbs and brick gutters, by extending the concrete walks and asphalt pavement to the new width of the street, by all necessary adjustment of the drainage and water systems, and cluster lights with their appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 26280 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against the several lots, tracts, parcels of land and other property as shown on said roll, establishing a Local Improvement Fund.
