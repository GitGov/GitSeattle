**Council Bill Number: 15196**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27127**
********

 An ordinance appropriating the sum of $75.00 out of the General Fund of the City of Seattle, for the purpose of sending one of the City Treasurer's deputies to Spokane, Portland and Tacoma to investigate methods employed in those cities in the collection of delinquent local improvement taxes in order to aid in the establishment of a similar system in the City of Seattle as provided in Chapter 98, Session Laws 1911, of the State of Washington.
