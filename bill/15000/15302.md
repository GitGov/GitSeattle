**Council Bill Number: 15302**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27240**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 23rd Avenue North from East Lynn Street to Boyer Avenue; East Newton Street, from East Lynn Street to 23rd Avenue North, and from 23rd Avenue North to 24th Avenue North; 22nd Avenue North, from East Lynn Street to East Newton Street; 24th Avenue North, from Boyer Avenue to East Lynn Street; Boyer Avenue from west line of 23rd Avenue North to 24th Avenue North; East Lynn Street from 19th Avenue North to 24th Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, together with brick or asphalt gutters, constructing concrete or armored concrete curbs, and providing for the construction or reconstruction of all concrete sidewalks, sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; and further providing that the alley in block 4, H. S. Turner's Interlaken Addition to be improved with sewers only; all in accordance with Resolution No. 43883, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
