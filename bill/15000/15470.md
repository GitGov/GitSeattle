**Council Bill Number: 15470**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27339**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East 45th Street from 15th Avenue Northeast to 20th Avenue Northeast, all in the City of Seattle, by grading, sub-grading and paving the same with asphalt or some other suitable material laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing concrete curbs with armored concrete or granite corners, by doing all necessary grading and the by constructing concrete sidewalks where necessary, and including as a part of said work the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district all in accordance with Resolution No. 3203, of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improving district therefor, and providing for that payment for said improvement to be made by special assessment upon property in said district payable by the mode of "Payment in Bonds."
