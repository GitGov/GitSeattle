**Ordinance Number: 27090**
********

 An ordinance providing for the refund of 55 cents out of the general fund of the City of Seattle in favor of H. C. Ostrom to reimburse to said H. C. Ostrom in like sum which he paid as advertising costs in the purchase of lot 11, block 13, Brooklyn Addition at the condemnation sale of delinquent assessments of East 45th Street, et al, condemnation fund, Ordinance No. 17947, which property was inadvertently sold by the City Treasurer on March 31, 1911, when in fact the original assessment for the said condemnation had been appealed to the Supreme Court by the original owner where it is now pending a hearing.
