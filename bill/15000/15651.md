**Ordinance Number: 27542**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 25,158 entitled: "An Ordinance providing for the changing and establishing of the grades of Ninth Avenue North from Denny Way to Westlake Avenue North; and of Denny Way from Eight Avenue to Westlake Avenue, and approaches thereto, all in the City of Seattle; providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary for the grading and regrading of said avenue and street and approaches thereto, in conformity with such established grades; and for the construction of all necessary slopes for cuts and fills upon the property abutting upon said avenue and streets and approaches thereto; providing that the payment for such improvement be made by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law," approved on the 29th day of September, 1910.
