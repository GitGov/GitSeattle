**Ordinance Number: 27498**

 An ordinance submitting to the qualified voters of the City of Seattle, at the general election to be held in such city on Tuesday, September 5, 1911, the proposition of increasing the debt of said city in a sum not exceeding $546,000 by the issuance and sale of its general negotiable, serial bonds therefor, to provide money for strictly municipal purposes, providing for the form of such submission and providing for the payment of the principal and interest of said bonds.
