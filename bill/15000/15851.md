**Council Bill Number: 15851**
   
**Ordinance Number: 27697**
********

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 23596 entitled "An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Pine Street and East Pine Street, in the City of Seattle, from Belmont Avenue to the West margin of Nagle Place, by widening the pavement so that the roadway shall have a total width of forty-eight (48) feet, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading, the necessary removal of all curbs, catch basins, tees, bulkheads, retaining walls and concrete sidewalks; and by constructing and reconstructing all pavements, gutters, catch basins, concrete sidewalks, side sewers and fire hydrants, in a manner to make a complete and finished improvement, all in accordance with Resolution No. 2540 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the charter and ordinances of the City of Seattle," approved on the 18th day of March, 1910.
