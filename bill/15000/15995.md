**Ordinance Number: 27892**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2462 for the improvement of Maynard Avenue from Weller Street to Plummer Street; Dearborn Street from Sixth Avenue South to Maynard Avenue; Charles Street from Seattle Boulevard to Maynard Avenue; Seattle Boulevard from Sixth Avenue South from the south to Maynard Avenue and approaches thereto, all in The City of Seattle, by relaying all existing planking that is suitable; by relaying wood walks; by constructing wood curbs and gutters; by furnishing any new lumber necessary and providing for the necessary surface drainage; under Ordinance No. 26518, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
