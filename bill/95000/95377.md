 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Department of Parks and Recreation; authorizing a two party cooperative agreement between the City and Burlington Northern Railway for taking immediate corrective measures to control the emergency earth-slide problems in Golden Gardens Park; authorizing an agreement with Neil Twelker & Associates and others to effectuate such control and waiving competitive bidding requirements in connection with such emergency; making an appropriation from the Emergency Fund to provide the City's share of costs for such corrective measures and declaring the emergency therefor.
