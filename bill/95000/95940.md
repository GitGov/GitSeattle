**Ordinance Number: 104165**

 An ordinance providing for the improvement of 21st Ave. Northeast from Northeast 65th Street to approximately 300 feet north thereof; by constructing watermains together with fire hydrants and the necessary appurtenances; all in accordance with Resolution No. 24100 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, creating a local improvement district therefor, and providing that payment for said improvement be made by special assessments upon property in said district by the mode of installment notes.
