 AN ORDINANCE relating to gambling; amending Ordinance 102843 (Seattle Criminal Code) by adding thereto a new section designated Section 12A.36.200 to prohibit as of May 1, 1975 punch boards and pull-tabs in accordance with Chapter 218, Laws of Washington, 1973 1st Ex. Sess. as amended, and amending Section 1 of Ordinance 102459 to eliminate as of May 1, 1975 the tax levied upon the utilization of said punch boards and pull-tabs.
