**Ordinance Number: 104063**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to bowling alleys; amending Ordinance 72495 by adding thereto a new section designated Section 3.1, to impose an admissions tax upon charges made for the rental or use of equipment or facilities in bowling alleys, and amending Section 23 of the License Code (Ordinance 48022) to provide a uniform annual license fee for all bowling alleys.
