**Ordinance Number: 103667**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to Washington Park (commonly known as "the Arboretum") prohibiting any restriction of public rights or access by (1) building fences, barriers, or gates, (2) levying an admission charges or entrance fee, or (3) leasing park lands for the construction of university classrooms, offices, laboratories, administration buildings or other non-park uses.
