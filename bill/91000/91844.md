**Council Bill Number: 91844**
   
**Ordinance Number: 100220**
********

 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an agreement with the United States Department of Labor for a grant from the Emergency Employment Act of 1971 in the approximately amount of $813,200 to provide employment opportunity for citizens; creating an Office of Emergency Employment in the Office of Human Resources for the administration of said grant; establishing certain positions in the Office of Emergency Employment; establishing an Emergency Employment Operating Fund, making appropriation from the Emergency Fund and authorizing expenditures pursuant to those appropriations; authorizing the Office of Emergency Employment to enter into agreements with City departments for services outlined in the Emergency Employment Act of 1971, and authorizing a sub-contract with the Seattle School District No. 1 for services outlined in the Emergency Employment Act.
