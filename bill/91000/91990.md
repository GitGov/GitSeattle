**Council Bill Number: 91990**
   
**Ordinance Number: 100391**

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the Seattle Model City Program, adopting an amendment to the City's Grant Agreement with the United States to expand the geographic scope of the program city-wide and to undertake expanded projects, with a federal grant therefor; defining critical neighborhoods for emphasis in projects and activities; establishing certain positions and abrogating others; increasing certain budget accounts and making appropriations; authorizing an agreement with the Seattle Husing Authority; amending Ordinance 98770 as to authorization of vouchers; making a reimbursable appropriation from the Emergency Fund, and declaring the emergency therefor.
