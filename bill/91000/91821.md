 AN ORDINANCE adopting the findings of the Committee of the Whole of the City Council of The City of Seattle following public hearing held pursuant to Resolution 22906 to determine necessity for and location and design of a proposed pedestrian overpass across Rainier Avenue South, as modified to extend across that portion of Empire Way South, southwesterly of Rainier Avenue South; ordering and declaring that the public convenience and necessity require that the same be constructed at the designated location and authorizing the City Engineer to make application to the Federal Highway Administration and the Washington State Highway Department for additional funds in connection with said extension over Empire Way South.
