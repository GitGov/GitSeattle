**Ordinance Number: 99997**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to and specifying and adopting a plan or system of additions and betterments to and extensions of the existing municipal sewerage utility; declaring the estimated cost thereof as near as may be and providing for the issuance and sale of utility revenue bonds in the amount of not to exceed $15,500,000 to finance in part such plan or system; designating the bond redemption fund for the payment of the principal of and interest on such bonds and on revenue bonds hereafter issued on a parity therewith; and providing for the issuance of said bonds on a parity with those issued under Ordinance 87653 and like bonds hereafter authorized, as contemplated by Chapter 35.92, Revised Code of Washington; and abandoning and superseding the authority to issue $11,000,000 of unissued sewerage revenue bonds of a total of $18,000,000 of such bonds authorized by Ordinance 87653.
