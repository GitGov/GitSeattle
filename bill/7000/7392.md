**Council Bill Number: 7392**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20391**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 15th Avenue Northeast, in the City of Seattle, between East 50th Street and East 55th Street, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with stone, brick, concrete or asphalt gutters; with concrete curbs and armored concrete or granite corners, and by the construction of all water mains, sewers, and side sewer connections necessary and by the construction of a necessary surface and sub-drains and by the construction of a concrete sidewalks along the west side of 15th Avenue Northeast between East 50th Street and East 52nd Street; all in accordance with Resolution No. 1860 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
