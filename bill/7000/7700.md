**Ordinance Number: 20440**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Maple Leaf Place, from 5th Avenue Northeast to 6th Avenue Northeast; East 74th Street, from 5th Avenue Northeast to the intersection of East 73rd Street and from 9th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast; Emma Place, from East 73rd Street to 9th Avenue Northeast; Banner Place, from East 75th Street to Emma Place; 6th Avenue Northeast and 9th Avenue Northeast, each from East 73rd Street to East 75th Street; and East 75th Street, from 6th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, together with all necessary appurtenances for the proper surface drainage of said district in all in accordance with Resolution No. 1787 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
