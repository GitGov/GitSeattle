**Council Bill Number: 7701**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20360**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Western Avenue from the south margin of Virginia Street to Denny Way; Virginia Street, Lenora Street, Blanchard Street, Bell Street, Battery Street, Wall Street, Vine Street, Cedar Street, Clay Street, Broad Street, Eagle Street and Bay Street; each from Western Avenue to 1st Avenue; all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with stone blocks and vitrified brick or asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation; by constructing brick or stone gutters with granite curbs or with concrete or armored concrete curbs with granite corners; by the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks; by the construction, alteration of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district; by doing all necessary grading and regarding; by constructing approaches where necessary to Elliott Avenue, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1971 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
