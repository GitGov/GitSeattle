**Council Bill Number: 7096**
   
**Ordinance Number: 19989**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1768, for the improvement of East Aloha Street and East Roy Street, each from North Broadway to Eleventh Avenue North, Tenth Avenue North and Eleventh Avenue North, each from the north line of Lincoln Pontius Addition to the City of Seattle to East Prospect Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick gutters, concrete curbs with granite or armoured concrete corners and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regrading and the construction of all sewers and water mains with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage, water service and fire protection of said district, under Ordinance No. 18667, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
