**Council Bill Number: 7532**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20315**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Vine Street from Second Avenue to Fourth Avenue; Wall Street from Second Avenue to Fourth Avenue; Battery Street from Second Avenue to Third Avenue; Third Avenue from the south margin of Battery Street to Cedar Street, and Fourth Avenue from the south margin of Wall Street to Clay Street, all in the City of Seattle, by paving the same with asphalt or brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, by constructing brick, concrete or asphalt gutters and granite curbs, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regarding, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks the construction or extension of all sewers and water mains, with their appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1897 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle

**Status:** Passed
