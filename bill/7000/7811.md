**Council Bill Number: 7811**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20520**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of North Fifty-ninth Street, North Sixtieth Street, North Sixty-first Street, North Sixty-second Street each from Whitman Avenue to Phinney Avenue; North Sixty-third Street, from Aurora Avenue to Phinney Avenue; North Sixty-fourth Street, from West Green Lake Boulevard to Greenwood Avenue; North Sixty-fifth Street, from West Green Lake Boulevard to Dayton Avenue; Greenwood Avenue, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Fifty-ninth Street; Phinney Avenue, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Fifty-ninth Street, West Green Lake Boulevard, from North Sixty-third Street to North Sixty-fifth Street; Whitman Avenue, from North Fifty-ninth Street to North Sixty-second Street, and from the intersection of Whitman Avenue and North Sixty-second Street diagonally across unplatted land to the intersection of Aurora Avenue and North Sixty-third Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and other appurtenances, necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, in accordance with Resolution No. 1970 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
