**Ordinance Number: 20408**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East 70th Street, East 71st Street, East 72nd Street, East 73rd Street, each from 5th Avenue Northeast to 10th Avenue Northeast; 6th Avenue Northeast, 8th Avenue Northeast, and 9th Avenue Northeast, each from East 70th Street to East 73rd Street; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same, and by constructing concrete sidewalks and crosswalks thereon, and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1774 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
