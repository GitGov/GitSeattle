**Council Bill Number: 7611**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20442**
********

 An ordinance approving and confirming the assessment and assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1785, for the improvement of 1st Avenue in the City of Seattle on the westerly side thereof, from a point 160 feet south of the south margin of Union Street to a point 120 feet north of the north margin of Union Street, by constructing concrete sidewalks, where necessary, concrete retaining walls, and including as a part of said improvement the making of all necessary earth fills, the readjustment of granite curbs and the connecting of concrete walks to the existing improvements, under Ordinance No. 18684 and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
