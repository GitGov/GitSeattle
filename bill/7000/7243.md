**Ordinance Number: 20262**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1826, for the improvement of West Sixtieth Street and West Sixty-first Street, each from Twenty-second Avenue Northwest to Twenty-fifth Avenue Northwest, and West Sixty-second Street, from a point two hundred sixty-eight (268) feet east of Twenty-fourth Avenue Northwest to Twenty-eighth Avenue Northwest, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 19018, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
