**Ordinance Number: 19886**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1505, for the improvement of East Fortieth Street, from Latona Avenue to Tenth Avenue Northeast; and Sixth Avenue Northeast from Northlake Avenue to East Fortieth Street, by grading and curbing the same, provided, however, that East Fortieth Street, from Latona Avenue to Seventh Avenue Northeast, shall be improved by constructing a double roadway, including the necessary bulkheads and stairways, under Ordinance No. 16040, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
