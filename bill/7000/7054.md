**Ordinance Number: 19950**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1816, for the improvement of Lane Street from a point one hundred twenty (120) feet west of Twentieth Avenue South to Rainier Avenue; Rainier Avenue, from Lane Street to Dearborn Street and Dearborn Street, from Hiawatha Place to Rainier Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing a sewer therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all manholes, catch basins, flush tanks, and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 19008, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
