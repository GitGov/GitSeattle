**Council Bill Number: 7892**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20706**
********

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1880, for the improvement of Bagley Avenue, Meridian Avenue and Corliss Avenue, each from North Forty-fifth Street to North Thirty-seventh Street, and North Thirty-seventh Street, from Meridian Avenue to Sunnyside Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with such manholes, catch basins, flush tanks and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper sewering and draining of said district, under Ordinance No. 19548, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
