 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1917, for the improvement of Pine Street, from Ninth Avenue to Melrose Avenue, and Terry Avenue and Boren Avenue, each from Pike Street to Pine Street, all in The City of Seattle, by planking the same and by constructing wood walks thereon, provided that the planking on Boren Avenue and Terry Avenue form Pike Street to Pine Street be twenty (20) feet in width, under Ordinance No. 19788, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
