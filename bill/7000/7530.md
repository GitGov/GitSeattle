**Ordinance Number: 20424**

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of North and West 65th Street, from Greenwood Avenue to 8th Avenue Northwest on the south; 3rd Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to the north line of the Electric Motor Line Addition to the City of Seattle; 4th Avenue Northwest and 5th Avenue Northwest, each from West 65th Street to West 73rd Street; 7th Avenue Northwest, from West 65th Street to the north line of Peterson's Addition to the City of Seattle; 8th Avenue Northwest from West 65th Street to West 70th Street; all in the City of Seattle by grading and curbing the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1898 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
