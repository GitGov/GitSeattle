**Council Bill Number: 7937**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20720**
********

 An Ordinance authorizing the Mayor and City Comptroller and ex- officio City Clerk of the City of Seattle, in behalf of the city, to enter into a contract with The Seattle Electric Company whereby, for valuable consideration to be paid by The Seattle Electric Company, the city will grade for use of The Seattle Electric Company a twenty (20) foot roadbed upon and along the portion of East Green Lake Boulevard which is between North Fiftieth Street and North Seventy-seventh Street.
