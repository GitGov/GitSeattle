**Council Bill Number: 7320**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20385**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Garfield Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to 4th Avenue North; Hayes Street from 1st Avenue North to 4th Avenue North; Blaine Street from 1st Avenue North to 4th Avenue North; Warren Avenue North, 2nd Avenue North, 3rd Avenue North, and Nob Hill Avenue, each from Galer Street to Howe Street; all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick, asphalt or concrete gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regarding, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete walks, and providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district all in accordance with Resolution No. 1778 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
