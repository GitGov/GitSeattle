**Council Bill Number: 7092**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20368**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of East Denny Way from Eastlake Avenue to Belmont Avenue North; East Howell Steret from Bellevue Avenue to Boylston Avenue; East Olive Street, from Melrose Avenue to Belmont Avenue North; Loretta Place from Bellevue Avenue North to Summit Avenue North; Summit Avenue and Summit Avenue North from East Olive Street to East Thomas Street; Belmont Avenue from East Olive Street to East Howell Street; Bellevue Avenue from East Pine Street to East Denny Way; all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing concrete curbs with granite or armored concrete corners and brick or concrete gutters, including all grading, regarding and the repairing, alteration and extension of concrete walks, and providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district all in accordance with Resolution No. 1756 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
