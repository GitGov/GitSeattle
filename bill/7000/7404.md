**Ordinance Number: 20399**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 32nd Avenue South from Irving Street to the south line of All Around View Addition to the City of Seattle; 31st Avenue South, from Judkins Street to Massachusetts Street; and Atlantic Street from 31st Avenue South to 32nd Avenue South; all in the City of Seattle, by the construction of sewers and side sewer connections, together with all necessary manholes and flush tanks, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1868 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
