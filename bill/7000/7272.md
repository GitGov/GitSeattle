**Ordinance Number: 20002**

 An Ordinance repealing Ordinance No. 19257, entitled: "An Ordinance providing for the laying off, extending, widening and establishing of Fremont Avenue, from North Sixty-fourth Street to North Sixty-fifth Street, and from a point four and ten one-hundredths (4.10) feet south from the south margin of North Sixty-seventh Street, to a point one hundred twelve (112) feet south from the south margin of North Seventy-second Street; and North Sixty-fifth Street, from Fremont Avenue to West Green Lake Boulevard, all in the City of Seattle; and providing for the condemnation, appropriation, taking and damaging of land and other property necessary therefor; and providing that the payment for such improvement be made by special assessment upon property specially benefited, in the manner provided by law.", approved the 6th day of October, 1908.
