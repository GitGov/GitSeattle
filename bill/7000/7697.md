**Council Bill Number: 7697**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20519**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Broadway and North Broadway, from the north line of East Pike Street to the south line of East Roy Street, all in the City of Seattle, by the installation of a system of cluster lights, or of the Daniels Boulevard Lighting System, along the curb line of each side of said street, together with the installation of such poles, conduits, wires and other appurtenances as may be necessary for the proper installation and equipment of said system, and provided, that said lights be placed at a distance of about ninety (9) feet apart, in accordance with Resolution No. 1983 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Payment by Bonds," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
