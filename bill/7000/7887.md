**Council Bill Number: 7887**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20818**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1741, for the improvement of Fourth Avenue and Fourth Avenue South, in the City of Seattle, from the north margin of Washington Street to the south margin of Madison Street, by paving the same with vitrified brick, laid upon a proper concrete foundation, with granite curbs and concrete sidewalks, together with water mains of proper dimensions and materials, with all necessary appurtenances, under Ordinance No. 18473, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
