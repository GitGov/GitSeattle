**Council Bill Number: 7235**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20379**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Spokane Street and West Spokane Street from 1st Avenue South to Arizona Avenue; and 26th Avenue Southwest from West Spokane Street to West Andover Street, by piling and planking the same with a plank roadway 24 feet in width, with a 6 foot sidewalk, the location of the improvement to be as follows: the center line of the roadway from 1st Avenue South to a point 200 feet east of the drawbridge (which is located in the west waterway) shall be 28 feet from the south margin of Spokane Street and West Spokane Street; thence crossing the draw-bridge the improvement shall be located on the north side of the existing plank roadway to 26th Avenue Southwest; from 26th Avenue Southwest to Arizona Avenue, the center line of the roadway shall be 28 feet from the south margin of West Spokane Street and on 26th Avenue Southwest the bridge shall be located on the west side of the existing bridge, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1754 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
