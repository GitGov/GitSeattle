**Council Bill Number: 7321**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20386**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Howe Street from Queen Anne Avenue to 4th Avenue North; Newton Street form 1st Avenue North to 4th Avenue North; Crockett Street from Queen Anne Avenue to 4th Avenue North; 1st Avenue North from Blaine Street to north margin of McGraw Street on the west; Warren Avenue North and 2nd Avenue North, each from Howe Street to Boston Street; 3rd Avenue North from Howe Street to Crockett Street; Nob Hill Avenue, from Howe Street to Boston Street; McGraw Street, from Queen Anne Avenue to 1st Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick, asphalt or concrete gutters and concrete curbs with granite or armored granite corners, and including as a part of said work, all necessary grading, regarding, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side  sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1779 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
