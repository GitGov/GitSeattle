**Council Bill Number: 7184**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20375**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 3rd Avenue West, from West Howe Street to West Dravus Street on the east; 4th Avenue West, from West Howe Street to West Dravus Street; West Dravus Street, from 4th Avenue West to 3rd Avenue West; 5th Avenue West from West Garfield Street to West Bertona Street; West Bertona Street from 5th Avenue West to 4th Avenue West; 2nd Avenue West from West Crockett Street to West Ray Street; West Ray Street, from 2nd Avenue West to 3rd Avenue West; all in the City of Seattle by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1780 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
