**Council Bill Number: 7314**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20380**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of West Blaine Street, from 3rd Avenue West to the west margin of 7th Avenue West; West Howe Street from 3rd Avenue West to 7th Avenue West; West Crockett Street from 3rd Avenue West to 7th Avenue West; 3rd Avenue West from West Garfield Street to north margin of West Boston Street; 4th Avenue West, 5th Avenue West, and 6th Avenue West, each from West Garfield Street to West Crockett Street; 7th Avenue West from West Garfield Street to West Blaine Street; all in the City of Seattle by paving the same with asphalt laid upon a proper concrete foundation, and by constructing brick, asphalt or concrete gutters and concrete corners, and granite or armored concrete corners, and including as a part of said work all necessary grading and regarding, the construction, alteration or extension of concrete sidewalks, providing for the construction or reconstruction of all sewers, side sewers and watermains, with their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage, drainage, water service and fire protection of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1602 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
