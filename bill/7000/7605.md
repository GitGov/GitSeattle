**Ordinance Number: 20318**
********

 An Ordinance providing for the improvement of Occidental Avenue from the south margin of Connecticut Street to a connection with the existing bridge on Holgate Street; Atlantic Street and Massachusetts Street, each from First Avenue South to Occidental Avenue, all in the City of Seattle, by planking the same with a plank roadway twenty (20) feet in width to be placed in the center of the street, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1914 of the City Council of the City of Seattle, and providing for the payment therefor by the mode of "Immediate Payment," as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle
