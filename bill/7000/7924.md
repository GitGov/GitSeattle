 An Ordinance ratifying and confirming all proceedings relating to the issuance and sale of negotiable special fund bonds of The City of Seattle in the sum of Two Million Two Hundred and Fifty-Thousand ($2,250,000) Dollars, to pay for the making of certain extensions to the Cedar River Supply System of the City of Seattle; authorizing the City Comptroller to sell and deliver the unissued part of said bonds, and ratifying and confirming the establishment and creation of a special fund called "Cedar River Water Supply Fund of Seattle, Series No. 2", for the payment of the principal and interest of said bonds.
