**Ordinance Number: 20311**

 An Ordinance approving the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 1782, for the improvement of Beacon Avenue, from Lander Street to Hanford Street; Sixteenth Avenue South and Seventeenth Avenue South, each from Beacon Avenue to Hanford Street, all in the City of Seattle, by constructing sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, with all wyes, openings, flush tanks, manholes, lampholes, catch basins and other appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, under Ordinance No. 18681, and levying and assessing the amounts thereof against each parcel and lot of land and part thereof shown in said roll, and providing for the collection thereof.
