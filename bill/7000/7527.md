**Council Bill Number: 7527**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20421**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of Ray Street and West Ray Street, from the east margin of 2nd Avenue North to west margin of 1st Avenue West; Florentia Street and West Florentia Street, from the east margin of 1st Avenue North to the west margin of 1st Avenue West; Newell Street and West Newell Street, Armour Street and West Armour Street, Fulton Street and West Fulton Street, each from 1st Avenue North to 1st Avenue West; 1st Avenue North and Queen Anne Avenue, each from Ray Street to Florentia Street; and 1st Avenue West from West Ray Street to West Florentia Street; all in the City of Seattle by grading the same and providing for the necessary surface drainage, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1607 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
