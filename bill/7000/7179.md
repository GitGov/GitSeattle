**Council Bill Number: 7179**
   
**Ordinance Number: 20372**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for the improvement of 1st Avenue West from West Crockett Street to West Florentia Street; Florentia Street and West Florentia Street from 3rd Avenue West to Warren Avenue North; Warren Avenue North from Florentia Steret to Etrurua Street; Queen Anne Avenue, from Boston Street to Florentia Street; 1st Avenue North from Boston Street to Florentia Street; Warren Avenue North from Boston Street to McGraw Street; McGraw Street from Warren Avenue North to 1st Avenue North; all in the City of Seattle by the construction of sewers therein of proper dimensions and materials, together with all necessary wyes, openings, manholes, flush tanks, catch basins, and their appurtenances necessary for the proper sewerage and drainage of said district, all in accordance with Resolution No. 1783 of the city council of the City of Seattle; and providing payment therefor by the mode of "Payment in Bonds", as provided by the laws of the State of Washington and the Charter and Ordinances of the City of Seattle.
