**Ordinance Number: 63600**

 An ordinance relating to the municipal light and power plant and system; directing the Superintendent of Lighting to make application to the Reconstruction Finance Corporation for a loan for the use of the City of Seattle in developing the Ruby Dam unit of the Skagit River Hydro-electric Power project, and for such purpose to proceed to Washington, D. C., to negotiate with the officials of said corporation, and making an appropriation to defray the cost and expense thereof.
