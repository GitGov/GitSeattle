**Ordinance Number: 63392**
********

 An ordinance relating to and providing for a permanent overhead bridge or viaduct and roadway over and along Emerson Street, West Emerson Place, and Thurman Street from 15th Avenue West to 23rd Avenue West; directing certain railroad companies at their own cost and expense to construct and thereafter maintain and replace certain portions of said structure with necessary approaches and landings or in the alternative to contribute the sum of Two Hundred Seventy-three Thousand, Twenty Seven Dollars ($273,027.00) toward the cost of the construction of said structure.
