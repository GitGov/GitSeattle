 AN ORDINANCE, relating to the Seattle Department of Parks and Recreation, authorizing acceptance of a $75,000 grant from the Boeing Junior Mariners program to help renovate the ballfield at Judkins Park.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. The Superintendent of Parks and Recreation is hereby authorized to execute, deliver and perform for and on behalf of The City of Seattle, agreements with the Seattle Mariners, which provide Seventy-five Thousand Dollars ($75,000) to the City to make improvements to the ballfield at Judkins Park.

 Section 2. Any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
