 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 Be it ordained by the City of Seattle as follows:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twelve Million Five Hundred Nine Thousand Seven Hundred Forty Nine and 74/100 ($12,509,749.74) dollars on SFMS. Warrant Issue mechanical warrants numbered 40794065- 4079649 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Jan 30,1997 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2. Payment of the sum of Eighteen Million Nine Hundred Ninety Four Thousand Nine Hundred Thirty Two and 98 dollars on City General Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50199820--50203258 plus manuals, agencies, and direct deposits numbered 50001--57615 representing Gross Payrolls for payroll ending date Dec. 24, 1996 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Jan 30, 1997 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 3 day of Feb. 1997, and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this 3 day of Feb. 1997
