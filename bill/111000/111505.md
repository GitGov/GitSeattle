 AN ORDINANCE authorizing an expenditure from the Judgment/Claims Fund to be reimbursed by the Drainage & Wastewater Fund to settle the claim of SRO Properties, Inc. (C-59121), all by a two-thirds vote of the City Council.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1: Upon presentation of a proper release, the City Finance Director is authorized to draw and pay a warrant in the amount of Twenty-Seven Thousand Six Hundred Ninety-Two and 71/100 Dollars ($27,692.71). This sum constitutes settlement and full satisfaction of the claim of SRO Properties, Inc. for a side sewer repair on or about 10-3-95 at 206-210 S. Jackson, AKA, 318-322 Second Avenue S. in Seattle as specified in Clerk's File C-59121. The sum shall be paid from the Judgment/Claims Fund from sums heretofore appropriated for such purposes. The Judgment/Claims Fund shall be reimbursed by the Drainage & Wastewater Fund.

 Section 2: Pursuant to RCW 35.32A.060(3), this Ordinance, being an Ordinance required to allow a claim or appropriated money or other property to pay or satisfy the same, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
