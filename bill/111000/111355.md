 AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and ordering the payment thereof.

 Be it ordained by the City of Seattle as follows:

 SECTION 1. Payment of the sum of Twenty Four Million One Hundred

 Eighty Three Thousand Four Hundred and 63 on SFMS Warrant Issue mechanical warrants numbered 40728685-- 40731515 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Aug. 8,1996

 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 2 . Payment of the sum of Three Million Three Hundred Fifty Two Thousand Eight Hundred Forty Five and 03 dollars on City Light Salary Fund mechanical warrants numbered 50063082--50063680 plus manual and agency issues representing Gross Payroll for payroll ending date July 30, 1996 as detailed in the Payroll Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council on Aug.8, 1996 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.

 SECTION 3. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.

 SECTION 4. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the provisions of the City Charter.

 Passed by the City Council on the 12 day of Aug., 1996, and signed by me in open session in authentication of its passage this 12 day of Aug., 1996
