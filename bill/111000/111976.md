 ORDINANCE ___________________

 AN ORDINANCE related to public works contracts, extending the Public Works Apprenticeship Pilot Program authorized in Ordinance 117656 through March 31, 1998.

 BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:

 Section 1. The Apprenticeship Pilot Program established for public works projects by Ordinance 117656 and extended by Ordinance 118577 is hereby further extended from December 31, 1997 through March 31, 1998.

 Section 2. Any acts taken consistent with the intent but prior to the effective date of this Ordinance are hereby ratified and confirmed.

 Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect and be in force thirty (30) days from and after its approval by the Mayor, but if not approved and returned by the Mayor within ten (10) days after presentation, it shall take effect as provided by Municipal Code Section 1.04.020.
