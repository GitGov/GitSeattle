

********

**Council Bill Number: 111357**
********

 AN ORDINANCE relating to the acquisition of land; authorizing the Mayor or the Superintendent of Parks and Recreation to accept, for general municipal purposes, real property being conveyed to the City as the result of the efforts of the Woodland Park Zoological Society (WPZS), being a portion of the Northeast Quarter of the Northwest Quarter and the West Half of the Northeast Quarter, all in Section 33, Township 20 North, Range 6 East, W.M.; stating the City's intention to use the property for a Woodland Park Zoo Offsite Holding and Breeding Facility; authorizing acceptance of the deed; acknowledging the procuring of this land as partial fulfillment of the WPZS's fundraising commitment in connection with the 1985 Zoo Bonds; and authorizing the payment of expenses related to the transfer of the real property to the City.

**Status:** RETIRED 99
   
   
**Date introduced/referred to committee:** August 12, 1996
   
**Committee:** Parks, Public Grounds and Recreation
   
**Sponsor:** DONALDSON
   
   
**Index Terms:** ZOO, LAND-ACQUISITION
